Messenger corps (David Zebedee’s)

See also: UB 157:0.2; UB 163:5.2-3.

25 sentinels posted about Zebedee house. [1]26 sent forth as heralds of resurrection. [2]Bethlehem was Judean headquarters. [3]Carried funds to apostles and their families. [4]Carried news Jesus would be crucified. [5]Directed by David Zebedee. [6]Discontinued after Pella. [7]Employed 40–50 messengers. [8]Followed Jesus in boat. [9]Kept groups fully informed; great value of. [10]Maintained clearinghouse at Gethsemane. [11]Maintained permanent headquarters for kingdom. [12]Nathan of Busiris was herald at Alexandria. [13]Numbered over 400. [14]Provided fish for temple tax payment. [15]Safeguarded Jesus. [16]Spread report of Jesus’ triumphal entry into Jerusalem. [17]Unseen messenger ran by side of Jacob. [18]Would not heed words of doubt over resurrection. [19]
