Honesty

Base life on highest truth. [1]Cf: mistake always to speak one’s mind. [2]Early traders regarded it permissible to cheat strangers. [3]Face facts of life. [4]Following truth wherever it leads, and regardless of conclusions. [5]Fruit of the spirit. [6]Jesus embraced truth regardless of apparent source. [7]Regarding property, honesty originally rested on superstition. [8]Truth makes man free. [9]Truth must strike in heart with living roots. [10]
