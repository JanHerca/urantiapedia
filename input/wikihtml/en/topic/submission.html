Submission

See also: UB 178:3.3.

Active and alert submission to Father’s will. [1]Adjuster co-operation is ceasing to resist. [2]As concerns the morrow, we are in Father’s hands. [3]

Examples

Jesus did not change apostles’ seating at Last Supper. [4]Jesus humbled himself, became obedient unto death. [5]Jesus lived life in channel of its natural flowing. [6]Jesus submitted to humiliation by temple guards. [7]

Find God through submission to indwelling spirit. [8]Go forth in humble obedience to do God’s bidding. [9]Having done best, cheerfully accept one’s lot. [10]Holy Spirit, Spirit of Truth limited by our obedience. [11]If we do as Jesus taught, we abide in his love. [12]Jesus’ faith securely held him. [13]Jesus’ prayers were surrender of will. [14]Lean not upon one’s own understanding. [15]Letting go of God. [16]No survival if antagonism exists to Adjuster’s mission. [17]Pentecost was act of intelligent self-surrender. [18]Receiving that which awaits us. [19]Submission to the will of God. [20]Submitting to natural and ordinary course of events. [21]Surrendering every wish to the transforming embrace of spiritual growth. [22]Thinking men fear to be held by a religion. [23]To will of God does not submerge, but augments, personality. [24]When religion is permitted truly to possess man. [25]Yielding control of soul powers to indwelling spirit. [26]
