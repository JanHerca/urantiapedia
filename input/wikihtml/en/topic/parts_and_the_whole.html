Parts and the whole

See also: UB 12:7; UB 12:9.3; UB 131:8.4.

As it is with the parts, so it is with the whole. [1]Evolution of parts is segmented reflection of purposive growth of whole. [2]From standpoint of eternity, welfare of part and whole indistinguishable. [3]Group members reap benefits and suffer consequences of others’ actions. [4]Orvonton’s purpose is meaning-of-the-whole. [5]Parts and individuals of grand universe evolve as reflection of total evolution of Supreme. [6]Potential may fail to actualize with respect to a part, but never in aggregate. [7]Survival of part depends on identification with whole. [8]Trinity manifests regard for the whole, not the parts. [9]Understanding relationship of parts to each other requires understanding relationship of each to the whole. [10]Universe government rather risk rebellion than deprive one soul of eternal life. [11]Universe is integrated aggregation of real units subject to destiny of the whole. [12]Whole is dependent on total acts of manifold parts. [13]Whole often more than, or different from, sum of parts. [14]
