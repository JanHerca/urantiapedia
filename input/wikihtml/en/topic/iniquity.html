Iniquity

Defined as

Cosmic insanity; defiance of reality; indicates vanishing personality control. [1]Destroying connection of soul with spirit. [2]Eternally rejecting divine forgiveness. [3]Inherently and automatically suicidal. [4]Persistent pursuit of sin and error; transgression of divine law. [5]Reveals transient reality of God-unidentified selfhood. [6]Wholehearted rebellion against universe, divine reality. [7]

Depart, all who delight in iniquity. [8]Father abhors iniquity. [9]Habitual sin leads to. [10]Iniquiters’ doom is sealed when they lose the desire to know God and be like him. [11]Iniquiters never experience sorrow or accept forgiveness for misdeeds. [12]Jesus saw most men as weak rather than wicked. [13]Some embrace iniquity. [14]
