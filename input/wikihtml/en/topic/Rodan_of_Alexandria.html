Rodan of Alexandria

See also: UB 160; UB 161.

At Jesus’ 12th appearance. [1]Harmonized philosophy with Judaism. [2]Instructed by former apostle of John the Baptist. [3]Instructed inquiring Greeks. [4]Jesus assigned Nathaniel and Thomas to talk with. [5]Jesus declined to enter into conference with. [6]Martyred in Greece. [7]One of greatest of his race. [8]Pupils of Rodan converted Stephen and Barnabas. [9]
