Abner

See also: UB 147:5.10; UB 152:7.2; UB 162:9; UB 165:1.3.

Chief of John’s loyal supporters. [1]Head of Engedi Nazarite colony. [2]Home at Sebaste. [3]Head of Philadelphia church. [4]

During Jesus life

Associate of Andrew. [5]Baptized Abraham the Pharisee. [6]Synagogues open to. [7]Headquarters at Bethlehem. [8]Consolidated favorable sentiment in Judea. [9]In charge of 70 evangelists. [10]In charge of women’s corps. [11]Final visit with, message from, Jesus. [12]Jesus instructed Abner to go on with work. [13]At Jesus’ 10th appearance. [14]

After Jesus death

At variance with all leaders of early church. [15]Nathaniel joined for a year. [16]Sent uncompromising teachers to Lake Urmia. [17]Uncompromising. [18]Died at 89 in Philadelphia. [19]
