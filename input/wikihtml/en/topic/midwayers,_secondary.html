Midwayers, secondary

See also: UB 77:6.

All who survive receive Adjusters and eventually join Corps of Mortal Finaliters. [1]Can work with Master Physical Controllers. [2]Derived from Adams and Eves or their immediate progeny. [3]Energized electrically, by Adamic technique. [4]Exclusively attached to human ministry. [5]Exist just outside range of mortal vision; can make physical contact with material things. [6]Further cause of progressive civilization. [7]Male and female types. [8]Material but ordinarily invisible. [9]More material than primary midwayers. [10]Nearer men than angels.
