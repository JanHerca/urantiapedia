Psychic circles

See also: UB 110:6.

7th circle

1 pair of seraphic guardians for 1000 mortals. [1]Entry upon development of moral responsibility. [2]

6th circle

1 pair of seraphic guardians for 500 mortals. [3]

5th circle

1 pair of seraphic guardians for 100 mortals. [4]

4th circle

1 pair of seraphic guardians for 10 mortals. [5]

3rd circle

1 pair of seraphic guardians for each mortal. [6]Achievement of 3rd circle brought Adjuster prior to Spirit of Truth. [7]Adjutant influence diminishes after. [8]Possible immediate resurrection for those attaining. [9]Subject must attain for Adjuster’s self-activity. [10]

1st circle

Adjuster can speak directly and immediately with subject. [11]Termination of adjutant mind-spirit ministry as exclusive influence. [12]

Also known as cosmic circles of coordinate mortal attainment; cosmic levels. [13]

Attainment

Additional time for, afforded on mansion worlds. [14]Adds to ability of Adjuster to function. [15]Augments potential of human achievement. [16]By balanced growth. [17]Does not equate to Adjuster fusion. [18]Increases consciousness of relation to Supreme Being, universe citizenship. [19]Marks steps preliminary to fusion. [20]Only relatively related to God-consciousness. [21]Reflected by Adjuster attunement, soul evolution, personality reality. [22]

Jesus achieved psychic circles on Mount Hermon. [23]So designated after recognition requirements of Havona circuits. [24]Sum total of personality realization and maturity. [25]
