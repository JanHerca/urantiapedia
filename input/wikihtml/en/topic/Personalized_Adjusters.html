Personalized Adjusters

See also: UB 40:4; UB 109:6-7; UB 109:7.

3 very active on Urantia; one certifies plan for each human being. [1]Assigned to worlds following bestowal of Paradise Son. [2]Chiefs of their kind. [3]College on Divinington. [4]Embrace all known relationships of personality. [5]Executives for Architects of Master Universe. [6]Have experientialized by participation with mortals. [7]Have served with incarnated Paradise Sons or achieved unusual distinction. [8]In personality registers. [9]Master Spirit #1 is adviser to chief of. [10]Nontime beings. [11]Of Divinington certify life plans for human subjects. [12]Permit some fusion-status mortals to continue duties. [13]Personal agents of full ministry of Universal Father. [14]Personalized by Universal Father. [15]Personnel of training schools of Divinington. [16]Seldom seen at large. [17]Unassigned sovereign stabilizers and compensators of universes. [18]Values of nonsurviving human subjects become possession of. [19]Visible to beings who have attained Father. [20]
