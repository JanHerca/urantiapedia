Van (Prince’s staff)

See also: UB 67:6.

1-2-3 the first supported. [1]And entire council loyal in rebellion. [2]Could communicate with midwayers. [3]Most Highs’ decision sustaining Van marooned in relay energy transmitter for 7 years. [4]Spiritual insight and moral steadfastness. [5]Translated to Jerusem. [6]Tree of life sustained. [7]

Work

Battled for salvation of Prince’s staff. [8]Commanded loyal midway creatures. [9]Despite efforts, by 50,000 years after collapse earthly affairs disorganized and retarded. [10]Fostered natural evolution of human race. [11]Head of all superhuman personalities on Urantia until Adam. [12]Indicted Daligastia, Caligastia, and Lucifer as standing in contempt. [13]Led supreme court of tribal co-ordination and racial cooperation. [14]Now serves in behalf of Urantia. [15]Organized 350 groups along military lines. [16]Preached advent of racial uplifter. [17]Proclaimed Adam and Eve rulers of Urantia. [18]Protested Garden dwellers’ intent to worship Adam. [19]Remained 150,000 years after rebellion; left 7 years after Adam’s arrival. [20]Trained new generation of Garden workers; withstood desertions. [21]Withdrew to highlands west of India. [22]
