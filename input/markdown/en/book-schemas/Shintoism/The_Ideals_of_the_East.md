This is a short but very concise introduction to Asian art by the author of [The Book of Tea](/en/book/Shintoism/The_Book_of_Tea). Written from a Japanese perspective, and focusing on Japanese art, one of the major themes is the relationship between spirituality, particularly Buddhism, and the evolution of Asian art.
