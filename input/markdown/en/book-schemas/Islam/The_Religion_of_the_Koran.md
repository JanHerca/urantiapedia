This short entry in the _Wisdom of the East_ series includes a short introduction to Islam, and a set of topical quotations from the [Palmer](/en/book/Islam/Quran_Sacred_Books_of_the_East) translation of the Qur'an. The author was an English academic sympathetic to Islam who was writing at the turn of the 20th century, and this should be kept in mind while reading this.
