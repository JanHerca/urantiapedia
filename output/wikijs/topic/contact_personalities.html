In the contacts made with the mortal beings of the material worlds, such as with the subject through whom the Urantia revelations were transmitted, the midway creatures are always employed as contact personalities. [1]

There are less than twenty contact personalities in the Urantia destiny reservists who are aware of being prepared for a possible function in certain planetary crises. [2]
