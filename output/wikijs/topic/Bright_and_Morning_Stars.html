The Bright and Morning Star is not a creator, but he is a marvelous administrator, being the personal administrative representative of the Creator Son. [1] Ancients of Days delegate mass judgment and dispensational resurrections to Bright and Morning Stars. [2] They are beings of unprecedented versatility and unimagined brilliance. [3] They are the chief executives of local universes when a Michael Son is absent. [4] They are the creators of Spironga with Father Melchizedeks. [5] They were created fully endowed for his work, but he has gained experience with the growth and evolution of our local creation. [6] They are the first-born of local universes, personalized by Creator Sons and Mother Spirits. [7] They are catalogued inside the group Universe Aids. [8] In personality registers appear as a descending order inside dual-origin beings. [9] Intelligence corps seraphim report directly to Bright and Morning Stars. [10]

These chief executives of the universe, the personal associates of the Creator Sons, are like them in all aspects of character, though markedly limited in the attributes of divinity and their creative prerogatives. [11] Son-Spirit minister supreme seraphim are closely affiliated with intelligence service of Bright and Morning Star. [12] They would become chief local universe administrators if Creator Sons left for new universes in outer space. [13]
