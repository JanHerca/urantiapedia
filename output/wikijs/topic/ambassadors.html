Human ambassadors

On a neighboring planet the most superior nation is projecting to establish ambassadorial relations with surrounding nations. [1] Primitive peace treaties consisted in “blood bonds”, in which ambassadors sucked each other’s blood. [2] Sending ambassadors dates from Adam; great step forward. [3]

Universe ambassadors

Ambassadors are appointed by judicial decree and represent universes to other universes. [4] Melchizedeks serve as ambassadors to other universes. [5] The superuniverses do not maintain any sort of ambassadorial representation; they are completely isolated from each other. [6] Solitary Messengers serve as temporary ambassadors. [7] The Paradise Spiritual Sons, upon certification they may become spiritual ambassadors representing the local universes of their service. [8] Vorondadeks serve as ambassadors to other universes.
