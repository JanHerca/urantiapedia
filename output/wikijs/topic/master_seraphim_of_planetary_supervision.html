Mobilize reserve corps of destiny. [1] Seraphington graduates; serve in 12 corps. [2]

Types

  1. Angels of the epochs.
    • Angels of the current age. [3]
  2. Angels of progress.
    • Celestial artisans on Urantia work under. [4]
    • Disagreement with angels of churches regarding Urmia lectures. [5]
    • Labor incessantly to make things what they ought to be; checkmates of angels of churches. [6]
  3. Angels of the churches.
    • Disagreement regarding Urmia lectures. [7]
    • Religious guardians; checkmates of angels of progress. [8]
  4. Angels of national life.
    • Angels of the trumpets; directors of national political life; Most Highs rule in kingdoms of men through. [9]
  5. Angels of the races.
    • Work for conservation of mortal races. [10]
  6. Angels of the future.
    • Architects of successive eras. [11]
  7. Angels of enlightenment.
    • Occupied with mental and moral training. [12]
  8. Angels of health.
    • Promote health, prevention of disease. [13]
  9. Angels of the home.
    • Dedicated to preservation and advancement of home. [14]
  10. Angels of industry.
    • Work to improve economic conditions. [15]
  11. Angels of diversion.
    • Foster play, humor, and rest. [16]
  12. Angels of superhuman ministry.
    • Minister to superhuman life. [17]
