Angona is the name of a massive solar system made up of a dark giant from space, perhaps a black hole, which 4.5 billion years ago approached our solar system and gave rise to twelve planets and a cloud of meteors by disgorging material from the sun. [1]

It is possible that Angona was part of a large nebula neighboring Andronover whose approach distorted Andronover as we are told that this distortion occurred at the time of the birth of our solar system. [2]
