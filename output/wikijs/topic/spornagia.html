Animal caretakers of architectural spheres. [1] Artistic landscape gardeners. [2] Beautiful spornagia inhabit constellations, local systems. [3] Do not possess survival souls, but live very long lives and reincarnate. [4] Enjoy arrival of ascenders. [5] Have individuality but not personality; react to first 5 adjutant mind-spirits only. [6] Hospitable hosts to all beings they recognize. [7] Minister to physical needs of probationary nursery children. [8] Not evolutionary beings; possible spiritual destiny. [9] Procreate; traits of a horse, dog, and chimpanzee. [10] Visualize morontia, angelic, and lower spirit personalities, but. Not finaliters. [11] Work of botanic decoration directed by celestial artisans, ascending midway creatures. [12]
