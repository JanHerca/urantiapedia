Caesarea Philippi, also known as Dan. [1] It was the capital of Tetrarch Philip’s domain. [2] Celsus, a Jesus follower, was from Caesarea Philippi. [3] Veronica, the faithfully woman that was healed when she touched Jesus, was from Caesarea Philippi. [4]
