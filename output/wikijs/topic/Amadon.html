Outstanding human hero of the Lucifer rebellion; was Andonite and associate of Van, of Caligastia's entourage. [1] Supported by 1-2-3 the first, the dean of Urantia's primary order of midwayers. [2] Amadon is a clear example of Thought Adjuster-enhanced loyalty. [3] Remained on Urantia 150,000 years after the rebellion, until the arrival of Adam. [4] Welcomed Adam and Eve on their arrival. [5] He was transferred to Jerusem seven years after the arrival of Adam and Eve. [6]
