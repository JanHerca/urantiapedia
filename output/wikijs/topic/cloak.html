Jesus wore the cloak, a typical Jewish clothing. [1]

When Jesus instructed his apostles that they should, when one unjustly took away the coat, offer the other garment, he referred not so much to a literal second coat as to the idea of doing something positive to save the wrongdoer in the place of the olden advice “an eye for an eye”. [2]
