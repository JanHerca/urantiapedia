Apostles stayed with. [1] At Jesus’ 4th appearance. [2] Born March 16, A.D. 1. [3] Defended himself when assailed by playmates. [4] Faithful plodding worker; not too smart. [5] Formally installed as head of Jesus’ family. [6] Friction with Jesus. [7] Graduation from synagogue school. [8] Jesus took to Jerusalem for Passover. [9] Moved into family home in A.D. 24. [10] Permitted pride to interfere. [11] Tentative belief in Jesus. [12]
