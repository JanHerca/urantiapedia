This school of thought was dedicated to the pursuit of happiness. It did effectually combat ignorant superstition. [1]

Occidental religion thus languished until the days of the Skeptics, Cynics, Epicureans, and Stoics, but most important of all, until the times of the great contest between Mithraism and Paul’s new religion of Christianity. [2]
