In those regions of thicker clustering, collisions are not uncommon. When physical systems become stabilized, become physically settled, and are swung into the balanced and established circuits, no more collisions or other devastating catastrophes will occur. [1] In the collisional episodes enormous masses of matter are suddenly converted into the rarest form of energy. [2] Collisions among the giants of space are rare indeed, but gravity-tidal explosions of lesser bodies are quite common. [3]
