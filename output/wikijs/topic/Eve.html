Eve is the Material Daughter consort of Adam who accepted the mission of bringing the violet race to Urantia.

The Caligastia plot

Caligastia gave up trying to convince Adam, and decided to try a cunning indirect attack against Eve. [1] Eve listened to the insidious propaganda of personal liberty and independence of action. [2] Eve did not perceive the danger of increasingly private visits with Serapatatia, a Nodite chief. [3] Eve's great temptation occurred through Serapatatia. [4] Eve had found Cano attractive. [5] She consented to a secret conference with Cano. [6]

The fault

[10] She never fully recovered from Adam's 30 day absence following her absence. [11] She never intended to jeopardize planetary trust. [12]
