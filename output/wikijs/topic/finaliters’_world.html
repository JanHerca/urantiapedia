It is the transitional culture world with number 1. [1]

The Finaliter World is the headquarters of the finaliter corps of the local system and is surrounded by the receiving worlds, the seven mansion worlds, dedicated so fully to the scheme of mortal ascension. [2] The finaliter world is accessible to the inhabitants of all seven mansion worlds. [3]

The probation nursery of Satania is maintained by certain morontia personalities on the finaliters’ world, one half of the planet being devoted to this work of child rearing. [4]

This world now serves as the headquarters for more than one hundred thousand companies of finaliters, and there are one thousand glorified beings in each of these groups. [5]
