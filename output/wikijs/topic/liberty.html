Attainment

Consequent upon enhanced self-control. [1] Finaliters experience finality of. [2] Only after learning loyalty, love, and brotherhood. [3] Only under reign of law. [4] Predicated on justice, intelligence, maturity, fraternity, equity. [5] Progressively related to cosmic reality. [6] Proportional to degree of doing Father’s will. [7] Through capital and invention. [8] Through knowing truth. [9] Where spirit is, there is freedom. [10]

Child’s liberty should be restrained by intelligent discipline. [11]

Defined as

Daring to think, act, and live honestly, loyally, fearlessly, and truthfully. [12] Existing only within realm of one’s consciousness. [13] Ideal of society. [14] Quest of the ages. [15] Regardful of social equity, fairness, and divine obligations. [16] Right to love and be loved, privilege of worshiping God and serving fellows. [17]

Endowing imperfect beings with liberty entails inevitable tragedy. [18] Enjoy liberty without license. [19]

False liberty

Assumption of self-assertion; self-admiration. [20] Depriving others of privileges of existence. [21] Fallacies of personal liberty. [22] Liberty divorced from justice and moral duty is suicidal. [23] Unbridled personal liberty. [24]

Grants to any individual curtail those of all others. [25] Greek philosophy, Christian ideals comprised charter for. Human freedom. [26] Lucifer manifesto masqueraded as liberty. [27] Make evil and sin inevitable. [28] Mortals are entitled to. [29] Progress characterized by increasing. [30] Religious groups should enjoy liberty of freethinking. [31] Spirit world respects freewill choice if not detrimental to self or injurious to fellows. [32]
