Absoluta modified by Primary Master Force Organizers. [1] Also known as primordial force; pure energy. [2] An ultimate. [3] Ancestor of all relative, nonspirit functional realities. [4] Basic space force of Unqualified Absolute. [5] Fashioned after similitude of 3 Gods in 1. [6] First stage of individuation of absoluta into pre-energy form of cosmic force. [7] Focalized on nether Paradise. [8] Passage of particles through segregata gives rise to phenomena analogous to postulated ether. [9]

Phases of transmutation to universe power

  1. Puissant energy.
  2. Gravity energy.

Pre-energy not responsive to direct Paradise gravity. [10] Reactive to transcendental causation in proportion to absoluteness. [11] Reacts with elasticity of response in accordance with unknown but stable laws. [12] Response is universal, inescapable; inherent in Absolutes. [13] Under personal control of Universal Father.
