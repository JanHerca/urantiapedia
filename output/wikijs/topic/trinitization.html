Accomplishes what otherwise is nonattainable. [1]

By creatures

Adamic-finaliter pairs very successful at trinitization. [2] Always successful between finaliters and Paradise-Havoners. [3] Apparently an unlimited technique. [4] By 2 finaliters is paradisiacal effort to actualize original identical concept of Almighty Supreme; sometimes fails. [5] By finaliters and Paradise-Havoners repercusses in Supreme-Ultimate Mind. [6] By Paradise-Havoners. [7] Creature-trinitized sons in personality registers. [8] Engaged in only by Deities, finaliters, Paradise Citizens and Havoners. [9] Parents thereafter spiritually inseparable; participate only once. [10] Special corps exists for mixed finaliter and ParadiseHavoner parents. [11] Supreme creative performance of spiritual beings. [12] Unbelievably long time sometimes consumed. [13]

By Deities, trinitization constitutes authority to represent Trinity. [14] Precipitates unrealized potentials out of future time. [15] Secret of Vicegerington, Solitarington. [16] Technique by which Father divests himself of unqualified spirit personality. [17]
