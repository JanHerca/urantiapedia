Revelation; spiritual insight; spiritualism.

Adjuster leadings

Adjusters speak directly with 1st psychic circlers. [1] Adjusters constantly communicate with human subjects. [2] Autorevelation is work of Adjuster. [3] Man may hear Adjuster’s direct voice in supreme decision. [4] Many isms have arisen from garbled Adjuster communications. [5] Spirit bears witness with our spirits that we are children of God. [6]

Dangers involved with supposed spirit leadings

Blunder to exalt reaction of mind to divine dignity. [7] Product of intellect indistinguishable from that of Adjuster. [8] Strong emotions are not equivalent to spiritual leadings. [9] Unbalanced spiritual growth produces fanatical interpretation of spirit leadings. [10] Uprush from unconscious mistaken for spirit leadings. [11]

Jesus did not indulge in religious leadings. [12] Practical validations of inspiration. [13] Recognize thoughts for what they are; disregard what they might have been. [14]
