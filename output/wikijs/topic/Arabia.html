The more mixed and less progressive Indigo peoples east of the Sahara plateau migrated to Arabia, and then from there to Mesopotamia, India, and Ceylon. [1]

The Melchizedek teachings of the one God became established in the Arabian Desert at a comparatively recent date. As in Greece, so in Arabia the Salem missionaries failed because of their misunderstanding of Machiventa’s instructions regarding overorganization. [2] The serpent always has been revered in Arabia. [3]

The brilliant culture of the Nile was cut short by internal warfare and invasions by inferior tribes from Arabia. As a result, social progress of Egipt steadily declined for more than 500 years. [4]
