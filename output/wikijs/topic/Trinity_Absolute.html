2nd experiential Trinity. [1] Comprised of God the Supreme, God the Ultimate, and unrevealed Consummator of Universe Destiny. [2] Could be actualized only after completion of entire master universe. [3] Final expression of infinity, absolute actualization of all potentials. [4] Full function beyond master universe. [5] God the Absolute actualizes in consequence. [6] Involves all triunity relationships, undeified as well as deified. [7]
