Andonites assimilated better strains of. [1] Descendants of mid-mammals; immediate ancestors of humans. [2] Extinct 5000 years after man’s appearance. [3] Greatly subject to fear. [4] Last vital link in human evolution. [5] Lightning struck tree in which prospective mother slept. [6] Replenished fire kindled by lightning. [7] Skeletons very similar to primitive humans. [8] Threw stones; used clubs, spicules of flint, bone. [9] Walked upright, largely abandoned tree life; developed language. [10]
