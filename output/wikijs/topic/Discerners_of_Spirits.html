The Discerners of Spirits are able to present the naked soul of the reflected individuals, making a portrait of complete certainty and perfection. [1] The Discerners of Spirits carry on these intricate services by virtue of inherent “spiritual insight”. [2] A special liaison exists between the counselors and advisers of the second Havona circle and these reflective angels. [3] This secoraphic discerners reflect knowledge of Mother Spirit. [4] The discerners can and do reflect the presence of the Adjusters. [5] They are the only seconaphim attached to the Universal Censors but are probably the most uniquely specialized of all their fellows. [6]
