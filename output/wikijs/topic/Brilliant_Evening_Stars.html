Brilliant Evening Stars are also known as superangels. [1] They are easily visible to morontia personalities. [2] They appear mentioned in personality registers of descending order of dual-origin beings and as Universal Aids of Composite Personality Groups. [3] They serve in many capacities but chiefly as liaison officers of Gabriel, the local universe chief executive. [4] They always work in pairs—one a created being, the other an ascendant Evening Star. [5]

Origin

The Brilliant Evening Stars are a unique twofold order, embracing some of created dignity and others of attained service. Many of these ascendant Evening Stars started their universe careers as seraphim; others have ascended from unrevealed levels of creature life. [6] These brilliant creatures were planned by the Melchizedeks and were then brought into being by the Creator Son and the Creative Spirit. [7]

Headquarters

These beings have their residence at Satania in the second Jerusem circle. [8]

The fourth transition world of Jerusem, the Superangel World, is home of the Brilliant Evening Stars and a vast concourse of co-ordinate and near-co-ordinate beings. [9] The sixth group of seven Salvington worlds and their forty-two tributary satellites are assigned to the administration of the Brilliant Evening Stars. [10]

Numbers

The Nebadon corps of these superangels now numbers 13,641. There are 4,832 of created dignity, while 8,809 are ascendant spirits who have attained this goal of exalted service. [11]

Functions and actions

They accompany Magisterial Sons on bestowal missions. [12] A Brilliant Evening Star of Avalon periodically advised Melchizedek receivers after rebellion. [13] Assist Trinity Teacher Sons in establishing the final planetary age of light and life. [14]

It is the senior of these Brilliant Evening Stars commanders that follow Avonal Sons who, at the significant time and age, bids the Avonal bestowal Son, “Be about your brother’s business”. [15] There exists a close working connection between the finaliters, the Evening Stars, the superangels, and the Trinity Teacher Sons. [16] Destiny guardians requests a permission that is elevated to a Evening Star after subjects’ deaths. [17] They are the directors angelic armies, each one composed of twelve hosts or 71,663,616 angels. [18] They form the examining corps on Jerusem, who ascertain the degree of spirit insight. [19] There exists a liaison of tremendous power and import between the Evening Stars and the Gravity Messengers attached to the finaliter working groups. [20] One attended upon Jesus throughout his mortal bestowal. [21] Now this Brilliant Evening Star is the liaison of twety four councilors with Michael. [22] One or more of these beings function as Gabriel representatives at the capital of every constellation and system in Nebadon. [23] Portray philosophic teachings to finaliters that these offer to mortals in light and life ages. [24] Represent Gabriel at Salvington conclaves. [25] Represent Trinity Teacher Sons on planetary missions. [26] Some Urantia Book Papers have been dictated, sponsored or presentated by Brilliant Evening Stars: 37, 47, 85–89, 119. [27] The Brilliant Evening Stars, and their unnamed co-ordinates, frequently serve as teachers in the various educational enterprises of the universe. [28]

Nature

They are count in group Universe Aids. [29] None went astray in Lucifer rebellion. [30] The created beings of this interesting and versatile order possess a spirit force which can be manifested independently of their personal presence. [31] They are superangels created by Creator Sons and Mother Spirits. [32]
