Types of Master Force Organizers.

  1. Primary Eventuated. [12]
    • Bring energy to level of initial response to Paradise gravity. [13]
    • Inaugurated energy whirl of Andronover then withdrew at right angels. [14]
    • Modify absoluta into segregata by their passive presence. [15]
    • Nebulae creators. [16]
    • Transmute segregata into puissant energy. [17]
  2. Associate Transcendental. [18]
    • Comprise Council of Equilibrium. [19]
    • Leave local universe when Creator Son arrives. [20]
    • Make force-charge of space gravity responsive. [21]
    • Metamorphose cosmic force in outer space. [22]
    • Retire upon appearance of linear gravity response in favor of Universe Power Directors. [23]
    • Take over from Primary Eventuated Master Force Organizers when energy begins to respond to Paradise gravity. [24]
    • Transmute puissant energy into ultimatons and back to puissant energy. [25]
    • Transmute puissant energy to gravity-energy. [26]
