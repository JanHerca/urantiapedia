Always stimulates religious feelings. [1] Knowledge is power. [2] Our power is through renewing of Spirit. [3]

Physical power

Designates electronic level of linear-gravity responsive matter. [4] Term denotes postgravity stages of energy. [5] Universe power.

Position

All power given to Jesus. [6] Experiential power is earned power, eternity power is not. [7] Jesus avoided all display of power. [8] Might was right for primitives. [9] Primitives took for granted power would be used selfishly. [10] Refuse to use power for self-aggrandizement. [11]

Tendency to bow down before power. [12] There is no power but of God. [13] They who wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength. [14]
