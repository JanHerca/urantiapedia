The violet race

Adamites also known as violet race, is the 9th human race that has appeared on Earth. [1] Children of Adam were usually Adjuster indwelt. [2] Comprised of Adamites, children of Adam, and Adamsonites, children of Adanson, who was also a son of Adam. [3] They did not inherit Adam’s dual circulation system; only had one circulatory system. [4] Each generation had less of Adam’s special senses, who had the ability to see more acutely. [5] They had blue eyes, fair complexions, and yellow, red, brown hair color; his head was of the long type. [6] They were monogamous people. [7] They were a pacific people. [8] Violet race was the first to attempted to divorce mathematical from volitional, material from spiritual things. [9] They were a race from 35,000 B.C. to 2000 B.C., when its final disappearance happened. [10] Some members of violet race were:

  • Abel.
  • Adamson.
  • Cain.
  • Enoch.
  • Enos.
  • Eveson.
  • Jansad.
  • Kenan.
  • Seth, son of Adam and Eve.
  • Sansa.

In the Garden

On normal worlds candidates for mating are brought to Garden, and it is considered a high honor marry the daughters and sons of the Garden. [11] At 18 they entered a preparation course for marital responsibilities and were eligible for marriage at 20. [12] They displayed a unique heroism in second garden, surrounded by inferior tribes. [13] They were a long-lived people, but longevity gravitated to norm with each generation. [14] Mixed-race children could return to the evolutionary tribe of his parents for marry there, and they were pledged to monogamy. [15]

Migrations

Went forth as emissaries 25,000 years ago. [16] In 19,000 B.C. they were a nation of 4.5 million. [17] Plan was to amalgamate with colored races upon numbering 500,000 or 1 million. [18] Violet race retained the tradition of peace-seeking; this explains the long delay in making territorial conquests. [19] Climatic and geologic changes diverted early migration of Adamites to Europe, forcing them to migrate north and east into Turkestan. [20] Some Adamites produce a unremitting northward push into Europe. [21]

Around 15,000 B.C. there were already more descendants of Adam in Europe and central Asia than anywhere else. All Russia and Turkestan were occupied by Adamites. [22] A lot of Adamites entered Europe, ocuppied mainly by blue races. [23] They exterminated Neanderthal strains in blue race. Due to this several superior groups of blue men appeared, as the one denominated Cro-Magnons. [24] Admites favored blue races of Europe the most, who absorbed greater portion of biologic inheritance. [25] It was difficult for Adamites to teach religion to Cro-Magnoids because these had tendency to cheat and debauch maidens. [26] Modern white races incorporates surviving strains of the Adamic stock. [27]

No longer existed as a race in 15,000 B.C. In that moment a cultural renaissance occurred but it was undertaken by Andites, a mixed people of Adamites and Nodites. [28] Moses was the leader of a submerged remnant of Adamites. [29]

Benefits of Adam bestowal

Adamic blood did augment the inherent ability of the races and did accelerate the pace of economic development and industrial progression. [30] Humor is a legacy of violet race. [31] Interbreeding with Adamites immediately upstepped races. [32] Play, humor, and music are largely derived from Adam peoples. [33] Adam left a great intellectual and spiritual culture behind him, but it was not advanced in mechanical appliances. [34] Adan descendants were not so subject to fear than evolutionary races. [35] We would be more resistant to disease if we carry more of the Adamic life. [36]
