Today, men are not social slaves, but thousands allow ambition to enslave them to debt. [1] Early creditors controlled body after death. [2] Secret societies were employed by merchants to collect debts. [3] Governments may not incur debt in continental nation that is the most developed in a neighboring planet. [4] Jesus gave two parables about debt:

  • The parable of the moneylender, about what is the greatest love. [5]
  • The parable of the shrewd steward, on how one must show sagacity and friendship with eternal things to prepare for the future. [6]
