Confucius believed true patterns of civilization mirror eternal heavenly order. [1] Defeat is true mirror in which to view your real self. [2] Each superuniverse mirrors presiding Master Spirit. [3] Ethics is external mirror of internal spiritual progress. [4] Primary seconaphim are living mirrors for Ancients of Days. [5] Primitives regarded mirrors with superstitious awe. [6] Soul with clean heart reflects truth like a mirror. [7] Souls of Philosophy mirror wisdom of divinity. [8]
