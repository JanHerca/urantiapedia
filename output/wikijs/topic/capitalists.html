The herders were the first capitalists; their herds represented capital, and they lived on the interest—the natural increase. [1] The priesthoods thus became very rich; they were chief among ancient capitalists. [2]

On the neighbor planet that has a main continental nation the workers are increasingly becoming shareholders in all industrial concerns; every intelligent laborer is slowly becoming a small capitalist. [3]
