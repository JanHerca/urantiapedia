## HYMN CXCI. Agni.

<p id="v1"><sup><small>1</small></sup>  THOU, mighty Agni, gatherest up all that is precious for thy friend.
Bring us all treasures as thou art enkindled in libation's place
<p id="v2"><sup><small>2</small></sup>  Assemble, speak together: let your minds be all of one accord,
As ancient Gods unanimous sit down to their appointed share.
<p id="v3"><sup><small>3</small></sup>  The place is common, common the assembly, common the mind, so be their thought united.
A common purpose do I lay before you, and worship with your general oblation.
<p id="v4"><sup><small>4</small></sup>  One and the same be your resolve, and be your minds of one accord.
United be the thoughts of all that all may happily agree.
