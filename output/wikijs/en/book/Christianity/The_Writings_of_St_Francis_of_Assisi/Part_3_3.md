## III. PRAYER TO OBTAIN DIVINE LOVE.

The authenticity of this prayer, accepted by the Quaracchi editors, rests on the authority of St. Bernardine of Siena [^548] and Ubertino da Casale, [^549] both of whom are quoted in its behalf by Wadding. The prayer is here translated according to the text given by Ubertino in his _Arbor Vitae Crucifixae_ composed on La Verna in 1305. [^550] It follows:â€”

## PRAYER.

I beseech Thee, O Lord, that the fiery and sweet strength of Thy love may absorb my soul from all things that are under heaven, that I may die for love of Thy love as Thou didst deign to die for love of my love.


## Footnotes

[^548]: 145:1 St. Bernardine died in 1444. See his _Opera Omnia_, t. II, sermo 60, art. II, c. II.

[^549]: 145:2 See _Arbor Vitae_, l. v, c. iv.

[^550]: 145:3 See Ehrle, _Archiv_, etc., vol. II, pp. 374-416, as to the writings of Ubertino.
