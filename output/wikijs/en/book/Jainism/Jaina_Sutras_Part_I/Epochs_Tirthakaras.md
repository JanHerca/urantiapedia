<span id="p280"><sup><small>[ p. 280 ]</small></sup></span>

Since the time that the Arhat Nami died, &c. (all down to) freed from all pains, 584,979 years have elapsed, this is the eightieth year [^657]. (184) Since the death of Munisuvrata this is the year 1,184,980. Since Malli [^658] this is the year 6,584,980. Ara died 10,000,000 years before Malli; Kunthua quarter of a Palyopama before Malli; <i>S</i>ânti three-quarters of a Palyopama; Dharma three Sâgaropamas before Malli; Ananta seven Sâgaropamas before Malli; Vimala sixteen Sâgaropamas before Malli; Vâsupû<i>g</i>ya forty Sâgaropamas before Malli; <i>S</i>reyâ<i>m</i>sa a hundred Sâgaropamas before Malli. <i>S</i>îtala died a krore of Sâgaropamas, less 42,003 years and eight and a half months, before the death of Vîra. Suvidhi, surnamed Pushpadanta, died ten krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; <i>K</i>andraprabha a hundred krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; Supâr<i>s</i>va a thousand krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; Padmaprabha ten thousand krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; Sumati one hundred thousand krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; Abhinandana one million krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; Sambhava two million krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala; A<i>g</i>ita five million krores of Sâgaropamas before <i>S</i>îtala. (185-203)
