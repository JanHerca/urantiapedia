<span id="v1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></span>  When the heaven is cleft asunder,
<span id="v2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></span>  When the planets are dispersed,
<span id="v3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></span>  When the seas are poured forth,
<span id="v4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></span>  And the sepulchres are overturned,
<span id="v5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></span>  A soul will know what it hath sent before (it) and what left behind.
<span id="v6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></span>  O man! What hath made thee careless concerning thy Lord, the Bountiful,
<span id="v7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></span>  Who created thee, then fashioned, then proportioned thee ?
<span id="v8"><sup><small>8</small></sup></span>  Into whatsoever form He will, He casteth thee.
<span id="v9"><sup><small>9</small></sup></span>  Nay, but ye deny the Judgment.
<span id="v10"><sup><small>10</small></sup></span>  Lo! there are above you guardians,
<span id="v11"><sup><small>11</small></sup></span>  Generous and recording,
<span id="v12"><sup><small>12</small></sup></span>  Who know (all) that ye do.
<span id="v13"><sup><small>13</small></sup></span>  Lo! the righteous verily will be in delight.
<span id="v14"><sup><small>14</small></sup></span>  And lo! the wicked verily will be in hell;
<span id="v15"><sup><small>15</small></sup></span>  They will burn therein on the Day of Judgment,
<span id="v16"><sup><small>16</small></sup></span>  And will not be absent thence.
<span id="v17"><sup><small>17</small></sup></span>  Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Day of Judgment is!
<span id="v18"><sup><small>18</small></sup></span>  Again, what will convey unto thee what the Day of Judgment is!
<span id="v19"><sup><small>19</small></sup></span>  A day on which no soul hath power at all for any (other) soul. The (absolute) command on that day is Allah's.
