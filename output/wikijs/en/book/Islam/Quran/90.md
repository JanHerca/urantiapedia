<span id="v1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></span>  Nay, I swear by this city -
<span id="v2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></span>  And thou art an indweller of this city -
<span id="v3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></span>  And the begetter and that which he begat,
<span id="v4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></span>  We verily have created man in an atmosphere:
<span id="v5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></span>  Thinketh he that none hath power over him ?
<span id="v6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></span>  And he saith: I have destroyed vast wealth:
<span id="v7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></span>  Thinketh he that none beholdeth him ?
<span id="v8"><sup><small>8</small></sup></span>  Did We not assign unto him two eyes
<span id="v9"><sup><small>9</small></sup></span>  And a tongue and two lips,
<span id="v10"><sup><small>10</small></sup></span>  And guide him to the parting of the mountain ways ?
<span id="v11"><sup><small>11</small></sup></span>  But he hath not attempted the Ascent -
<span id="v12"><sup><small>12</small></sup></span>  Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Ascent is! -
<span id="v13"><sup><small>13</small></sup></span>  (It is) to free a slave,
<span id="v14"><sup><small>14</small></sup></span>  And to feed in the day of hunger.
<span id="v15"><sup><small>15</small></sup></span>  An orphan near of kin,
<span id="v16"><sup><small>16</small></sup></span>  Or some poor wretch in misery,
<span id="v17"><sup><small>17</small></sup></span>  And to be of those who believe and exhort one another to perseverance and exhort one another to pity.
<span id="v18"><sup><small>18</small></sup></span>  Their place will be on the right hand.
<span id="v19"><sup><small>19</small></sup></span>  But those who disbelieve Our revelations, their place will be on the left hand.
<span id="v20"><sup><small>20</small></sup></span>  Fire will be an awning over them.
