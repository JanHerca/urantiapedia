<span id="v1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></span>  Hath there come unto thee tidings of the Overwhelming ?
<span id="v2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></span>  On that day (many) faces will be downcast,
<span id="v3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></span>  Toiling, weary,
<span id="v4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></span>  Scorched by burning fire,
<span id="v5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></span>  Drinking from a boiling spring,
<span id="v6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></span>  No food for them save bitter thorn-fruit
<span id="v7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></span>  Which doth not nourish nor release from hunger.
<span id="v8"><sup><small>8</small></sup></span>  In that day other faces will be calm,
<span id="v9"><sup><small>9</small></sup></span>  Glad for their effort past,
<span id="v10"><sup><small>10</small></sup></span>  In a high Garden
<span id="v11"><sup><small>11</small></sup></span>  Where they hear no idle speech,
<span id="v12"><sup><small>12</small></sup></span>  Wherein is a gushing spring,
<span id="v13"><sup><small>13</small></sup></span>  Wherein are couches raised
<span id="v14"><sup><small>14</small></sup></span>  And goblets set at hand
<span id="v15"><sup><small>15</small></sup></span>  And cushions ranged
<span id="v16"><sup><small>16</small></sup></span>  And silken carpets spread.
<span id="v17"><sup><small>17</small></sup></span>  Will they not regard the camels, how they are created ?
<span id="v18"><sup><small>18</small></sup></span>  And the heaven, how it is raised ?
<span id="v19"><sup><small>19</small></sup></span>  And the hills, how they are set up ?
<span id="v20"><sup><small>20</small></sup></span>  And the earth, how it is spread ?
<span id="v21"><sup><small>21</small></sup></span>  Remind them, for thou art but a remembrancer,
<span id="v22"><sup><small>22</small></sup></span>  Thou art not at all a warder over them.
<span id="v23"><sup><small>23</small></sup></span>  But whoso is averse and disbelieveth,
<span id="v24"><sup><small>24</small></sup></span>  Allah will punish him with direst punishment.
<span id="v25"><sup><small>25</small></sup></span>  Lo! unto Us is their return
<span id="v26"><sup><small>26</small></sup></span>  And Ours their reckoning.
