<span id="v1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></span>  Read: In the name of thy Lord Who createth,
<span id="v2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></span>  Createth man from a clot.
<span id="v3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></span>  Read: And thy Lord is the Most Bounteous,
<span id="v4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></span>  Who teacheth by the pen,
<span id="v5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></span>  Teacheth man that which he knew not.
<span id="v6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></span>  Nay, but verily man is rebellious
<span id="v7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></span>  That he thinketh himself independent!
<span id="v8"><sup><small>8</small></sup></span>  Lo! unto thy Lord is the return.
<span id="v9"><sup><small>9</small></sup></span>  Hast thou seen him who dissuadeth
<span id="v10"><sup><small>10</small></sup></span>  A slave when he prayeth ?
<span id="v11"><sup><small>11</small></sup></span>  Hast thou seen if he relieth on the guidance (of Allah)
<span id="v12"><sup><small>12</small></sup></span>  Or enjoineth piety ?
<span id="v13"><sup><small>13</small></sup></span>  Hast thou seen if he denieth (Allah's guidance) and is froward ?
<span id="v14"><sup><small>14</small></sup></span>  Is he then unaware that Allah seeth ?
<span id="v15"><sup><small>15</small></sup></span>  Nay, but if he cease not We will seize him by the forelock -
<span id="v16"><sup><small>16</small></sup></span>  The lying, sinful forelock -
<span id="v17"><sup><small>17</small></sup></span>  Then let him call upon his henchmen!
<span id="v18"><sup><small>18</small></sup></span>  We will call the guards of hell.
<span id="v19"><sup><small>19</small></sup></span>  Nay, Obey not thou him. But prostrate thyself, and draw near (unto Allah).
