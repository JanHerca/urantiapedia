This is the acknowledgments page from the original book. It is included for completeness and for copyright research purposes. Additional bibliographic information, not in the original book, is included in green text in square brackets.

The editor wishes to thank the following for permission to reprint the material included in this volume:

GEORGE ALLEN & UNWIN LTD.—for selection from F. Rahman, _Prophecy in Islam_ \[London, 1959\]; and The Macmillan Company (New York) for selections from A. J. Arberry, trans., _Sufism_ \[London 1950\];—and The Macmillan Company (New York) for selections from Montgomery Watt, _The Faith and Practice of Al-Ghasali_ \[London, 1953\];—and for selections from A. J. Arberry, _The Koran Interpreted_ \[London, 1955\].

BOLLINGEN FOUNDATION—for selections from _Ibn Khaldûn: The Muqaddimah_ \[London, 1958\]. Trans. from the Arabic by Franz Rosenthal. Bollingen Series XLIII. By permission of Bollingen Foundation, New York.

P. R. E. BROWNE & E. M. A. BROWNE—for selection from E. G. Browne, _Literary History of Persia_ \[London, 1902\].

CAMBRIDGE UNIVERSITY PRESS—for selection from R. A. Nicholson, _Divani Shamsi Tabriz_;—for selection from E. G. Browne, _Literary History of Persia_ \[London, 1902\];—for selection from A. J. Wensinck, _The Muslim Creed_ \[Cambridge, 1932\];—and for selections from R. A. Nicholson, _Studies in Islamic Mysticism_\[Cambridge, 1921\].

ISLAMIC RESEARCH ASSOCIATION (Bombay)—for selection from A. A. A. Fyzee, _A Shī‘īte Creed_ \[Oxford, 1942\].

THE MACMILLAN COMPANY (New York)—for selections from A. J. Arberry, _The Koran Interpreted_ \[London, Allen & Unwin; New York, Macmillan, 1955\]. Reprinted by permission of the publishers.

RICHARD J. MCCARTHY, S. J.—for selection from _Kitab Al-Lum’a_ \[Beirut, 1955\].

MOUTON & Co. N. V. (The Hague)—for selection from Marshall G. Hodgson (trans.), _The Order of the Assassins_ \['s-Gravenhage, 1955\].

JOHN MURRAY (London) and THE GROVE PRESS—for selection from Sardar Sir Jogendra Singh, _The Persian Mystics_ \[New York, 1939\].
