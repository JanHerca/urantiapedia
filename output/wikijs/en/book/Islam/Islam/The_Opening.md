<span id="p13">[<sup><small>p. 13</small></sup>]</span>

_In the Name of God, the Merciful, the Compassionate_.

_Praise be to God, the Lord of the Worlds,_
_The Merciful One, the Compassionate One,_
_Master of the Day of Doom_.

_Thee alone we serve, to Thee alone we cry for help._
_Guide us in the straight path_
_The path of them Thou hast blessed._
_Not of those with whom Thou art angry_
_Nor of those who go astray_.

The Fattiḥa, the “Lord's Prayer” of Islam, is the opening _sūra_ of the Qur’ān. It is recited for each new beginning.
