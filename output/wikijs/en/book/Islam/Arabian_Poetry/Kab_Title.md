<p style="text-align:center;">
THE “BURDA,”<br>
<br>
<i>i.e.</i>,<br>
<span class="text-h3">THE POEM OF THE MANTLE,</span><br>
BY<br>
<br>
<span class="text-h5">KA‘B, SON OF ZUHAYR, SON OF ABŪ SULMA</span><br>
<br>
RECITED BY HIM BEFORE MUHAMMAD, AT MADĪNA.<br>
<br>
IN THE NINTH YEAR OF THE HIJRA,<br>
<br>
AFTER THE CONQUEST OF MAKKA.<br>
<br>
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_<br>

J. W. REDHOUSE, Esq., M.R.A.S.,<br>

HON. MEMB. R.S.L., ETC.<br>
W. A. CLOUSTON.<br>
