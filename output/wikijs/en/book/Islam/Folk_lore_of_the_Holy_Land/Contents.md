EL KHUDR<br>The fountain of youth.—Dhu’lkarnein and his companions.—El Khudr popularly identified with various saints.—His haunts and habits.—The insane asylum near the “Pools of Solomon.”—The holy stone.—Other shrines.—Elijah's cave on Carmel.—An English doctor's story.—St George and the Dragon.—Elijah's Synagogue at Jerusalem.—El Khudr and Moses. | [51](1_9#p51)
