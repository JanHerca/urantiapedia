## DÎNÂ-Î MAÎNÔG-Î KHIRA<i>D</i>. CHAPTER XXXVIII.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: ‘Wherefore _is it_ when they do not allot the happiness <span id="p76"><sup><small>[ p. 76 ]</small></sup></span> of the worldly _existence_ according to worthiness, and they make the soul a seizer upon the spiritual _existences_ by worthiness of action?’

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'On account of the compassion of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the lord, as regards the creatures, he allots all happiness alike among the good and alike among the bad. 5. But when it does not always come upon them, _it is_ on account of the oppression of Aharman and the demons, and the extortion of those seven planets [^1].

6\. ‘And they make _one_ [^2] a seizer upon the spiritual _existences_, by worthiness of action, on this account, because the wickedness of any [^3] one arises through the performance of his own actions.’
