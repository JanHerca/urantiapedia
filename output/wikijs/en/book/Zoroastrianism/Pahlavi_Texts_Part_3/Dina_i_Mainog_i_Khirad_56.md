## DÎNÂ-Î MAÎNÔG-Î KHIRA<i>D</i>. CHAPTER LVI.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: ‘Wherefore are these mountains and rivers [^1] made, which are in the world?’

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'Of these mountains, which are in the world, there are _some_ which are moderators of the wind, and there are _some_ which are [^2] warders off; (5) there are _some_ which are the place and vent, the resting-place and support of the rainy cloud; (6) and there are _some_ which are smiters of Aharman and the demons, and maintainers and vivifiers of the creatures and creation of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the lord.

7\. ‘And these rivers, which are in the world, the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> has formed, from the borders of Albûr<i>z</i> [^3], for providing the protection and _for_ the vivification of his own creatures and creation.’
