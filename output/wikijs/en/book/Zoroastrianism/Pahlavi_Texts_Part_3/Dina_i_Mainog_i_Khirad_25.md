## DÎNÂ-Î MAÎNÔG-Î KHIRA<i>D</i>. CHAPTER XXV.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: ‘Of the rich who is the poorer, and of the poor who is the richer?’ <span id="p56"><sup><small>[ p. 56 ]</small></sup></span> 3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'Of the rich he is the poorer who is not content with that which is his, (5) and suffers anxiety for the increase of anything.

6\. ‘_And_ of the poor he is the richer who is content with that which has come, (7) and cares not for the increase of anything.’
