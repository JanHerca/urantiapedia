## DÎNÂ-Î MAÎNÔG-Î KHIRA<i>D</i>. CHAPTER XXXIV.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: ‘_Has_ the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> produced the creation of anything whatever for the worldly _existence_ [^1], unto which Aharman is not able to bring disturbance?’

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: ‘To him who is a wise and contented man it is _but_ little possible to bring disturbance.’
