## DÎNÂ-Î MAÎNÔG-Î KHIRA<i>D</i>. CHAPTER L.

1\. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: ‘Which is that opulent _person_ who is to be considered as fortunate, and which is that _one_ who is _to be considered_ as evil-conditioned?’

3\. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: ‘That _one_ who has produced opulence by proper exertion is to be considered as fortunate; and that _one_ who has produced _it_ by dishonesty, as evil-conditioned.’
