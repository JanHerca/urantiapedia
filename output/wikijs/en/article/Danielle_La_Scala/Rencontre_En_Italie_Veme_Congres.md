**Vth Italian Congress of Urantia Book Readers**

Thursday October 24 to Sunday October 27, 2019

My dear friends, Urantian brothers and sisters, it is always with great joy that I invite you to participate in our meeting near Lake Garda whose theme is:

**spiritual maturity**

It will be held at:

25015 Desenzano del Garda
Hotel Europa — TF 00390309142333
Lungolago Cesare Battisti 71

from Thursday October 24 to Sunday October 27, 2019 I will be happy to welcome my faithful French and Spanish reader friends, interested in our fraternal meeting, and - hopefully - also new participants!

Price per day and per person in full board (wine, mineral water and coffee included)
- in a room occupied by two people: €60
- in a room occupied by one person: €70
- Meal (without accommodation) extras included €19

Please let me know by email if you intend to participate-

Confirmation will be sent to the hotel before September 20 - details will follow. Looking forward to meeting you on Thursday October 24 from 6 p.m. for an aperitif, followed by a friendly meal, I send you my friendly thoughts and wish you a summer just as you like it.

Brotherly friendships

Danielle LaScala
