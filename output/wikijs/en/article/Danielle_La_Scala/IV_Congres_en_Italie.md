_5 to 7 or October 2018 in Lazise (Lake Garda)_

Dear friends, readers, I have the pleasure of inviting you to the IVth Congress of Italian-speaking readers with the participation of French readers of the _Urantia Book_ — Spanish and German-speaking readers will all be welcome.

<figure id="Figure_15" class="image urantiapedia image-style-align-right">
<img src="/image/article/Le_Lien/images_02/141.jpg">
</figure>

Our meeting will take place in LAZISE, a charming village on Lake Garda, not far from Verona (airport approximately 15 km away) from Friday 5 to Sunday 7 or Monday 8 October 2018. We will be staying at the CONFINE hotel (Tel. 003904575700 98) where we will have our first evening meal upon our arrival on Friday evening (19€: wine, mineral water and coffee included).

Saturday and Sunday we will eat in a pizzeria (with fish specialty) near the hotel. We will have a room available Saturday and Sunday for our meetings. Registration is 20€ and includes coffee break and welcome aperitif.

The price of the room with buffet breakfast is €45 for a double room occupied by one person, and €60 for two people including breakfast.

The detailed program will be sent to you soon — think now about reserving these days at the beginning of October for this meeting!
