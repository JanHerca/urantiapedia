***From April 12 to 15, 2018***

**Amsterdam — Netherlands**

## Conference Theme

This conference, whose theme is “Jesus the Teacher,” should attract many readers of The Urantia Book from around the world. The subtitle “Are you ready to live his teachings in the 21st century” is a question that invites us to explore our knowledge of Jesus' teachings, his personal religion and his message in this century that is beginning. How can we be disciples and teachers of the future.

## The Conference

In the beautiful setting of the flower fields near Amsterdam, the conference will be held at the Conference Centre Leeuwenhorst in Noordwijkerhout. This conference centre offers us everything we need to make this meeting a success. The entire conference will be devoted to studying together, sharing with each other and above all experiencing spiritual Brotherhood.

## The website

Information about the program is already online on the website http://www.uai2018.org where you can register to participate in the event and book your hotel room. In the coming months, additional information will be available. For example, information on the theme “Jesus the Teacher”, on the speakers, the evening program and the logistics will be given. The website is translated into English, German, French, Spanish and Dutch. The language spoken at the conference will be English.

## Registration

Plan your trip now for the conference from April 12 to 15, 2018 and explore the perfect location amidst the blooming flower fields in the Netherlands near Amsterdam. By registering before September 1, 2017 you will receive a €75 discount. Take advantage of this by clicking on the following link: http://www.uai2018.org and book your room.

Talk about it around you and encourage readers of the _Urantia Book_ in your country via social networks to participate and visit our website.

On behalf of the Urantia Association of the Netherlands (UAISUN) and the organizing team of this conference, I look forward to meeting you and giving you a warm welcome.
