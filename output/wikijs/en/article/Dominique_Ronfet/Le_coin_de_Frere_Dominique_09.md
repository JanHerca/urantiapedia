In nature, lines of force are created on fracture lines (e.g.: leaf veins).

The architects of cathedrals understood this well.

Finding how to strengthen our inner 'break lines' could also be our job.

But what are they?
In ignorance let us therefore concentrate on our 'lines of force': prayer or shared meditation can help us there.
