I asked to write me a poem based on The Urantia Book And it's bombastic, with well-constructed images, not bad at all, a little strange, but rather machine-like, let's not forget.

You'd swear he read it (The Urantia Book)

<p style="text-align: center;">
In the pages of The Urantia Book, a knowledge awakens,<br>
A journey through the stars, truths that amaze.<br>
Sacred cosmology, revelations of the heavens,<br>
An ocean of wisdom, marvelous horizons.<br>
<br>
In the beginning, the First Source and Center,<br>
An eternal creation, a celestial canvas.<br>
Expanding Universes, life unfolding,<br>
In the infinity of space, harmony dances.<br>
<br>
Celestial beings, guardians of the worlds,<br>
Guide souls, like a sweet praise.<br>
Human beings, mortals on Earth,<br>
Seeking truth, love and light.<br>
<br>
In each soul, a divine particle,<br>
A Fragment of God, a prone jewel.<br>
Universal brotherhood, an eternal bond,<br>
Weaving hearts together, a solemn hymn.<br>
<br>
The teachings reveal the purpose,<br>
A supreme destiny, a divine calling.<br>
The evolution of the soul, an eternal journey,<br>
Towards the future age, real ethereal love.<br>
<br>
In the quest for the Father, spiritual ascension,<br>
A celestial dance, a universal symphony.<br>
The heavenly realms, an infinite brotherhood,<br>
In the embrace of love, a blessed harmony.<br>
<br>
This is how the fabric of The Urantia Book is woven,<br>
From stars to souls, a magnolia vision.<br>
In the shadow of mysteries, light shines,<br>
Divine knowledge, eternal love that sparkles.<br>
<br>
Dominique Ronfet<br>
</p>
