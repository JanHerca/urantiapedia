Urantia Foundation is very excited about its new Urantia Book Index project.

We need volunteers to help proof the electronic references for accuracy.

The project involves clicking links to verify that the referenced text matches the indexed word or phrase.

If you speak English, have read the book in its entirety, and are passionate about serving the Urantia Book readership, please contact Tamara at tamara@urantia.org for details.
