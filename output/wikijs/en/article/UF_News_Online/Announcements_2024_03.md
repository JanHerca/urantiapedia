## The Marriage and Family Adventure! An Online Symposium

<p style="text-align:center;">
<b>Urantia Foundation Education Committee</b><br>
<br>
<b>presents</b><br>
<br>
<b>The Marriage and Family Adventure! An Online Symposium</b><br>
<br>
<b>Friday and Saturday, June 14–15, 2024</b>
</p>

Join us as we examine the rich tapestry of marriage and family as viewed in _The Urantia Book_, academia, and personal experience. This enlightening and comprehensive series of presentations will explore the multifaceted aspects of relationships, unions, and familial dynamics.

Our goal is to offer inspiration and guidance for individuals and families seeking to navigate the complexities of human relationships, they are the most spiritual of adventures.

Our panel of presenters includes Chuck and Angie Thurston; Alice Wood and Vicki Arkens; Brad Garner and Ruthie Wilde; Marvin Gawryn; Guy Perron and Agnès Lazar; Stephanie “Stevie” Schaefer; and trustees Gard Jameson, Georges Michelson-Dupont, Olga López, and Chris Wood, who will be joined by his band of brothers.

Each live webinar session will be 30 minutes long, followed by an interactive panel discussion. Emcees Bill and Share Beasley will infuse the event with experiential wisdom and humor.

Mark your calendars now. Additional details will be emailed in early May.

If you received this newsletter, you should get future notifications. If you are not yet on our mailing list, go to [https://www.urantia.org/inset/join-our-mailing-list](https://www.urantia.org/inset/join-our-mailing-list) .
