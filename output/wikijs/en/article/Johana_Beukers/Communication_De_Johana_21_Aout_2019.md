AUGUST 21, 2019

For the second time, Guy Antéquera welcomed us to his home in Mouries.

There were 17 of us readers, 10 readers from the Alpilles, 6 from Marseille and one from Paris. (Questioning of participants about readers from Var).

The study began with a thought for our friend Jean Royer who has already started his journey to Paradise.

This study was based on the soul, on a text that Guy had prepared, a vast and complex subject which raised several questions and lively debates among readers.

Superb weather, as always in the south, and a large buffet after an afternoon filled with intense discussions about the soul closed this meeting full of fraternity. A big thank you to Guy for his kindness, his hospitality and the beautiful group photo.

Johanna
