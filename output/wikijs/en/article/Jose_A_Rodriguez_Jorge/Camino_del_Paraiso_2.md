<p style="text-align:center;">
I go by your hand<br>
path to paradise<br>
even though my steps<br>
continue on earth;<br>
happy, smiling<br>
I turn to you<br>
my father<br>
with great effort<br>
true to my destiny<br>
guided by your light<br>
in which I,<br>
despite the darkness,<br>
Do not doubt, but I trust.
<p>
