What did his apostles admire about Jesus?

- Sincerity, dignity without affection (Andrew).
- Supreme tenderness (Simon Peter).
- Compassionate affection (James Zebedee).
- Love and altruism (John Zebedee).
- Generosity (Philip the Curious).
- Tolerance (Nathaniel the Honest).
- Sorry (Matthew Levi).
- Balance (Thomas Didymus).
- Humility (James and Judas Alpheus).
- Calm, security, balance and serenity (Simon the Zealot).
- Charming and attractive personality (Judas Iscariot).
