As we come to the end of 2012, I would like us to draw closer to the Spirit, for you see, this is how we will come to understand the directives of the Adjuster, the recommendations of the Angels to use the Spirit of Truth effectively and to fully feel our Divine Minister Mother.

We are also preparing a series of annual meetings based on the inner evolution of the Self up to God, which will follow the studies on the outer evolution of the Planet up to Paradise.

Of course, inner evolution occurs through inner communion, but also through experiences with others and on three levels, energetic, mental and spiritual, which complicates the planning of these studies.

And, we'll start with freedom and free will!

So take up your pens, dear Brothers!

Ivan Stol
