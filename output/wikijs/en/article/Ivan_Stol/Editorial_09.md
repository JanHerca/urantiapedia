Dear readers,

Despite a sustained controversy over the origins of The Urantia Book, those who have read it seem to be attached to it by discovering the true spiritual values it contains. This is how, throughout the world, under the impetus of small, increasingly numerous groups, spirituality and fraternity are developing gradually and almost uniformly. On the other hand, the methods differ according to the continents and the peoples, allowing imagination and creativity to express themselves widely, announcing future instructive exchanges. I will speak about this later.

And once this work is better understood, some wonder how to make it known to others (not all, only 1% of us).

As you read these pages you will find some ideas submitted by a few people followed by a report on the meetings in Italy and Spain.

And perhaps you would like to exercise your brain with the abstract concepts of infinities, absolutes, deities...?

So I wish you a good read.

Fraternally

Ivan
