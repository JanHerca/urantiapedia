**Central Universe**

1. What are the 7 planetary physical types?
2. God is the absolute Father but how does he operate outside of Havona?
3. In the 7 groups of Supreme Spirits, there are some who: see everything, hear everything, feel everything and even know everything that happens in the seven superuniverses and in Havona. Who are they?

**Local Universe**

4. Following the creation of Gabriel, a new form of creative union occurs between the Creator Son and the Creative Spirit. What is the being that is born from this union?
5. In the universe of Nebadon, who constitute the supreme advisory cabinet of the Creator Son?
6. At the head of the Brilliant Evening Stars is a leader, a superangel, who is he?
7. On Jerusalem, what is the harp of God used for?

**Urantia**

8. On Urantia, Caligastia had a chief of staff, but who was the chief of staff?
9. What is the motto of the United Medians?
10. On Urantia, who was the first to teach the Truth?
11. Who was Noah but, not the Noah of the biblical ark and winemaker of Aram?

**Jesus**

12. At his fifth bestowal, Michael bestowed himself on Uversa as an ascending pilgrim. By what name was this pilgrim known?
13. In his twenty-third year, in the year 17, in Jerusalem, Jesus met a young Greek who was coming to Jerusalem for the first time. This young man, 15 years later, was stoned by angry Jews thus becoming the first martyr of the new nascent Christian faith, who was he?
14. In Rome, a person proposed to Jesus that they establish a new religion together. Who was this person?
15. In February of the year 26, Jesus chose 4 apostles, which ones?
16. Let us keep our feet on the ground and not become fanatics like a certain Phoenician woman who, at the camp of Bethsaida, lost her mind. Who was this woman?
17. On what date, approximately, give or take a few days, did Micaël begin organizing his universe?
