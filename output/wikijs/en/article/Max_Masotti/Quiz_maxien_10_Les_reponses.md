**Superuniverse**

1. The Forty-nine Reflective Spirits. They are of Trinitarian origin. The Reflective Spirits have names, but their appellations are not revealed on the worlds of space. <a id="a40_169"></a>[UB 17:3.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/17#p3_1)
2. Usatia, the superuniverse leader of the Orvonton census directors. <a id="a41_70"></a>[UB 24:2.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/24#p2_5)
3. Malvorian. "And Malvorian, the first of that order, welcomed and instructed the pilgrim discoverer of Havona. <a id="a42_113"></a>[UB 24:6.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/24#p6_6)

**Local Universe**

4. The entire judicial mechanism of Nebadon is placed under the supervision of Gabriel. <a id="a46_88"></a>[UB 33:7.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/33#p7_2)
5.
	1. The seat of the Life Bearers.
	2. The sphere of life projects.
	3. The sphere of conservation of life.
	4. The sphere of life evolution.
	5. The sphere of life linked to the mind.
	6. The sphere of mind and spirit in living beings.
	7. The unrevealed sphere of life. <a id="a54_35"></a>[UB 36:2.2-8](/en/The_Urantia_Book/36#p2_2)
6. An average pace of between 893,000 and 899,400 kilometers per second of your time. <a id="a55_86"></a>[UB 39:3.9](/en/The_Urantia_Book/39#p3_9)
7. The present constellation government has, however, been expanded to include twelve Sons of the Vorondadek order. <a id="a56_116"></a>[UB 43:5.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/43#p5_2)

**Urantia Rebellion**

8. Manotia, second in command of the seraphim of Satania headquarters. <a id="a60_71"></a>[UB 53:6.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/53#p6_1). This angel is still in service on Urantia where he functions as deputy chief of the seraphim. <a id="a60_208"></a>[UB 53:6.6](/en/The_Urantia_Book/53#p6_6)
9. On Panoptia, Ellanora, a young woman from that mortal realm, took command of the human races, and not a single soul from that conflict-torn world enlisted under Lucifer's banner. <a id="a61_182"></a>[UB 53:7.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/53#p7_1).
10. The new System Sovereign — Lanaforge, number 2,709 of the primary order and successor to the apostate Lucifer. <a id="a62_115"></a>[UB 45:3.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/45#p3_2).

**Urantia**

11. Monmatia, name of the planetary family of your sun, the solar system to which your world belongs. <a id="a66_102"></a>[UB 57:5.3](/en/The_Urantia_Book/57#p5_3).
12. The twins were about ten years old—the spirit of worship first came into contact with the thoughts of the female twin, and soon after with those of the male twin. <a id="a67_167"></a>[UB 62:6.5](/en/The_Urantia_Book/62#p6_5).
13.
	1. The Council on Food and Material Well-being. Directed by Ang.
	2. The advice of domestication and use of animals. Good
	3. Consultants tasked with triumphing over beasts of prey. Dan
	4. The college responsible for propagating and preserving knowledge. Fad
	5. The Committee on Industry and Commerce. Nod
	6. The college of revealed religion. Hap
	7. The guardians of health and life. Lut
	8. The Planetary Council of Arts and Sciences. Mek
	9. The governors of higher tribal relations. Tut
	10. The Supreme Court of Tribal Coordination and Racial Cooperation. Van
14. His name was Cano. <a id="a79_23"></a>[UB 75:4.7](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p4_7) It is true that I found Cano pleasant to look at... <a id="a79_117"></a>[UB 75:3.8](/en/The_Urantia_Book/75#p3_8).

**Jesus**

15. In March of the year 8 BCE (the month Joseph and Mary were married). <a id="a83_73"></a>[UB 122:7.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/122#p7_1)
16. On Saturday, April 9, in the year 7, Jesus was qualified to go to Jerusalem with his parents and participate with them in the celebration of his first Passover. <a id="a84_165"></a>[UB 124:6.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/124#p6_1).
17. John the Baptist was executed, by order of Herod Antipas, on the evening of January 10, year 28. <a id="a85_101"></a>[UB 144:9.1](/en/The_Urantia_Book/144#p9_1)
18. When James of Safed had brought the young man, Jesus asked him: “How long has the boy been afflicted in this way?” The father answered: “Since he was a little boy.” The father knelt down again at Jesus' feet and implored the Master, saying: “If you can heal him, I beg you to have compassion on us and deliver us from this affliction.” Hearing these words, Jesus lowered his gaze to the father's anxious face and said: “Do not doubt the power of my Father's love, but only the sincerity and scope of your faith. All things are possible for the one who truly believes._” Then, Jacques de Safed pronounced these words mixed with faith and doubt, which will be remembered for a long time: “_Lord, I believe, I pray you to come to the aid of my unbelief._” <a id="a86_757"></a>[UB 158:5.2](/en/The_Urantia_Book/158#p5_2).
19.
	1. Returning evil for evil—the positive but unjust method.
	2. .To bear evil without complaint or resistance — the purely negative method.
	3. Returning good for evil, asserting one's will so as to dominate the situation and triumph over evil with good—the positive and just method. <a id="a90_144"></a>[UB 159:5.12-14](/en/The_Urantia_Book/159#p5_12).
