**Jesus**

1. What is the present status of Machiventa?
2. When the Master died, who took possession of his body for burial?
3. Where did Jesus' body rest after his death?
4. Who were the people who carried the body of Jesus?
5. At the crucifixion, John returned from the city and stood with Mary and Jude. What did Jesus say to John?
6. What was Jesus' last conscious thought?
7. After the Master's death, what did John do?
8. What did the Jewish leaders plan to do with Jesus’ body?
9. Who requested the body of Jesus to ensure a decent burial?
10. What were the names of the women who lingered near the tomb that Friday evening?
11. What did the spokesperson of the Sanhedrin ask Pilate to do to protect Jesus' tomb?
12. After their request to Pilate to guard Jesus' tomb, what did the Sanhedrin do?
13. On other neighboring planets, what is Urantia called?
14. Do you think sin can be passed from parents to children? And what is sin?
15. What are the lessons of the cross of Jesus?
16. When and at what time did the resurrected morontia form and personality of Jesus emerge from the tomb?
17. What happened to Jesus' body?
18. A little before three o'clock on Sunday morning in the year 30, five of the women left for Jesus' tomb. Who were these women?
19. When the five women, who had seen Jesus, returned to warn the apostles, which two apostles went immediately to the tomb?
20. What were the stages of Jesus' morontia career?
21. Where did the Master's last morontia appearance take place?
22. There is a spirit nucleus in the mind of man—the Adjuster of the divine presence. There are three distinct proofs that this spirit indwells the human mind: What are they?
23. James Zebedee asked Jesus to teach him a prayer and, in response, Jesus offered him the one he had taught his brothers in Nazareth, what was this prayer?
24. Subsidiary question: Not recorded in The Urantia Book. What did Nicodemus say to Joseph of Arimathea?

<ins>When all is said and done, the idea of Father still remains the highest human concept of God.</ins>

***End of quizzes***
