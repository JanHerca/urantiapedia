**Superuniverse**

1. Our system, our constellation and Salvington are all registered. Do you remember their registration numbers?
2. We all know about the Master Physical Controllers, but do you know the functions of the Frandalanks?
3. What is the name of the first Agondontary of Finalitarian status and survivor of the first rebellion of time and space?

**Local Universe**

4. Where does the misconception that angels have wings come from?
5. In a cluster of how many spheres is the government of Norlatiadek located?
6. In the Garden of Gethsemane, Jesus spoke of 12 legions of angels. How many angels are in a legion?
7. We will all go to the mansion world, at least we hope so, but do you know what the mansion worlds are called?
8. I hope you never have to deal with him: what is the name of the jailer and curator of the system?
9. Who are the landscape gardeners of the headquarters worlds?

**Urantia**

10. What number of the order of Primary Lanonandeks did Lucifer wear?
11. Since the Lucifer Rebellion, who is the new Vicegerent Planetary Prince of Urantia?
12. How many years ago was Urantia recorded as an inhabited world?

**Jesus**

13. In the year 28, during the second period of preaching in Galilee, a person maintained for the work of the kingdom and on his own initiative, a headquarters and a relay station for the service of messengers. Who did this?
14. In what year did Amos, Jesus' youngest brother, die?
15. On November 19, 29 A.D., in the camp of Magadan, Jesus ordained 70 teachers and preachers of the gospel. Who was placed at the head of these preachers?
16. In AD 29, in Bethany, Jesus gave sight to a blind man named Josiah after putting mud on his eyes and telling him to go and wash in the pool. What was the name of this pool?
17. On Friday, March 31, in the year 30, Jesus stayed with Simon, the chief nobleman of the village of Bethany. At the end of the feast, a woman began to anoint Jesus' head with a precious ointment. Who was this woman?
18. In March of the year 30, Jesus entered Jerusalem riding a donkey, why on a donkey rather than on a horse?
19. What was the name of the village where Jesus resurrected Lazarus?
