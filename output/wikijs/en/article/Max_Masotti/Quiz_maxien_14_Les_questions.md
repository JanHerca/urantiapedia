**Central Universe**

1. Who will make up the Ultimate Trinity?
2. The human mind is a gift. Who gives us this gift?
3. Blessed are the Paradise Sons of God. But who then personalizes the Creator Sons, the Magisterial Sons and the Instructor Sons of the Trinity?

**Local universe**

4. In the government of each of the hundred constellations of a local universe, there are three Very High Fathers Vorondadeks. How long are these 3 Vorondadeks, heads of the constellations, assigned?
5. What is the destiny of the seraphim?
6. What are the neighboring universes of Nebadon? (Give 3 names)
7. How many adjutants do spornagias have?
8. What is the name of the system archivist?

**Urantia**

9. During the time of Adam and Eve, what were the carrier birds called?
10. Laotta is a nice name, but who was she?
11. We know that: 1-2-3, the first is the oldest of the medians of the primary order but, do we know what function he has already fulfilled since Pentecost?
12. What are the nine races of Urantia?
13. How long did it take for Machiventa to complete his bestowal mission on Urantia?
14. 190 With regard to the survival of the personality, there are three kinds of deaths, which ones?

**Jesus**

15. In what month of what year were Joseph and Mary married?
16. Among the Greek believers, in the year 30, Jesus knew that the hour of his death was approaching, he was troubled and glorified his Father. What happened following this glorification?
17. We all know the Pool of Siloam, but another monument bore this name. Which monument was it?
18. When we really want something, we must always insist. On Wednesday evening in the year 30, at the camp of Gethsemane, when Jesus wanted to leave alone to pray to his Father, a boy insisted on following him and he even had the audacity to hold the handle of the basket of food. Who was this boy who wanted so much to accompany Jesus?
19. In the year 30, on Tuesday evening, on Mount Olivet, Peter asked the Master the following question: “How will we know when you will return?” What did Jesus answer?
20. At the end of the last supper, on Thursday evening of the year 30, Jesus gave a new commandment to his apostles. What was this commandment?
