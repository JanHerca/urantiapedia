**Superuniverse**

1. We all know that Lone Messengers go very fast but do we know their exact speed?
2. You all know that the Palonia system is undergoing a rebellion, but do you remember the name of the rebel System Sovereign?
3. In the Grand Universe, the tutelary spirits are classified into 7 orders. Can you name these 7 orders of classification?

**Local Universe**

4. A trinitarian personality is Michael's heavenly brother, what is his name?
5. Do you know about the worlds of the Melchizedeks? These wonderful spheres—70 primary and 420 tributaries—are often called the Melchizedek University.
6. The Director of the Census of the universe of Nebadon is automatically informed of the birth and death of the will. Do you know his name?
7. What is the name of the receiving area of Edentia?
8. These educators from the world of houses are nice, but who are they?

**Urantia**

9. On Urantia, what is the Nebadon standard day equivalent to?
10. An easy question? It will change things up a bit. Who gives us the adjutant mental spirits? Do you know them?
11. Before the rebellion, do you remember how many worlds Lucifer ruled over?
12. A fifth order angel, a seraphim and chief of the planetary helpers in service at that time, proclaimed the failure of Adam and Eve's plan. But who was this angel?
13. Come on, an easy question. What is the name and if possible the first name of the first red man who led his people to the worship of the “Great Spirit”?

**Jesus**

14. Jesus was a musician and he loved to play an instrument. Do you remember what instrument Jesus played?
15. A question that Yves G. will have no problem answering. Jesus had many brothers and sisters, what were their names?
16. Among all the apostles, 3 of them were closest to understanding the Master's teaching on prayer and worship, who were they?
17. During the transfiguration, Jesus took with him 3 apostles, which ones? Was there a reason for the choice of these 3 apostles?
18. Jesus gave Josiah his sight at the Pool of Siloam, but he healed another blind man in Jericho. What was his name?

Max Masotti
