**Urantia**

1. When did Adam and Eve arrive on Urantia?
2. What are the religions of the twentieth century?
3. In Salem, Machiventa promulgated seven commandments modeled on the ancient supreme law of Dalamatia, which ones?
4. Who was Abraham's father?
5. Melchizedek made a formal covenant with Abraham in Salem, which one?
6. Thought Adjusters are classified into 7 orders, which ones?
7. At the time of Gabriel's roll call in A.D. 30, how many complete dispensational resurrections did Urantia experience?

**Jesus**

8. During his ministry, Jesus was accompanied by 12 apostles, what were their names?
9. After decreeing the death of Jesus, in whose house did the Sanhedrin meet to formulate the accusations that would allow Jesus to be indicted?
10. At the last Passover, on Thursday evening in the year 30, there was a violent dispute among the apostles concerning places of honor and the like. Do you remember the places the apostles occupied?
11. Jesus informed his apostles that it was necessary for him to leave them to return to the Father. Why was this necessary?
12. Following the arrest of the Master and after discussing among themselves, do you remember what the apostles did?
13. Do you remember the people who stood near the cross at one point or another during the crucifixion?
14. At the burial of Jesus, some women had hidden and lingered near Joseph's tomb. These women judged that the body of Jesus had not been properly prepared for burial. They agreed to return on Sunday morning and embalm the body of the Master as was fitting for mortuary repose. Who were these women?
15. What was the ninth morontia appearance of Jesus?
16. The actual realization of the attainment of the total divinity of Jesus passed through seven stages of consciousness, through faith, of his progressive divinization. What are these seven stages?
17. When Jesus was resurrected, who was with Joseph of Arimathea?
18. What should be the primary concern of believers?
19. After the Master's death, what did David Zebedee do?
20. What was Jesus' last request as a mortal?
