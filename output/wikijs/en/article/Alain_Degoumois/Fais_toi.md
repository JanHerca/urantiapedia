Make your life a work of art.
May every moment of this precious present,
Be dressed in your most beautiful thoughts,
Of your noblest acts.

Make your life a hymn to joy,
Penetrate deep into the matter,
And fertilize the darkness with luminous thoughts,
Flowers of thoughts to sow the inner and outer world.
Sow the seeds of your destiny,

With a happy hand,
With a loving hand,
With a confident, humble and generous hand.
That through your earthly experiences,
May you make your soul vibrate,
Like a celestial harp,
So that it takes shape and expresses itself within the earth itself.

Make your life a work of art,
And communicate your work in matter,
Materializes celestial Ideas,
Idealizes earthly matter.
That each of your gestures,
May each of your actions, your thoughts,
They bear within them the seal of your soul,
And blossom in the Love of the eternal present.

Make your dreams of light a reality without borders,
Without prejudice or attachment.
Detach yourself from the futile and too servile
And believes without hesitation, slowly and surely,
In the Unconditional Love of time, your most beautiful present.

Alain Degoumois
