_Humor is the cleaning lady of the mind!_

_We can laugh at everything, but not with everyone!_ Pierre Desproges

_A sense of humor was given to men to console them for what they are!_

_For a successful evening, you should know that the feast is not only in the dishes, but also in the mood of the guests!_

_You can't judge someone's personality better than by discovering what makes them laugh!_

_My favorite pastime? Laughter!_ The Dalai Lama

_It's not because we're getting old that we don't have fun anymore, it's because we don't have fun anymore that we get old!_ Michael Pritchar

_Humor is such a blessing... Why leave it to chance?_ Joël Goodman

_I don't have two faces. If I had two, I wouldn't go out with that one!_ Abraham Lincoln

_He who makes his companions laugh deserves paradise!_ The Koran

_There's no need to teach people how to be funny. Just give them permission!_ Dr. Mindess

_People are willing to pay more to be entertained than to be educated!_ Johnny Carson

_The shortest distance between one person and another is laughter!_ Victor Borge

_I am convinced that humans succeed where they have fun!_ Greg Norman
