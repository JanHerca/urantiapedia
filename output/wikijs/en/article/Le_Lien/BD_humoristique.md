Christophe Delvallé, a committed Christian, has collaborated with numerous newspapers and magazines and has published a dozen comic book albums, humorous drawings and press cartoons.

Extracts taken from _Jesus among the Pharisees: Edition Husky Productions — 2005, script and drawings: Delvallé Humorous black and white strips on the world of Jesus and his disciples._

His website: www.Delvalle.fr
