Deep down inside, I give thanks for the love that I am. For the love that is in my life and for the love that surrounds me, THANK YOU.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the miracle of life that I am.<br>
<br>
For the wonderful gift of life that lives within me and surrounds me, THANK YOU.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for this perfect body.<br>
<br>
For my health and for my well-being, THANK YOU.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the abundance that I am.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the abundance I see around me.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for all this wealth.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the richness of my life.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the flow of money that comes to me and flows through me.<br>
<br>
For the adventure of my life and the myriad of wonderful possibilities<br>
Which are offered to me, THANK YOU.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the wonder.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the joy.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the beauty and harmony.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the peace of mind.<br>
<br>
THANKS for the laughs.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU for the entertainment.<br>
<br>
And for the privilege of serving and sharing the gift that I am.<br>
<br>
THANK YOU, THANK YOU, THANK YOU.<br>
