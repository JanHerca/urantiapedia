Organized by “The Blue Club” Budapest — September 15 - September 18, 2016

This meeting is organized by Le Blue Club, an association of European readers who have given several conferences mainly in Germany.

It will be held at the Novotel Budapest Central hotel, Rakoczi ut 43-45, BudAPEST 1088

Hungary from September 15 to 18, 2016.

The theme of the conference is “Who is Jesus” (Part Four of The Urantia Book)

For more information, contact Christian Ruch at chruch@gmx.ch or Alexander Hehlert at amadonfv@live.de
