131:8.1 The messengers of Melchizedek penetrated far into China, and the doctrine of one God became a part of the earlier teachings of several Chinese religions; the one persisting the longest and containing most of the monotheistic truth was Taoism, and Ganid collected the following from the teachings of its founder:[2]

How great and mighty are his overflowing influence and drawing power! True goodness is like water in that it blesses everything and harms nothing. 131:9.2 ‚ÄúWhat Heaven appoints is without error. Truth is real and divine. Everything originates in Heaven, and the Great Heaven makes no mistakes.
