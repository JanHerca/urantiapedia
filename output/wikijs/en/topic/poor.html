The poor are always with us, so we must minister to them whenever it seems good to us. [1] The gospel is preached to all, regardless of status, as seen in the greater numbers of the poor accepting it readily. [2] Jesus blessed the sincere and pious poor, condemning the wanton and irreligious rich. [3] Minister to and share with the poor to let the oppressed go free and break every yoke, as Jesus taught.
