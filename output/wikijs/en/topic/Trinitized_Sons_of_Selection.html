The Trinitized Sons of Selection, recruited from various celestial beings who have traversed Havona and attained Paradise, are assigned to courts of Ancients of Days. [1]

Composition

The Trinitized Custodians, comprised of ascendant seraphim and translated midway creatures, are commissioned by the Paradise Trinity to serve the superuniverses as trusted custodians and administrators of group affairs. [2]

  • Trinitized Ambassadors.
  • Trinitized Custodians.

Trinitized Sons of Selection undergo classification in Paradise. [3]

Functions

The Trinitized Sons of Selection are recruited from ascendant seraphim, midway creatures, Spiritfused, and Son-fused mortals, trained in Havona, and assigned to the courts of the Ancients of Days. [4]

Trinitized Sons of Selection, such as Mighty Messengers, attain destiny through the Trinity embrace, receiving everything finaliters will achieve as seventh-stage spirits in the present universe age. [5]
