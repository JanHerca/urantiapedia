Arrested Jesus. [1] Compelled Simon of Cyrene to carry crossbeam. [2] Entered into full fellowship of kingdom. [3] Gave Jesus a sponge of wine. [4] Helped bear body of Jesus to tomb; rolled door-stone. [5] Led procession to Golgotha. [6] Stationed at cross. [7] Stood astride Master’s body with drawn sword. [8] Told Luke story of thief on cross. [9] Took Jesus to Annas by prearrangement. [10]
