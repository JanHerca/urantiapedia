At Jesus’ 4th appearance. [1] Born April 14, A.D. 2. [2] Brought Jude home after he ran away. [3] Graduated from school in A.D. 16. [4] Jesus took Simon to Jerusalem for Passover. [5] Married March, A.D. 24. [6] Placated bellicose playmates by persuasion and nonresistance. [7] Started school. [8] Well-meaning boy; dreamer. [9] Worked with Jacob the stone mason. [10]
