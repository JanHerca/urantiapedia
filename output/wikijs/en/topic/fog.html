Neanderthalers were deathly afraid of clouds, more especially of mists and fogs. [1]

When the clouds gather overhead, our faith should accept the fact of the presence of the indwelling Adjuster, and thus we should be able to look beyond the mists of mortal uncertainty into the clear shining of the sun of eternal righteousness on the beckoning heights of the mansion worlds of Satania.
