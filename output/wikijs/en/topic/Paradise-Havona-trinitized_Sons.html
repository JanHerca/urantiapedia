The Paradise-Havona-trinitized Sons were created by Paradise Citizens and Havoners. [1] The Paradise-Havona-trinitized Sons are recorded in Uversa's personality register. [2]

Evangels of Light, celestial personalities attached to mortal finaliter companies, may serve in transient assignments before resuming their former status. [3]
