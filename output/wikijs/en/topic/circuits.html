Tracing any circuit, whether it be personality, material gravity, cosmic force, spirit gravity, or mental circuitry, all lead back to the Father's presence on Paradise, his central abode. [1] Angels are sustained directly by the intake of the spiritual energy of the primary circuits of the universe. [2]

Stationed in the local universes are one hundred Power Centers that modify the seven circuits of power that emanate from the superuniverse headquarters. The centers provide lanes or lines of intensified energy that are much used by the Creator Sons. [3]

God is only discernibly present on Paradise; outside of it, God limits himself to respect the sovereignty of his coordinates. That is why the divine presence must be obtained through a series of presence circuits that are perfectly equivalent to the presence of the Father. [4] The universal circuits of Paradise do actually pervade the realms of the seven superuniverses. These presence circuits of Paradise are: [5]

  1. The personality gravity of the Universal Father.
  2. The spiritual gravity of the Eternal Son.
  3. The mind gravity of the Conjoint Actor.
  4. The material gravity of the eternal Isle.

The master circuits of the grand universe are derived from the personality presences of the Paradise Deities. [6] Reflectivity phenomena seems to be independent of all subabsolute universe circuits. [7] Paradise and the Havona circuits are the ancestor creations of all universe creation. [8] In addition to the universal Paradise circuits there function two energy-circuit divisions or power segregations: [9]

  • The Superuniverse Circuits:
    1. The unifying intelligence circuit of one of the Seven Master Spirits.
    2. The reflective-service circuit of the seven Reflective Spirits.
    3. The secret circuits of the Mystery Monitors.
    4. The circuit of the intercommunion of the Eternal Son with his Paradise Sons.
    5. The flash presence of the Infinite Spirit.
    6. The broadcasts of Paradise.
    7. The energy circuits of the power centers.
  • The Local Universe Circuits:
    1. The bestowal spirits. The Spirit of Truth, the spirit of Michael on Urantia.
    2. The circuit of the Divine Ministers.
    3. The circuit of the adjutant mind-spirits.
