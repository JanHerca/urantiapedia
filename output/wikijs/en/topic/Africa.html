Geology evolution

  • 800 million years ago there was the first land elevation of a single continent, emerging from the Pacific Ocean, which already contained the original Africa. [1]
  • 500 million years ago Africa moved south creating the Mediterranean basin. [2]
  • 370 million years ago there was the sinking of Africa, along with many other continents, which lasted five million years. [3]
  • 270 million years ago Africa was emerged, like all continents, but five million years later it was flooded. [4]
  • 210 million years ago Africa was very high. South America was connected to Europe through Africa. 180 million years ago the Carboniferous period that had given rise to coal in North Africa ended. [5]
  • 170 million years ago the continent that had connected South America to Africa disappeared. [6]
  • 160 million years ago only land animals lived continuously in Africa. A limestone layer formed in Africa during this period. [7]
  • 65 million years ago there was one of the largest lava eruptions of all time to affect North Africa. [8]
  • 50 million years ago, during the Eocene, chalk deposits were produced off the coasts of Africa. [9]
  • 10 million years ago the Mediterranean Sea covered a large part of North Africa. [10]
  • 5 million years ago, at the beginning of the ice age, Africa was little affected by ice. [11]

Evolution and race migrations

Africa was linked to Europe by the land bridge of Sicily, which led to the migration of the Andonites to Africa. [12]

Few Andites penetrated beyond the headwaters of the Nile on their migrations in Africa. Some mongrel Andites descended below the equator, but none reached Madagascar. [13] The natives of Africa are among the most recent pastoral peoples. [14] Neanderthals spread everywhere from what is now China to France and even descended to North Africa over the land bridge of that time. [15] The Nodites Spread across North Africa. [16]

Colored races were unable to penetrate Africa for long because of the vast Mediterranean Sea. When they were able to do so thanks to a land passage, only the orange, green and indigo races migrated to Africa. [17] Prereptiles first evolved in Africa from frogs. [18] Africa was the first appearance of reptiles, 140 million years ago. [19]

Costums and believes

African tribes have resorted to cannibalism as a measure of warfare, to frighten their enemies. [20] Much worship of fetish stones has been had in Africa. [21] Even today in Africa beliefs are held such as that the rainbow is a snake. [22] The umbilical cord is still regarded today as a highly prized fetish object. [23] Egyptians and many African tribes believed in ka and ba, two factors of existence in the individual. [24] Africans still purchase their wives following an ancient belief that property is more valuable than conjugal love. [25]

Gospel in Africa

The practices of Christian missionaries in Africa trying in a single generation to change ancestral customs have only caused confusion and a loss of authority. [26] Salem missionaries penetrated the most remote tribes of Africa. [27] Simon Zealots died in the heart of Africa preaching across the Nile. [28] Thomas preached among other places in North Africa. [29]
