From Honan to Shensi, the arrival of Andite immigrants stimulated the rapid development of advanced Chinese civilization through metalworking and manufacture. [1] The hydraulic problems of agriculturists in Shensi led to group co-operation and the promotion of peace among farming communities. [2]
