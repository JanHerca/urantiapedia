Adam deliberately impregnated Laotta, the brilliant Nodite woman, out of love for Eve and a fear of being alone on Urantia. [1]

Before reaching Mesopotamia, Laotta perished giving birth to Sansa, who grew up to become a woman of great ability and married Sargan, chief of the northern blue races. [2]
