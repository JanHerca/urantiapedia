About 12,000 B.C. a brilliant tribe of Andites migrated to Crete. [1] In Crete that the mother cult of the descendants of Cain attained its greatest vogue. [2] Philistines were a political refugees from the higher civilization of Crete. [3]

Jesus and the apostles

When Jesus and the apostles were in Jerusalem in attendance upon the Passover festivities one Jacob, a wealthy Jewish trader from Crete, and he came to Andrew making request to see Jesus privately. [4]

Jesus and Ganid won at Crete many souls to higher levels of thinking and living and thus laid the foundation for the quick reception of the later gospel teachings. [5] Thomas preached in Crete. [6]
