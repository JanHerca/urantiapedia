The seraphic, secoraphic, and superaphic transport is facilitated by the autotransport capabilities of Master Physical Controllers, who are the mobile subordinates of the Supreme Power Centers, enabling them to traverse space with speeds likened to Solitary Messengers. [1]

Upon natural death, seraphic, secoraphic, and superaphic transport ceases to function for the physical body, but continues for the morontia and spirit forms. [2]

The mystery of seraphic transport is just one of the secrets locked up in the sacred sectors of Seraphington, guarded by transport seraphim who do not communicate their knowledge to others. [3] Trinity-origin beings can move independently through the universe without the need for seraphic transport. [4]

Instances of use

Forty loyal members of Prince's staff transferred to Jerusem after the final roll call, with Van and his noble band leading the way. [5]

Upon completion of the deep sleep preparatory to being enseraphimed, the Material Sons under the guidance of the System Sovereign are transported through space from their home of associated service to new realms of opportunities and potential dangers. [6] Seventy days after Eve's default, the Melchizedek receivers arrived in Urantia, signaling Adam's realization of their failure. [7] Melchizedeks use seraphic transports for the departure of Adam and Eve to Jerusem. [8] Mortals advance through mansion worlds by receiving new morontia bodies during transit sleeps with the seraphic transport. [9]

Primary seconaphim operate as transport personalities, carrying pilgrims from the superuniverses to Havona and back, with the first step being to thank them for their safe journey. [10]

Prince’s staff brought to Urantia by seraphic transport were Jerusemite volunteers enseraphimed until equipped with dual-natured bodies for special planetary service. [11] After 1000 years, seraphic transport removes midsonite creatures from their worlds, depriving them of reproductive prerogatives forever. [12] The seraphic transports returned 3/4 of Adam's children to Edentia, leaving a sorrowful caravan to journey on from the Garden of Eden. [13] Tertiary supernaphim from circuit six transport failed Deity adventure candidates on planet forty in the outermost circuit. [14] Until Paradise, ascenders must depend upon seraphic, secoraphic, and superaphic transport for interstellar travel. [15] Pilgrims in Havona can freely visit their assigned circuit worlds without being ensupernaphimed, as achieved circuits in Havona do not require it. [16]

Orders of transporters

Transport seraphim serve as interplanetary carriers in Satania, ensuring the safe passage of spirit travelers between Jerusem and other worlds in the system. [17] All angelic groups have transport corps dedicated to journeying those unable to travel between spheres. [18]

Non-seraphic transporters can attain incredible speeds by simultaneously utilizing the three lines of energy, reaching velocities of up to 559,000 miles per second, influenced by mass, proximity of matter, and universe power circuits. [19]

Numerous beings similar to seraphim can utilize all three lines of energy simultaneously to achieve velocities ranging from 555,000 to almost 559,000 miles per second in their transport methods. [20] Planetary helper seraphim serve as transporters bringing enseraphimed beings to and from Urantia and other planets within the universe circuits. [21] The superior seraphim serve as space traversers in the ministry of transporting personalities between spheres. [22]

Supervisor seraphim operate as personality transporters, carrying beings to and from the headquarters of the constellations with precise control and incredible speed. [23]

Technical matters

The seraphic, secoraphic, and superaphic transport corps handle all technical matters related to transporting personalities between spheres. [24]

Mobile subordinates of Supreme Power Centers traverse local space with remarkable autotransport capabilities, requiring assistance to overcome gravity when departing a sphere. [25]

Celestial artisans are technical advisers to the corps of transport advisers, proficient in collaborating with star students and assisting chiefs of transport on inhabited planets like Urantia. [26]

Angels cannot transport combustion bodies, like flesh and blood, but they can transport all others, from the lowest morontia to the higher spirit forms. [27] Celestial beings depart at midnight from universe energy pole of planet when enseraphimed for transfer between worlds. [28]

Factors affecting velocity in seraphic, secoraphic, and superaphic transport include mass, proximity of neighboring matter, and strength and direction of nearby main circuits of universe power. [29]

Before departure, the inspection of the enseraphimed carriage ensures proper encircuitment and energy adjustment for the departing flash into the universe circuits. [30] Enseraphimed beings brought to Urantia merely stop over in transit, in custody of their own special seraphic transporters stationed on the planet. [31]

Mechanical controllers and energy transmitters facilitate the departure of seraphic transports by adjusting essential, undiscovered energies for interplanetary communication and transport. [32]

Mechanical devices on Mount Seraph provide energy for the rapid departure of transport seraphim, launching every three seconds to overcome planetary gravity and air resistance, reaching standard velocity over two thousand miles from Jerusem. [33]

Supervisor seraphim transport beings safely without risk of collision, consciously navigating through space with precision and the ability to change direction as needed. [34] The Edentia sea of glass greatly facilitates landings for transport seraphim. [35]

The mystery of seraphic transport in Seraphington involves enveloping nonmaterial orders within spirit forms for interplanetary journeys, guarded closely by transport seraphim. [36]

Transport seraphim take off at 175 miles per second from Mount Seraph on Jerusem, the highest elevation where numerous mechanical developments provide initial energy for escaping planetary gravity and overcoming air resistance, departing every three seconds and arriving at the crystal field where various orders of beings traverse space. [37] Transporters take on energy in transit to fulfill the exhaustive journey demands required for crossing from Uversa to Havona. [38] Solitary Messengers can reach speeds of up to 558,840 miles per second to serve as ambassadors between distant realms within the superuniverse. [39] The Master Physical Controllers oversee the transformation of material beings into a morontia-like state within the sacred temple of power on Jerusem. [40] Conscious and adept at navigating the cosmos, transporters of celestial beings maintain precise control over speed, direction, and location. [41] Transporters in space junctions of intelligence circuits may receive changed orders during superaphic transport. [42]

The seraphim undergoes a metamorphosis and becomes enshrouded in an amber light before being prepared to travel along the energy currents of universe circuits with the aid of transport controllers and living energy transmitters. [43]

Transit sleep

Angels do not have wings for flying; they use energy insulators during transport sleep for interplanetary travel. [44]

During seraphic transport, you enter a conscious unconscious state where the Adjuster is exceptionally efficient, akin to a peaceful slumber with an automatic time element in transit sleep, allowing for creative and transforming work beyond your control. [45]

Enseraphimed, you journey through space in peaceful slumber, awakening at the designated moment, as induced by liaison of Adjusters and seraphic transporters. [46]

During seraphic transport, the transportee is not directly aware of the passage of time, similar to a peaceful slumber that leads to awakening in a distant metropolis after journeying through space while resting. [47]
