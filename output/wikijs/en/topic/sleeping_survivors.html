When repersonalized on the mansion worlds, sleeping survivors become Spirit-fused mortals through the individualization of the spirit of the Divine Minister, making them equals in mind and spirit to their contemporaries. [1] Magisterial Sons preside over the awakening of sleeping survivors and execute the mandates of dispensation terminators. [2]

Sleeping survivors, mortals without personal guardians, must rest in unconscious sleep until the judgment day of a new epoch arrives, unable to go directly to mansion worlds. [3] The sleeping survivors of Adamic dispensation were raised for the inauguration of the general resurrection on Urantia by Gabriel and the archangels. [4] Personality, mind transcripts, and memory held by Adjusters; mind-matrix and potential of identity in soul held by.

The reassembling of creature personality for resurrection of sleeping survivors involves the spirit-mind trust of the Adjuster and the morontia-soul trust of the seraphic destiny guardians. [5] On archangels' worlds, personality records are classified and preserved between mortal death and repersonalization. [6] Survivors are repersonalized at the end of a dispensation associated with the advent of a divine Son of planetary service. [7] Rest in unconscious sleep until new dispensation, waiting for the Son of God to call the rolls and adjudicate the realm. [8] Group guardians facilitate the resurrection by gathering sleeping survivors in the mansion spheres, as recorded in the dispensational roll call. [9]
