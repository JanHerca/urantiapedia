Celestial beings never proffer advice unless asked. [1] It is said in morontial mota that it must be given advice only when asked. [2] In The Urantia Book is told Jesus’ counsel to a rich man when he asked what he would do with wealth. [3] Jesus seldom offered advice unless asked. [4] Prince’s staff never sent emissaries except upon request. [5]
