The alternate zoning and motion of space levels, with their clockwise and counterclockwise flow, play a crucial role in stabilizing physical gravity and preventing disruptive activities. [1] Without the successive space levels of the master universe, cosmic energies would shoot off into infinite trackless space in straight-line paths. [2]

Space levels are elliptical regions of motion surrounded by zones of motionlessness, constituting a curved path of lessened resistance to cosmic force and emergent energy in the master universe. [3] Force, energy, and matter ever swing onward in the successive space levels of the master universe. [4]

List of levels

The listed space levels encircle the central Isle of Paradise in the master universe. [5]

1st: Havona

Three master planners of the Havona Level, orchestrated by the pre-eventuated senior Architect, coordinate the one billion perfect spheres of the central universe. [6] The three Paradise circuits and the seven Havona circuits rotate clockwise in a processional around Paradise. [7]

2nd: 7 superuniverses

The seven Architects of Master Universe coordinate on the superuniverse level as superco-ordinators of the grand universe. [8] The seven superuniverses rotate counterclockwise around Paradise on a well-charted cosmic path. [9]

3rd: 1st outer space level

70 Architects of Master Universe coordinate the ultimate plans for the first universe of outer space, beyond the present superuniverses. [10] The Outer Space Levels extend 400,000 light years beyond the superuniverses, assembling vast circuits of force and energies. [11] The end of our universe age will witness the appearance of new levels in developmental growth. [12]

It is believed that the finalitersâ€™ destiny to administer in the new order of universes in outer space will bring about enthralling spectacles and divine illumination in the endless future. [13] The vast procession of galaxies races around Paradise, separated by enormous elliptical regions of quiescent space activities. [14]

4th: 2nd outer space level

Beyond the first outer space level, 50 million light years mark the emerging creation of the second outer space level. [15] 490 Architects of Master Universe coordinate energy mobilizations in the second universe of outer space at the Secondary Space Level. [16]

5th: 3rd outer space level

3,430 Architects of Master Universe coordinate plans for the third universe of outer space. [17]

6th: 4th outer space level

The Quartan Space Level consists of 24,010 Master Architects coordinating in the Master Universe. [18]

7th: postulated

The postulated never-ending universe of infinity beyond the four outer space levels is a creation destined to eventuate-evolve the ultimacy of creation. [19]

Midspace zones

The mid-zone of the force center serves as a control mechanism for the midspace zones, reciprocally adjusting the inner and outer spaces of the master universe. [20]

Outer space levels

The organized and inhabited post-Havona creation includes the seven superuniverses, each encompassing an equal number of local universes, while beyond the grand universe lies four stupendous elliptical circuits of materializing energies extending beyond the outer space levels. [21] Mind gravity in space levels accounts for 15% of the total intellectual drawing response to the Conjoint Actor within the grand universe. [22]

Outer space levels, creations at present wholly physical, uninhabited, are part of the vast and new system of universes gradually organizing beyond the peopled and organized grand universe. [23]

The organization of the future universes of the primary, secondary, tertiary, and quartan space levels is destined to eventuate the ultimacy of creation. [24]

The space levels beyond the seven superuniverses are the domains of the Unqualified Absolute, where the outworking of the Architects of the Master Universe's plans unfolds. [25] The eternal future will bring universe evolution phenomena far surpassing anything experienced in the eternal past. [26]

In the next universe age, Creator Sons and Mother Spirits will serve in the outer universes, evolving new relationships and functions within the cosmic administration. [27] Inhabitants of future outer universes will lack finite experience; a very different kind of growth will characterize their existence. [28]

The future destiny of outer universes will involve time creatures of ascension potential, who will see Ascendington as their Paradise home world, reflecting the ultimate journey from mortal man to Deity embrace. [29] Other-than-personal beings are assembling on Paradise worlds of the Son for service in the projected new universes of outer space. [30]

In contemplating the triune development of creatures, universes, and Deity, can we not speculate about the imminent culmination of something new and unrevealed in the expanding master universe, involving the upstepping of mortals to divine perfection and their eventual mobilization on Paradise in the enigmatic Corps of the Finality, destined for service in the outer space levels where over 70,000 aggregations surpassing superuniverses can already be identified? [31]

The Uversa star students observe that the Paradise force organizers metamorphose cosmic force on the outer space levels, surrounding the grand universe with ancestral energy and matter equal to many times the total mass and energy of the seven superuniverses. [32] The personnel of the grand universe is definitively undergoing reorganization in accordance with the vast energy evolutions in outer space. [33]

Solitary Messengers patrol the master universe on exploration expeditions to uncharted regions of outer space, contributing valuable information to celestial astronomers. [34]

When Urantia astronomers peer through their increasingly powerful telescopes into the mysterious stretches of outer space, they should realize that some Paradise personality influences present in the amazing evolution of almost countless physical universes are the outworking of the unsearchable plans of the Architects of the Master Universe. [35]

Supreme Being will disclose tertiary function in the uncharted regions of outer space, where the finaliters of the Paradise Corps await their eternal destiny. [36] Urantian astronomers view the metamorphosis of spiral nebulae in space. [37] Ninety-five percent of Paradise gravity is utilized in controlling the outer space levels beyond the borders of the present organized universes. [38]
