Solonia, chief of Urantia planetary helper seraphim, facilitates the physical and intellectual upliftment of the material races on evolutionary worlds through her ministering work. [1] Solonia reproved Adam and Eve for disobedience in the moonlit Garden, proclaiming the miscarriage of the divine plan. [2]

Solonia, the seraphic voice in the Garden, rebuked Adam and Eve for their departure from the ordained plan, illustrating how Urantian traditions often attribute supernatural events to the Lord God. [3] Solonia, the seraphic “voice in the Garden,” narrated and presented Papers 73-76. [4]
