Jesus expressed his thirst while on the cross, prompting the captain of the guard to offer him wine on a javelin. [1] In the Divine Presence, there is no thirst, only the eternal satisfaction of spiritual enlightenment and moral strength. [2] Believe in Jesus, never thirst spiritually, and receive eternal life.
