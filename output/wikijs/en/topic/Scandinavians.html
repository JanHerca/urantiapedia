Scandinavians were Bronze Age mother worshippers in 3000 B.C. [1]

For thousands of years, Scandinavians practiced cremation as part of their mother cult, reflecting their extended contact with the Danubians and their advanced culture. [2] The ancestors of Scandinavians were descendants of Andite and blue races. [3] The Scandinavians, like many other cultures, worshipped triad gods long before the revelation of the Paradise Trinity. [4]
