1st triunity

The triunity of infinite volition reveals personal Deity to creatures throughout all of time. [1]

The triunity of absolute relationships between Father, Eternal Son, and Infinite Spirit is an inevitable and eternal union that manifests in the divine fraternal, creature-loving, and ascension-promoting association of three eternal Paradise personalities. [2]

The triunity of infinite volition is the personal-purposive association of the Universal Father, the Eternal Son, and the Infinite Spirit, revealing volitional infinity to the evolving cosmos. [3]

2nd triunity

The Second Triunity consists of Father-Son, Infinite Spirit, and Paradise, serving as the power-pattern for all cosmic configurations. [4]

The power-pattern of the Second Triunity determines cosmic configurations across all scales of the universe, from ultimatons to superuniverses, all derived from the function of this triunity. [5]

3rd triunity

The Third Triunity consists of members Deity Absolute, Son-Spirit, and Universal Father—the foundation of spiritual evolution. [6] The Third Triunity embodies spiritual evolutional essence from primordial genesis to final destiny. [7]

4th triunity

The Fourth Triunity eternalizes the beginnings and endings of all energy reality, from space potency to monota, encompassing the Father-Spirit, Paradise Isle, and the Unqualified Absolute, with Paradise serving as the center of force-energy activation and the source of all energy. [8] The Fourth Triunity consists of energy infinity, encompassing the beginnings and endings of all energy reality, from space potency to monota. [9]

5th triunity

The Fifth Triunity consists of the Universal Absolute, Universal Father, and Unqualified Absolute in an association of reactive infinity. [10]

The Fifth Triunity embodies the eternalization of functional infinity realization within nondeity reality through the Universal Father, Universal Absolute, and Unqualified Absolute, demonstrating unlimited reactive capacity. [11]

6th triunity

The Sixth Triunity represents the cosmic association of the Universal Father, the Deity Absolute, and the Universal Absolute, uniting immanence and transcendence in the cosmos. [12] The association of Deity with the cosmos is the immanence of Deity reaching out toward undeified reality. [13] The Sixth Triunity consists of members Deity Absolute, Universal Absolute, and Universal Father. [14]

7th triunity

The Seventh Triunity embodies the unity of infinity, uniting actuals and potentials in time and eternity through the Universal Father, the Conjoint Actor, and the Universal Absolute. [15]

The Seventh Triunity consists of Infinite Spirit, Universal Absolute, and Universal Father, embodying infinite unity and coordinating actuals and potentials in time and eternity. [16]

Other

Fifteen triunities exist, with eight remaining unrevealed, encompassing realities beyond our comprehension. [17] The Seven Master Spirits represent sevenfold Deity, enabling the functions of the eternal trio without comprising the Trinity itself. [18]

How is defined

Triunity relationships are the functional associations of the seven Absolutes of Infinity, eternalizing the potential of all reality and realizing infinity as universal function. [19]

The triunities embody the balance wheel of infinity, uniting the uniqueness of the Seven Absolutes to enable the Father-I AM's experience of functional infinity unity. [20] The human mind is prepared by the Trinity association to understand other triunities where the Infinite functions in non-Father capacities. [21] The triunities enable the Father-I AM to experience functional unity and manifest his reality in subinfinite and subabsolute ways. [22]

The triunity of absolute relationships is a functional unity, not an organic entity, eternalized by the personal union of the Father, the Son, and the Spirit. [23]

The triunity of absolute relationships is inevitable when the three Paradise personalities eternalize the functional unity of the Father, the Son, and the Spirit. [24]

Functions

Existentials become functional in the triunities, setting the stage for the diversification of cosmic energy and the unification of Deity and Paradise derivatives. [25] The triunities allow the Universal Father to exercise immediate and personal control over infinity. [26]

The Universal Father is the primal member of all triunities, unifying the Seven Infinity Absolutes into functional balance and enabling infinite unity. [27] The triunities do not function on nether Paradise, but the region on the outer margins focuses on space potency and force-energy. [28]
