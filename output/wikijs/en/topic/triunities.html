1st triunity

Mechanism whereby personal Deity reveals itself to creatures. [1] Members are Father, Eternal Son, and Infinite Spirit. [2] Personal-purposive; triunity of infinite volition. [3]

2nd triunity

Members are Father-Son, Infinite Spirit, Paradise. [4] Power-pattern; determines patterns of cosmic configuration. [5]

3rd triunity

Members are Deity Absolute, Son-Spirit, and Universal Father. [6] Spiritual evolutional. [7]

4th triunity

Energy infinity. [8] Members are Father-Spirit, Paradise, and Unqualified Absolute. [9]

5th triunity

Members are Universal Absolute, Universal Father, and Unqualified Absolute. [10] Reactive infinity. [11]

6th triunity

Cosmic-associated Deity. [12] Immanence of Deity; outreach toward undeified reality. [13] Members are Deity Absolute, Universal Absolute, and Universal Father. [14]

7th triunity

Infinite unity. [15] Members are Infinite Spirit, Universal Absolute, and Universal Father. [16]

15 exist in all; 8 are unrevealed. [17] Cf: non-Trinity acts of Father, Son, and Spirit in Master Spirit Number Seven. [18]
