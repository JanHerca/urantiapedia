A gibbon raid killed parents of Andon and Fonta, the parents of humanity. [1]

The early lemurs concerned in the ancestry of the human species were not directly related to the pre-existent tribes of gibbons and apes then living in Eurasia and northern Africa, whose progeny have survived to the present time. [2]
