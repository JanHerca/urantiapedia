Parables about seeds

From growth to harvest, the kingdom of heaven is like a man who sows good seed and reaps a bountiful fruit. [1] The kingdom of heaven is like a mustard seed, growing from generation to generation with increasing vitality and depth of spiritual power. [2] The seed of the living being is a living truth that unfolds in new manifestations and grows acceptably from generation to generation. [3]

The sower went forth to sow, some seed fell by the wayside, some on rocky places, some among thorns, and some on good ground, yielding different levels of abundance. [4]

Plants bearing seeds

The appearance of seed plants afforded animals a better food supply during times of climatic change. [5]

Seed plants, which appeared in Permian, provided a better food supply for land-animal life during times of climatic change, while insects evolved radical changes to survive winter and drought. [6] The evolution from spores to seeds marked a significant advancement in plant development. [7] Sometimes the death of our fondest hopes is necessary for the planting of new opportunities. [8]
