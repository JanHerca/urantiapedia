Harmony is the keynote of the central universe, with detectable order prevailing on Paradise, guiding pilgrims towards the spiritual heights of spontaneous worship. [1] Maintain temporal order in fellowship, as Jesus taught, to ensure fairness and justice in the community. [2] The kingdom is a realm of order, providing authority of leadership when two or more creatures act in cooperation. [3] Social groups may maintain discipline, unlike the kingdoms of this world which often rely on physical force for order. [4]
