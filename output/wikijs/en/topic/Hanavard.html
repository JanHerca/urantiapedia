Hanavard is the 67th Primary Lanonandek in Nebadon; he is a member of the higher body of universal counselors and co-ordinators. He functions as acting chairman of Satania's executive council. Hanavard is the twelfth of this order to serve on Jerusem since the Lucifer rebellion. [1]
