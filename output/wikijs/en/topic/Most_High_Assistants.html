Most High Assistants assist Nebadon Paradise personalities with advanced and complex plans and rulings, serving as invaluable resources to universe rulers and administrators. [1]

The Most High Assistants, high celestial volunteers from outside Nebadon, serve as central and superuniverse representatives or observers, aiding in bringing Nebadon into harmony with Orvonton and Paradise ideals. [2] Most High Assistants are count as part of the Universe Aids group. [3] Most High Assistants are recorded in Uversa's personality register. [4] Many tertiaphim function as Most High Assistants, providing temporary help from higher spirits outside the local creation. [5]

Most High Assistants operate within local universe and constellation roles without ties to system or planetary governments, fulfilling a wide range of functions in Nebadon activities. [6] Millions of Most High Assistants from beyond the local universe serve as representatives in local creations. [7]

The Most High Assistants, supervised by the Union of Days, operate in Nebadon under the oversupervision of the ambassador of the Paradise Trinity, following their assigned mission and regulations of the local universe. [8]
