The fruits of the Spirit - love, joy, peace, long-suffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, meekness, and temperance - illuminate the path of spirit-led mortals towards eternal life and the kingdom of God. [1]

Jesus preached temperance and taught consistency through proportionate adjustment of life problems, warning against extremes in virtue that may lead to vice. [2]
