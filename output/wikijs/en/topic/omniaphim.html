Michael, accompanied by an omniaphim, embarked on a Melchizedek bestowal mission that left the inhabitants of Salvington eagerly awaiting news. [1] Omniaphim are registered in the registers of personalities in the universe. [2]

Omniaphim serving as Technical Advisers may become Paradise Companions, but they are not of permanent status and typically return to their previous duties after completing their ministry. [3] Omniaphim, exclusive servants and messengers of the Seven Supreme Executives, are not directly involved in the ascent of mortals. [4] The omniaphim serves as the personal representative of the Assigned Sentinel at the system headquarters. [5]

Omniaphim are exclusive servants of the Seven Supreme Executives, created by Infinite Spirit, residing in Uversa, but not directly involved in mortal progression. [6]
