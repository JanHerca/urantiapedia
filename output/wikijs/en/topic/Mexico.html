In Mexico and elsewhere, the sacrament of cakes and wine replaced human sacrifices in the Mother of God cult. [1]

The civilizations of Mexico, Central America, and South America were founded by an amalgamated race, predominantly red but with mixtures of yellow, orange, and blue. [2]
