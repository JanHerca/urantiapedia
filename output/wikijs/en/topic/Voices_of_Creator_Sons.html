Spirit-fused mortals and Son-fused ascenders, as children of Reflective Spirits, embody the intricate connection between the local and superuniverse levels of God the Sevenfold. [1]

The primary seconaphim of the Creator Sons possess the remarkable gift of reflecting the minds of these Master Sons, allowing the Ancients of Days to perceive their voice unfailingly. [2]
