The Personalized Adjuster of Jesus, chief of his kind in Nebadon, guided him faithfully and wisely through his human life, leading him to the Father's perfect will. [1]

The Thought Adjuster of Jesus, who previously served with Machiventa Melchizedek, guided him step by step during his human life and aided in the choosing of the path of the Father's perfect will. [2]

The Thought Adjuster of Jesus collaborated in creation of Part IV, utilizing existing records from the life of Jesus on Urantia, including the Gospels of Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. [3] The Thought Adjuster arrived with Jesus on February 11, 2 B.C., marking his first personal moral decision. [4] The advice of Gabriel was to let Jesus, as a Creator Son, have the power to resurrect himself. [5] At healing at sundown, Jesus passed the responsibility of this decision to his Father, resulting in a miraculous wave of healing for 683 individuals. [6]

At Jesus’ baptism

Jesus was in constant communion with the Personalized Adjuster, speaking on behalf of the Universal Father. [7] Only Jesus saw the Personalized Adjuster descend and speak as Jesus observed his former divine spirit returning in personalized form. [8] The Personalized Adjuster of Jesus was directed by the Personalized Adjuster of Michael of Nebadon, guiding him step by step in his human life. [9]

During Jesus life

Jesus commanded celestial hosts in accordance with his Father's will, limiting their involvement to only specific acts or episodes of his earth career. [10] The Thought Adjuster of Jesus could not limit his Creator prerogatives with respect to time. [11]

Jesus achieved perfect synchrony with his Thought Adjuster through his perfected mortal evolution and self-identification with the spirit during his baptism in the Jordan. [12]

Jesus gradually realized that his Thought Adjuster had permitted water to be turned into wine at the marriage feast of Cana, despite his initial reluctance to perform a wonder. [13] Jesus detained Lazarus's Adjuster, revealing the fascinating truth behind what seemed like a natural event in human affairs. [14] Jesus' Thought Adjuster was present after crucifixion, guiding celestial hosts. [15]

Moments Thought Adjuster spoke

At the baptism of Jesus, a final and authoritative proclamation was made by the Personalized Adjuster, affirming him as the beloved Son of God. [16] The Thought Adjuster of Jesus appeared at transfiguration from a silvery cloud, confirming his divine sonship. [17] In Gethsemane, the Personalized Adjuster of Jesus appeared, pausing noticeably to speak of glorifying the Father's name. [18]

On Mount Hermon, Jesus relinquished superhuman co-operation for his earth career, except by divine will or departures from natural earth order as to time, to his Personalized Adjuster. [19]
