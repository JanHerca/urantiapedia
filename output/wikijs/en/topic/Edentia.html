Edentia is the headquarters of Norlatiadek, our constellation. [1] It is the seat of Constellation Fathers, the Most Highs of Norlatiadek. [2]

The construction of Salvington was immediately followed by the creation of the one hundred headquarters worlds of the projected constellations, as Edentia. [3]

Description

On Edentia there are ten Supreme Power Centers, ten associated mechanical controllers and ten frandalanks who are in perfect and constant liaison with the near-by power centers. [4]

The seventy major spheres surrounding Edentia are about ten times the size of Urantia, while the ten satellites which revolve around each of these seventy worlds are about the size of Urantia. [5]

All animal life in Edentia is most intelligent and exquisitely serviceable, and all the various species are surprisingly gentle and touchingly companionable. There are no carnivorous creatures; there is nothing in all Edentia to make any living being afraid. [6] Edentia is a hundred times larger than Urantia. [7]

On Edentia living embellishment is more generally utilized, and it is this preponderance of life—botanic artistry—that causes these worlds to be called “the gardens of God. About one half of Edentia is devoted to the exquisite gardens of the Most Highs. [8]

The Edentia highlands are magnificent physical features, and their beauty is enhanced by the endless profusion of life which abounds throughout their length and breadth. [9] Morontia vegetation is purely an energy growth; when eaten there is no residual portion. [10] There are tens of thousands of sparkling lakes and thousands upon thousands of interconnecting streams. [11] There are no rugged mountain ranges, no great oceans nor torrential rivers; there are no storms or hurricanes; neither is there summer nor winter. [12] Most transportation is in the air. [13]

The sea of glass, the receiving area of Edentia, is near the administrative center and is encircled by the headquarters amphitheater. It is one enormous circular crystal about one hundred miles in circumference and about thirty miles in depth. [14] It is energized by universal space currents. [15]

The tree of life is shrub of Edentia, and from it can be extracted antidotal complements that allow mortal beings to live on indefinitely in a mortal planet. [16]

Edentia and its associated worlds have a atmosphere with three-gas mixture which embodies oxygen, nitrogen, and a morontia gas suitable for the respiration of morontia creatures. [17] At the center of the circles of the ascending mortals on Jerusem there is working model of Edentia that is forty miles in diameter. [18]

Education

Ethical sensitizers will quicken our appreciation of the truth of fraternity in the brotherhood schools. [19] The Melchizedeks also maintain two special colleges on Edentia, the emergency school and the bestowal school. [20]

Administration

At Edentia we will always be welcome as an observer in the legislative assemblies. [21] After Satania rebellion, archrebels continued to attend councils on Edentia as they had on former occasions. [22]

Mortal ascenders

Situated on the summit of the seventh highland range are the resurrection halls of Edentia, wherein awaken the ascending mortals of the secondary modified order of ascension. [23] On Edentia, ascenders are midway between their former and their future estates, midway in their passage from evolutionary animal to ascending spirit. [24] Our sojourn on Edentia and its associated spheres will be chiefly occupied with the mastery of group ethics. [25] The entire sojourn on the constellation training worlds culminates in Edentia citizenship. [26] There we live with univitatia, the native people of Edentia. [27] We learn how better to socialize with the univitatia. [28]
