Not long since a conclave extraordinary was held in Paradise of 150,000 Creator Sons belonging to Sovereign Michaels group, assembled in the parental presence and engaged in deliberations having to do with the progress of the unification and stabilization of the universe of universes. [1] From time to time, great conclaves take place on these Paradise satellites of the Spirit. [2] The Eternals of Days are visible to all will creatures dwelling in Havona. They preside over the regular planetary conclaves. [3] Every millennium of sector standard time the various orders of the divine Sons forgather for their periodic conclaves. [4]

As chief executive of Nebadon, Gabriel is ex officio chairman of, or observer at, most of the Salvington conclaves, and as many as one thousand of these are often in session simultaneously. [5]
