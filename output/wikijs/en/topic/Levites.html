Levites officiated in the Jerusalem temple during the feast of feasts, combining vacation pleasures with solemn religious worship, including sacrifices, chants, and trumpet blasts. [1]

The priests and Levites sent a delegation to John the Baptist to inquire if he claimed to be the Messiah and by whose authority he preached, to which John responded with a quote from the prophet Isaiah. [2]
