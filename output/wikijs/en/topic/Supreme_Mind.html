Reflectivity in the cosmos is accounted for by the evolving experiential mind of the Supreme. [1] A Supreme Mind activates the living mechanism of the grand universe, guiding the emerging personality of the cosmos towards mastery of energy-matter. [2] The Supreme Mind was bestowed on Supreme by Infinite Spirit for the unification of three phases of Deity reality. [3] The Supreme Mind's cosmic coordination with the Seven Master Spirits ensures unity and harmony across the superuniverses. [4] Trinitized Sons of Destiny resulting from finaliter and Paradise-Havoner trinitization repercuss in phases of the Supreme-Ultimate Mind. [5] Majeston focalizes presence of the Supreme Mind in the cosmic scheme of creation. [6] The dominance of the Supreme Mind is indicated by progressive evolution just as appreciative creature mind is implied by the existence of beauty. [7]

The creation of Solitary Messengers by the Infinite Spirit may be related to the bestowal of Supreme-Ultimate Mind, as they demonstrate the ability to co-ordinate all types of personalities and make contact with the master universe overcontrollers. [8] The Supreme Mind unifies the spirit person of the Supreme with the attained power of the Almighty, resulting in a unified and personal Deity. [9]

The unknown potential of Supreme Mind complements the spirit-personal qualities of the Supreme and power prerogatives of the Almighty, illustrating the unification of the cosmos. [10]
