Jesus passed through this town with his parents when they returned from Egypt to Nazareth, seeking a western route that would avoid unwanted encounters. [1] Jesus passed through this town at the age of twenty when he visited Jerusalem alone for the Passover. [2] He visited this town again three years later when he took his younger brother Simon to Jerusalem for the Passover. [3]
