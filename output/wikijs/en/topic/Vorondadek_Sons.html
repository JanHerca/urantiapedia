The Most High observer of Urantia, a Vorondadek Son, asserted his authority and began emergency administration with a full staff of his choosing. [1] In the government of Norlatiadek, twelve Sons of the Vorondadek order administer and assist with administrative work during times of stress. [2]

Twelve Vorondadek Sons sit en banc as a high court of review for special cases concerning planetary status, with their broader focus on legislative functions in constellation governments. [3] Vorondadek Sons, created by Creator Son and Mother Spirit, are known as Constellation Fathers leading the governments in each local universe. [4] The Vorondadek Sons, although cited for error on occasion, have never fallen into rebellion. [5] The Vorondadek Sons serve as historians of local universes, overseeing high court cases and legislative functions within constellation governments. [6]

The Vorondadek Sons are in charge of the 2nd Salvington circuit, where ascending mortals undergo their highest level of education in universe legislation. [7] In emergencies, the Vorondadek Sons exercise absolute authority over the planet's celestial beings on isolated worlds in spiritual darkness. [8] Vorondadek Sons exhibit unique characteristics in personality registers. [9] The government of Norlatiadek includes twelve Sons of the Vorondadek order, with number 617,318 serving as the current Most High ruler. [10]

Known as Most Highs when commissioned as Constellation Fathers, they have a significant role in guiding the architectural headquarters spheres of constellations, presided over by three Vorondadek Sons, known as the Most Highs. [11]

Vorondadek Sons observe quarantined planets, exercising absolute authority in emergencies and sometimes serving as regents even on non-rebellious worlds. [12] Despite their inability to reproduce, the number of Vorondadek Sons in Nebadon remains fixed at one million. [13] The recorded number of Vorondadek Sons in Nebadon is one million, with no ability to increase their ranks. [14] Personalized Adjusters confer with Vorondadek rulers on the headquarters worlds of the constellations. [15] The Vorondadek Sons are reliable, efficient rulers with stability of purpose and divinity of judgment. [16]

Vorondadek Sons reside in the fourth circle of Jerusem, where they are joined by other visiting observer Sons, Most High Constellation Fathers, Perfectors of Wisdom, Divine Counselors, and Universal Censors. [17] Vorondadek Sons serve as ambassadors and consuls, trusted with full sovereign powers in critical universe situations. [18] The work of the Vorondadek Sons pertains to legislative functions within the constellation governments, making them historians of the local universes. [19] The Vorondadek Sons, having wrote Paper 36, understand the limits of the Life Carriers' involvement in the evolution of moral creatures. [20]
