Ensa is our minor sector, the third in major sector of Splandon, that is comprised of one hundred minor sectors. [1] Uminor the third, the headquarters of Ensa, is surrounded by seven spheres satellites. [2]

The rotational center of Ensa is situated far away in the enormous and dense star cloud of Sagittarius, around which your local universe and its associated creations all move, and from opposite sides of the vast Sagittarius subgalactic system you may observe two great streams of star clouds emerging in stupendous stellar coils. [3]
