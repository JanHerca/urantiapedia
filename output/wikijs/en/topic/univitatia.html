The univitatia administer constellation morontia worlds, exhibiting intellectual variations while resembling the morontia state of existence and functioning in diverse orders of personality. [1] Ascending mortals are guided by ethical sensitizers on Edentia, fostering appreciation of morality and interpersonal relationships. [2]

The Univitatia, nonreproducing beings between Material Sons and Spirit-fused mortals, serve ascending mortals in the constellation spheres like Havona natives in the central creation. [3] Univitatia do not function outside the constellation headquarters. [4] Constellation Univitatia appear in personality registers. [5] The univitatia stand out in Nebadon for their artistic skill, social adaptability, and co-ordinating cleverness. [6]

The univitatia are the most skilled and adaptable celestial artisans in Orvonton, enhancing the artistic possibilities of transition culture on the constellation worlds. [7] Learning to abide joyfully with ten univitatia is essential for co-operating heartily with them as part of a larger group. [8]

The univitatia, a nonreproducing beings existing on a plane between Material Sons and Spirit-fused mortals, come in 70 different forms on the constellation headquarters worlds. [9]

The univitatia, permanent citizens of constellation headquarters worlds, provide ministry and assistance to ascending mortals during their traversal of the constellation spheres. [10] The univitatia are the possible future citizens of Havona, currently permanent citizens of the local universe constellations. [11]
