The argumentative defense of any proposition is inversely proportional to the truth contained. [1] Overpowering arguments and mental superiority are not to be employed to coerce men and women into the kingdom. [2]

Andon taught his children to settle disputes by each beating a tree with a stick, meanwhile cursing the tree; the one whose stick broke first was the victor. [3]

Disputes in heavens

Conciliating commissions function to resolve arguments upon petition from member of each contention. [4]

Jesus and arguments

Jesus said: “Do not strive with men—always be patient. Do not strive with men, even with unbelievers”. [5] Jesus seemed to be utterly free from all egoistic desire to win an argument merely to experience logical triumph over his fellows. [6] His unfriendly hearers sought to engage Jesus in unprofitable argumentation, but he refused to debate with his enemies. [7]
