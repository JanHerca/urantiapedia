Achievement is predicated upon perseverance in accordance with the Father's plan for finite progress and personality development. [1] Always pray with persistence, seeking wisdom and discernment in your requests, never becoming discouraged in the process. [2] Believers persist in well-doing, undaunted by obstacles that challenge the exertions of kingdom building. [3]

Persistence in the face of the evils of inaction is the key to unlocking one's true potential and finding the way to escape from personal sorrow and defeat. [4] You must have industriously exhausted human capacity for adjustment. [5]

Jesus persisted in restating his message to free his apostles from the idea of offering animal sacrifices for religious duty, never growing weary in his teaching. [6] We regret the loss of the magnificent persistence of primitive man, a trait that bordered on grandeur and sublimity. [7]

Persistence in seeking the Father's will, like the parable of the loaves, leads to receiving the bread of life from the willing hands of the Father in heaven. [8]

Persist in prayer, for your persistence is not to win favor with God but to change your earth attitude and enlarge your soul’s capacity for spirit receptivity. [9] To validate prayers, possess cosmic stamina and sincerely face universe reality. [10]
