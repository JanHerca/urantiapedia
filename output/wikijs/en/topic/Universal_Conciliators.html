7 reflectively created for each Havona Servital. [1] Acquire unique grasp of Supreme Being. [2] Created in 7 orders by Seven Master Spirits and Reflective Spirits. [3] Eventually translate to Paradise. [4] Fourth creatures discern spiritual and material worlds. [5] In personality registers. [6] Innately segregate into groups of four. [7] Levels of universe service. [8] None went astray in Lucifer rebellion. [9] Serve under Reflective Image Aids. [10] Technical Advisers aid. [11] Types are Divine Executioners, Judge-Arbiters, Recorders, Spirit-Advocates. [12] Unerringly faithful but not infallible. [13]
