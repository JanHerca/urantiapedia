Sayings of Jesus using examples about houses

A house divided against itself is soon brought to desolation. [1]

The apostles continued to preach and baptize believers, while they kept up the practice of visiting from house to house, comforting the downcast and ministering to the sick and afflicted. [2] Move not from house to house, for the laborer is worthy of his sustenance and peace be to this household. [3]
