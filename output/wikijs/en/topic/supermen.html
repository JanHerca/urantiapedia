Early man regarded all unusual persons as superhuman, worshiping them in reverential awe and believing them to be indwelt by the gods. [1]

The mythology surrounding the Prince's staff, transformed into supermen on Urantia, drove the tribes near Mesopotamia to adopt nonflesh diets, while also infusing legends and teaching throughout the region for almost a millennium. [2] Prince’s staff were supermen who accelerated human evolution through conscious social selection without robbing mankind of its religion and morals. [3]
