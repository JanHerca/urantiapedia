The personal abodes of the Ancients of Days are the point of spiritual polarity within their administrative sectors on Uversa. [1]

On Urantia, the archangels' circuit operated for the first time, signaling the resurrection of the dead and the departure of seraphim for the mansion worlds. [2]

Spiritual reflection of the characteristics of the Trinity is achieved through the unique nature of the seven Reflective Spirits assigned to each superuniverse, each reflecting a different combination of divinity characteristics. [3]

Reports from space are received at noon at the designated headquarters, while seraphic transports depart from there at midnight for interplanetary transfers. [4]
