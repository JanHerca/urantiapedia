The most holy mount of assembly is the dwelling place of the Faithful of Days, representative of the Paradise Trinity on Edentia, in constant communication with all universes. [1] Morontia mortals assemble on Mount Assembly to hear the Faithful of Days share the journey of progressing mortals through Havona and on to Paradise. [2]
