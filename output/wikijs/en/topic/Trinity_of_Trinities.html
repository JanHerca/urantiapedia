The Trinity of Trinities completes the cycle of reality through unlimited integration. [1]

The Universal Absolute is the impersonal presence of the unified Trinity of Trinities, manifesting as the primal cause and unqualified source of both the Trinity and Absolute. [2]

The Trinity of Trinities is the ultimate culmination of all that is implied in the fifteen triunities and associated triodities, where the experiential infinite attains identity with the existential infinite. [3]

The Trinity of Trinities is the ultimate culmination of all triunities and triodities, embodying the unity of experiential and existential infinity in the pre-experiential I AM. [4]

Levels

  • The Trinity of Trinities represents levels of increasing unification, with the Paradise Trinity being the first level and the Ultimate and Absolute Trinities being derivatives of the first. [5]
  • The second level of the Trinity of Trinities involves the Supreme, Ultimate, and Absolute Deities in a unified and experiential union. [6]
  • The realization of the I AM as experiential is the ultimate goal of the Third Level of the Trinity of Trinities. [7]
