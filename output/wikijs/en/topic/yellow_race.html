Consolidating central Asia in 15,000 B.C. [1] Drifted into darkness following Caligastia apostasy. [2] Drove Andonites from Asia. [3] Drove red race out of Asia into North America. [4] Entered China in 300,000 B.C., drove off Neanderthalers. [5] Escaped admixture with debased stocks; strengthened by superior red and Andonic. [6] Factors of superiority were genetic, social, spiritual, and geographic. [7] Fought red race in Asia for 200,000 years. [8] In Central and South America. [9] In India. [10] Migrated to Asia. [11] Mongoloid type. [12] On Pacific islands. [13] Small but potent Adamic legacy. [14] Somewhat broad-headed. [15] Strains incorporated in blue, white race. [16] Suffered from absorption of green race, Indian inferiors. [17] Usually enslaves green race. [18]
