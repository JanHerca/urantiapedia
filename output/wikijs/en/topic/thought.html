Abrupt presentations of subconscious emanations in the mind are sometimes influenced by the Adjuster, while more often arising from natural psychic functions inherent in the evolving animal mind. [1] As a man thinks in his heart, so is he, trusting in Yahweh with all his heart, acknowledging him in all ways, and receiving divine guidance. [2] Celestial beings excel in ideograph techniques to infinitely enhance thought preservation beyond concept picturization. [3]

Attaining cosmologic levels of thought involves a hunger for harmony and beauty, a love of the beautiful, and a sensitivity to ethics that leads to the pursuit of divine reality values. [4] The Conjoint Actor unerringly draws all divine thoughts into the absolute circuit of universal mind. [5] Dreams illustrate the struggle between unexpressed desires and divine messages during unconscious sleep. [6] Few mortals are real thinkers, spiritually developing and disciplining their minds to favorably connect with their divine Adjusters. [7] The word God symbolizes the approach to supreme reality and recognition of divine value, with human likes and dislikes not determining good and evil. [8]

The spread of civilization hinges on the effectiveness of language as the greatest thinking tool, requiring further development to express evolving thought. [9] Let the unrighteous man forsake his thoughts and return to the Lord, for he will abundantly pardon. [10]

Feeling, not thought, was the guiding force in evolutionary development, blurring the lines between fear, avoidance, respect, and worship in the primitive mind. [11]

Recognize and disregard the interference of preconceived opinions, settled ideas, and long-standing prejudices, in order to allow for the prompt recognition of every thought for what it truly is. [12]

Religion must navigate the paradox of utilizing thought while recognizing the limitations of intellectual overdevelopment in attaining higher spiritual attainment. [13] Melody of thought—the thinking of spiritual thoughts can be so perfected as to burst forth in the melodies of Havona. [14] Thought, not feelings, lead us Godward as the divine spirit makes contact with mortal man in the realm of the highest and most spiritualized thinking. [15] God's thoughts are higher than man's, but deliverance from guilt comes from realizing one's highest ideals may not align with God's will. [16] Life on the divine worlds of the central universe transcends human concepts, including the technique of Havona thought unlike ours on Urantia. [17]
