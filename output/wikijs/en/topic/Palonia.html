The first system rebellion in Nebadon took place in Palonia, where the Savior Sovereign was ardently loved and widely honored, bringing order and forgiveness to the confused and demoralized local system. [1]

In system 11 of constellation 37, a rebellion erupted after a Lanonandek Son's misunderstanding led to dissatisfaction and a disastrous act against the sovereignty of the Creator Son, despite being adjudicated by higher authorities in the universe of Nebadon. [2]
