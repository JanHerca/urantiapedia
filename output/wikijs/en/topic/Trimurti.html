The Trimurti, supreme trinity of Hinduism, consists of Brahma, Vishnu, and Siva, with Brahma being self-created and identified with fate, while Vishnu periodically incarnates and becomes real in the imagination of Indians, and Siva is lord of life and death as well as destruction and fertility.
