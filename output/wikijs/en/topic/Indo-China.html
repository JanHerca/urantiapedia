In Indo-China, the cultures of India and China blended to create rich and enduring civilizations, with the presence of the vanished green race lingering prominently. [1] Hinayana Buddhism persisted in Ceylon, Burma, and Indo-China, clinging to the early or asocial doctrine. [2]
