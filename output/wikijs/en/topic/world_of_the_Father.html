The seven circling worlds around the Father's world serve as detention spheres for interned groups of the Lucifer rebellion in Satania, while Edentia, the constellation capital, lacks similar prison worlds. [1]

The silent sphere of transitional culture world #7, Jerusem satellite, is the World of the Father, where loyal Panoptians serve as caretakers and builders under the guidance of Michael. [2]

Ellanora of Panoptia led her people to remain loyal to Michael, serving as caretakers under the Melchizedeks on the Father's sphere and its detention worlds, guided by the personal orders of Michael for their future use on the seventh Jerusem transition world. [3] Salvation was offered to rebels by Michael, but only the angels and lower orders accepted, now laboring with the Panoptians on the Father's world. [4]
