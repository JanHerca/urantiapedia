Paradise knowledge in local universes is secured only through indirection and reflectivity. [1] Data supplied to Divinington by the reflectivity technique ensures Thought Adjusters are fully informed before volunteering to indwell mortal minds. [2]

The focus on superuniverse headquarters worlds allows for the phenomenon of universe reflectivity, enabling the simultaneous recognition of the mental, material, and spiritual throughout the seven superuniverses. [3]

Description

The ability to reflect wisdom's source allows one to see its expression clearly. [4]

The intricate operations of reflectivity in the cosmos can only be accounted for by the evolving relationship between the finite cosmic mind and the divine absolute mind as experienced in the Supreme Mind. [5]

The reflectivity service between local universes and superuniverses appears to be automatic, but is actually driven by personal and intelligent cooperation. [6]

The Conjoint Actor's universe reflectivity allows for the simultaneous recognition of the mental, material, and spiritual, ultimately focalizing on Paradise. [7]

The reflectivity organization is a constant function, gathering news and disseminating decrees across creation, in contrast to the periodic operation of broadcast services. [8]

The extra-Paradise reflectivity service operates independently of time and space, its range seemingly confined to the periphery of the seven superuniverses. [9] Reflectivity, an indispensable feature of normal universe work, remains an impenetrable mystery of Spiritington. [10]

The reflectivity of the mind levels of the Infinite Spirit, Master Spirits, and Supreme Being is a mysterious yet transmissible attribute in the vast scheme of universal intelligence. [11]

Reflectivity on the superuniverse headquarters worlds showcases the most complex interassociation in all creation, unifying spirit, physical energy, and mind in an exceptional and unique way. [12]

The reflectivity service, a divine attribute of the Supreme Being and the Master Spirits, is the news-gathering and decree-disseminating mechanism of universal intelligence. [13] Reflectivity embodies omniscience within a finite experiential realm, potentially revealing the consciousness of the Supreme Being. [14]

Utilizing reflectivity facilitates partial contact with the consciousness of the Supreme Being, representing an emergence of presence-consciousness within experiential limits. [15]

Universe reflectivity allows the Conjoint Actor to coordinate all levels of actuality, enabling simultaneous recognition of mental, material, and spiritual phenomena throughout the superuniverse, with the power to see, hear, sense, and know all things as they transpire. [16] The impenetrable mysteries of reflectivity are a secret of Spiritington, operating on the headquarters worlds of the seven superuniverses and beyond. [17]

The Conjoint Actor's reflectivity enables the simultaneous recognition of mental, material, and spiritual aspects, coordinating universe actuality across all levels. [18]

Reflectivity, a transmissible attribute from the Conjoint Actor, Supreme Being, and Master Spirits, is a great mystery in the vast scheme of universal intelligence. [19]

Beings involved

The Conjoint Actor coordinates universe actuality to enable simultaneous recognition of mental, material, and spiritual realms through the unique power of reflectivity. [20] Reflectivity in seconaphim demonstrates the infinite mind of the Conjoint Actor. [21] Reflectivity is inherent in seconaphim, reflective beings produced by Reflective Spirits assigned to superuniverse headquarters. [22] Father fragments avail themselves of reflectivity content without actively participating. [23]

The Census Directors in synchrony with the reflectivity technique are uniquely created to be sensitive and responsive to intelligent will, always aware of the birth of will in the grand universe. [24]

The local universe Mother Spirits operate within the constraints of time, except when utilizing the personal prerogatives of the Universe Son and the function of universe reflectivity. [25]

Reflectivity is personalized in Majeston, the chief and center of all reflective phenomena throughout the universes, a true person created by the Supreme Being in conjunction with the Deity Absolute. [26] The reflectivity mechanism of the local universes and the superuniverse is a great mystery only explainable by the activity of the Master Spirits. [27]

Reflectivity unveils the evolving relationship between the finite cosmic mind and the Supreme Being, representing the emergence of the presence-consciousness of the divine absolute mind. [28]

The Reflective Spirits, of Trinity origin, reflect the natures and characters of the seven divinity combinations, requiring seven on each superuniverse capital for perfect reflection of all the Paradise Deities' manifestations. [29] The Unqualified Supervisors of the Supreme, representing God the Supreme, function as supreme unifiers and may be involved in universe reflectivity. [30]

While Thought Adjusters do not participate in the operation of the universal reflectivity system, all Father fragments are fully cognizant of transactions and can access the content, but their knowledge and plans are not reflectible. [31] The personnel of the reflectivity service, excluding Majeston and Reflective Spirits, are all offspring of Infinite Spirit. [32] Reflectivity is inherent in the offspring of Reflective Spirits, reflecting the universal intelligence circuits of the Infinite Spirit. [33]

Value to superuniverse government is reflected equally among primary, secondary, and tertiary seconaphim, denoting orders of procedure rather than status or function. [34]

In Urantia

Urantia, as the 606 of Satania in Norlatiadek of Nebadon, is linked by reflectivity to the Ancients of Days. [35]

Reflectivity circuits are restricted on Urantia due to quarantine, limiting the full privilege of utilizing angels of the reflective order on the planet. [36] A reflective phenomenon, occurring just before death, shapes our perception of angels based on prior beliefs held in the mind. [37]

Reflective beings, always interposed between ascenders and reflectivity, aid ancestral Spirits with an unbelievable host of helper seconaphim, but ascending mortals do not directly interact with them in the training worlds. [38] Seas of glass on architectural worlds portray reflectivity for modifying energy currents in space. [39] The chief of seraphim on Urantia is always aware of reflective transference from Uversa, yet remains ignorant of what is sought or how it is secured. [40]
