Cain departed for the land of Nod and became a great leader, promoting peace between Nodites and Adamites throughout his lifetime, marrying his cousin Remona, and seeing his son Enoch lead the Elamite Nodites. [1] Cain found wife in Nod, pre-Adamites recognized in early teachings by Moses. [2]

The Elamite Nodite settlement was the origin of the Nodites, descendants of the rebellious planetary staff led by Nod, who were doomed to mortal existence. [3]
