37 seceded in Lucifer rebellion. [1] Administrations. [2]

Arrival

Installed by Life Carriers. [3] Late arrival on Urantia. [4] Usually appear about time of will development. [5] Usually arrive 100,000 years after man walks erect. [6]

Assigned to all planets inhabited by mortals. [7] Belong to order of Lanonandek Sons. [8] Commissioned by System Sovereigns upon Life Carrier report that will is functioning. [9] Controllers and transformers collaborate in rendering Planetary Princes visible to mortals. [10] Early rule is autocratic. [11] Early staff averages about 1000. [12] Elevated to Planetary Sovereigns in light and life. [13] In bestowal as emergency Material Son, Michael effected repentance of defaulting Planetary Princes. [14] Invisible to mortals. [15] Lucifer allowed Planetary Princes to rule worlds as supreme executives. [16]

Mission

Make first revelations of higher truth and universe organization. [17] Schools primarily concerned with philosophy, religion, morals. [18] Sole representatives of divinity on individual planets. [19] To foster planetary civilization. [20]

Nearest personal approach of Eternal Son aside from incarnation. [21] No high trust deemed more sacred than that reposed in. [22] Permanently attached to planets of original assignment. [23] Planetary Princes on neighboring planet defaulted. [24] Rebellion by Planetary Princes in Nebadon. [25] Rebellion by Planetary Princes instantly isolates planet. [26] Regimes are identical. [27] Secondary Lanonandeks; number 10,234,601 in Nebadon. [28] Simple planetary headquarters; schools. [29] Spring from Creator Son and Mother Spirit. [30] Subject to executive mandates of System Sovereigns and Gabriel. [31] Successors to defaulted Planetary Princes not immediately installed. [32] Sympathetic rulership increases risk of being led astray. [33] System council of Planetary Princes met during time of tomb. [34] Tribunals reflect fatherly, discretionary attitude. [35] United administrations with Planetary Adams. [36]
