Adjusters best typify fragmented entities of Deity. [1] There exists other fragmentations apart from Thought Adjusters. [2]

The Eternal Son, as an infinite and exclusively personal being, cannot fragmentize his nature, cannot distribute and bestow individualized portions of his selfhood upon other entities or persons as do the Universal Father and the Infinite Spirit. [3]
