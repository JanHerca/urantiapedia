Andovontia, tertiary Universe Circuit Supervisor #572,842 stationed on Salvington, eternally serves in the group of his original assignment, studying agelong problems of spirit and morontia circuits. [1] Universe Circuit Supervisors cannot annul material currents; they direct pure mind circuits for all spirit messages and personalities. [2] Universe Circuit Supervisors intelligently oversee circuits of relative spiritual energy outside Paradise with precision and control. [3]

The Universe Circuit Supervisors are created for specific tasks and eternally serve in their original assignment, making an agelong study of the problems found in their realms without rotation in service. [4] Universe Circuit Supervisors, exclusively created by the Infinite Spirit, serve as agents of the Conjoint Actor in four orders. [5]

Universe Circuit Supervisors have their headquarters on superuniverse capitals, possessing tremendous endowments of antigravity, unlike Solitary Messengers who roam the universe of universes. [6] Universe Circuit Supervisors appear in Personality registers. [7] The Universe Circuit Supervisors isolate evolutionary worlds in rebellion, utilizing all agencies and forces in the universe of universes. [8]

Nature

All Universe Circuit Supervisors are distinct individuals, possessing a unique other-than-Father personality not found in any other beings. [9]

Universe Circuit Supervisors attend strictly and efficiently to their business, dealing solely with entities overseeing circuits under their supervision. [10] The Universe Circuit Supervisors possess tremendous antigravity as they maintain headquarters on the capitals of the superuniverses. [11]

Orders

Universe Circuit Supervisors, created by the Infinite Spirit, function as exclusive agents of the Conjoint Actor across four orders - Supreme, Associate, Secondary, and Tertiary. [12]

  1. Supreme Circuit Supervisors.
  2. Associate Circuit Supervisors.
  3. Secondary Circuit Supervisors.
  4. Tertiary Circuit Supervisors.

The Universe Circuit Supervisors are subject to the Seven Supreme Power Directors in their oversight of mind circuits associated with spirit and physical-energy. [13]
