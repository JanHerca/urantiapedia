The brotherhood of men is founded on the fatherhood of God. [1] When all is said and done, the Father idea is still the highest human concept of God. [2] The Fatherhood of God and the brotherhood of man present the paradox of the part and the whole on the level of personality. [3]

Fatherhood becomes, or may become, a universe reality to all moral creatures because the Father has himself bestowed personality upon all such beings and has encircuited them within the grasp of the universal personality circuit. [4]
