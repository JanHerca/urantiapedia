At Jesus’ 4th appearance. [1] At Jesus’ crucifixion. [2] Born April 17, A.D. 9. [3] Endeavored to elude Pharisees. [4] Jesus had long talks with. [5] Married David Zebedee; moved to Philadelphia. [6] Moved to Capernaum. [7] Thoughtless of speech but sincere of heart; beautiful. [8] Unswervingly loyal to Jesus; chief family comfort. [9]
