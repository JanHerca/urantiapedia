The Persians freed Jews from Babylon, only for them to fall back into bondage under their own priestly laws and rituals. [1]

The concept of triad gods appeared in religion long before the revelation of the Paradise Trinity, and was prevalent among various civilizations, including the Persians, Hindus, Greeks, Egyptians, Babylonians, Romans, and Scandinavians. [2]
