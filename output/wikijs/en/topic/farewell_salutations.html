Jesus is not given to farewell ceremonies:

  • At the end of his first incarnation he took his leave suddenly and unceremoniously. [1]
  • Leaving Rome he did not say goodbye to any of his friends. [2]
  • When he left Cana after the miracle of the wine he did not say goodbye to anyone. [3]

Melchizedek merely bid his human companions goodnight before disappearing from his sight forever. [4]
