The first group specialists were potters who made plain or fancy pottery, while women or men in different tribes specialized in sewing and weaving. [1]

Before Jesus was eight years old, he was known to all in Nazareth, especially the mothers and young women, who often saw him at the spring near his home, one of the town's social hubs. [2]
