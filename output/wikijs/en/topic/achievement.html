All beings seek new achievement; there is always time for new achievements. [1] Ambition, judgment, and wisdom are essentials of achievement. [2] Artistic achievement is effected by personal efforts. [3] Attained goals are simply milestones, but none are denied transient satisfaction from achievements. [4] Advancement grows out of the divine capacity for action, and achievement is the child of imaginative adventure. [5] Father works to improve our earthly achievement, but especially our heavenly estate. [6] Greatness lies not in possessing strength, but in making wise and divine use of one’s strength. [7] Humor helps in earthly careers. [8] We increase our success by not taking self too seriously. [9] Life is not real to those who cannot do one thing expertly. [10] We need to learn to maintain a well-balanced poise in success. [11] We will never be held responsible for the accomplishment of that which is beyond your abilities. [12] The Father has predicated finite progress upon effort, creature achievement upon perseverance. [13] Psychic circle attainment augments success potential. [14] Purposeful service produces highest satisfaction. [15] Achievement is the real test of Havona. [16] The recognition indicative of achievement is granted to all. [17] It is the self element that exhausts, not the effort to achieve. [18] Sometimes ignorance is essential to achievement. [19] Lucifer rebellion threatened the thrill of adding something personal and unique to experiential wisdom. [20] Your power and achievement is “according to his mercy, through the renewing of the Spirit.”. [21] Van’s achievement resulted from clear thinking, unselfish purpose, discipline, dedication to Father. [22] When man and God enter into partnership, no limitation can be placed upon future possibilities. [23]

Adjusters

They are deeply interested in our real achievement on earth. [24] They cannot materially alter our career struggles. [25] We should permit that adjusters fight with us and for us. [26]

Material success

It is the Father’s will that mortal man should work persistently and consistently toward the betterment of his estate on earth. [27] Success requires not only devotion to one’s work but also that one should function as a part of some one of the channels of material wealth. [28] Righteousness yields satisfactions transcending material rewards. [29] Some men’s lives are too great and noble to descend to the low level of being merely successful. [30]

When man’s ascending soul stands before the Supreme Judge, the decision of eternal import will not be determined by material successes or quantitative achievements. [31] Jesus had very nearly mastered the technique of utilizing the energy of the spiritual drive to turn the mechanism of material achievement. [32]

Teamwork

Accomplish more permanent things by working with brethren. [33] Depends on wisdom, charm that wins co-operation. [34] Partnership technique quadruples possibilities. [35]
