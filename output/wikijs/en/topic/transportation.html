Mobile subordinates of the Supreme Power Centers engage in a remarkable variety of autotransport, but they, like all space traversers, need assistance to overcome gravity when departing a sphere. [1] Improved transportation facilitates commerce, promoting the peace of Urantia through international trade organizations. [2]

Transportation on Jerusem is facilitated by birds and vehicles, allowing material and morontia beings to travel at speeds ranging from two to five hundred miles per hour. [3]

Spirit personalities proceed by liaison with spirit energy, enabling travel at a pace varying from two to five hundred miles per hour in the transportation system allied with circulatory streams of energy movement. [4] Fused mortals traverse space freely through Adjuster transit before being clothed with morontia forms in the resurrection halls of higher spheres. [5]
