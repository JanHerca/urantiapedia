Siva is the Hindu lord of life, death, fertility, and destruction, while Vishnu is believed to periodically incarnate in human form. [1]

Siva is a member of the Hindu trinitarian concept that includes Brahma and Vishnu, brought to India by Sethite priests and developed further by native intellectual evolution. [2]
