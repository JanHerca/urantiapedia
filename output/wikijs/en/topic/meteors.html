The atmospheric oxygen was exhausted by the combustion of heavy meteoric showers on the primitive planetary surface. [1] The shooting star, awesome to early man, sparked reverence for all stones. [2]

Nonbreathing worlds in Satania are protected from meteor bombardment by advanced races with electrical installations, but still face great danger when venturing beyond these protected zones. [3] Comets and meteors comprise an enormous aggregate of energy and material substance in space. [4] Meteors are a source of solar energy by incessantly diving into blazing suns. [5]

Millions of meteors could penetrate the primitive planetary atmosphere in the volcanic age, but fewer are able to resist the oxygen-enriching atmosphere in later eras. [6] Shooting stars and Saturn's rings are fragments of disrupted space bodies influenced by tidal gravity from larger space bodies. [7] Meteors, generally composed of heavy materials, accumulated on ocean bottoms, contributing to the increasing weight of the Pacific Ocean. [8] Primitive cultures viewed shooting stars and meteors as signs of special spirits descending to earth. [9] The sun recaptured a large portion of meteoric material during the early times of erratic orbits in the solar system's infancy. [10]

2 billion to 1 billion years ago, during the meteoric era, incessant impacts of space bodies kept the surface of the planet heated, causing heavier elements to settle towards the center. [11]

Millions upon millions of meteorites enter the atmosphere of Urantia daily at a rate of almost 200 miles per second, posing significant danger to nonbreathing worlds' inhabitants who must rely on electrical installations for protection. [12] Planets grow by accretion from meteoric collisions in space. [13]

The primitive atmosphere afforded little protection against meteoric impacts in the volcanic age, but as time passed, fewer meteors were able to penetrate the oxygen-enriching atmosphere. [14] Angona drew away ancestral material from the solar system, but did not secure any solar matter itself. [15]

In Satania, inhabited worlds without a protective atmosphere are rare due to the abundance of meteoric space bodies, including comets that consist of swarms of smaller disrupted matter. [16]
