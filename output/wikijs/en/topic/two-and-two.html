Union of souls is essential for the mobilization of wisdom, as exemplified in the 70 evangelists sent out in pairs by the Master to spread the gospel. [1]

Jesus sent out the apostles two-and-two to teach the glad tidings of the kingdom and acquire practical experience in dealing personally with their fellow men. [2]
