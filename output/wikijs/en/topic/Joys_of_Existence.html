Collaborating with reversion directors, the Joys of Existence promote joy among angelic hosts and lower will creatures, seeking to demonstrate inherent joy in freewill existence. [1]

Promoting reactions of joy is the principal activity of the Joy of Existence beings, who seek to upstep pleasure reactions and develop a superhumor among mortals and angels. [2]

The Joy of Existence is reflected in the superaphic harmony supervisors, promoting joy among angels and will creatures while demonstrating inherent joy in freewill existence. [3] Reflective associates of Divine Counselors promote joy among angelic hosts and lower will creatures, attuned to superaphic harmony supervisors above. [4]
