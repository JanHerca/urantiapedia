Accompanied Michael on 3rd bestowal. [1] Children of Reflective Spirits. [2] Classification as primary, etc., does not indicate status differential. [3]

General service of seconaphim

As Most High Assistants. [4] Assist members of superuniverse assemblies. [5] May become Paradise Companions, Technical Advisers. [6] May serve as Evangels of Light attached to mortal finaliter companies. [7] Serve on planets in light and life. [8]

In personality registers. [9] Limited in spirit polarity for circuits of Eternal Son. [10] Living mirrors and presence projectors. [11] Ministering spirits of superuniverses. [12]

A. primary seconaphim

Living mirrors to Ancients of Days. [13] Transport ascenders to Havona. [14] Transporters can traverse from Uversa past dark gravity bodies to Havona. [15]

Types

  1. Voices of Conjoint Actor.
  2. Voices of Seven Master Spirits.
  3. Voices of Creator Sons.
  4. Voices of angelic hosts.
  5. Broadcast receivers.
  6. Transport personalities. [16]
  7. Reserve corps. [17]

B. secondary seconaphim

Assigned to Divine Counselors, Perfectors of Wisdom, and Universal Censors. [18]

Types

  1. Voices of Wisdom.
  2. Souls of Philosophy.
  3. Union of Souls.
  4. Hearts of Counsel.
  5. Joys of Existence.
  6. Satisfactions of Service.
  7. Discerners of Spirits.

C. tertiary seconaphim

Assigned to Mighty Messengers, Those High in Authority, and Those without Name and Number. [19] Guide ascenders in perplexity, uncertainty, and emergencies. [20]

Types

  1. Significances of Origins.
  2. Memories of Mercy.
  3. Imports of Time.
  4. Solemnities of Trust.
  5. Sanctities of Service.
  6. Secrets of Greatness.
  7. Souls of Goodness.
