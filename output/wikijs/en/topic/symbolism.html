Symbolism in religion can be positive or negative based on whether it replaces the original worshipful idea, but is distinct from direct idolatry. [1]

The cult type of social organization persists by providing a symbolism for perpetuating inspiring ideals, fostering moral sentiments and religious loyalties, and ensuring cultural survival and realization. [2]

Those with greater spiritual insight should be patient and tolerant of those who rely on symbolism to mobilize their feeble spiritual insight, avoiding disdain for the weak. [3]
