Material light

Material light signifies physical-light manifestations in the illuminated universe, alongside intellectual insight and spirit luminosity, all interconnected through mind's intervention between spirit and matter. [1] Calcium and sodium atoms ride light beams, facilitating their dissemination throughout space. [2] Life on a planet with marine origins thrives in shallow inland seas where sunlight can penetrate oceans up to 600 feet deep. [3] A comet's tail pointing away proves light has weight and is a real substance, not simply waves of hypothetical ether. [4] Sunlight is highly explosive when confined by opaque retaining walls, and is economical at $1,000,000 per pound. [5] Light escapes suns only with aid of X rays, which penetrate all space and play a crucial role in maintaining ultimatonic associations of energy. [6] Light confined at high temperatures by opaque walls is highly explosive. [7]

Light and electricity are not a basic energy of space, but rather secondary and subsidiary manifestations controlled by Supreme Power Centers in our local universe. [8]

Light, heat, electricity, magnetism, chemism, energy, and matter are all manifestations of the same fundamental physical energy undergoing endless transformations throughout the universe. [9]

Science may measure the force of light, but can never truly define what it is; true understanding comes from correlating physical and eternal values with wisdom. [10] Light, in deciphering spectral phenomena, is sometimes modified by energy and matter in space, including the floating derelicts of sodium and calcium. [11] Light, like other forms of energy, moves in a straight line through space, obeying linear gravity and the circular gravity of the Isle of Paradise. [12] Energy, in its flight through space, moves straight forward subject to gravity of material mass and the circular-gravity presence of Paradise. [13] Light, a real substance, moves in straight lines through space as a succession of energy particles, not waves. [14] Suns convert energy almost wholly into light, illustrating the unimaginable store of energy contained within each unit of matter. [15] The white light contains the whole visible light of the suns. [16]

Light without heat

The distribution of light outside of Havona is controlled by specially constructed spheres operated by Supreme Power Centers. [17]

The spiritual luminosity emitted by the seven sacred worlds of the Eternal Son shines brightly throughout Paradise and Havona, illuminating the seven secret spheres of the Universal Father in their reflective splendor. [18] Light, a nonspiritual manifestation of the Deities, is derived from the divine activity of the primal cause of universal physical phenomena. [19] Some suns shine without heat, illuminating and heating architectural headquarters worlds near the center of evolutionary universes. [20]

Spiritual light

All souls will survive by cooperating with their Adjusters and seeking God, guided by the true light within. [21] The apostles were the light of the world, shining their good works for all to see and glorify their Father in heaven. [22] Believe in Jesus, the light of the world, and receive eternal life and divine truth. [23] Let your light shine with wisdom and discretion, for nothing is hidden that will not be revealed. [24] Let your light shine brightly, guiding others without drawing attention to yourself. [25] Material Sons saturated with celestial light stand equal but differential, serving in pairs with Material Daughters in divine harmony. [26] The intensification of spiritual light is like music created by control. [27]

When light is not honestly lived up to, it becomes darkness within the soul, which is why Jesus permitted Judas to continue on his path, striving to transform and save him despite his failings. [28]

The seven sacred worlds of the Eternal Son channel pure spirit luminosity to the superuniverses, illuminating Paradise, Havona, and the central universe. [29] The luminous emanation of spirit beings is the personality manifestation of light. [30]

Upon the primary eruption upon personalization of a local universe Mother Spirit, the proclamation of life is projected in the newly organized universe. [31] Jesus proclaimed “I am the light of the world, he who follows me shall not walk in darkness but shall have the light of life.”. [32]

The spirit luminosity accompanying Adjusters is known as the “pilot light” in Nebadon and the “light of life” in Uversa, and has been called the “true light” on Urantia. [33] The spiritual luminosity of Father’s personal presence is beyond mortal comprehension, dwelling in a light no material creature can approach. [34]

The threefold fluctuation of the Deity abode signals the fulfillment of the divine heart's satisfaction by the worship of the residents of Paradise, a triumph of technique in the eternal plan of the Gods. [35] The seven luminous spheres of the Eternal Son are the source of the threefold light of Paradise and Havona, illuminating the central universe. [36]

The universe is illuminated by material light, intellectual insight, and spirit luminosity, representing three kinds of light on all levels of intelligence. [37]
