The Designers and Embellishers are a group belonging to the Celestial Artisans who are organized into the following corps: [1]

  1. Craftworkers of color. [2]
  2. Sound designers. [3]
  3. Emotion designers. [4]
  4. Artists of color. [5]
  5. Presence embellishers. [6]
  6. Taste designers. [7]
  7. Morontia synthesizers. [8]
