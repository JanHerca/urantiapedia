System Co-ordinators appear in Uversa personality registers as part of the Morontia Power Supervisors, a group inside the Universe Power Directors. [1]

System co-ordinators harmonize differing power systems on morontia worlds to create a working unit for ascending mortals progressing from the physical to the spiritual. [2]
