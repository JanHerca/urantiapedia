Rejoice in the ever-present possibility of immediate spiritual communion with the Father, as you progress towards his Paradise presence. [1] The personality of God can be grasped in spiritual communion with Jesus, who realized the full potential of spirit personality in human experience. [2]

Deliverance from the sense of guilt stemming from interrupted spiritual communion or lowered moral ideals comes from realizing that one's highest moral standards may not align with the will of God; striving to find God and emulate Him is the true purpose. [3]

The spiritual communion of Adjuster involves moral status, mental motivation, and spiritual experience, leading to self-realization through the manifestation of the fruits of the spirit. [4]

Lack of consciousness of spiritual communion does not disprove the presence and transforming power of divine influences surrounding and impinging upon you, as evidenced by the fruits of the spirit yielded in your life experience. [5] Maintaining unbroken communion with the Maker through sincere prayer and true worship enriches the capacity of receptivity in individual believers. [6]

Modern man thinks he is too busy for spiritual meditation and religious devotion, leading to a barren existence devoid of real values and eternal assurances. [7] Civilization must deliver mankind from fear and intermediaries in communion with Deity for spiritual progress. [8] True spiritual communion is not fostered by excessive ornateness or ostentatious art, but by simplicity, beauty, and holiness in worship spaces. [9] Perversity, self-indulgence, and intolerance separate us from spiritual communion with the Father. [10]

The Remembrance Supper is a parable of spiritual communion with Jesus, symbolizing the emergence from bondage into spiritual fellowship and liberty, free from traditional interpretations and formal limitations. [11] Believers should renew their spirits by stepping aside from the rush of life and having implicit faith in the certain triumph of righteousness. [12] Living truth is a spirit-discerned reality, existing only on high spiritual levels of communion with God. [13]

Effect of spiritual communion

Gradually developing assurance of spiritual communion confirms our divine contact and kinship with God. [14] The divine presence most fully discovered in the indwelling Mystery Monitor within your own mind transcends nature and mortal experience. [15] Divine union attained by progressive reciprocal spiritual communion is the key to achieving unity with God. [16] The presence of God is determined by the choices and will-attitudes of individual beings, reflecting the inherent variability in spiritual communion. [17]

Spiritual communion with the Father in heaven, as practiced by Jesus, relieves tension and augments resources of personality, enabling the soul to bravely face and solve life's highest problems. [18] Spiritual communion with the source of energy is the key to awakening latent powers for good and accomplishing great things. [19]

Through solitary reflection and communion with divinity, Jesus sought wisdom, energy, purpose, and transformation to better serve others and align with God's will. [20]

Jesus

Michael's only concern was to maintain unbroken communion with his Paradise Father, for through this relationship the universe would witness a new revelation of the Universal Father. [21]

Jesus held unbroken communion for 6 weeks on Mount Hermon, becoming absolutely assured of his divine nature and certain of his triumph over the material levels of time-space personality manifestation. [22] Prepared for crisis by solitary communion, Jesus made pivotal decisions during his forty days in the mountain wilderness. [23] Peter, James, and John spent nights in mysterious communion with Jesus and the Father in heaven. [24]
