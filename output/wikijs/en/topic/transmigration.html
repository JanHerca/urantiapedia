Ganid ceased to believe in soul transmigration after Jesus explained the limitations of animals' spiritual nature. [1]

The stultifying belief in weary and monotonous lives led to a fear of the nonevolutionary perpetuation of self through reincarnation, robbing struggling mortals of hope and spiritual advancement. [2]
