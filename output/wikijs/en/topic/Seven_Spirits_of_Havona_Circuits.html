Ascenders do not hold personal communion with the Spirits of the Circuits in Havona, despite experiencing their spiritual influence. [1] Omniaphim were created by the Infinite Spirit in conjunction with the Seven Supreme Executives. [2]

Tertiary supernaphim, offspring of the Seven Spirits of the Circuits, are key intermediaries connecting beings in Havona with the vast creature family of the Third Source and Center. [3] Each Havona circuit is ruled by one of the Seven Spirits of the Circuits, coordinating celestial affairs with exquisite organization and balance. [4] The Seven Spirits of the Havona Circuits function in personality registers. [5] Just as Thought Adjusters are to mortal creatures, Circuit Spirits are to Havoners, except Havoners do not assimilate with them. [6] Local universe Mother Spirits train with the Spirits of the Circuits in Havona to prepare for their roles as co-creators of the local universes. [7]

The Seven Spirits of the Circuits, collectively produced by the Master Spirits in response to the Supreme Being, oversee the planetary circuits of Havona. [8]

The Seven Spirits of the Circuits operate within a single Havona circuit, aligning with the Supreme Being's presence and working exclusively in Havona, separate from the Eternals of Days' dominion. [9]

The Seven Spirits of Havona Circuits were not part of the original pattern universe, but were created by the Master Spirits to supervise the seven planetary circuits. [10] The Seven Spirits are the unified servants of the Master Spirits, representing the Infinite Spirit in the central universe circuits. [11]

The Seven Spirits of Havona Circuits provide a uniform impersonal representation of Infinite Spirit and Seven Master Spirits to Havona, overseeing the perfectly balanced and exquisitely organized billion worlds arranged in seven concentric circuits surrounding Paradise satellites. [12]

The Seven Spirits of the Circuits focus solely on permeating a single Havona circuit, not involving themselves with the Eternals of Days, but collaborating with the Seven Supreme Executives and aligning with the Supreme Being in Havona. [13]
