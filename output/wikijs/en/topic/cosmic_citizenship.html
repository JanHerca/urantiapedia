In the fraternal realities of the kingdom of heaven the faith sons of God find final deliverance from the isolation of the self, both personal and planetary. The God-knowing believer increasingly experiences the ecstasy and grandeur of spiritual socialization on a universe scale—citizenship on high in association with the eternal realization of the divine destiny of perfection attainment. [1] Cosmic citizenship is the honest recognition of your progressive obligations to the Supreme Being. [2] Angels teach about the responsibility and morality of cosmic citizenship to mortals. [3]
