One Census Directors is stationed on capital of each local universe. [1] Another one is stationed on each Havona circuit. [2] They are persons with recognizable spirit form. [3]

They are personally sensitive and responsive to intelligent will. They can give us the number, nature, birth, death, and whereabouts of all will creatures. [4] They have the headquarters on superuniverse capitals; their reserves are on Paradise worlds of Spirit. [5] They are infallible in function; they never default, neither do they falsify. [6] They are not concerned with the records of your life and its doings; they are not in any sense recording personalities. [7] They are so created as to be able to maintain perfect synchrony with the reflectivity technique of the superuniverses. [8] Possess tremendous endowment of antigravity. [9] Salsatia is Nebadon Census Director and maintains headquarters within the Gabriel sector of Salvington. [10] The Census Directors are a special and completed creation of the Infinite Spirit, and they exist in numbers unknown to us. [11] Usatia is the superuniverse chief of all Orvonton directors. [12] They appear in personality registers. [13]
