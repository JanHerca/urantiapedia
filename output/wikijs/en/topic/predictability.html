In considering universal phenomena, it is important to recognize the unpredictability arising from complex interassociations and personality actions. [1]

Predicting physical phenomena becomes increasingly challenging as we venture away from Paradise and encounter unpredictable forces beyond our current understanding. [2]

The presence and performance of the Ultimate and Absolutes cause unpredictability in the reactions of primordial force, nascent spirit, and other nonpersonal ultimates. [3] The reactions of the spirit-gravity circuit are predictable, but the actions of mind and personality are not. [4]
