Corps of Transcendental Finaliters is one of the seven finaliter corps. [1] A Havona native can join each company of the Corps of Transcendental Finaliters of Paradise, one of the three supreme goals for the citizens of Havona. [2] They are based on Paradise. [3] They appear in Uversa personality register. [4]
