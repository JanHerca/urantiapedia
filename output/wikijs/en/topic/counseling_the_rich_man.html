These are the pieces of advice that Jesus offered to a wealthy Roman citizen who practiced Stoicism:

  1. Manage inherited wealth responsibly for future generations.
  2. Share discovered wealth for the benefit of many.
  3. Earn commercial wealth through fair trade.
  4. Return unjust wealth from exploitation to those wronged.
  5. Ensure wealth from interest is fair and not exploitative.
  6. Share wealth from talent fairly, considering societal contributions.
  7. Use accidental wealth for the benefit of the community.
  8. Return stolen wealth to its rightful owners.
  9. Manage deposited wealth responsibly for others.
  10. Use earned wealth from personal effort responsibly.

See also: UB 132:5.
