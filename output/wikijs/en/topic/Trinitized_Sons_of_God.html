The Melchizedek receivers advised after rebellion, governing Urantia until Adam's arrival. [1] Completion seraphim are attached to the high trinitized beings of the universes, serving in light and life ministries. [2] Trinity-embraced sons of God are constantly increasing in numbers, never having strayed or been lost in the service of the superuniverse governments. [3] Trinitized Sons of God function as self-governing groups across the superuniverses, serving under the direction of the Ancients of Days. [4] Trinitized Sons of God have their sole homes on Paradise, near the Most Holy Sphere. [5]

Trinitized Sons of God are classified as: trinitized sons of attainment, selection, perfection, and creature-trinitized sons, all in personality registers. [6] None of the Trinitized Sons have ever gone astray, remaining steadfast in their service to the superuniverse governments. [7] Trinitized Sons of God serve as Most High Assistants in harmonizing Nebadon with the ideals of Paradise. [8] Trinitized Sons of God serve on commissions certifying Havona pilgrims for Deity adventure. [9]

Types

  • A. Deity-trinitized Sons, unrevealed. The unrevealed Deity-trinitized Sons of God are part of the trinitized order, along with Trinity-embraced and Creature-trinitized Sons. [10]
  • B. Trinity-embraced Sons. Trinity-embraced Sons are forever devoted to Trinity service and assignment, comprising seven orders of personalities for superuniverse service. [11]
    1. Trinitized Sons of Attainment.
      • Mighty Messengers.
      • Those High in Authority.
      • Those without Name and Number.
    2. Trinitized Sons of Selection.
      • Trinitized Ambassadors.
      • Trinitized Custodians.
    3. Trinitized Sons of Perfection.
      • Celestial Guardians.
      • High Son Assistants.
  • C. creature-trinitized sons.
    1. Paradise-Havona trinitized Sons. The parental beings of Paradise-Havona trinitized Sons are inseparable in assignment and function, functioning as one while being reckoned as two personalities in the Corps of the Finality and Architects of the Master Universe. [12]
    2. Ascender-trinitized sons. The ascender-trinitized Sons of God seek to trinitize conceptual realities of the Almighty Supreme on their journey to Paradise. [13]
