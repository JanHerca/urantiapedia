Advised Melchizedek receivers after rebellion. [1] Completion seraphim attached to. [2] Constantly increasing in numbers. [3] Function as self-governing groups. [4] Homes are on Paradise. [5] In personality registers. [6] None have ever gone astray. [7] Serve as Most High Assistants. [8] Serve on commissions approving Havona pilgrims circuit advancement. [9]
