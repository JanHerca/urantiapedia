Seraphim, angels of local universes, are embraced by the Ministering Spirits and are the initial contact for mortal ascenders. [1] Cherubim and Mansion World Teachers may become seraphim after being re-embraced by the Universe Mother Spirit. [2] Cherubim and sanobim, functionally united, serve as faithful and efficient aids to seraphim in mortal worlds. [3] 500 million pairs of seraphim on Urantia in 1934 help THE Most Highs rule in the kingdoms of men through many celestial forces and agencies. [4] The attainment of Seraphington by angelic beings far surpasses mortal's achieving eternal survival. [5]

The Seraphim are directed by almost paternal System Sovereigns who oversee local affairs and serve as custodians of legislative and judicial mandates in the universe administration. [6] Seraphim, including evolutionary seraphim, appear in personality registers. [7] The apostles were instructed by Jesus about the ministry of seraphim during the evening meal. [8]

Men, sometimes permitted to see, have shaped the concept of angels by observing seraphim undergoing transport service with what appear to be double sets of wings that are actually energy insulators. [9] Michael, as a supreme seraphim, disappeared on his fourth mission of bestowal, hidden in plain sight among the angels. [10] Seraphim gather for millennial reunions at various headquarters, alternating attendance with partners to fulfill duty responsibilities. [11] Morontia progressors on Edentia are like angels, evolving between animal and ascending spirit, maintaining a general morontia status. [12] Efforts to understand and love angels result in only good. [13] During the Havona sojourn, accredited seraphim part with ascendant mortals for intense training towards achieving perfection and supremacy in service. [14]

The Most Highs rule in planetary government through the ministry of seraphim, ensuring effective supervision in unique ways due to planetary circumstances and celestial forces. [15] Residence in Jerusem circles is determined by the hierarchy of angelic beings, stationed in concentric levels according to their functions and orders. [16] Sacred books on Urantia confuse the associate personalities of Deity with the Universal Father, portraying seraphim as God. [17]

The worlds of the seraphim in the Salvington circuit are characterized by beauty, vastness, and specialized training schools, where pairs of seraphim live and carry out their tasks with affection and personal connection. [18]

The traditional concept of angels with wings comes from misconceptions about the appearance of seraphim during moments of reflective phenomena before death. [19] Do not tempt your angelic overseers to guide you towards dangerous paths, even in times of peace and prosperity. [20] Seraphim undergo extensive training on the seraphic worlds before being commissioned as ministering spirits in Nebadon. [21]

Completion seraphim from the Seraphic Corps of Completion are specially trained attendants who have achieved Paradise and the personal embrace of the Eternal Son. [22] About completion seraphim see link to Corps of Seraphic Completion.

Destiny. What they may become

The Brilliant Evening Stars are a unique twofold order of superangels, with some of created dignity and others of attained service, numbering 13,641 in the Nebadon corps. [23]

Achieving perfection as a celestial artisan allows entry into the Paradise seraphic abode as a Technical Adviser or Celestial Recorder, leading to eternal service and advice among the seraphim. [24]

Ascendant seraphim from the local universes, known as Celestial Recorders, execute all records in duplicate with their unique ability to manipulate spiritual and material energy. [25]

Completion seraphim serve as everlasting associates of mortal finaliters on Paradise, while also ministering in various nonmortal finaliter corps and the Corps of Seraphic Completion. [26]

Father-indwelt finaliters are admitted into the Corps of Mortal Finaliters after their ascendant career, including faithful seraphic guardians who join their mortal wards in embracing eternity's destiny. [27]

The Paradise Companions are a diverse group of celestial beings recruited from various ranks, serving temporarily before returning to their original duties. [28] Achieving perfection as a Technical Adviser enables entry into the Paradise seraphic abode and eternal ministry among celestial beings. [29] Trinitized Custodians are ascendant seraphim embraced by the Paradise Trinity and serving the Ancients of Days. [30]

The Trinitized Sons of Selection serve in the courts of the Ancients of Days, having been recruited from selected evolutionary seraphim, translated midway creatures, Spirit-fused and Son-fused mortals who have ascended to Paradise. [31] The seraphim guide beings to methods of attaining Paradise and eternity. [32]

Seraphim strive towards the eternal goal of Seraphington, the angelic threshold to Paradise and Deity attainment, where they achieve their ultimate destiny. [33] Some angels, under certain conditions, can be summoned to Seraphington and achieve Paradise quicker than mortals. [34]

Nature

Seraphim, like humans, acquire knowledge and language through study and experience, seeking to serve in lower orders to achieve their highest potential in universe service. [37] Seraphim can sometimes discern Adjusters' spirit luminosity, suggesting their high and exclusive divine origin and nature. [38] Can work individually, but pair required for communication,. Seraphim can work singly, but must be in pairs for communication and service on higher circuits. [39] Seraphim can only be summoned to Seraphington by the tertiary supernaphim, as they cannot initiate departure on their own. [40]

Cherubim, like seraphim and other spirit beings, are continuously engaged in self-improvement through study and seeking new achievements in their roles as ministering spirits of time. [41]

The seraphim, created but a little over humanity, possess powers far beyond human comprehension and perform numerous duties with exceeding ease, functioning on both spiritual and literal levels. [42]

The seraphim, created by local universe Mother Spirit, are the primary creators of the vast creature family of the Third Source and Center, working together with their Infinite Mother Spirit. [43]

Certain beings, such as Havona Servitals, conciliators, angels of time and space, and ascending mortals, possess the unique ability to discern both spirit and material beings throughout their spiritual journey. [44] Angels, being of spirit nature, perceive mortal beings without physical bodies and fully comprehend their emotions and struggles. [45]

Seraphim possess adequate energy range for local and superuniverse tasks, but lack the capability for the demanding journey from Uversa to Havona, which requires the unique abilities of primary seconaphim transporters. [46]

The seraphim, across all seven superuniverses, exhibit minimal variation in their fairly uniform design, making them the most standard of all spirit beings. [47] Seraphim reside in pairs in their beautiful and vast headquarters worlds within Nebadon. [48]

Each seraphim has both an individual name and number, with names used in universe headquarters registration and numbers in records for assignment to world service. [49] Seraphim have no childhood, but can acquire functional skills through experience and education. [50]

The angels, highly individualized personalities, are the ministering-spirit associates of all space's evolutionary and ascending will creatures, as well as the working colleagues of the higher hosts of the divine personalities of the spheres. [51]

The Infinite Spirit reveals the combined love of the Father and the Son to individual minds through a divine series of attenuations, culminating in the presence of angels guiding us in earthly existence. [52] The immortal angels in heaven, though not all-wise nor all-powerful, remain pure and holy unless tainted by sin. [53] Nonreproducing ascenders gain parenthood experience by assisting Material Sons and Daughters on Jerusem. [54]

Seraphim are sustained by the intake of spiritual energy from the primary circuits of the universe and partake in circulating teachings of Teacher Sons. [55]

Seraphim exhibit dual phases denominated as positive or aggressive and negative or retiring, enhancing their versatility and functionality in assignments. [56]

Seraphim, like mathematical prodigies, possess powers far beyond human comprehension and effortlessly perform numerous duties that would be tremendous tasks for mortals. [57] Seraphim, though affectionate and sympathetic, are not sex-emotion creatures, commonly referred to by feminine pronouns on sex planets. [58] Seraphim in Nebadon are still being created, as the Universe Mother Spirit continues to bring forth new creations in the evolving universe. [59] Angels are the sure heavenly guides of souls of man, escorting them to the mansions in heaven. [60]

Seraphim are sustained by spiritual energy from the primary circuits of the universe, unlike mortal beings on Urantia who obtain light-energy through vegetation. [61]

Seraphim can traverse space at triple velocity, moving at 558,840 miles per second, utilizing gravity for transit, unlike most nonenseraphimed beings restricted by time and space limitations. [62] Seraphim usually retain their original complements of being, working together in pairs for tasks, but not necessarily in all assignments. [63]

Particular actions

Jesus, presented with the vision of twelve legions of seraphim, decided to only use them if it was his Father's will, yet they remained with him throughout his earthly life. [94]

In the Lucifer rebellion in Satania, 25% of lower orders were misled, while few justice guides were lost, causing a significant loss of seraphim and cherubim. [95] The unseen seraphim accompanied the evangelists' urgent call to salvation and unity with the heavenly kingdom. [96]

Before embarking on his sixth bestowal, Michael's departure from Salvington was witnessed by all, as he was accompanied by a lone seraphim and the Bright and Morning Star of Nebadon. [97] Chaldean priests were informed by seraphim about Jesus' birth, as certain wise men were aware of Michael's impending arrival on Urantia. [98]

The Andon and Fonta posterity were chosen by the Life Carriers to provide living material for the assembled Andonic counterparts for Prince's staff, guided by Thought Adjusters and seraphim. [99] Seraphim dispatched messages during rebellion quarantine, ensuring no communication from or to the fallen worlds could be utilized by Lucifer. [100] Manotia's courageous loyalty during the Lucifer rebellion remains a thrilling example of devotion and faithfulness among the celestial beings. [101]

As Jesus prayed in Gethsemane, a seraphim touched and strengthened him, affirming his divine will to fulfill the Father's desire for his earthly sacrifice. [102] Seraphim opened prison doors for early teachers of gospel, while secondary midway creatures may have performed similar phenomena ascribed to angels. [103]

At sundown, seraphim participated in healing as a vast retinue of celestial beings stood ready to act at the signal of their Sovereign, seeking refuge in appeal to the Father's will. [104]

At the noontide birth of Jesus, the seraphim of Urantia sang anthems of glory over the Bethlehem manger, unheard by mortal ears, until priests from Ur arrived to pay homage. [105] Some rebel seraphim on earth were mistaken for evil angels by older writers up to Christ's death. [106] Assigned messenger bids Jesus 'Be about your Father’s business' before Passover Sabbath flood tides of spiritual illumination. [107]

Sentiments

Seraphim, after being commissioned, long to serve in the reserves of their inherent group, seeking assignment to the lowest orders of universe service in order to achieve the highest level of experiential destiny. [108] Seraphim, incapable of understanding fear, struggle to comprehend the overwhelming influence of animal fear on human intellect and faith. [109] Seraphim volunteers ignite the will-to-service in ascending mortals through personal friendship. [110] Angels, superior in spiritual status, do not judge mortals, nor should mortals judge each other. [111]

From seraphim we learn that our most disappointing disappointments can become our greatest blessings when we accept our lot after faithfully performing duty, making fewer personal plans concerning others. [112] The seraphim long to achieve perfection and supremacy of service as they train intensely for their supernal assignment. [113] Angels, beings of spirit nature and origin, love human beings and are devoted to guiding them on their journey towards divinity and eternity. [114] Angels, though invisible to mortals, intellectually understand mortal life and share all of man’s nonsensuous emotions. [115] The seraphim share most of humans' emotions and would develop a warm affection for them, as they are very near mortals in personality attributes. [116]

Service of seraphim

Many Father fused guardian angels of mortals, after taking the finaliter oath, join their human wards in eternal destiny. [117] Seraphim work through the social, ethical, and moral environment to promote circle-making decisions in mortals. [118] The director of the Satania seraphic hosts serves on the Jerusem executive council. [119] The guardian seraphim work outside-in to stimulate circle-making decisions in human mind within the domains of action. [120]

Seraphim equally serve mortal series 2 on nonfusion planets, providing devoted service and cooperation for upstepping animal-origin creatures towards spiritual fusion. [121] Seraphim, created just above humanity, play an essential role in mortal progression facilitated by the working of the Supreme Being. [122] Execute mandates of resident governor general with 191,924,362 angels absent. [123]

Seraphim transmit drafts of ancestry and projected patterns of mortal life conduct to Divinington, where Thought Adjusters, believed to be volunteers fully informed about the candidate for indwelling, make their decision to indwell. [124]

Seraphim, created to function on spiritual and literal levels, possess powers far beyond human comprehension and effortlessly perform tasks that would be tremendous for mortals. [125] Seraphim help Spirit-fused survivors repossess mortal memory through retelling experiences and accessing recorded files. [126] The seraphic hosts in constellation legislature number one hundred and are nominated by supervising personalities. [127] Seraphim are indispensable for reassembling mortal personality, whether through personal or group custodians. [128] The master seraphim intensify existing higher human ideals through their various functions. [129] Angels of God ascend and descend to keep worlds in touch with each other. [130]

Seraphim manipulate circumstances to influence human activity through liaison with Master Physical Controllers and midwayers, serving as co-ordinators, interpreters, and manipulators of the physical environment in order to enhance cosmic insight and spiritual cooperation. [131]

The cosmic mind and adjutant mind-spirits collaborate to create a unique physical tabernacle for humans, while seraphim supply the undifferentiated morontia material necessary for morontia life to commence and evolve into diverse, individualized forms. [132]

Reserves of the supervisor seraphim are not inactive, instead serving important roles throughout the constellations, including as messenger aids to rulers and attached to Vorondadek Sons on special assignments. [133] Seraphim retain records of human life for final evaluation. [134] Seraphim urge humans to pray for enhanced cosmic insight and increased cooperation with the divine presence. [135]
