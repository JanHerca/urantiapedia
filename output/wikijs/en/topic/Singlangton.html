Singlangton, a Chinese spiritual leader 100,000 years ago, influenced the cultural development of the Chinese people through worship of the One Truth. [1] Singlangton was the first of the yellow men to teach and lead his people in the worship of “One Truth,” one of 24 counselors. [2] Singlangton, the first yellow man to teach his people the worship of “One Truth” thousands of years ago. [3]

The teachings of Singlangton on the concept of Shang-ti continued to influence the Chinese people, leading to a shift from the pursuit of new truths to the preservation of established beliefs. [4]
