Hap led the college of revealed religion in the Prince's staff. This group had made considerable progress in their attempt to substitute Creator fear for ghost fear before their labors were interrupted by the later confusion attendant upon the Lucifer's secession upheaval. [1] In rebellion Hap and the entire college of revealed religion remained loyal with Van and his noble band. [2] Hap presented the early races with a moral law. This code was known as “The Father’s Way” and consisted of seven commands. [3]
