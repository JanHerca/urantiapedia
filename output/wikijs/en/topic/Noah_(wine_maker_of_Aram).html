Noah, the wine maker of Aram, advocated for houses to be built like boats to survive the impending flood. [1] Noah, the wine maker of Aram, was reminiscent of the Mithraic flood legend. [2]

The story of Noah, the ark, and the universal flood was woven into Jewish tradition, influenced by the Babylonian story of the flood, with Mount Ararat being the sacred mountain of northern Mesopotamia. [3]
