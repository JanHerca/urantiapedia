Although seraphim are not sex-emotion creatures, they are beings descending from Father and Son, much like the sons of God. [1] God's divine entities are part of him and his knowledge is universal and perfect. [2]

The Sons of God in Uversa's personality register include Creator Sons, Magisterial Sons, Trinity Teacher Sons, Melchizedek Sons, Vorondadek Sons, Lanonandek Sons, Life Carrier Sons, Father-fused Mortals, Son-fused Mortals, Spirit-fused Mortals, Evolutionary Seraphim, Ascending Material Sons, Translated Midwayers, Personalized Adjusters, Mighty Messengers, Those High in Authority, Those without Name and Number, Trinitized Custodians, Trinitized Ambassadors, Celestial Guardians, High Son Assistants, Ascender-trinitized Sons, Paradise-Havona-trinitized Sons, and Trinitized Sons of Destiny. [3]

The local universe Sons of God, including Creator Sons, Avonal Sons, and Trinity Teacher Sons, serve as powerful and effective leaders and teachers within Nebadon, each fulfilling unique roles in the grand design of the universe. [4] Sonarington is the Paradise headquarters of the descending and ascending Sons of God. [5]

Mortals as sons of God

God abundantly pardons sinners, for mortals as sons of God are divinely kind and mercifully received when they return to righteousness. [6]

All who are led by the spirit of God are sons of God, strengthened with power through His spirit in the inner man, on the path to the perfection of faith, service, and the fullness of God. [7] Mortals are sons of God, potentially evil but not sinful, ascending toward divine perfection through spiritual rebirth. [8] The Spirit bears witness with our spirits that we are children of God, born of the spirit with the power to overcome doubt through faith. [9] Those receiving Jesus know they are sons of God, as Joseph learned in a dream about Mary's extraordinary child. [10] All mortals are sons of God, belonging to the divine family and entitled to grace and mercy. [11]

Orders of Sons of God

A. descending orders

All descending Sons of God have high and divine origins and are dedicated to facilitating the progress of lowly creatures on their evolutionary climb towards Paradise. [12] Descending orders of sonship are of direct and divine creation, while ascending sons achieve status through evolution. [13]

Types

  1. Creator Sons.
  2. Magisterial Sons.
  3. Trinity Teacher Sons.
  4. Melchizedeks.
  5. Vorondadek Sons.
  6. Lanonandek Sons.
  7. Life Carriers.
  8. Archangels.
  9. Bright and Morning Stars.
  10. Brilliant Evening Stars.
  11. Father Melchizedeks.

11 unrevealed Sons of God and Universe Aids. [14]

B. ascending orders

The ascending orders of Sons of God, including Father-fused, Son-fused, Spirit-fused, Ascending Material Sons, Evolutionary Seraphim, Translated Midwayers, and Personalized Adjusters, culminate in the eternal purpose of divine love and gracious condescension throughout the entirety of creation. [15] Sons of God achieve status by experiential participation in the creative techniques of evolution or direct divine creation. [16] Mortals are divinely beloved representatives of the lowest order of creation, known as ascending sons after fusion with eternal spirit. [17]

Types

  1. Adjuster-fused mortals.
  2. Son-fused mortals.
  3. Spirit-fused mortals.
  4. Evolutionary seraphim.
  5. Ascending Material Sons.
  6. Translated midwayers. [18]
  7. Personalized Adjusters.
  8. Unrevealed ascenders. [19]

C. stationary orders, dual origin

Types

  1. Abandonters.
  2. Susatia.
  3. Univitatia.
  4. Spironga.

The “Sons of God” are 5. Unrevealed Dual-origin Beings. [20]

D. trinitized orders

The Sons of God in the superuniverse of Orvonton are classified as Descending, Ascending, and Trinitized, encompassing all beings embraced by Trinity. [21]

Types

  1. Mighty Messengers.
  2. Those High in Authority.
  3. Those without Name and Number.
  4. Trinitized Custodians.
  5. Trinitized Ambassadors.
  6. Celestial Guardians.
  7. High Son Assistants.
  8. Creature-trinitized Sons.
  9. Paradise-Havona-trinitized Sons.
  10. Trinitized Sons of Destiny.
