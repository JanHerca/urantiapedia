First order transition minister seraphim assist in the education of surviving mortals on the seven mansion worlds. [1] On Urantia, seraphic evangels counsel human teachers to preach the goodness and love of God for repentance and fearlessness. [2] Seraphic evangels assist morontia progressors in selecting optimal routes to Havona and other key destinations. [3] The seraphic evangels whispered the 23rd Psalm to the shepherd boy. [4]
