Andites contributed to this trait; they were adventurous; never stopped until they had circumnavigated the globe. [1] We should be patient and and be not tempted by cheap and sordid adventure. [2] God has embarked on eternal adventure with man. [3] Love of adventure suggests that with death begins eternal discovery. [4] High spirit personalities are not given to the gratification of purposeless curiosity, purely useless adventure. [5] Supreme adventure is man seeking and finding God. [6]
