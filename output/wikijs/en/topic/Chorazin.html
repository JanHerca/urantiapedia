In no place where Jesus had taught had he met with such a general rejection of his message as in the city of Chorazim. [1]

Due to that the words of Jesus were: “Woe upon the light-rejecting inhabitants of Chorazin, Bethsaida-Julias, and Capernaum, the cities which did not well receive these messengers”. [2]
