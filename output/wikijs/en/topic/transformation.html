You are transformed by Spirit of Truth, endowed with power to perform the perfect will of God. [1]

Cures effected by Jesus for Elman’s patients resemble miracles, but are transformations in expectant and faith-dominated persons under the influence of a positive personality. [2]

The supreme desire to do the Father's will, coupled with the joy of faith realization, leads to loving spiritual ministry, intellectual communion, and social service being a byproduct of spiritual transformation. [3]
