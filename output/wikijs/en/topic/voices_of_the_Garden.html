The personal seraphim of Adam and Eve, known as the “Voices of the Garden,” serve as planetary helpers in the evolution of human races. [1]

Solonia reproved Adam and Eve for disobedience in the Garden, highlighting the confusion of Urantians in attributing supernatural events to the Universal Father. [2]
