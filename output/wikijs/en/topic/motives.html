Despite receiving patient instruction and loving warnings about their critical tendencies and pride, Caligastia and Lucifer misconstrued helpful advice as unwarranted criticism, projecting their own evolving selfishness onto their well-meaning advisors. [1] Eve believed Cano's reassurance that those with good motives would not commit evil. [2] Do not prejudge others’ motives; leave judgment to those with superior spiritual status. [3] The motive for gaining entrance into the kingdom of heaven is what truly matters, as it is by their fruits that false prophets are known. [4] God judges based on motives, not outward actions, emphasizing the intimate parent-child relationship with divine love and mercy. [5] The motivating thought behind prayer validates supplication; words hold no value. [6] Jesus' merciful intercession for his executioners on the cross revealed a lifetime of unselfish service and affectionate devotion. [7] Primitives rendered judgment based on injury done, disregarding the motives of transgressors. [8]

Secoraphic Discerners of Spirits and Sanctities of Service reveal the true motives of individuals through reflective scrutiny, laying bare the deep motives of the human heart and angelic mind. [9] To understand others, fathom their motives, and discover the true values of life through love and empathy. [10] In the realm of wisdom, the Universal Censors rule as to motives with divine guidance and discernment. [11] Beliefs, not knowledge, dictate behavior and performance, particularly when emotionally activated through religious engagement. [12]
