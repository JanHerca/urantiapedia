Visitors may travel to any realm they have experienced, hosted by celestial beings dedicated to entertaining superhuman students and other celestial visitors. [1]

Travel between planets is facilitated by transport seraphim, who carry passengers to different worlds but never allow a mortal to return to their native planet during their temporal existence. [2] The human desire for travel will be fully satisfied during the long journey to Paradise through the local, super-, and central universes. [3]

Travel greatly furthers civilization and brotherhood through the multiplication of international social contacts, development of a common language, and interchange of people from different races and nations. [4] Olden wars promoted travel and cultural intercourse, but modern methods of transport and communication now serve these ends more efficiently. [5]
