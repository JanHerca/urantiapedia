Nebuchadnezzar overthrew Necho, destroyed Jerusalem, and carried the population into captivity, marking the end of Judah and the Yahweh-Baal struggle. [1] Jeremiah counseled surrendering Jerusalem to Nebuchadnezzar, the king of Babylon, during the siege. [2]
