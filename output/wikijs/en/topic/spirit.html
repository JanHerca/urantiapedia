Inside Man

The divine Spirit within man leads towards the ultimate goal of spiritual progress and eternal sonship with God. [4]

The Adjuster can only communicate directly with a human once they reach the 1st psychic circle, representing the highest level of mind-Adjuster relationship before the liberation of the soul from the body. It is perilous to speculate on the Adjuster content of dreams or differentiate it from conscious reception of mortal conscience, better to err in rejecting an Adjuster's expression than to elevate a mortal mind reaction to divine dignity. [7] In every mortal, a conspiracy of spiritual forces delivers us from the bondage of matter. [8]

Augmenting universe insight is achieved by enhancing the ability to grasp eternal meanings in insignificant activities, in order to further the progressive co-ordination of the Paradise-ascension career. [9]

Men are profoundly impressed with the conviction that they ought to believe in God, as religious longings and spiritual urges compel them to recognize their evolutionary duty and obligations. [10]

In the realm of the highest and most spiritualized thinking, inner spiritual communion allows mortal man to perceive the divine nature through the pure mind. The ultimate goal for modern man is to achieve spirit-consciousness, the knowledge of sonship with God, through better communication with the divine Monitor within. [13]

Man's threefold endowment of Adjuster, Spirit of Truth, and Holy Spirit ensures that Spirit must always and ultimately become Trinity-unified in final realization. [14]

Countless spiritual connections link Urantians directly with Deities, including Thought Adjusters to the Universal Father, the spiritual-gravity urge of the Eternal Son, and the presence of the Conjoint Creator. [15]

Man can attain the art of living, transcending the blind urge of the animal, to reach celestial joy and spiritual ecstasy with self-conscious insight into the meaning of meanings. [16]

The human mind can only discover, recognize, interpret, and choose moral values and spiritual meanings rather than create real values or gain universal insight. The flesh does not naturally bear the fruits of the divine Spirit, but with effort and faith, one can ascend to higher spiritual values and escape the warfare between the physical and spiritual natures. Civilized man's enlightened spiritual consciousness seeks the truth of living and the good and right technique of reacting to ever-recurring situations. Nature of spirit

The nature of spirit bridges the gap between mortal realms and spiritual spheres, guiding the goal of personality existence towards spiritual ascension. [29]

The absolute convergence of spirit in the Father is the goal of all personalities, with material manifestations being relative and the cosmic mind intervening between these universal opposites. Spirit is always intelligent, minded, and possessed of varied phases of identity, with the ability to communicate with similar entities and personalities, transcending the consciousness of mind. [33] Spirit, as divine purpose, establishes mutual kinship between energy and spirit in eternity. [34]

[36] The goal of all personalities is spirit, the highest personal reality that transcends both mind and matter. Mankind must recognize that matter and morontia are the skeletal reflections of enduring spirit reality, and learn to view time as the motionless image of eternity and space as the transient shadow of Paradise realities. [40] The Infinite Spirit interassociates physical and spiritual energies and organizations through the cosmic mind. [41] The divine Spirit gently leads willing learners to reach eternal goals with intelligent and cheerful cooperation, never driving but always guiding. [42] The Most Holy Sphere on upper Paradise is a wholly spiritual realm, appearing nonexistent to purely material beings. [43] On Paradise, energy and spirit are essentially one, resulting in the maximum function of the cosmic mind in the time universes of space. [44] Spirit ultimately finds unity in the eternal Trinity, originating from a single source and expressing itself in a threefold manner. [45] Spirit-perceived time reveals motion Godward and the ascent to higher levels of divinity. [46] The divine Spirit quickens and gives life, providing continual ministry and encouragement to humanity. [47] Spirit patterns do not occupy, displace or contain space, as they exist in relation to space but do not necessarily fill it. [48]

The evolving human being's physical tabernacle and morontia form are highly individual and characteristic, shaped by the creative mind that dominates them. [49]

Certain angels must work in complemental pairs to synchronize with the circuits of the Universal Father and the Eternal Son, demonstrating the necessity of spirit polarity in their functions. [50] In personality, spirit strives for dominance over matter, reflecting the transcendency of the spiritual over the material. [51] On Paradise, the unchanging spirit transcends both mind and matter, co-ordinating the three energies in the evolutionary cosmos. [52] Spirit energy remains undiminished in transmission, transcending time and space independently of any external forces. [53]

Spiritual forces, originating from the Eternal, unfailingly return to their original levels, bringing with them those who have embraced the inner guidance of the indwelling Adjuster and are truly born of the Spirit.
