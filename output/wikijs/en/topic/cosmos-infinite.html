The cosmos-infinite is a kind of illimitable cosmos on beyond the master universe, in which it is postulated that the Absolute Trinity will achieve final expression in the creations of infinity, completing the absolute actualization of all potentials. [1] Cosmos-infinite is a limitless, boundless, never-beginning, never-ending existence the comprehension of which is really beyond man's ability. [2]

The fourth triunity contains the energy potential of the cosmos-infinite, of which the grand universe and the master universe are only partial manifestations. [3]
