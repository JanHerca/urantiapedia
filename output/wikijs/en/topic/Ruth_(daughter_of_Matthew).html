Ruth, daughter of Matthew, was a member of women’s corps chosen by Jesus for ministering work of the kingdom on January 16. [1]

At Jesus' 16th appearance, he instructed his disciples to proclaim the gospel of the kingdom, emphasizing the reality of the fatherhood of God and the truth of the sonship of man. [2]
