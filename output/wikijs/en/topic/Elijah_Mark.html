Elijah Mark was the father of John Mark, the famous follower of Jesus; he was the husband of Mary Mark. [1] His home in Jerusalem was a regular meeting place for believers. [2] The last supper of Jesus and his apostles took place in an upper room of the house of Elijah Mark. [3] Judas and the temple guards came to Elijah Mark's house looking for him. [4] Some appearances of Jesus took place in the home of Elijah Mark. [5] He died suddenly on May 3, A.D. 30. [6] In the upper room of the house of Elijah Mark is where 120 believers gathered at Pentecost, after the departure of Jesus. [7]
