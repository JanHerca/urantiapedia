All three-brained, one-brained, and two-brained races share the same type of life plasm, guiding their planetary evolution in similar ways. [1] The Andonic life plasm transplanted into Caligastia 100 brought corporeal, relatively human forms of special planetary service to Urantia. [2] The vital spark of life, the mystery of life, is bestowed through the Life Carriers, who catalyze and organize inert material to become living matter. [3] Life plasm consists of essential units of life energy manipulated by universe physicists and electrochemists. [4]

The metamorphosis of the germ plasm by the action of system life currents resulted in the creation of a new type of mortal designed for social sovereignty, though the project ultimately failed. [5]

The mutant traits in the first Nodite generation were caused by modifications induced by X-rays on the Andonic germ plasm, influenced by the Satania life-maintenance circuits. [6]

The original life plasm of evolutionary worlds must contain full potential for all future development, including the appearance of seemingly useless forms essential to the final function of higher intelligent life. [7] Life springs only from life, as the Spirit of God contributes the vital spark to all living beings. [8]
