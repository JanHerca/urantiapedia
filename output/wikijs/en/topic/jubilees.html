After ascending through the stages of spiritual realization, the seventh jubilee marks entrance into the eternal service of the Finality Corps.

  1. First Jubilee: Agreement with the Thought Adjuster and sealing the intention to survive.
  2. Second Jubilee: Awakening in morontial life.
  3. Third Jubilee: Fusion with the Thought Adjuster.
  4. Fourth Jubilee: Awakening in Havona.
  5. Fifth Jubilee: Discovery of the Universal Father.
  6. Sixth Jubilee: Awakening in Paradise after the final transit sleep of time.
  7. Seventh Jubilee: Entry into the mortal finaliter corps and beginning of service in eternity.

The jubilee celebration marks the settling of a local universe in light and life. [1]
