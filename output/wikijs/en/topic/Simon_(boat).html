Jesus taught from Simon the boat, built by his own hands, as the people crowded near the water's edge. [1]

The old fishing boat of Andrew and Peter, kept at Jesus' disposal, became the platform for his teachings as he shared the parable of the sower with the multitudes gathered by the seaside. [2]
