Spirit-fused mortals, when required on Uversa, are escorted by Celestial Guardians, who serve as messengers and agents of the Ancients of Days. [1]

Spirit-fused mortals, unlike their ascendant brethren, do not retain human memory after death, relying on seraphic associates and records for the immediate reconstruction of their past experiences. [2] Spirit-fused mortals go through mansion worlds on their path to immortality and eternal fusion with the local universe Mother Spirit. [3] Spirit-fused mortals failed to personalize on the mansion worlds, prompting guardian angels serving as Mind Planners to organize their teamwork. [4] The Spirit-fused mortals are in charge of the 8th Salvington circuit worlds. [5]

Spirit-fused mortals in personality registers differ greatly from Adjusters, as they are not prepersonal entities nor dwell on Spiritington, traverse mind-gravity circuits, or indwell mortal creatures during life in the flesh. [6]

Spirit-fused mortals are included in Father’s personality circuit, having fused with individualizations of the premind spirit of the Third Source and Center in the morontia existence. [7] Spirit-fused survivors may revisit their nativity worlds in subsequent planetary dispensations to learn about their past lives in the flesh. [8]

Spirit-fused mortals are few in number in Nebadon, attaining immortality through fusion with a Mother Spirit fragment, journeying with you to Salvington before serving the local universe eternally. [9]

The resurrection procedure for spirit-fused mortals involves the reawakening of primitive beings through guardian seraphim and a portion of the immortal spirit of the Third Source and Center. [10]

Spirit-fused mortals are bestowed with fragmentations of the premind spirit, which differ greatly from Adjusters and do not indwell mortals during life in flesh. [11] Spirit-fused mortals of the Nebadon Corps of Perfection are closely associated with the Susatia on Salvington. [12] Urantia mortals will not become Adjuster-fused, but Spirit-fused mortals share portions of their universe career. [13] Spirit-fused mortals are generally confined to a local universe. [14] The worlds of Spirit-fused mortals evolve identically with the seven stages of light and life on an evolutionary world. [15]

Destiny

Through their permanent residence in the local universes, Spirit-fused mortals achieve unsurpassed wisdom in mastering Nebadon problems, enhancing future stability and diversification. [16]

Spirit-fused mortals, though not destined to attain Paradise or become finaliters, achieve immortality through eternal fusion with a fragment of the local universe Mother Spirit. [17]

Spirit-fused mortals, after obediently obeying the Father's command to “Be you perfect,” may eventually become enrolled in the local universe Corps of Perfection, where they can serve as Universe Aids and interpret the viewpoints of evolving creatures to celestial authorities. [18]

Spirit-fused mortals, who attain immortality through fusion with the local universe Mother Spirit, generally become permanent citizens of local universes, with only a few ascending to higher levels and a select few reaching Paradise. [19]

May become

The High Commissioners are Spirit-fused ascendant mortals, distinct from those destined for Adjuster fusion in the universe-ascension career. [20]

Trinitized Ambassadors, recruited from Spirit- and Son-fused mortals who reach Paradise through Havona, are commissioned as the second order of the Trinitized Sons of Selection on Uversa. [21]

Trinitized Sons of Attainment, like Mighty Messengers, attain their destiny through the Trinity embrace, gaining everything a finaliter will achieve as a seventh-stage spirit. [22]

Trinitized Sons of Selection, including Spirit-fused and Son-fused mortals, embrace the courts of the Ancients of Days after their embrace by the Paradise Trinity. [23]

Spirit-fused ascenders who attain the Nebadon Corps of Perfection may become Universe Aids, interpreting the viewpoints of material world creatures to celestial authorities in the local universe. [24]

Only a few Spirit-fused mortals ascend to Uversa and Paradise, while the majority remain as permanent citizens of the local universes, contributing their evolved wisdom to the administration for the benefit of all. [25]

Spirit fusion

Adjusters on series 1 planets provide experiential training for self-culture, while on series 2 planets they are loaned as temporary helpers for spiritual attainment. [26] Andon’s immediate descendants achieved Spirit fusion on Jerusem. [27] Spirit-fused mortals experience fusion only upon morontia awakening, never overlapping with Son or Father fusion. [28]

Surviving souls achieve immortality through fusion with a fragment of the local universe Mother Spirit, forming a rare group on the mansion worlds of Nebadon, journeying with ascending mortals until Salvington, potentially advancing further but mostly serving within the local universe. [29]

Spirit-fused mortals undergo fusion with individualizations of the premind spirit of the Third Source and Center, distinct from Adjuster-fused mortals. [30] Spirit-fused mortals are guided by loaned Adjusters to build up their souls. [31]

Spirit-fused mortals are not Third Source personalities; their fusion only occurs at mortal reawakening in the morontia existence, never during natural life. [32]

Nonbreathers on nonbreathing worlds, unlike those on atmospheric planets, vary in life technique but are candidates for Spirit fusion, carrying out realm activities with similar trials and joys as atmospheric world mortals. [33] Primitive races undergo a dispensational adjudication upon the arrival of Planetary Princes, leading to their eventual Spirit fusion. [34]
