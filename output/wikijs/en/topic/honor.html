Honor, pride, ambition - futile without an audience to parade before, forming the origins of art, ceremonial, and sports. [1] To live unselfishly, one must confront the constant demand for honor from within. [2] Seek only what honestly belongs to you in matters of honor and recognition. [3]

Historically

Primitives desired social and political prestige through buying into a commercialized nobility based on special services or money. [4] Accumulating wealth has long been a symbol of social status and power, showcased through extravagant gift-giving and philanthropic acts. [5] The Zebedee sons sought honor, but Jesus taught them the true nature of his kingdom. [6] Jesus' spiritual faith harmonized personal honor with practical judgments. [7]
