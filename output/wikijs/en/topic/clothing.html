Andon and Fonta use of animal skins for warmth. [1] Caligastia one hundred taught treating skins for use as clothing, and weaving was later introduced by the teachers of art and science. [2] The seventy evangelists were sent out without extra clothing. [3] Jesus’ clothing was divided by soldiers before crucifixion. [4]

“Be reminded that a wise tailor does not sew a piece of new and unshrunk cloth upon an old garment, lest, when it is wet, it shrink and produce a worse rent”. [5]
