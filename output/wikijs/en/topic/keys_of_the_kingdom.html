Jesus gave his chosen apostles and their successors authority over things temporal, entrusting them with the affairs of the kingdom of heaven. [1]

The keys of the kingdom of heaven are sincerity, more sincerity, and more sincerity; all men possess these keys and can advance in spirit status through decisions, more decisions, and more decisions, always choosing to do the will of God to achieve the highest moral value. [2]
