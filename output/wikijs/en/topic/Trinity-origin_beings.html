Co-ordinate Trinity-origin beings play vital roles in superuniverse administrations. [1] Trinity-origin beings in personality registers express the diverse forms of divine creation. [2] Trinity-origin beings have transit prerogatives allowing them to reach any point in Orvonton from Uversa in less than one year. [3] We intercommunicate instantaneously with all Trinity-origin beings except the Inspired Spirits. [4] Trinity-origin beings, brought into existence by the Paradise Trinity, are all freewill endowed and none have ever gone astray. [5]

Types

Trinity-origin beings, created for specific service, are divided into seven orders, such as Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy, Eternals of Days, and Ancients of Days, responsible for administration and wisdom in the superuniverses, with the Perfectors of Wisdom representing divine insight and the Universal Censors as judgment of the Paradise Trinity.
