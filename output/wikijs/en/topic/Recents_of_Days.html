Three Recents of Days preside over each minor sector, directing the administration of the component local universes. [1] 21,000 recently created Trinity personalities coordinate over minor sectors as the youngest supreme directors. [2] The Recents of Days gained experience apprenticed under Eternals of Days before overseeing minor sectors in the superuniverses. [3] At headquarters of a minor sector, one Recent of Days is always on duty, even when others are away fulfilling their supervisory duties. [4] Recents of Days appear named in Personality registers. [5] Resident ascending mortals serve alongside celestial beings in the administration of minor sectors by the Recents of Days. [6] The Recents of Days are Supreme Trinity Personalities. [7] Trinitized Custodians are officers of tribunals of the Ancients of Days, the Perfections of Days, and the Recents of Days. [8]
