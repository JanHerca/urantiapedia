The evidence that man is indwelt by God is shown through manifestations of divine mercy and tenderness towards the weak and needy, illustrating the unique blending of human and Creator. [1]

It is a great error to teach boys that it is unmanly to show tenderness; true courage and happiness come from being sensitive and responsive to human needs. [2] Indiscriminate kindness may breed social evils, Jesus preached wisdom, tolerance, and forgiveness. [3] The mature human looks upon all others with tenderness and tolerance, embracing the journey towards emotional and intellectual maturity. [4]
