Ascending mortals on superuniverse training worlds become wards of the Ancients of Days, embarking on a unique journey of spiritual progression and growth towards their potential destiny in the Corps of the Finality. [1] Superuniverse wards, confirmed by Creator Son and Mother Spirit, have never gone astray or faltered in their ascent. [2] Journeying through all ten major sectors is essential for those coming from Splandon to reach Uversa in the superuniverse. [3]
