Gamala was one of the cities visited during the second public preaching tour of Galilee began on #3#, October 3, A. D. 28, and continued for almost three months, ending on December 30. There were Jesus, the twelve and the 117 evangelists. [1] During that tour, at Gamala Jesus taught the truth about the fear the Lord. [2] Gamala is one of the towns visited during the second tour of the Decapolis between August 18 and September 16, 29. [3] Here took place the episode of the meeting of the apostle John with a strange preacher whom he disavowed. [4]
