In Jesus’ time, every rock and tree believed to be possessed by spirits. [1] Prayer is little associated with animism, but such beliefs may exist alongside emerging religious sentiments. [2] The savage looked upon all nature as alive, as possessed by something. [3] Slaves brought a crude animism to Greece, with bloody rites where criminals where executed in a ceremony. [4]
