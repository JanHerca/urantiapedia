Types of Adjusters

Thought Adjusters exist in seven orders, from virgin to personalized entities, with varying experiences and destinies, serving as divine guides within human minds. [229]

Virgin Adjusters

The elaborate training and development of Thought Adjusters prepare them for the next stage of cosmic life as they gain experience through mortal creatures' growth and development. [230]

Although Thought Adjusters have less ability to communicate with fellows, their powers of selfhood are commensurate with mind and they possess unlimited ability to communicate with each other. [231]

  1. Virgin Adjusters often indwell series 2 mortals on primitive series 1 planets for self-culture and progressive development, while experienced Monitors are in liaison with higher human types. [232]

Virgin Adjusters are rarely assigned to individuals with unquestioned survival capacity on two-brain worlds after the presence of a Paradise bestowal Son, as it is believed that most Adjusters indwelling those with survival capacity are of the advanced or supreme type. [233]

Virgin Adjusters serve on primitive worlds, series 1 planets, indwelling the minds of creatures for experiential training in self-culture and progressive development, gaining valuable experience to benefit superior beings on other worlds. [234] Virgin Adjusters, uniform in nature, serve initial assignment in the minds of evolutionary candidates for eternal survival. [235]

Advanced Adjusters

Advanced Adjusters have served on non-Adjuster-fusion worlds where final fusion occurs between will creatures and a portion of the Third Source and Center. [236]

Supreme Adjusters

Supreme and self-acting Thought Adjusters can leave the human body at will, divine superimpositions not indispensable to mortal existence. [237] During planetary quarantine, communication between worlds is facilitated by the liaison of the Adjusters. [238]

Supreme Adjusters gain experience through serving in the adventure of time on evolutionary worlds, even when previous human partners decline eternal survival. [239]

Vanished Adjusters

Adjusted Monitors roam the universe on detached assignments or enjoy refreshing association with the Father. [240]

Liberated Adjusters

Thought Adjusters are eternally liberated from the service of time, with their destinies culminating in fusion with mortals, fusion by the Universal Father, or liberation from known assignments. [241]

Fused Adjusters

Fused Adjusters work uniformly across planetary types, systems, and universes, adapting their detailed tasks based on the nature of their assignments as liaison or fusion Adjusters. [242] Fused Adjusters have become one with ascending creatures, merging their divine nature with the mortal's eternal journey. [243]

Personalized Adjusters

Personalized Adjusters who have served with incarnated Paradise Sons or achieved distinction are classified as ascending Sons of God. [244]

Types of Adjusters, other classifications

Detached Adjusters

The Caligastia 100, unfused with their Thought Adjusters, were detached upon volunteering for planetary service. [245]

Liaison Adjusters

Most Adjusters gain valuable experience on loan planets, with many being highly experienced and having previously indwelt numerous mortals on other spheres. [246] Thought Adjusters are loaned for subjects' temporal lifetimes, providing temporary help in perfecting a survival character for eternal life. [247]

Self-acting Adjusters

Supreme and self-acting Adjusters possess a marked degree of will and participate in numerous activities within and outside the mortal subjects of attachment, communicating with each other for the purpose of preserving custodial data essential to their ministry. [248] During human sleep, Thought Adjusters can temporarily detach from mortal minds to perform extrahuman spiritual services. [249]

Higher and more experienced types of Adjusters can communicate with fellows in other realms for the purpose of preserving custodial data essential to their ministry. [250]

Fewer mortals function safely with self-acting Adjusters, prompting the need for measures to foster and conserve the higher spiritual types of the Urantia races. [251]

Self-acting Adjusters demonstrate a significant level of will in areas unrelated to the human personalities they inhabit, engaging in a variety of activities both within and outside of their mortal hosts. [252] A Thought Adjuster has served in a time of crisis to facilitate a cosmic achievement crucial to the planet's spiritual well-being. [253] In quarantine, interplanetary communication can be had by liaison of Adjusters on isolated worlds. [254] The Adjuster becomes supremely self-acting, progressing independently towards maturity along with you. [255]

Reservists with self-conscious Adjusters serve as pivotal individuals in planetary destiny alongside personal destiny guardians appointed by the planetary chief of seraphim. [256]

The subjects who have achieved the 3rd psychic circle, made the supreme decision, and are reservists, have acquired the balance of spiritual power and are betrothed to their Thought Adjusters. [257]
