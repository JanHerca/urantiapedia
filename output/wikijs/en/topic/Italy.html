Andites settled Italy and Spain then mingled in Switzerland near Carthage. [1] Jesus' trip to Rome influenced the religious leaders, instigated the spread of Christianity, and taught a lesson in mercy over self-defense. [2] The tribes in Italy dwelt in houses erected on piles over lakes, part of the expanding fringes of African, Aegean, and Danubian migrations. [3]
