The apostles and their disciples experienced emotional tension, leading Jesus to suggest 3-day vacations on Mount Sartaba for rest and relaxation. [1] Jesus enjoyed the annual Jewish vacation time of the feast of tabernacles with John. [2]

In the main continental nation of a nearby inhabited planet, vacation is a vital part of the adult-education program and is funded through methods similar to old-age insurance, with travel being a common way to spend the one month off each year. [3]
