Man is afraid of the unknown, leading him to crystallize science, formulate philosophy, and dogmatize truth due to his mental laziness in adjusting to the progressive struggles of living. [1] Paul initiated the Hellenization of Christianity by introducing the Athenians to the concept of “the Unknown God” at the Areopagus council in Athens. [2]
