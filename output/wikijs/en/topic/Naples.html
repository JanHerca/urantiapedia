In Naples, a beggar lacked the ability to respond to spirit leading. [1] Claudus preached in Rome, Naples, and Spain after being inspired by Jesus in Malta. [2] Jesus’ stop at Naples provided an opportunity for Ganid to learn about compassion and discernment in helping those in need. [3]
