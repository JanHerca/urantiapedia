Time is eternity, the absolute limit. [1] Time alone ripens fruit on tree, everything must await the coming of its time. [2]

Animals lack self-consciousness of time, unlike man who progresses inward to perceive time as a whole cycle, moving beyond the linear sequence of events towards circular simultaneity. [3] Faith in Jesus brings salvation from time, granting eternal life of unending progression in God-recognition and God-service. [4]

Consulting the Imports of Time enables the forecasting of future events with the same reliability as other indicators used by the authorities of the universes of time. [5]

Lucifer's futile attempt to short-circuit time threatened the freewill choice of all beings in Satania and stood as a monumental threat to steal personal liberty on an unprecedented scale. [6]

Days and years

On Havona

Every two billion Urantia years marks a complete expansion-contraction cycle within the master universe and horizontal space activities, while little is known about the pure-spirit abodes of Paradise satellites teeming with beings of the Eternal Son, being assembled for ministry in the new universes of outer space. [7] Havona day is equivalent to 1000 Urantia years, based on the length of time for the first Havona circuit to complete one revolution around Paradise. [8]

The Paradise-Havona standard day, equivalent to a watch in the night for God, is just seven minutes, three and one-eighth seconds less than one thousand years on Urantia. [9] Time on Uversa is standardized as 30 Urantia days, with a year equaling 100 days. [10]

On Nebadon

Time in Nebadon is calculated by special beings on Salvington, with a day equal to 18 Urantia days, 6 hours and 2.5 minutes, while a year is equivalent to 100 days of standard universe time or approximately 5 years on Urantia. [11] Time in Nebadon is meticulously calculated by a special group on Salvington, where a year is equivalent to 100 days or 5 years on Urantia. [12] Chronology in Nebadon is calculated and synchronized by beings on Salvington, where standard time is the measure for all local universe activities. [13]

On Jerusem

On Jerusem, a week consists of ten days, each slightly shorter than three days on Urantia. [14]

Constellations use Edentia time for all orders of celestial intelligences in their headquarters worlds, mirroring the universe capital's time reckoning and distance measurement. [15]

On Urantia

Early peoples based their time on the lunar month, with the seven-day week introduced later by Dalamatia teachers as one fourth of twenty-eight days, incorporating a spiritual element. [16] The 7-day week was created because 7 was 1/4 of 28, a time measurement based on the lunar month. [17] The Passover supper was eaten on Friday night, as Jews reckoned day beginning at sunset. [18] Old Testament confusion on months and years led to exaggerated reports of long human lives. [19]

The origin of the almost sacred sanction of the 7-day week was based on the tradition of Adam and Eve spending six days in their initial survey of the Garden. [20] The Urantia Papers use present-day calendar time for historical accuracy. [21]

Function of time

Time is the criterion by which self evaluates life, reflecting the direct relationship between maturity and unit of time consciousness within any given intellect. [22]

Time cushions premature escape from barriers to human action, slowing down cultural progress and distributing the potentially lethal results of overfast advancement. [23]

Time enables finite to coexist with Infinite, allowing for creature participation in divine creation and compensation for imperfection in the evolutionary process. [24] Time is both the greatest aid and most formidable obstacle to reality perception for finite creatures. [25]

Time permits growth for that which grows, bridging the gap between the constitutively perfect and the evolutionally perfected in eternity relationships. [26]

The time lag of mercy before fruition of evil and judgment ultimately allows for the triumph of higher level personality over lower level personality in any universe contest of reality. [27]

Nature of time

As man ascends, the enlarging view of time transitions from a linear sequence to a perfectly related cycle, where circular simultaneity displaces consciousness of events. [28]

Personality's unique time sense transcends both mind-perceived time and spirit-perceived time, combining insight into Reality with a consciousness of presence and an awareness of duration. [29] The coming into existence of time marks the genesis of reality for all creatures, with no actuality conceivable prior to the finite. [30] Time exists as a bestowal of Paradise, dependent on motion and the inherent awareness of sequentiality by the mind. [31] Deity's relation to the cosmos is experienced as an eternal now. [32]

The nonbeginning, nonending continuum of time reveals the co-ordinate relationship of past-present-future, allowing the self to gain insight into the wholeness of events. [33] Time, like space, is an indirect bestowal of Paradise, rooted in motion and the mind's understanding of sequentiality. [34]

In the grand scheme of the universe, time is relative: Though it may seem swift, the process of adjudicating Lucifer's crime spans an incomprehensible length compared to mortal actions on Urantia. [35]

The struggle in this universe age is between potential and actualization, as mortal man chooses to follow or reject the motions of time within the stream of eternity. [36] The Ultimacy of Deity transcends both time and space, making neither absolute nor infinite. [37] Time is nonexistent on Paradise, where all events are present at any moment in the mind of Infinity. [38] Time was nonexistent to Lazarus as he awoke in the garden, unaware of the four days that had passed since his death. [39] Time is not measured by space; rather, space is conditioned by time in seven different conceptions. [40] Achievement, not time, is the ultimate test on the Havona circles. [41] Time is but the transient scaffolding bridging over to the promised land of spiritual reality and supernal existence in relation to eternity. [42] Rest is negative utilization of time, while work is its positive employment; both are crucial on the mansion worlds. [43]

At the upper limits of the finite, where time conjoins transcended time, sequence blurs and blends, suggesting a forecasting of universe presence onto supertime levels by the Supreme. [44]

In the battle of reality's triumph, the sons of God are united against the partial shadows of existence, with the support of divine forces and the universe, transforming time into the shadow of eternity. [45]

Through the integrating insight of personality, Deity unifies time-space manifestations to the finite conception, perceiving time as a succession-arrangement whereby events are analyzed and segregated. [46] Time and space are inseparable in superuniverses, a vital factor in all cosmic realities. [47]

Wisely using time

It is always difficult to induce evolutionary minds suddenly to accept advanced revealed truth about Islam. [48]

Dedicating time to contemplation of eternal realities is essential for discovering God in all souls and creations of the universe, allowing the Father to appear as a God of supreme loyalties and divine ideals. [49] Time is essential to all human adjustments, physical, social, or economic, except moral and spiritual. [50] Time is fatally squandered when buried in neglect, hindering the journey of ascent for all will creatures. [51] Human identity is transient, existing in a fleeting moment of time as humans are born, live, and die. [52]

In morontia, time is no longer available to avoid unpleasant responsibilities, fostering the eradication of procrastination and insincerity in mortal survivors. [53] Life on Urantia is a short and intense test; pass through it well. [54] Maturity is directly correlated to the unit of time consciousness, shaping the way the self evaluates past experience and plans for the future. [55] Time is the one universal endowment, crucial for ensuring the survival of all intelligent beings. [56]

Independence of time

Jesus' miracles abridged time while his Creator prerogatives remain independent of it. [57] Spirit, mind, and matter gravity responses are independent of time and space, unlike the non-related force circuits of the universe. [58]

The Personalized Adjuster reminds Jesus of the non-time nature of their function in accepting the command of the universe hosts in attendance upon Christ Michael. [59] The extra-Paradise reflectivity service operates independently of time and space within the constraints of the seven superuniverses. [60]
