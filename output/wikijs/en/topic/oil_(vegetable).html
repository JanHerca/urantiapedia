Early medicine for treating wounds, including oil and wine, was passed down from the Greeks and Egyptians, who acquired their medical knowledge from the Euphrates valley. [1] The Good Samaritan poured oil onto wounds, showing mercy and compassion. [2] Jesus' apostles would not agree to anoint with certain forms of oil, despite the push from John's followers. [3]
