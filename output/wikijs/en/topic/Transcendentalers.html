Finaliters on Paradise strive to understand over one thousand groups of transcendental supercitizens, assisted by transcendental ministers, residing exclusively in the west of Paradise and playing a vital role in the superadministration of the master universe. [1]

Absoniters, also known as Transcendentalers, exist beyond time and space, without beginnings or endings, eventuated rather than created, embodying the Ultimacy of Deity in transcendent acts throughout the master universe. [2]

The perfected mortal finaliters on Paradise strive to comprehend the nature and function of the transcendental supercitizens, aided by transcendental ministers, in the restricted area where the Transcendentalers exclusively reside. [3]

Transcendentalers are solely focused on superadministering the master universe, beyond mortal ascent to Havona or the affairs of Havona and the superuniverses. [4] Transcendentalers are not involved in the superuniverses or Havona. [5]

In Paradise classification, eventuated transcendental beings are loyal to the Paradise Trinity and obedient to the Ultimate, existing on four ultimate levels of personality activity and functioning on the seven levels of the absonite in twelve grand divisions. [6]

In the next universe age, Transcendentalers may replace the finaliters as society's initiators of new supermaterial activities, possibly stepping in for the latter during the seventh epoch of evolutionary stability for outer space ministry. [7]

Transcendentalers, ever loyal and eventuated, are neither creators nor creatures, existing on an absonite level beyond beginnings or endings, transcending time and space, and obedient to the Ultimate. [8]

Upon reaching Paradise, mortal ascenders fraternize with upwards of three thousand different orders of Paradise Citizens, as well as with various groups of Transcendentalers and other unrevealed inhabitants. [9]

The eventuation of transcendental beings on Paradise reveals their absonite nature as neither finite nor infinite, loyal to the Paradise Trinity, and functioning across various levels of personality activity and the absonite. [10] Solitary Messengers and Inspired Trinity Spirits maintain an organic relationship with Transcendentalers and the Master Architects. [11]

The senior Architect of Master Universe, chief of Transcendentalers, administers the Trinity oath to mortal finaliters, marking their first serious contact with the governing corps of Paradise Transcendentalers. [12]

You can conceive of a Creator as a creature, yet struggle to comprehend the existence of the Transcendentalers, who create no beings, were never created, and are subject to God the Ultimate. [13]

Orders

The orders of Transcendentalers embody loyalty to the Paradise Trinity and obedience to the Ultimate on four levels of personality activity and are functional on seven levels of the absonite through twelve grand divisions. [14] The absonite Architects of the Master Universe, those unique universe planners who are neither creators nor creatures. [15]

Associate Transcendental Master Force Organizers are extraordinary beings who transmute primordial force into energy under the supervision of the Architects of the Master Universe, residing on Paradise and working throughout unorganized space. [16]

Primary Eventuated Master Force Organizers are extraordinary beings resident on Paradise but operate throughout the master universe, manipulating primordial forces for the Architects of the Master Universe. [17] Transcendental Recorders are personal gravity traversers, unlike local universe administrators. [18]
