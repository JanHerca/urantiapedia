Angels, highly individualized beings, are the ministering-spirit associates of evolving will creatures and divine personalities, possessing a social nature beyond that of humans. [1]

The chiefs of assignment, designated by the chief supernaphim, preside over the organization of all orders of angels, who are self-governing except for the first angel of Paradise. [2] Supernaphim are custodians of creature identity in terminal transition sleep. [3] The supernaphim in personality registers. [4]

Supernaphim are limited in spirit polarity when utilizing the exclusive circuits of the Father, requiring them to work in pairs for power reception in the Father's direct ministry. [5] The original pattern angel, chief of supernaphim, designates a group to preside over all orders of these self-regulating spirit personalities. [6] The self-governing supernaphim are designated by their chief to organize all three orders of angels. [7]

Orders

A. primary supernaphim

PRIMARY supernaphim, the skilled ministers on Paradise, are perfect beings who serve the Deities with unwavering light and righteousness, participating in various duties, especially in worship, philosophy, knowledge, conduct, ethics, assignment, and rest. [8]

Supernaphim, angels of Paradise, serve as the ministering spirits of the central universe, contributing to the essential training and mutual understanding of Paradise Citizens and ascendant pilgrims. [9]

Primary supernaphim command seraphic hosts on rebellion-isolated worlds, including on Urantia where the current “chief of seraphim” is the second to serve since the bestowal of Christ Michael. [10]

The primary and secondary supernaphim are the general custodians of the creature-trinitized sons, preparing them for future work and a common destiny in the Paradise Corps. [11]

The primary supernaphim have existed since eternity, serving on the Isle of Light and leading missions to the worlds of space, now classified only since the arrival of the Havona pilgrims on Paradise, ministering in seven orders of service. [12]

Primary supernaphim, offspring of Infinite Spirit, serve as supernal servants alongside their Infinite Mother Spirit in creating the vast creature family of the Third Source and Center. [13]

Paradise arrivals associate with seven groups of primary supernaphim before becoming finaliters on observational and co-operative service to the far-flung creation. [14] PRIMARY supernaphim are perfect beings, serving the Deities on Paradise, neither absonite nor absolute, but supreme in perfection. [15]

The planetary chief of seraphim on Urantia, a primary supernaphim of Paradise, presides over the council of seraphic chiefs and serves as the successor to the former chief who defaulted during the Caligastia secession. [16] Primary supernaphim on Paradise, supreme in perfection, work interchangeably as messengers of the Deities. [17]

Types

The primary supernaphim have served on the Isle of Light and gone forth on missions of leadership to the worlds of space, functioning as now classified only since the arrival on Paradise of the Havona pilgrims of time, ministering chiefly in seven orders of service. [18]

  1. Conductors of worship.
  2. Custodians of knowledge.
  3. Directors of conduct.
  4. Interpreters of ethics.
  5. Chiefs of assignment.
  6. Instigators of rest.

B. secondary supernaphim

The secondary supernaphim are devoted ministers to the seven planetary circuits of the central universe, assisting pilgrims of time and eternity in their journey towards Paradise perfection. [19]

The angels of Havona, known as supernaphim, are the ministering spirits of the central universe, created by different entities within the divine hierarchy to serve in various capacities within the Paradise-Havona system. [20]

The primary and secondary supernaphim are the general custodians of the creature-trinitized sons, preparing them on the inner Havona circuit for future work with high Paradise Citizens. [21] Secondary supernaphim minister to ascenders and Paradise Citizens in Havona. [22]

Secondary supernaphim were not created until the arrival of Grandfanda in Havona, with tertiary supernaphim originating from the Seven Spirits of the Circuits receiving first consideration. [23]

The vast creature family of the Third Source and Center is created by the Supreme Spirit groups, including the primary, secondary, and tertiary supernaphim offspring of the Infinite Spirit, Master Spirits, and Seven Spirits of the Circuits. [24]

Types

Supernaphim types work in seven groups based on their ministry to ascending pilgrims. [25]

  1. Pilgrim helpers.
  2. Supremacy guides.
  3. Trinity guides.
  4. Son finders.
  5. Father guides.
  6. Counselors and advisors.
  7. Complements of rest.

C. tertiary supernaphim

Tertiary supernaphim serve as Harmony Supervisors, Chief Recorders, Broadcasters, Messengers, Intelligence Co-ordinators, Transport Personalities, and in the Reserve Corps, ensuring smooth operation and mutual understanding in the Paradise-Havona system. [26]

The creation of the tertiary supernaphim in response to mortal ascenders on Havona circuits is indicative of God the Supreme's modifications to the central universe. [27]

Certain senior chief recorders among the tertiary supernaphim in Havona are chosen as Custodians of Records, keepers of the formal archives on the Isle of Light. [28]

The tertiary supernaphim, offspring of the Seven Spirits of Havona Circuits, fulfill the needs of the central universe as empowered by the Infinite Spirit. [29]

The supernaphim, servants of the Seven Master Spirits, minister to both ascending and descending pilgrims on the study worlds of Havona, fostering fraternal relationships and mutual understanding among all beings. [30] Summon the successful destiny guardians to Seraphington. [31]

Supernaphim transporters operate on Havona circuits, landing on the peripheral surface of Paradise, where personality transports destined for Paradise must also land. [32]

Types

  1. Harmony supervisors.
  2. Chief recorders.
  3. Broadcasters.
  4. Messengers.
  5. Intelligence co-ordinators.
  6. Transport personalities. [33]
  7. Reserve corps. [34]

Service of supernaphim

The unknown seraphim from Nebadon, accompanied by a solitary supernaphim and Gabriel of Salvington, arrived unannounced and was quickly assigned to the teaching counselors. [35]

Evangels of Light, including supernaphim, serve temporarily with finaliter companies for transient assignments, resuming their former status upon conclusion. [36] Supernaphim serve as Most High Assistants, aiding us in aligning Nebadon with Orvonton's ideas and Paradise's ideals. [37]

The Supernaphim, as Paradise Companions, are the primary supernal servants of the Deities on the eternal Isle of Paradise, working interchangeably and at will in all phases of their manifold duties, and ascending in large numbers to become Technical Advisers. [38]

Throughout grand universe, SUPERNAPHIM serve as ministering spirits from Paradise to the worlds of time and space, encompassing every part of organized and inhabited creation. [39]
