Triata is threefold organizations of energy-matter responding to linear gravity in Havona, the superpower mother systems of time and space creations. [1]

The triune existential energy of Havona ensures perfect balance and harmony within the immense worlds of triata physical constitution, utilizing antigravity to organize both material functions and spiritual activities. [2]
