The lava flows diversifying the earth's crust insulated the planet from space-energies, facilitating control of terrestrial energy and regulating its flow through the functioning of Earth's magnetic poles. [1]

Magnetism, along with light, heat, electricity, chemism, energy, and matter, are fundamentally interconnected and ultimately trace back to the same source, evolving and transforming through endless cycles in the cosmos. [2]

Sunspots, functioning as enormous magnets, have the power to alter light frequencies and hurl charged particles into the earth's outer atmosphere, resulting in spectacular auroral displays and even affecting the direction of a compass needle. [3]
