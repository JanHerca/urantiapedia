Broadcasters, inside superior seraphim, are a specialized subdivision of the seraphic recorders, being concerned with the dispatch of records and with the dissemination of essential information. They can simultaneously transmit 144,000 messages. [1]

Maintain reciprocal contact with both the intelligence co-ordinators of the tertiary supernaphim and the glorified intelligence co-ordinators of the Seraphic Corps of Completion. [2] They use superaphic ideographic techniques. [3]
