Jerusalem temple was called by Jesus “his Father’s house.”. [1]

Paradise is sometimes called “the Father’s House” since it is his eternal residence, and these seven zones are often designated “the Father’s Paradise mansions. [2]

Jesus said that each of the many, many stations in his Father’s house, that is, the universe, is a stopping place, a life designed to prepare us for the next one ahead. [3]
