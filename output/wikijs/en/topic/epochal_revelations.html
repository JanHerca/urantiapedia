Only two influences can modify and uplift the dogmas of natural religion: the pressure of the slowly advancing mores and the periodic illumination of epochal revelation. [1] There have been many events of religious revelation but only five of epochal significance. These were as follows: [2]

  1. The first was Dalamatia teachings, performed by the 100 corporeal members of Prince Caligastia’s staff. [3]
  2. The second was Edenic teachings, by Adam and Eve and their descendants. [4]
  3. The third was the one from Melchizedek of Salem. [5]
  4. The fourth was the one from Jesus of Nazareth. [6]
  5. The fifth is Urantia Papers. [7]

Will it be the sixth in 1000 years? Not known, although some hints in the Urantia revelations seem to suggest it. [8]
