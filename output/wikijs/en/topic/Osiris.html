Osiris, son of Isis in Egyptian mystery cult, was not recognized by Jeremiah as a deity compared to Yahweh, God of all nations and peoples. [1] Isis was the consort of Osiris, miraculously resurrected from a cruel death at the hands of Set. [2] Departed souls passed through the judgment hall of Osiris to be weighed in the balances for admission to the realms of bliss or consignment to hell. [3]
