Militarism: autocratic and cruel, promotes social organization among conquerors, disintegrates the vanquished. [1] The struggle between nationalistic militarism and industrialism on Urantia mirrors the historic conflict between the herder-hunter and the farmer. [2] A nation's level of militarism directly impacts a woman's social position in society. [3]
