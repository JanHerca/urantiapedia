Mary saw the family funds diminishing before Miriam sister of Jesus sold milk. [1] The magical milk of a black cow was revered, like all ancient objects, in a civilization wary of new practices and mediums. [2] The use of cow's and goat's milk reduced infant mortality, leading to earlier weaning of babies and the bearing of more children by relieved mothers. [3]
