Trinities are always deity reality, never personality reality, encompassing and correlating personal aspects within a collective sense. [1] Experiential Trinities:

  • 1st.
  • 2nd.

The Universal I AM is the primal cause and unqualified source of both the Trinity and the Absolute, culminating in the personalization of the Deity Absolute. [2] The ultimate Trinities include creature personalities to achieve true infinity. [3]

Trinities are truths of relationship and facts of co-ordinate Deity manifestation, encompassing Deity realities seeking realization and personalization, culminating in God the Supreme, God the Ultimate, and God the Absolute as divine inevitabilities. [4]
