Avoid loss of time through trivialities and focus wholeheartedly on preaching the gospel. [1] Jesus wasted little time on trifles, focusing instead on the essentials of his work as a painstaking craftsman. [2]

Many of you focus on trivialities, neglecting the essential realities of attuning to your divine Adjuster and achieving an understanding consecration to eternal aims. [3]
