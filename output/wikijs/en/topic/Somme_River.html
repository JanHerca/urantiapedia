The Cro-Magnoids resisted white invaders along the Somme River, where decisive struggles between the blue man and the white man determined the fate of their territories. [1] The evidence of Andonites along the Somme River reflects the ancient river dwellers of France. [2]

The Somme River flows today in the same preglacial channel as it did tens of thousands of years ago, where evidence of the Andonic tribes can still be found. [3]
