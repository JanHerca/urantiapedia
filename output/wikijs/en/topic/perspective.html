Acquire a long-distance view of destiny to lighten burdens of the soul and find peace in your humanity. [1]

The sons of God are united in the battle for reality's triumph, with consciousness that God and divine hosts support them in the eternal struggle for life and divinity. [2] Understanding God's connection to the cosmos aids in humanity's cosmic alignment. [3]

The danger of losing one's sense of proportion in the exaltation of one's own mind over the will of the Supreme Rulers is a constant risk for those in positions of authority on newly inhabited worlds. [4]

Each generation smiles at their ancestors' foolish superstitions while continuing to entertain fallacies that will be mocked by future enlightened generations. [5] Gain true perspective from studying origin, history, and destiny of situation, not from the simple to the complex or from historic approach alone. [6]

Sons of God, united in the battle for reality's triumph over partial shadows, now gaze upon the universe from within, from God's viewpoint, transforming uncertainty into eternal spiritual progression. [7] Enhance insight by aligning socialization techniques with Paradise-ascension career progression to uncover eternal meanings in ordinary activities. [8] The laws of God, though often mistaken as arbitrary, are merely His repeated habits done perfectly, and discernible as time frame increases. [9]

The length of an individual's life serves as their yardstick for measuring time, influencing their perspective on the duration of evolution in comparison to those on Paradise where time is nonexistent. [10] Insight into the wholeness of past-present-future events reveals the panoramic perspective of time's eternal continuum. [11] Only one thing is really worth while, the lesser matters of life should give way before the greater things of the heavenly kingdom. [12] All intellects, whether partial, incomplete, or evolving, rely on erroneous postulates to form rational thought within the frame of their universe. [13] Human dignity and nobility through defending family from danger. [14] In social reconstruction, a cosmic perspective provides the stabilizing loyalty and steadying goal needed by mortal man. [15]

A one-eyed person, and likewise single-eyed material scientists and spiritual mystics, cannot fully grasp the depth of universe reality or the true values of creature experience. [16]

The present is exasperatingly trivial when divorced from the past and future, yet it is through mutual affection and association that we find enhanced defense against all evil. [17] Religion offers a stabilizing loyalty and far-reaching perspective above temporal goals in social reconstruction. [18] Rest and relaxation shrink the size of problems, a valuable lesson taught by Jesus during a memorable mountain retreat. [19] Perspective reveals evil against the backdrop of ultimate good, highlighting the beauties of spiritual truth over the phenomenon of evil. [20]

All levels of creation are unified in the divine oneness overspreading universal phenomena, revealing the single purpose of the Architects of the Master Universe. [21] Values and facts must remain in proper perspective in religion, avoiding disproportionate extremes to grasp the central truth of Jesus' teachings. [22]

The vastness of God’s plan makes it impossible to see much in one life, but all of our mortal struggling is part of an immense and eternal project overseen by the First Great Source and Center. [23] Look beyond mortal uncertainty to the eternal righteousness shining through the clouds of opportunity. [24]
