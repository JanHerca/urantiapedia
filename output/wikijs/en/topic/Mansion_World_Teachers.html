Billions in Satania. [1] Commissioned by Melchizedeks; supervised by Morontia Companions. [2] Deserted cherubim and sanobim embraced by Mother Spirit. [3] Headquarters is 3rd mansion world. [4] In group Universe Aids. [5] In Uversa personality register. [6] Many went astray in Lucifer rebellion. [7] May be re-embraced by Mother Spirit and emerge as seraphim. [8] May return to former associative work with seraphim. [9] Train children in probationary nursery. [10] Usually work in pairs. [11] Very near morontia level of existence. [12]
