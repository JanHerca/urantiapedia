Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle believed that virtue is knowledge and it is better to suffer injustice than to be guilty of it. [1]

Socrates' ideals dominated the Western world through the fusion of Melchizedek teachings brought from Egypt, resulting in the Christian church's religious philosophy based on Jesus. [2]
