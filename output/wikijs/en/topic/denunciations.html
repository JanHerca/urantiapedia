Jesus denounced scribes, Pharisees, and hypocrites. [1] Jesus spent little time in negative denunciations. Jesus’ antagonism to the Jewish traditions and slavish ceremonials was always positive. [2] John the Baptist denounced Herod Antipas and the religious leaders. [3]
