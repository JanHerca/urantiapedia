When a Michael Son is ready to embark on the adventure of universe organization, 1000 tertiaphim accompany him and retire with the appearance of native angels. [1] Michael, accompanied by a lone tertiaphim, departed his headquarters sphere and a new Lanonandek Son was appointed as acting System Sovereign. [2] The tertiaphim, as children of the Infinite Spirit, serve in liaison between Creator Sons and the Ancients of Days. [3] Tertiaphim are dedicated to facilitating liaisons between Creator Sons and Ancients of Days. [4] The Tertiaphim may become Technical Advisers. [5] The tertiaphim retire from active service in the local universe when native angels appear. [6] Tertiaphim serve as Most High Assistants in the ministry of Paradise-origin beings. [7] Tertiaphim's appear registered in personality registers. [8]
