Creator Sons and local universe Mother Spirits work in a complemental way in universe creation and administration. [1]

The experiential nature of the evolutionary type of personality is the natural cosmic complement of the ever-perfect natures of the Paradise-Havona creatures. [2]

Complemental relationships of angles may be transient; they are not necessarily of a permanent nature. Seraphim can work singly as discrete and localized personalities, but they are able to encircuit only when polarized as liaison pairs. [3]
