God not willing that any perish. [1] Father takes from vine branches not bearing fruit. [2] Mortal mind subservient to matter is destined to become increasingly material and consequently to suffer eventual personality extinction. [3] The wages of sin is death—eternal obliteration. [4] What mercy cannot rehabilitate, justice annihilates it. [5]

Process

Planetary council first approves petition for extinction. [6]

Mandates of judgment originate in the local universes, but sentences involving the extinction of will creatures are always formulated on, and executed from, the headquarters of the superuniverse. [7] Extincition may not transpire until all moral values are extinct in evildoer and all sympathizers. [8] Cessation of existence is usually decreed at the dispensational or epochal adjudication of the realm or realms. [9] Magisterial Sons can render judgments of extinction, but do not execute them. [10] Solitary Messengers function as executioners. [11]

Ancients of Days

Ancients of Days alone may decree extinction of a creature. [12] The act is carried out by executioners of Ancients of Days. [13] Ancients of Days of Orvonton mandated new rebels’ instantaneous extinction during Michael’s bestowal. [14] They seldom execute wrongdoers without full hearing. [15]

Local universe courts may not decree extinction. All sentences of extinction are carried out upon the orders, and through the agencies, of the rulers of the supergovernment. [16]

Described as

Extinction is the automatic result of conscious and wholehearted embrace of evil. [17]

The history records of every mortal in his local universe ends with his departure from Salvington being recorded, is “blotted out of recorded existence” by the mandate of the Ancients of Days. [18]

The mandate of dissolution originates in the higher courts of the superuniverse following an unbroken confirmation of the indictment originating on the sphere of the wrongdoer’s residence; and then, when sentence of extinction has been confirmed on high, the execution is by the direct act of those judges residential on, and operating from, the headquarters of the superuniverse. [19]

The verdict that will effect the annihilation of the rebellious interned followers of Lucifer will be announced by means of the Uversa executionary broadcast. [20] There is no resurrection from extinction; it is everlasting and eternal. [21] Sin-identified individuals have destroyed themselves by becoming wholly unreal through their embrace of iniquity. [22]
