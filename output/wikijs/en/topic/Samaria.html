In the time of the prophets

The gangster-nobility of Samaria ruled with tyranny as prophets began to oppose their suppression of freedom of speech, sparking the creation of the Jewish and Christian Bibles. [1]

In the time of Jesus

Pontius Pilate was procurator of Samaria when Jesus was brought before him for trial. [2]

Jesus' 17th appearance at Sychar was a powerful and transformative experience for Nalda and the Samaritan believers, leading them to spread his message of love and salvation to all. [3] Revival in Samaria brought many souls to the kingdom, preparing the way for future works of faith. [4]
