Historically

The doctrine of immortality took form at Babylon, shifting focus from social justice to theology for the Jews. [18]

Lucifer contended immortality was inherent, asserting that the executioners of the Ancients of Days could be debarred if beings would assert their independence, and that all would live eternally except for unjust acts. [19]

Onagar's concept of the Great Beyond originated in the settlement of Oban on the northern shores of the ancient Mediterranean, where he sent out teachers to spread his new doctrines of one Deity and his vision of the afterlife. [20]

The belief in reincarnation among ancient civilizations stemmed from their observations of hereditary traits and resemblance, rather than a concept of immortality. [21]

Early man believed in a next life resembling their current one without misfortune, but as the concept of heaven and hell emerged, many primitive races feared growing old and frail in the afterlife. [22]

Primitive beliefs in survival after mortal death were influenced by a desire to accumulate wealth for the next life, leading to the custom of burying personal belongings; however, the teachings of Christianity emphasized salvation for all, regardless of wealth. [23]

Despite their vague Andonite ideas about survival after death, Sontad and his wife, assisted by his eldest sister, immediately assumed leadership of the clan and buried their dead family members upon the death of his parents, despite Sontad's injured foot. [24]

Adjudication of survival

All beings will have one undoubted chance to make a final, self-conscious choice, ensuring that no soul is deprived of the opportunity to reveal its true intent and purpose. [25] Creator Sons may decree survival, but only the Ancients of Days hold executive judgment on eternal life and death. [26]

In doubt, universe governments invariably rule in the personal interests of individuals, ensuring divine justice and mercy for all souls on their ascending career towards eternal joy. [27] Justice Guides defend cases of doubtful mortal survival in the tribunals of the universe with a perfect understanding of every detail. [28] Magisterial Sons render judgment on survival, decreeing the fate of evolutionary races but leaving execution to superuniverse authorities. [29] Each being is given one ultimate chance to make a definitive choice, with no possibility of a second opportunity if it is rejected. [30]

Graduate Guides ensure the success of the all-perfected plan for our survival and ascension. Survival of the evolving immortal soul is dependent on spiritualization by the ministry of the Adjuster, as only conscious resistance to the divine leading can prevent it. [37]

Survival of the immortal soul is assured when the mortal mind believes, knows, and desires God, regardless of intellectual, educational, or social limitations. [38] The lavish mercy credits established by the Sons of God ensure the survival of all who desire divine citizenship. [39]

How is attained

Acceptance of sonship is the key to evolutionary survival and spiritual progression. [40]

The great struggle of this universe age is between seeking actualization and potential; mortal man can either accept participation in the adventure of eternity or move counter to the stream of events in the finite universes. [44] Salvation and survival of mortal death is attained by faith alone in the teachings of Jesus. [45] All true personalities have the sovereign choice to embrace the eternal adventure through their freewill choice. [46]

Survival of mortal death is dependent on being first born of the eternal spirit, possessing eternal life by faith, and bearing the fruits of the spirit in loving service to all creatures. [47]

The joint effort by Adjuster and human leads to the evolution of a candidate for further growth and development in the Supreme, ensuring a faithful and efficient ascent towards the actual presence of the Father on Paradise. [48]

The universe potential of eternal personality arises from the living relationship of personal mind and spirit, centered around the divine spirit nucleus of mortal man. [49] Loving God with all our heart and our neighbor as ourselves is the key to eternal life. [50] The desire of the mortal mind for eternal existence ensures survival and spiritual growth in the universe. [51] Survival in the universe requires cooperation with the divine will and a willingness to learn from mistakes. [52]

The survival of mortal man depends on the immortality of the soul, as the real spiritual values persist in the continuing experience of the Thought Adjuster. [53]

Survival of mortal death is achieved through self-identification with indwelling spirit by choosing to do the Father's will, allowing for eternal union with God. [54]

Survival beyond mortal death is achieved through the sincerity and steadfastness of God-seeking decisions, transferring the seat of identity from the material body to the immortal nature of the morontia soul to eventually attain spirit reality. [55]

The key to surviving mortal death lies in wholehearted spiritual motivation, unreserved consecration to the Father's will, and the supreme desire to be Godlike, willing to do and be anything essential for attaining that ultimate goal. [56] About surrender to will of God see link to submission.

Survival of mortal death is achieved by transferring the seat of identity from material intellect to soul through the evolution of the human mind into spirit union. [57]

Rejecting survival

Not all accept the opportunity for the Paradise career despite possessing freewill choice. [58] Choose the righteous realities of eternity over sinful pleasures of time to find true deliverance and progress in the new life of faith and hope. [59]

Those with unfit ancestors may be disqualified for survival, but they can still benefit from serving as a conduit for evolutionary minds, preparing them for greater assignments. [60]

Humans fully occupied with material problems will perish unless they make wholehearted moral decisions and unqualified spiritual choices that lead them to become increasingly identified with the divine spirit, ultimately achieving survival and eternity of personality existence. [61] To ensure survival beyond mortal death, one must exhibit continuous spiritual growth and fruit-bearing, not solely rely on initial sonship. [62]
