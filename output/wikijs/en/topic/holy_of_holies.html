Only high priests and Nazarites, like John, were permitted to enter the holy of holies in the Jerusalem temple, revered with the same respect and veneration as the sanctified Nazarites. [1]

Young Jesus, after wondering what lay behind the veil of separation, spent most of his time at teaching conferences in the temple, seeking answers about the holy of holies. [2]
