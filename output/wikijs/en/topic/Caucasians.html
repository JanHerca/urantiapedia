It is Andite inheritance that gives to the polyglot mixture of the so-called white races that generalized homogeneity which has been called Caucasoid. [1]

The Caucasoid race is the Andite blend of the Nodite and Adamic stocks, further modified by primary and some secondary Sangik admixture and by considerable Andonic crossing. The Occidental white races, together with some Indian and Turanian peoples, are included in this group. [2]
