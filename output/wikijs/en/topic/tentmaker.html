Jesus worked as a tentmaker in Antioch, unknowingly preparing the way for the coming of Peter, Paul, and other Christian teachers in Rome. [1]

Paul was a devotee of the cult of renunciation and humiliation, influencing Christianity with teachings that looked down upon women and could lead to the end of the human race if universally obeyed. [2]
