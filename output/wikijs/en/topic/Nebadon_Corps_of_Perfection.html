Spirit-fused ascenders who attain the Nebadon Corps of Perfection may become Universe Aids and interpret the viewpoints of evolving creatures to celestial authorities. [1]

Members of the Nebadon Corps of Perfection acquire authoritative wisdom about Nebadon from witnessing the ascending processional of planetary ages on ten million inhabited worlds. [2]
