Seeking God with a whole heart will lead to deliverance from captivity and new opportunities for a better life. [1] The government's prime mission is to define rights, regulate class differences, and enforce equality of opportunity under the rules of law. [2]

Hasten off to where great things wait to be done, for the big and real things of this world are on your side, and your potential for achievement lies within you. [3] Only untrustworthiness stands between us and the sanctity of service. [4]

Society should allow each individual the opportunity for self-realization and self-respect, ultimately leading to a culturally rich and evolutionarily advanced society with minimal government intervention. [5] From the death of fondest hopes arise new opportunities for growth and blessings, as noted by angels who have experienced disappointments themselves. [6]

Fusion with a fragment of the Universal Father opens the career of universal service to Adjuster-fused mortals, leading to ultimate glory and divine validation of eventual Paradise attainment. [7]
