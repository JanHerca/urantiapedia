Religious experience unfailingly yields the “fruits of the spirit” in the daily life of the spirit-led mortal. [1]

The convincing evidence of this spiritual certainty consists in the social fruits of the spirit which such believers, faithers, yield as a result of this genuine spiritual experience. [2] The Father requires of us only that we shall bear much fruit. [3] God’s will become increasingly discernible in the spirit fruits which are borne in the lives of the spirit-led children of God. [4] We must bring forth the fruits of the Father’s spirit if we would continue to ascend in the progressive life of the divine fellowship. [5]

Defined as

The fruits of the spirit are the essence of the highest type of enjoyable and ennobling self-control, even the heights of terrestrial mortal attainment—true self-mastery. [6] Fruits of the spirit are life reactions of spirit-led mortal. [7] Fruits of the spirit are simply traits which Jesus manifested in his own earthly lif. [8] The fruits of the spirit are the substance of the Supreme as he is realizable in human experience. [9]

The continuous bearing of spiritual fruit consists in yielding the ministry to one’s fellows of that which has been received from one’s spiritual benefactors. [10]

Enumerated

Intellectual peace, social progress, moral satisfaction, spiritual joy, and cosmic wisdom. [11] Love, joy, peace, long-suffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, meekness, temperance. [12]

Loving service, unselfish devotion, courageous loyalty, sincere fairness, enlightened honesty, undying hope, confiding trust, merciful ministry, unfailing goodness, forgiving tolerance, and enduring peace. [13]
