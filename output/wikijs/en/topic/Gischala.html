Place near the Sea of Galilee. The apostles preached there on their first preaching tour of the lake between June and July 26. [1] It is one of the towns visited during the first preaching tour of Galilee between January and March 28. [2] Jesus and the apostles passed by on their return from Phoenicia. [3]
