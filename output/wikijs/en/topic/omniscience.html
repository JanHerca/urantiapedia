In the grand universe, Creator will and creature will operate within established limits and possibilities, without abridging sovereignty or invalidating authenticity, suggesting foreknowledge of those who may elect to contribute to reality. [1] Supreme directors of the grand universe maintain sacred secrecy to honor the privacy and sanctity of all beings, whether lowly or high. [2]

The Solitary Messengers investigate undiscovered energy systems of space, knowing of them prior to experience but never revealing such information to others. [3]

God's foreknowledge of sin does not diminish human freedom, yet we are unsure if He chooses to foreknow it; however, what is definite is that God is never caught off guard. [4] God's foreknowledge of sin does not limit free will, guaranteeing God's unwavering omniscience. [5]

God's all-encompassing knowledge is derived from Adjusters, personality circuits, divine Sons, and the Infinite Spirit, making his consciousness universal and his power to know all things unlimited. [6]

God's omniscience surpasses our comprehension, but we can grasp the avenue through which the Universal Father intimately knows all beings in all creation. [7] God's preknowledge is existential, as the infinite Deities are compensated for lack of experience, knowing all even before they experience all. [8] God's understanding, infinite and unsearchable, remains a mystery to his lowly creatures. [9] Jesus self-limited his divinity consciousness to interact with humanity in a way they could comprehend and accept. [10] The Holy Spirit on your world knows mortal man fully, reflecting with the divine Spirit in making knowledge immediately available. [11] Reflectivity is omniscience within the experiential finite, potentially connecting us to the consciousness of the Supreme Being. [12] The Seven Master Spirits collectively disclose the attributes of omnipotence, omniscience, and omnipresence. [13] Foreknowledge and omniscience do not diminish the freedom of choice within established limits by the Master Architects. [14] Transcendental reality encompasses omnipotence, omniscience, and omnipresence. [15]
