During a violent storm, Andon and Fonta decided to flee their home treetops. [1]

Advanced races on nonbreather worlds must protect themselves from meteor damage with electrical installations, but are also subject to disastrous electrical storms unlike those on Urantia. [2]

The crustal cooling era was marked by frequent and violent storms, as the earth's original planetary crust was continually mixed with lava flows and ocean deposits. [3] Jesus' fall down the stairs during an unexpected sandstorm in July led to the construction of a balustrade by Joseph to prevent further accidents. [4] There are thousands of small lakes on Jerusem, but no storms. [5] On morontia worlds like Edentia, there are no storms or seasonal variations, allowing for beautiful outdoor embellishments. [6] Jesus calmed a violent storm on the Sea of Galilee that left his apostles afraid, showcasing his power over nature. [7] During the sandstorm at Jesus' crucifixion, only a small group of devoted followers remained at the foot of the cross. [8] In a year full of events, Jesus experienced a rare two-foot snowstorm in Galilee, the deepest of his lifetime. [9]
