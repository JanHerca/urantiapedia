The critical centrifugal breakup stage marked the beginning of the great breakup in Andronover ages ago. [1] Some nebulae can produce up to one hundred million suns, varying greatly in size and progeny. [2]

Observation of star clouds, spiral forms, and concentric contraction rings in the grand universe of Orvonton reveals the vast and intricate structure of the superuniverse. [3]

Paradise force organizers are responsible for initiating the spiral and other nebulae, the mother wheels of the direct-origin suns and their varied systems in outer space. [4] The life cycle of nebulae begins as circular and gaseous entities, evolving into spiral forms before giving birth to suns and planetary systems. [5] The origin of Urantia is in the force-charge of space in the superuniverse of Orvonton. [6]

The power centers and physical controllers direct the evolution of spiral and other nebulae in the spheres of space, initiated by Paradise force organizers. [7] Superuniverse boundaries never divide a nebular family or cross a local universe in the organized post-Havona creation. [8]
