Beauty is always triumphant over ugliness in the hearts of all who are illuminated by the love of truth. [1] Beauty aroused the envy of spirits; it betokened sinful human pride. [2]

We can consciously augment Adjuster harmony by sincerely basing the human life on the highest consciousness of truth, beauty, and goodness, and then co-ordinating these qualities of divinity through wisdom, worship, faith, and love. [3] The concepts of truth, beauty, and goodness are not inherent in either physics or chemistry. [4]

The discernment of supreme beauty is the discovery and integration of reality: The discernment of the divine goodness in the eternal truth, that is ultimate beauty. [5] Jesus impressed upon his apostles the great value of beauty as an influence leading to the urge to worship, especially with children. [6]

The understandings of truth, beauty, and goodness, morality, ethics, duty, love, divinity, origin, existence, purpose, destiny, time, space, even Deity, are only relatively true. [7] Beauty is most religious when it is most simple and naturelike. [8] The mortal attempt to depict the human recognition of divine beauty can never be truly satisfying if ununified. [9] Pleasing surroundings contribute to happiness. [10] Service of beauty is not a substitute for genuine religious experience, spiritual reality. [11] The sincere pursuit of goodness, beauty, and truth leads to God. [12] Truth, fact, and relationship are intellectually inseparable and associated with the philosophic concepts of beauty. [13] Variety is essential to the concept of beauty. [14]

Defined as

Beauty is the discernment of goodness in truth. [15] Represent finite maximum of ideational experience. [16] Embraces the harmonious relations and rhythms of the cosmic creation. [17] Represents man’s effort to discern God in mind, matter and spirit. [18] Universal beauty is the recognition of the reflection of the Isle of Paradise in the material creation. [19] Embrace the revelation of Deity to the realms of time and space. [20] The spirit which the Father and Jesus send into the world is not only the Spirit of Truth but also the spirit of idealistic beauty. [21] The supreme beauty, the height of finite art, is the drama of the unification of the vastness of the cosmic extremes of Creator and creature. [22] It is a value-realization of God-consciousness.
