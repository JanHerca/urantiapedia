All superuniverses are finite and evolutionary, except for Havona, which is perfectly settled and eternal. [1] Boundaries do not divide a nebular family in the Seven Superuniverses. [2] The derivation of age of superuniverses reveals the relative nature of time across different cosmic perspectives. [3] The superuniverses exhibit a dual constitution, stemming from the singular source of Paradise, which precedes both space and time. [4] Superuniverses are dual physical systems mobilized by Universe Power Directors. [5]

Circuits

  • The circuits of the superuniverse, including the unifying intelligence circuit of a Master Spirit and the Divine Ministers circuit, play a crucial role in the spiritual harmony and eligibility for membership in the superuniverse confederation. [6]
  • The broadcasts of Paradise and Havona transmit cosmic information across the superuniverses. [7]
  • The cosmic-mind circuit of a Master Spirit unifies intelligence within a single superuniverse. [8]
  • The power centers and physical controllers direct energy circuits in the superuniverses, converting and channeling energies for useful application. [9]
  • The flash presence of the Infinite Spirit. [10]
  • The intersonship circuit of Eternal Son and Paradise Sons allows for direct and immediate communication between the Original Mother Son and all Sons of God throughout creation. [11]
  • The Voices of Wisdom transmit the essence of universe-wide wisdom from the near infinite volume of information circulating on the master circuits of the superuniverses to the Perfectors of Wisdom. [12]
  • Only planets settled in light and life and incorporated into the main circuits of the superuniverse are guaranteed eternal survival under the authority of the Master Creator Son and the supreme council. [13]
  • The reflective-service circuit of the seven Reflective Spirits in each superuniverse is essential for galactic coordination. [14]
  • The Thought Adjuster circuits are interassociated and routed by Divinington to the Universal Father on Paradise. [15]

Destiny of superuniverses

The destiny of superuniverses is to unfold seven major purposes, each finding fullest expression in one of the unique superuniverses, such as Orvonton, known for its merciful ministry and demonstration of love and mercy. [16] Demarcations between the isolated superuniverses will disappear with the actualization of Supreme. [17]

Each superuniverse has a separate destiny, reflecting a unique function and nature in the evolution of the seven major purposes underlying their creation. [18] Ultimate destiny of superuniverse citizens remains unknown, as outer-spacers may one day approach Havona through the seven superuniverses. [19]

Superuniverses and Havona form an interdependent relationship essential for the full development and perfection of both ascending and descending beings. [20]

The superuniverses are striving towards perfection, encompassing physical and spiritual growth, administrative development, and fraternal unity, with the ultimate aim of achieving a state akin to Havona. [21]

Organization

Each superuniverse is constituted by a specific number of planetary systems, constellations, universes, minor sectors, major sectors, and inhabitable worlds. [22] There are 1,000 minor sectors within the superuniverse government, each overseeing about 1,000,000,000 inhabitable planets. [23]

Capitals

On the headquarters worlds of the seven superuniverses, the seven Reflective Spirits serve as perfect reflections of the Seven Master Spirits, giving rise to the seven created orders of conciliators. [24]

Three currents of primary energy of ten segregations each come in to the 1000 superuniverse centers stationed on the capital sphere of each of the seven superuniverses. [25] There are 84,691 secondary Circuit Supervisors stationed on Uversa, overseeing the local universes of time and space. [26]

The chief of Census Directors stationed on each superuniverse oversees thousands upon thousands of directors assigned to local universes, with one presiding at the headquarters of each superuniverse. [27]

Conciliating commissions on superuniverses maintain expert advisers and wise teachers for ascending pilgrims, reporting to the Ancients of Days and the Master Spirit until advanced to Paradise. [28]

The headquarters spheres of the superuniverses efficiently directionize energy to their component local universes, influencing the balance and control of physical energies in organized space. [29]

Each superuniverse has a world near the center, with a sun that provides light but no heat, inhabited by accredited personalities and specially constructed for its unique purpose. [30]

The secoraphic voices serve as the personal representatives of the Infinite Spirit in superuniverse supergovernments due to the lack of specialized personalization. [31] Uversa, the headquarters of Orvonton, your superuniverse, is encircled by 490 spheres of advanced spiritual training for ascending will creatures. [32]

Universe Power Directors tone the physical controllers throughout the seven superuniverses, allowing for uniformity with the possibility of retoning in Paradise. [33]

Composition

One seventh of organized post-Havona creation is encompassed within each of the Seven Superuniverses. [34]

Out of the 70 major sectors in a superuniverse, each one consists of 100 minor sectors and about 100 billion inhabitable worlds, overseen by three Perfections of Days. [35]

Over 100,000 local universes, each with a magnificent headquarters and a ruler called a Creator Son, are blessed by the presence of a Union of Days from the Paradise Trinity. [36]

There are 70,000,000 constellations, each made up of one hundred systems with about 100,000 inhabitable planets each, totaling 10 million constellations. [37] There are 7,000,000,000 local systems within the superuniverse, each consisting of about one billion inhabited or inhabitable worlds. [38] The Grand Universe consists of seven superuniverses, totaling around 1 trillion inhabitable planets. [39]

The co-ordinate councils in each superuniverse are made up of diverse executive groups, sector rulers, and regional overseers, all working together to govern and oversee the vast cosmic realms. [40]

Government

The seven Architects of the superuniverses are the supercoordinators of the grand universe. [41]

Confederation membership requirements for a local universe include physical stability and spiritual loyalty to the Sovereign Son of God, ultimately leading to eligibility for admission into the superuniverse confederation. [42] The courts of the superuniverses are supreme, rendering unanimous decisions with no room for appeal in the councils of perfection. [43]

The deliberative assembly on Uversa unites representatives of perfection and evolution in perfect harmony, showcasing the wisdom of the superuniverse's governance. [44]

The executive branch personnel of the superuniverse government are guided by the Seven Master Spirits of supreme supervision, overseeing the high spiritual government of time-space domains. [45]

Superuniverse headquarters are specially constructed worlds near the center of their respective space segments, inhabited by accredited personalities and independently lighted and heated. [46]

The superuniverses are presided over and pervaded by one Master Spirit, each overseeing a specific realm and governing all aspects of their administration. [47] Three Ancients of Days rule each superuniverse, overseeing vast sectors of inhabitatable planets with glorious headquarters worlds. [48] The present organized Grand Universe, consisting of seven trillion inhabitable worlds and Havona, is ruled indirectly by Seven Master Spirits. [49]

In light and life

Defeated candidates in Deity adventure are remanded to different superuniverse for retraining and preparation before second attempt in Havona. [50]

In light and life, sweeping changes would occur in the entire organization and administration of every unit of creation when a superuniverse becomes settled. [51] The mortal ascension plan varies considerably in each superuniverse, presenting a glimpse of the average plan of mortal progression. [52]

Mortals swing around superuniverses many times during ascension, yet can expect to ultimately stand in the divine presence and see God face to face through the attainment of spiritual levels. [53]

The power charge of a superuniverse, with its three phases of energy consisting of ten segregations each, spreads throughout the grand universe like a vast ocean of energy, distinct from Havona energy. [54]

The Custodians of Records guard archives on the eternal Isle dating back to the personification of the Infinite Spirit, while on Uversa Celestial Recorders can show records from the arrival of the Ancients of Days. [55]

Son-fusion confers permanent ascendant citizenship to a select group within each superuniverse, enhancing the diversity and capabilities of its inhabitants. [56]

When a universe settles in light and life, it enters established superuniverse circuits with the Ancients of Days proclaiming the supreme council of unlimited authority, presided over by the Union of Days and upheld by the one hundred Faithfuls of Days, who affirm the sovereignty of the Master Creator Son. [57] The age of the first outer space level will liberate superuniverses from present age restrictions, allowing for limitless growth and evolution. [58]

Movement

The superuniverses revolve counterclockwise around Paradise, but the galaxies of outer space appear to be revolving in a clockwise direction. [59]

Superuniverse number one holds a position almost due north in relation to Paradise, opposite the Great Sources and Centers, while number seven, to which Nebadon belongs, swings between superuniverses one and six along the space level. [60]

Within the limited range of the records, observations, and memories of the generations of a million or a billion of your short years, to all practical intents and purposes, Urantia and the universe to which it belongs are following the orderly and predetermined path of the superuniverse space level. [61] The seven superuniverses traverse a great ellipse, following a well-charted cosmic path around the central universe. [62]

The mass of Havona, the eternal central universe of one billion spheres of sublime perfection, far exceeds that of all seven sectors of the grand universe due to the presence of dark gravity bodies. [63] The radial boundaries of superuniverses converge on the Paradise headquarters where the Master Spirits oversee their central supervision. [64]

Relations among superuniverses

Direct communication between the superuniverses is prevented by an energy differential, necessitating interaction through the Paradise clearinghouse maintained by the Seven Master Spirits. [65]

The superuniverses remain isolated, lacking any ambassadorial representation among them, relying solely on the Paradise clearinghouse for knowledge of mutual affairs. [66]

Only Solitary Messengers and Inspired Trinity Spirits possess the ability to travel directly from one superuniverse to another, facilitating communication between the Ancients of Days who govern the seven superuniverses. [67]

Travel between superuniverses is by way of Havona, worlds of Master Spirits, facilitated by Solitary Messengers for direct communication between the rulers of different superuniverses. [68]

Superuniverse #1

Some Havona pilgrims from superuniverse #1 may experience delay in attaining the Infinite Spirit on their first attempt, but ultimately find success in the Deity adventure. [69]

Master Spirit Number One is the direct representation of the Paradise Father in the first superuniverse, closely resembling the Universal Father in character. [70]

Superuniverse #2

Master Spirit Number Two governs superuniverse number two, representing the Eternal Son and overseeing its divine destiny. [71]

Occasionally, pilgrims from superuniverse number two fail to attain the Father on their first attempt, suggesting a reason for these apparent failures. [72]

Superuniverse #3

Master Spirit Number Three presides over superuniverse number three and directs the movements of high personalities resembling the Infinite Spirit, speaking on behalf of the Infinite Spirit in the councils of the Seven Master Spirits. [73]

Some pilgrims from superuniverse #3 may fail to attain the Eternal Son or Father on their first attempt, indicating a plausible and necessary delay in their Deity adventure. [74]

Superuniverse #4

Master Spirit Four, embodying the essence of the Father and the Son, guides and influences policies within the Seven Master Spirits, directing ascendant beings who have reached the Infinite Spirit and are on the path to experiencing the Son and the Father, overseeing the creation of personalities derived from the Father and the Son, and representing them in the councils of the Universal Father and the Eternal Son within the fourth superuniverse. [75]

Superuniverse #5

Master Spirit Number Five, adviser and director of the fifth superuniverse, resembling Father-Spirit in all aspects. [76] Some pilgrims from superuniverse #5 fail to attain Eternal Son on first attempt, indicating unescapable delays with a good reason. [77]

Superuniverse #6

Master Spirit Number Six, resembling Son-Spirit, guides the affairs of the sixth superuniverse with the combined character of the Eternal Son and the Infinite Spirit. [78] Some Havona pilgrims, predominantly from superuniverse #6, face delays in attaining the Father on their first attempt in the Deity adventure. [79]

Superuniverse #7

Master Spirit Number Seven is the unique embodiment of the Universal Father, the Eternal Son, and the Infinite Spirit, guiding triune-origin beings and ascending pilgrims through their journey to glory. [80]
