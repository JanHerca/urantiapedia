It was one of the places visited during the period of preparation of the twelve, the first preaching tour to the Sea of ​​Galilee, June 23-July 7, 26, which the twelve made alone. [1]

It was also one of the places visited by Jesus, the twelve, and the 117 evangelists during the second preaching tour in Galilee, October 3, 28-December 30, 28. [2]

It was one of the towns visited by the twelve, the twelve evangelists, and other evangelists during the second tour of the Decapolis, August 18-September 16, 29. Here Jesus preached a sermon on forgiveness in response to a disciple's question. [3]
