Master Spirit Number Seven personally sponsors ascension candidates from the worlds of time in grasping the unity of the Trinity of Supremacy, facilitating their growth towards comprehending the eternal Deities of Paradise. [1]

The grand universe is the threefold Deity domain of the Trinity of Supremacy, actualizing in Creator Sons, Ancients of Days, and Master Spirits through the power of Almighty Supreme, evolving in concert with evolutionary creatures. [2] The Supreme Being reflects the attitude of the Trinity of Supremacy within certain limits and during the era of incomplete power-personalization. [3]
