The Mongoloid race is a blend of red, yellow, and blue Sangiks, encompassing the Chinese, Amerinds, Europeans modified by secondary Sangik and Andonic mixture, Andite infusion, as well as the Malayan and other Indonesian peoples with a high percentage of secondary Sangik blood. [1]
