Jesus asked his disciples not be perturbed by famines or earthquakes; neither be concerned when they were delivered up to the civil authorities and were persecuted for the sake of the gospel. [1] Hunger brought prodigal son of the parable to senses. [2] Man was taught to provide for the hazards of famine, which periodically decimated the world. [3]
