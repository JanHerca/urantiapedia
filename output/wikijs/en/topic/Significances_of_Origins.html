These angels of seven reflective types are collectively assigned to Mighty Messengers, Those High in Authority, and Those without Name and Number. [1] The Significances of Origins teach ascending creatures to apply spirit ethics and the Memories of Mercy guide them to true mercy. [2]

The living reference genealogies of reflective seconaphim provide vital insights into the significance of origins for beings across the superuniverses. [3]
