All Adjuster-indwelt beings on probationary-dependent orders of ascension are on the roll calls of justice in the eyes of the universe. [1]

Justice requires guardian angels to respond on behalf of nonsurviving personalities during the dispensational roll call, while Adjusters remain silent, symbolizing the formal end of their existence. [2] The resurrection of sleeping survivors precedes the roll call of justice, where guardians respond for nonsurviving personalities. [3] Rejoice, for your power over spirits is temporary, but your names are written on rolls of heaven for an eternal spiritual conquest. [4] On roll call of justice see death and extinction. On roll call of mercy see resurrection.
