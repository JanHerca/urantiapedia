The Adamites and Nodites were long-heads among the Urantia races. [1] The secondary Sangiks were somewhat long-headed, distinguishing them among the Urantia races. [2] The superior Saharans migrated to Spain and Europe, forming the Mediterranean long-headed brunet races. [3] The typical early Nordics were long-headed, tall, and blond, a mixture of blue, Andite, Andonite, red, and yellow Sangik races. [4]
