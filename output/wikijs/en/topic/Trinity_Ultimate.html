The mobilization of the seven finaliter corps by the Ultimate Trinity signifies the gathering together of potentials, personalities, minds, spirits, absonites, and experiential actualities for inconceivable developments in outer space. [1] The Trinity Ultimate actively co-ordinates the transcendental in eternal unity. [2]

Beings that comprise

The evolving Ultimate Trinity is comprised of the Supreme Being, the Supreme Creator Personalities, and the absonite Architects of the Master Universe. [3]

  1. The Supreme.
  2. God the Sevenfold.
  3. Architects of Master Universe.

How is defined

The Master Architects, Paradise Creator Sons, and evolving Supreme Being form the first experiential Trinity, guiding the integration and evolution of the master universe. [4] Even now, the Trinity Ultimate is a qualified reality with Qualified Vicegerents of the Ultimate. [5]

Goals

The Supreme Being's full appreciation requires understanding its relationship to the originating Trinity, the universe of activity, and the Trinity Ultimate, its immediate destiny. [6] Michael will collaborate with Supreme in the first experiential Trinity for and in the universes of outer space. [7]

The Trinity Ultimate enables group attainment of ultimate eventualities, where group associations surpass individual capacities, even beyond the finite level, leading to the promulgation of the Paradise Deities' purposes by the Corps of the Finality in the ages to come. [8]

God the Ultimate unifies the Trinity upon the actualization of God the Ultimate, representing the absonite unification of the first experiential Trinity and signifying Deity expansion on the second level of creative self-realization. [9]
