God permits disharmony in the universe to allow for free will, leading to the possibility of disagreement and misunderstanding as evidence of the existence of personality in our evolving universe. [1]

In the future, the superuniverses will exhaust possibilities for misadaptation as they strive towards perfection in all aspects of physical, spiritual, administrative, governmental, and fraternal development. [2] Imperfection and potential evil stem from misadaptation inherent in evolutionary growth. [3]
