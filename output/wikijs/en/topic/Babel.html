City also known as Bablod, Bablot. [1]

Tower of Babel

The first tower built 50,000 years after death of Nod, that is, about 150,000 years ago. [2] The largest group of Nodites, almost one half, desired to see the tower built as a memorial of Nodite history and racial superiority. [3] The second attempt to rebuild by Andites, 12,000 years ago, failed. [4]

The third effort to build was by Andites that assimilated the civitization of Mesopotamia at the beginning of historic annals and adopted the term as their national name, Babylon. [5]
