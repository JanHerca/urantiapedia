Bethsaida hospital provided material and spiritual treatment under Elman's leadership, with Jesus personally visiting and making a profound impact on each patient. [1]

Spiritual healing

Healing is impossible except in accordance with Father’s will. [2] John's apostles anointed with oil for healing, while Jesus' apostles declined to bind themselves by such a regulation. [3]

The kingdom cannot be built upon wonder-working and physical healing alone, as Jesus explained to his apostles in detail for over four hours, seeking to make them understand the true reasons behind his mission. [4] Works of faith come not forth from doubting unbelief, but from sincere belief and trust in the power of love. [5]
