The seven-day week was introduced by the Dalamatia teachers, stemming from the fact that seven was one fourth of twenty-eight, a reminder of spiritual significance with no natural origin. [1] The sacred 7-day week's incidental origin was based on Adam and Eve's 6-day inspection of the Garden of Urantia. [2]
