Assisted by several billion seconaphim, the Trinitized Custodians serve faithfully in groups of seventy thousand, with one seventh of each group dedicated to a specific superuniverse. [1] Trinitized Custodians administer group affairs and foster collective projects, dealing solely with groups, not individuals. [2] Trinitized Custodians, embraced by Trinity, serve in the courts of all three divisions, from the Ancients of Days to the Recents of Days. [3] Trinitized Custodians in Uversa personality register. [4]

The Trinitized Custodians, numbering 70 million, serve as officers of the superuniverse major and minor sector governments, overseeing collective projects and acting as custodians of various undertakings in the three divisions. [5] Trinitized Custodians are recognized for valiantly cooperating with subsequently trinitized mortals. [6]

The Trinitized Sons of Selection, recruited from evolutionary seraphim, midway creatures, Spirit-fused, and Son-fused mortals who have traversed Havona and ascended to Paradise, are embraced by the Trinitized Custodians and Ambassadors before being assigned to the courts of the Ancients of Days after training in Havona. [7]
