Anaxagoras, the mechanistic philosopher, recognized the existence of a First Cause. [1] Assignment of causes leads to confession that ultimate factors are ignored and to a great first cause. [2] True religion turns the Universal First Cause of logic into the personal God of human salvation. [3] There is only one uncaused Cause in the whole universe. [4] Reason may lead to a First Cause but religious faith is needed to transform the First Cause into a God of salvation. [5]
