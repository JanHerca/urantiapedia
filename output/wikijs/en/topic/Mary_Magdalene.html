Mary Magdalene was won for the kingdom in a nefarious resort by Martha, sister of Andrew, at Magdala. [1] Mary Magdalene was the chief spokesman for women's corps and the most effective teacher among the twelve women evangelists commissioned by Jesus. [2] Mary Magdalene discovered the empty tomb where Jesus had been laid. [3] Mary Magdalene witnessed Jesus' appearances and was instructed by him to proclaim the gospel of the kingdom. [4]

Mary Magdalene witnessed Jesus' crucifixion with a group of earnest women believers and friends, holding their peace as they gazed upon his intense sufferings. [5] Mary Magdalene wept when the apostles refused to believe Jesus risen, despite her encounters with him at the tomb. [6]
