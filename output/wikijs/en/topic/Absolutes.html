Of actuality

It is comprised of Son, Spirit, and Paradise. [1] Eh existents of the cosmos have their center in the Absolutes of actuality. [2] The Absolutes of actuality is that which was and is. [3] The triodity of actuality is the Actual, the union of the Absolutes of Eternal Son, Infinite Spirit and Paradise Isle. [4]

Of potentiality

It is comprised of the Deity Absolute, the Universal Absolute and the Unqualified Absolute.

The seven Absolutes

Infinity is constituted by seven Absolutes. [5] All seven Absolutes encompass the circle of infinity. [6] They are eternal; all things in the universe change except the Absolutes. [7] They eternalize the foundations for all reality. [8] The Universal Father is the personal cause of the Absolutes; he is the absolute of Absolutes. [9]

There are seven co-ordinate Absolutes: the Father, the Son, the Spirit, the Paradise, the Deity Absolute, the Unqualified Absolute, the Universal Absolute. [10] Total, infinite reality is existential in seven phases and as seven Absolutes. [11] The existencial realities of the seven Absolutes is not experiencible; the experiential reality is God the Supreme. [12] The I AM established the basis for a sevenfold self-relationship through the seven Absolutes. [13] Triunities are unifications of the seven Absolutes. [14] All them are unified in Father. [15]

The three Absolutes

When talking about three Absolutes they are comprised of Deity Absolute, Universal Absolute, and Unqualified Absolute. [16] In infinity the seven Absolutes are ONE. [17] The seven Master Spirits are not directly associated with the three Absolutes. [18] The three Absolutes are co-ordinated in Ultimate; other three absolutes are one in the Trinity. [19] Below the ternal levels the three Absolutes manifest with the Ultimate and upon the Supreme. [20] Never function as such on subabsolute levels. [21] They render predictions uncertain, provoking variational and unpredictable phenomena. [22] They seem to supersede matter, mind, and spirit. [23] Triodity of potentiality, or The Potential, is the union of the three Absolutes. [24] The three Absolutes can hardly be unified short of the complete realization of all infinite potentials. [25] The union of three Absolutes constitutes the latency of I AM. [26] Unqualified Absolute unifies with Deity Absolute in Universal Absolute. [27]
