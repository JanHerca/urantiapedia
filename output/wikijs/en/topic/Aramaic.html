Jesus spoke, read, and wrote fluently in Aramaic, Greek, and Hebrew. [1]

In the days of Jesus three languages prevailed in Palestine: The common people spoke some dialect of Aramaic; the priests and rabbis spoke Hebrew; the educated classes and the better strata of Jews in general spoke Greek. [2]
