Truth, beauty, and goodness are combined in a single divine ideal, unified in the power of Supremacy and personalized as a God of paternal love. [1]

Divine love, mercy, and ministry are the meaningful values ​​of reality revealed by the Father, the Son, and the Spirit, which combine in harmony to represent loving mercy to all creatures. [2] Truth, beauty, and goodness in harmony reveal full divinity to universe mortals. [3] In their embrace, man is glorified to live and do righteousness, transcending the limitations of the material world through divine love. [4]

Health, sanity, and happiness are integrations of truth, beauty, and goodness in human experience, achieved through the unification of energy systems, idea systems, and spiritual systems. [5] Love embraces truth, beauty, and goodness, as Jesus revealed. [6] The relationship between reason, wisdom, and faith leads to the unity of mind, soul, and spirit in the pursuit of truth, beauty, and goodness. [7]

The sum of truth, beauty, and goodness is love, the manifestation of God as the spiritual Father in the loving ministry of the Infinite Spirit and the special ministry of the Sons of Paradise, who reflect the Isle of Paradise in the material creation. [8] The unity of truth, beauty, and goodness is realized only in spiritual experience. [9]
