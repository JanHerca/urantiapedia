In Uversa personality register, Supreme Center Supervisors are listed. [1]

The seven Supreme Center Supervisors regulate the master energy circuits of the grand universe, each headquartered on a special world of the Seven Supreme Executives. [2]

The Supreme Power Directors and Supreme Center Supervisors oversee cosmic phenomena below gravity energy levels, working individually and collaboratively like the Seven Supreme Executives and Seven Master Spirits in their respective domains. [3]
