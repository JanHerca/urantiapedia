Man's eternal personality is bestowed by God's choice, with the option of participating in the foreordained cycle of attaining Deity destiny. [1] Mortals are subjects of predestination, but have the freedom to reject any part of it. [2]

The supreme correlation of all past, present, and future choice does not negate free will but rather hints at the predetermined course of the universe. [3]
