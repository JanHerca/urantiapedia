The bestowal Son, Christ Michael, did not bring the usual reign of peace on Urantia; wars and rumors of wars persist. [1]

Jesus, the Prince of Peace, rejects battle and contention, choosing instead to reveal a God of love and establish a kingdom of spiritual understanding. [2] The Jews believed the Messiah would reign over them as the Prince of Peace, leading all nations in the truth of the one God. [3]
