Elizabeth was a distant cousin of Mary, the mother of Jesus. [1] She was a descendant of Aaron's daughters. [2] Gabriel first appeared to Elizabeth to announce her pregnancy. [3] Mary visited her while pregnant. [4] Elizabeth believed that Jesus would become the Messiah. [5] She received a visit from Jesus when he and his son were 5 years old. [6] She kept John informed about world affairs. [7] Her husband Zacharias died in July of the year 12. [8] She died on August 17, 22 AD. [9]
