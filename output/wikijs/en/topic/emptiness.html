Throughout all organized space there are gravity-responding energy currents, power circuits, and ultimatonic activities, as well as organizing electronic energies. Practically speaking, space is not empty. Emptiest space has 100 ultimatons per cubic inch. [1] Emptiness does have its virtue, for it may become experientially filled. [2]
