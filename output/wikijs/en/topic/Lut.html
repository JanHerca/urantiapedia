Lut and the entire council of health were lost in rebellion. [1] Lut led the guardians of health and life, focusing on sanitation and primitive hygiene promotion. [2]

Serapatatia, a descendant of the onetime chief of the Dalamatia commission on health, became the leader of the western Nodite tribes after his father's death. [3]
