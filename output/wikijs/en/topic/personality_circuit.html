God's spirit-born sons have access to the bosom of the Father through the personality circuit. [1] All personality is centered in Father, transmitting worship to the Original and Eternal Personality. [2] Personality circuit dispatches worship to Father through the Spirit presence and Adjusters' direct channels. [3] Father maintains parental contact with all persons by means of his personality circuit. [4] Through the personality circuit, the Universal Father has personal knowledge of all thoughts and acts across all creation. [5] The Universal Father bestows choice-liberty upon non-Adjuster-indwelt beings, embracing them in the divine love circuit of personality. [6] Personality circuit perfectly unifies all constituent realities in intelligent existence. [7] Personality Gravity is noncomputable, as we acknowledge the circuit's existence but cannot quantify its impact. [8]

Personality responds to the exclusive circuit of the Father, while spirit responds to the circuit of the Son, and mind responds to the circuit of the Conjoint Actor. [9] Third Source personalities are represented in the grand universe by a variety of beings who are not in the Father's personality circuit. [10]
