The first stone pyramid in Egypt was erected by Andite Imhotep, showcasing the decline in building artistry from his time. [1]

The sloping entrance to the great pyramid directed the souls of the worthy dead straight to the Pole Star, symbolizing their journey to the afterlife among the eternal constellations. [2]
