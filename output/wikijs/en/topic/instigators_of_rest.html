Upon reaching the end of their journey on the first inner circle of Havona, ascending pilgrims encounter the primary order of supernaphim, known as the instigators of rest, who prepare them for the transition into eternal sleep before the final resurrection. [1] The primary supernaphim are the instigators of rest for ascending pilgrims nearing the end of their first-circle sojourn. [2]
