The ancients believed winds were produced by birds, fearing and worshiping all winged creatures in accordance with the golden rule. [1] Different regions regard wind as either good or evil, just as diverse cultures worship various natural phenomena. [2] Those born of spirit are like the wind, unseen but their effects are felt. [3] Those who seek the Lord with all their heart shall find salvation in abundance. [4]
