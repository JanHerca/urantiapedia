Ancient civilizations viewed new practices such as writing, printing, and pictures with disfavor due to their perceived evil magical nature. [1] The Master's love creates a new world in the old one, through sharing his joy and service. [2] We should not embrace new things solely because they are novel, nor should we despise things simply because they are old. [3]
