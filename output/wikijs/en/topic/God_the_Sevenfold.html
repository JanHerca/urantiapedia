Comprised of. [1]

  1. Creator Sons, local universe Mother Spirits.
  2. Ancients of Days.
  3. Seven Master Spirits.
  4. Supreme Being.
  5. Infinite Spirit.
  6. Eternal Son.
  7. Universal Father.

The Creator Sons are the ultimate focalizations of the power and personality of the mighty time-space attributes of the Sevenfold God. [2] The Sevenfold God is the personality of Deity functioning in any part of time-space. [3] The first three levels, the Mother Spirits, the Ancients of Days, and the Master Spirits, are known as the Supreme Creators of time and space. [4] The union of the Supreme Creators, the Supreme Being, and the Master Architects are the members of the first Trinity experiential. [5] The Sevenfold embraces the controllers as well as the creators of the grand universe. [6] The Universal Father has established seven successive levels of approach to Deity. [7] The Supreme is not fully real to creatures but is real to God the Sevenfold. [8]

Function

Maintains control of power-energy-matter. [9] Integrates the perfect, the perfected, and the perfecting. [10] Mortal man appears to be necessary for the full function of the Sevenfold God. [11] Provides a progressive approach to the revelation of the Father's personality. [12] The Seven Master Spirits appear to co-ordinate the activities of the Sevenfold God. [13] The Sevenfold is, in all phases, the source of the relative unity of the functional reality of the present grand universe. [14]
