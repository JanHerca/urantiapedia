Also known as cosmic force; space potency; space-force; space energy; mother force of space; universal force-charge of space. [1]

Definitions

It has been defined as a superultimate. [2] It also has been definied as absolute, concealed nonspiritual reality of Father. [3] Constitutes the free space presence of Unqualified Absolute. [4] It is a component homogeneous and undifferentiated. [5] It is about potencies existing within space. [6] It is not an actual level of reality but a prereality. [7] It is also called the primal endowment of Paradise. [8]

Origin

It has a Paradise origin. [9] Present form originates in space, not nether Paradise. [10] It is ancestral to all manifestations of force-energy, ultimatons. [11]

Its transformations

Energy transformers change physical forms of absoluta. [12] Exists in comparative energy rest, namely, without heat. [13] Flows into and out of nether Paradise. [14] Follows paths of less resistance to motion. [15] It is a kind of form of energy unknown on Urantia. [16] Matter for making untold universes circulates in the form of a supergravity presence of absoluta. [17] It is nonresponsive to Paradise gravity or any form of gravitation. [18] The passage of energy particles through absoluta creates wave appearance. [19] Permeates all space and it is a factor in atomic cohesion. [20] Primary Master Force Organizers transmute absoluta into segregata. [21] Cosmic force responds to mind even as cosmic mind responds to spirit. [22] Absoluta is responsive only to personal grasp of Universal Father. [23] Ultimatons exhibit mutual resistance to absoluta. [24] Assembly of ultimatons causes vibrations in space. [25]
