As you age, becoming more tactful and tolerant helps avoid unnecessary misunderstandings in social interactions. [1] Display wisdom and sagacity in ironing out minor misunderstandings with civil rulers, seeking peace in all dealings. [2]

Misunderstandings are the inevitable result of ignorance and the power of choice, requiring provision for fair adjustment and harmonization in the evolving universe. [3]

The apostles recognized Jesus' unaffected friendliness and independence from human judgment, as he seldom paused to correct misunderstandings or seek advice. [4]

Revelators used existing terminology even at cost of restricting cosmic consciousness and spiritual perception, in efforts to convey advanced truth within the limitations of circumscribed language. [5]
