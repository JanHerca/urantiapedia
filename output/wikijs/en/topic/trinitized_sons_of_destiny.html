Trinitized Sons of Destiny, beings produced by finaliters and Paradise-Havoners, embody aspects of the unrevealed master universe function of the Supreme-Ultimate. [1] In Uversa personality register, the Trinitized Sons of Destiny and the Corps of Unrevealed Sons of Destiny are listed. [2]
