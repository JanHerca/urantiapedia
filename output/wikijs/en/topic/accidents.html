The belief in so-called accidents is substituted by a law wherein all effects are preceded by definite causes. [1] Accidents, as time passes, always work out for welfare and progress of the universes. [2] Believers are not immune to accidents. [3] Blind and unforeseen accidents do not occur in the cosmos. [4] If psychic circles not attained due to unavoidable accident, a probation time is granted in mansion world. [5] Material accidents are not arbitrarily interfered with by celestial personalities. [6] Primitive man did not believe in accidents. [7]

Spirit-born individuals know that catastropghes are but redirecting cataclysms which wreck temporal creations preliminary to the rearing of more enduring realities. [8] To God-knowing believer, does not matter if all things earthly crash. [9]

Jesus teachings

The death of Joseph, father of Jesus, is something that was permitted to occur. [10] His advice regarding accidental wealth was that we should not consider ourselves as owners but trusters in benefit of all the group. [11] Jesus suffered an accident falling in the stairs in a sandstorm. [12] His teaching about angels was that they are not involved in avoid accidents. [13]

Jesus taught that catastrophes of nature, accidents of time, and other calamitous happenings are not visitations of divine judgments or mysterious dispensations of Providence. [14]

How to end them

They are part of finite drama; vicissitude of evolution. [15] Scientific knowledge, leading to scientific action, is the only antidote for so-called accidental ills. [16]
