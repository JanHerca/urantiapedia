Choosing to do the will of God leads to the evolutionary attainment of consciousness of kinship with the Supreme. [1] Differences in status among races and groups are vital for fostering tolerance and altruism in humans. [2] The direction of your progress in eternity is infinitely more important than your current status in mortal life. [3] On Paradise worlds, status is determined by service and origin, not by assumptions based on Creator Sons or Vicegerington. [4]
