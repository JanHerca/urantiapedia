Members of the Melchizedek brotherhood promise to obey the seven commandments and proclaim the good news to all. [1]

The Melchizedek brotherhood nearly disappeared after his sudden departure, leaving Abraham and his followers struggling to reconcile the loss of their wonderful leader and their great organization at Salem. [2] At its height, the Melchizedek brotherhood had over 100,000 tithe payers on clay-tablet rolls. [3]
