There are 84,691 Secondary Circuit Supervisors stationed on Uversa, with their numbers increasing regularly across the grand universe. [1]

Secondary Circuit Supervisors, who serve on superuniverse headquarters, are stationed to oversee the local universes of time and space, with 84,691 currently on Uversa and being created in groups of seventy on the worlds of the Supreme Executives. [2]
