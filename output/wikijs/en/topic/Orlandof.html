Orlandof, the prince and teacher of the blue race 200,000 years ago, led a revival of learning and religion, guiding many tribes back to worshiping the true God as the “Supreme Chief.”. [1] Orlandof, one of 24 counselors, is a prince of the blue men and their leader in recognizing the divinity of “The Supreme Chief.”. [2]
