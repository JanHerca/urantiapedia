Lakes in heavens

Thousands of sparkling lakes adorn the highlands of Edentia, without the presence of oceans or rivers. [1] Many lakes on Jerusem provide the essential moisture for physical and morontia life, without need for rainfall or storms. [2]

Lakes in the Earth

Lakes are a distinctive glacial product that showcases the profound impact of glaciers on the landscape. [3] For a list of lakes and seas that its name in El Libro de Urantia, see their enlaces.
