At the apostles' discussion at the olive press, Nathaniel and James reminded the group of Jesus' teachings on nonresistance, leading to their decision to scatter and await further information. [1] Alone on the olive press near the entrance to Gethsemane, Jesus awaited his apprehenders in solitude. [2]
