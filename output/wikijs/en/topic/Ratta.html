Ratta, the superhuman 20-year-old Nodite, became the 2nd wife of Adamson and together they had a remarkable family of 67 children. [1]

Ratta's lineage, descended in unbroken line from pure Nodite staff, contributed to the cultural legacy of the Greeks who believed in their divine ancestry. [2] Mary, the earth mother of Jesus, was a descendant of a long line of remarkable women from various cultures, including Ratta. [3]
