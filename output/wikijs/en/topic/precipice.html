Jesus would have effectively disarmed his violent enemies, but a tactical blunder by one of his own apostles led to hirelings trying to shove him off a precipice. [1]

Jesus decided to exercise normal watchcare over his human safety and refrain from superhuman intervention, even when faced with the temptation to cast himself off the precipice before him. [2]
