It is the task of the conductors of worship so to teach the ascendant creatures how to worship. [1] Conductors of Worship are the first order inside primary Supernaphim. [2]

Never since the times of Grandfanda have the supernaphim been able fully to accommodate the spirit of worship on Paradise. Always is there an excess of worshipfulness. [3]
