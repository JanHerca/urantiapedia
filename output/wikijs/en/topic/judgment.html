By man

Dangers and unfairness of sitting in personal judgment. [1] Do not prejudge fellows. [2] Judge not by a single unfortunate human episode. [3] Judge savages according to their enlightenment. [4] Let others give account of themselves to God. [5] Must be lodged in hands of social group. [6] Spirit of Truth enables wise. [7]

Concomitant of lengthening time unit in experience. [8]

Day of judgment

Taught by Egyptians and Zoroaster. [9] Under Mithraism. [10]

Divine judgment

3 questions asked of the rich. [11] A thousand or a million witnesses may testify. [12] Absolves person from all future criticism. [13] Account of endowments and stewardships asked in next world. [14] At death, mortals stand in immediate presence of. [15] Based on faithfulness, not material success. [16] Belongs to Ancients of Days. [17] Conciliating commissions settle minor difficulties. [18] Delay in execution justified if it might conduce to repentance. [19] Ever fulfills divine love of God. [20] Exclusive function of beings of high training and creature experience. [21] Father is source of all true judgment. [22] Final application of justice. [23] Human lives recorded in seraphim and Censors. [24] If in doubt, Judges invariably rule in personal interests of individual. [25] Judges will not deprive any being of personality who has not finally and fully made eternal choice. [26] Justice prevails when mercy exhausted. [27] Magisterial Sons adjudicate to close planetary dispensations. [28] Memories of Mercy testify before courts of Uversa. [29] Mighty Messengers are defense attorneys; Those High in Authority are prosecutors; Those without Name and Number are jurors. [30] Must prove satisfactory to all related persons. [31] Never destroys what mercy can save. [32] No adjudication for probationary nursery children who do not choose survival. [33] Of nonsurvivors. [34] System Sovereigns enforce mandates of constellations and local universe. [35] Those rejecting Jesus and his teachings will be brought to judgment. [36] Time is part of evidence before Ancients of Days. [37] Unbiased testimony given by reflective seconaphim. [38] Universal Censors are organic part of dispensational adjudications. [39] Universe authorities would rather risk system rebellion than deprive one mortal of ascending career. [40] Vested in hands of those knowing antecedents, motives. [41]

God merciful with primitive mortals. [42] Jesus came not to judge but to offer salvation. [43]

Universal Censors

Appropriate Adjuster and seraphic records for judgment. [44] Are judgment of Deity. [45] Judges and censors decree mortal survival. [46]
