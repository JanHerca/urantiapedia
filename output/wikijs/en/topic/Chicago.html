Chicago was the city of the visitation of the Urantia Book's revelators. [1]

Chicago is mentioned to establish a comparison of the energy provided by the sun. If the city had to pay for sunlight at two cents per kilowatt-hour, the bill would be more than 100 million dollars a day. [2]
