Reservist chiefs on Urantia may be appointed by permanent councils for specific tasks, typically lasting only a few hours. [1]

962 Urantia destiny reservists, chosen by their respective corps and trained by celestial beings, are prepared for possible planetary emergencies, with only 20 contact personalities aware of their roles. [2]

The cosmic reserve corps of universe-conscious citizens on Urantia, numbering over one thousand mortals, possess insight into cosmic citizenship beyond terrestrial boundaries, their unique function remains undisclosed. [3]

Reservists are the guardians of destiny, enjoying continuous ministry and watchcare from personal seraphim once enrolled in the reserve corps of destiny. [4]

Reservists, through contact with Thought Adjusters and midway creatures, function in rare emergencies to prevent the breakdown of culture or extinction of truth on evolutionary worlds. [5]

The reserve corps of destiny comprises living men and women enlisted for the superhuman administration of world affairs in aiding the ministry of mercy and wisdom to humanity. [6]

Reservists are chosen as protectors of planetary destiny, confirmed by the planetary chief of seraphim, and appointed personal destiny guardians as they function in higher cosmic circles. [7]

The mortal reservists of the twelve groups of Urantia destiny, consisting of 962 persons, are trained in deep mind by Adjuster and seraphic ministry for planetary emergencies. [8]

The contact guardians of the Urantia reserve corps of destiny, like rebel midwayers before Christ Michael, influence mortals' minds and control their actions through unperceived personal-liaison associations. [9]

Through the skillful penetration of the minds of indwelling mortals, reserve corps members advance spiritual civilization and enable the Most Highs to rule, yet they remain rarely recognized in human history. [10] Selected for inherent capacity and wholehearted dedication, reservists serve without human recognition. [11] Reservists unknowingly safeguard crucial planetary knowledge, passing it on to younger successors through Thought Adjusters. [12]

Adjusters of reservists

Reservists are like possessing a Thought Adjuster of extraordinary versatility and experience in navigating planetary challenges and world emergencies. [13] Reservists with self-acting Adjusters serve as pivotal individuals in the plans of planetary destiny. [14]

Through a heartfelt plea delivered by one Adjuster, a destiny reservist was urged to faithfully cooperate, endure, carry out, persist, cheerfully tread the path, humbly receive credit, exhort him to survival, and ultimately submit all to the tribunals of the Ancients of Days. [15]

During the making and breaking of contacts with reservists, messages are sometimes transmitted by indwelling Adjusters in a plea for sincere cooperation and faithful perseverance. [16] Reservists are used for interplanetary communication on quarantined worlds to circumvent the handicaps of planetary isolation. [17]
