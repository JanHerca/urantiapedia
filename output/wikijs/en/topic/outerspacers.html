The incoming spiritual aristocracy of the successive outer universes may become future citizens of Havona. [1]

The ultimate destiny of outer-spacers may involve approaching Havona through the seven superuniverses, contributing to the maintenance of experiential equilibrium while welcoming Paradise pilgrims. [2]
