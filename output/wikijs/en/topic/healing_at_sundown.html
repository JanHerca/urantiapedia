At sundown, 683 were healed by the ruling of Jesus' Father, with celestial beings descending to make them whole in a thrilling spectacle. [1] The majority did not experience lasting spiritual benefits from the sundown healing demonstration. [2] Only seven of the 683 enrolled at school for evangelists were among the large number healed at the sundown scene. [3] Jesus became as much a physician as a preacher due to the unintended demonstration of physical healing at sundown. [4]
