The merchant of Damascus asked Jesus to enter his import business after developing a great liking for the Nazareth couple during their stay in Jerusalem. [1] Jesus refused the offer to establish a school in Damascus despite the insistence of the merchant and his supporters. [2] Jesus translated Greek into an Indian language while working in Damascus for four months. [3]
