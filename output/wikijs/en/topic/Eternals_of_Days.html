They are Supreme Trinity Personalities. [1] They are close of kin to, and are the divine equals of, the Ancients of Days, who preside over the destinies of the seven supergovernments. [2] There are one billion and were created by Trinity. Each of them directs one of Havona spheres. [3] Each embellishes his sphere according to own original plans. [4] Eternals of Days are not creators, but they are perfect administrators. [5]

Forever have Eternal of Days and Secrets of Supremacy ruled their exquisite worlds of the Paradise-Havona system, and they function without rotation or reassignment. [6] They appear in personality registers as the Stationary Sons of the Trinity. [7] Most superuniverse personnel apprenticed under the Eternals of Days on the various worlds of the perfect Havona universe. [8] When an Eternal of Days is absent from his sphere, his world is directed by a Trinity Teacher Son. [9] The Eternals of Days are visible to all will creatures dwelling in their domains. [10] Prior to the times of Grandfanda, high Paradise Citizens were the first executive assistants to the Eternals of Days. [11]
