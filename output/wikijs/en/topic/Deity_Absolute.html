Definition

Deity Absolute is the Absolute of potentiality; the activator of static potentials. [1] He is destiny inceptor; one of the seven Absolutes of Infinity. [2] The Deity Absolute is experiential in actualization but existential in potentiality. [3] The experiential Trinities culminate in the experiential personalization of the Deity Absolute. [4] He is called Infinite Potential or I AM self-qualified. [5] He is limitless, timeless, spaceless, boundless, and measureless—truly infinite. [6] He is not the equivalent of the Infinite. [7]

The Deity Absolute is that potential which was segregated from total, infinite reality by the freewill choice of the Universal Father, and within which all divinity activities—existential and experiential—take place. [8] The Deity Absolute is the Qualified Absolute, within which all divinity activities—existential and experiential—take place. [9] The Unqualified Absolute and the Deity Absolute are unified in the Universal Absolute. [10]

There are things still unknown: Is the Unqualified Absolute a force presence independent of the Trinity? Does the presence of the Deity Absolute connote the unlimited function of the Trinity? and is the Universal Absolute the final function of the Trinity, even a Trinity of Trinities? [11]

Description

God the Absolute is becoming experientializing God but now is existential as the Deity Absolute. [12] Deity Absolute is member of triodity of potentiality. [13] He is member of Third and Sixth Triunity. [14] Overcontrols all material reality. [15] He has reactions hardly personal, unfathomable unpredictable. [16]

The Eternal Son and the Deity Absolute appear to be related in the following way: The Eternal Son dominates the realm of actual spiritual values, whereas the Deity Absolute seems to pervade the vast domain of potential spirit values. [17]

Every response of Deity Absolute appears to be made in terms of the welfare of the whole creation of things and beings, not only in its present state of existence, but also in view of the infinite possibilities of all future eternity. [18] It seems likely that Universal Censors are in some manner in liaison with the Deity Absolute. [19] Majeston was created by a new and far-reaching reaction occurred in the Deity Absolute. [20]

The Deity Absolute response to the creation of Majeston by the Supreme Being and his associates was vastly beyond their purposeful intent and greatly in excess of their conceptual forecasts. [21] Spirit seems to emerge from the potentials of the Deity Absolute. [22] The Absolute Trinity functions even to the borders of the nonpersonal, and its unification in universality would experientialize Absolute Deity. [23]
