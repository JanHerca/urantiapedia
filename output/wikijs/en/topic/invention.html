Invention drives cultural acceleration, benefiting science and enabling cultural advancement worldwide. [1] Economic freedom secured through capital and invention enhances societal cooperation. [2]

The inestimable benefits of mechanical inventions liberate mankind from the necessity for unremitting toil, allowing civilization to flourish through leisure and innovation. [3] The post-Adamic dispensation is an age of great invention, energy control, and mechanical development. [4]
