On the Uversa personality record he is the third personality in existence. [1] The personification of the Infinite Spirit is the first record of Paradise. [2] The seven sacred worlds of the Infinite Spirit are the Paradise headquarters of the seven superuniverses, the executive worlds of the entire universe. [3] He accepted Michael's bestowal on the Mount of Transfiguration through a representative who spoke through the Father Melchizedek. [4] As the God of Action he is the apparent ancestor of motion, change, and relationship. [5] The Father treats the Infinite Spirit as an equal. [6] One of the energy circuits of the superuniverses is the instantaneous presence of the Infinite Spirit. [7]

Pure mind is the nearest relative of infinite mind, and infinite mind is the theoretical co-ordinate of the absolutes of spirit and energy, is apparently a law unto itself. [8] The mind of the Third Source and Center is infinite and is adequate for the mind-giving of an unlimited number of creatures. [9] Adjusters are wholly outside the mind circuits of the Infinite Spirit, though they have premind, and something like thought. [10]

Alongside the Adjusters or fragmentations of the Father are the fragmentations of the premental spirit of the Third Source and Center, though the two are quite distinct. [11] The presence of the Infinite Spirit enshrouds the Father and the Son. [12] Reflectivity is a secret of the Infinite Spirit. [13] Havona is a compensation to the Infinite Spirit for his extensive unselfish work in the universes of space. [14] Seconaphim interpret the mind of the Spirit Infinite for the Ancients of Days. [15] Seraphic guardians correlate the influences of the Infinite Spirit. [16] In the superuniverses the Infinite Spirit speaks only through the Seven Master Spirits. [17] The Seven Master Spirits distribute the Infinite Spirit to the superuniverses. [18] Solitary Messengers can traverse space by the mental circuits of the Infinite Spirit. [19] The love and sorrows of the Infinite Spirit are real. [20]

Functions of Infinite Spirit

The Infinite Spirit is the activator of the Paradise archetype. [67] In all universes the agents of the Conjoint Actor are ceaselessly manipulating the forces and energies of all space. [68] The agencies of the Infinite Spirit manage our practical daily affairs, and we cooperate with them. [69] The Conjoint Creator is the ancestor of the cosmic mind, and the mind of man is an individualized circuit, an impersonal portion, of that cosmic mind. [70] The Infinite Spirit possesses a unique and awesome power—antigravity. [71] The Infinite Spirit bestows Third-Type Personality Source, which is different from the personality given by the Father. [72] He can also act on behalf of the Father to bestow personality of the First Source type. [73] He seems to compensate for the incompleteness of evolutionary Deity: the Supreme and the Ultimate. [74] He is to be thought of as a correlator of all present reality. [75] He draws to himself all divine and perfect ideas and values ​​into his mental circuit. [76] He draws likewise all intellectual values ​​Paradiseward. [77] He approaches all creatures as a person through the angels. [78] The only way to reach the Eternal Son is through the Infinite Spirit. [79] The Infinite Spirit acts truly and literally as the joint executive of the Father and the Son not only in creation but also in administration. [80] He exerts the influence of his personal presence within the confines of the Paradise-Havona system; elsewhere that influence is delegated. [81]

The finite domains of energy and spirit are literally held together by the mind presences of the Conjoint Actor. Mind is that flexible reality which connects matter and spirit. [82] It fragments portions of its spirit to indwell Spirit-fused mortals. [83] It has dedicated all to the plan of mortal ascension devised by the Father and accepted by the Son. [84] It is believed to be the initiator of motion in space, but it is not certain whether it is the cause of the motions of space itself. [85] Life comes into being through the action of the Universal Mind. [86] It makes possible universal reflectivity, the power to see, hear, feel, and know all things things as they occur throughout the universe. [87] Acts as the universal manipulator of the forces and energies of Paradise, thus bringing into existence the universal and absolute mind. [88]

The Infinite Spirit is engaged in the unending ministry of revealing the combined love of the Father and the Son to the individual minds of all the children of every universe. [89] Ministers to the minds of even the most primitive living beings. [90] On Urantia he is known as an omnipresent influence, but in Havona we shall know him as a personal presence of true service. [91] The providence of God is the realm of the Conjoint Creator. [92] His relationship to the universe is set forth in paper 9 of The Urantia Book. [93] The Infinite Spirit is the source of mortal mind. [94]

Nature of the Infinite Spirit

The Infinite Spirit is absolute in the domain of mind. [95]

The Conjoint Creator is not energy, nor its source or destination, he is the manipulator of energy, the action: the movement, change, modification, coordination, stabilization and equilibrium. [96] He is wholly like the Father. [97] He is eternal; he is an actual divine personality. [98]

The Conjoint Actor is the absolute of mind reality, the co-ordinate of absolute spiritual reality, and the existential synthesis, in the form of Deity, of personality and power. [99] He is independent of space and time. [100] Only the Infinite Spirit and the Eternal Son know the Father as infinite. [101] The Spirit is ministry—the ministry of divine love and endless mercy to all intelligent creation. [102] The outstanding attribute of the Infinite Spirit is his omnipresence; pervades all space. [103] Is omniscient; is omnipotent in the domain of mind; is personally conscious of every mind in all creation. [104] Forms a unit with the Father and the Son; the three are one. [105] Became personalized as a limitless spirituality, co-ordinated with absolute mind, and with unique prerogatives of energy manipulation. [106] Is revealed in the bestowals of the Paradise Sons. [107] The Conjoint Actor is the revelation of the unity of God. [108]

Participations of the Infinite Spirit

Participates in the birth of the Mother Spirits of the local universes. [109] Participated in the creation of Havona. [110] Not directly related to physical gravity, spirit gravity, or the personality circuit, but participates more or less in all other universe activities. [111] Functions on the spheres of mind, matter, and spirit. [112] The Infinite Spirit participated fully in the seven transcendental appearances of the Eternal Son in Havona. [113] Also participated with the original Michael Son in the seven bestowals on the circuits of Havona. [114]

Attaining the Infinite Spirit

Ascenders recognize the Infinite Spirit on the fifth circuit of Havona; He is the first Deity to be recognized, his discovery rarely fails to come to fruition. [115] Mortals may know the presence of the Infinite Spirit in the Holy Spirit. [116] Not all who reach Paradise can immediately discern the Infinite Spirit. [117] Trinity guides help Havona pilgrims find the Infinite Spirit. [118]
