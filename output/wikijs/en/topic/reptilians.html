Birds are descended from ancient reptiles. [1] Snakes, lizards, alligators, turtles survive as the descendants of the ancient reptilian kingdom. [2] From frogs sprang the reptilian kingdom, leading to the evolution of birds, mammals, and ultimately, the human species. [3]
