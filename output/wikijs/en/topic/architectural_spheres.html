The architectural spheres are worlds like our planets but they have not arisen by planetary evolution, but have been made to order. [1] These are the worlds which are built according to plans and specifications for some special purpose. [2]

Figures

It is projected the final existence of 500 billion in all seven superuniverses. [3] In local universe of Nebadon there are 647,591 architectural spheres. [4]

Characteristics

The climatic and other physical conditions prevailing on the architectural worlds make roofs wholly unnecessary in abodes. [5] They are independently lighted, heated, watered, and energized by the co-ordination of the Satania Power Center and the Master Physical Controllers. [6]

They have just double the number of elements of the evolved planets. They have one hundred physical elements and exactly one hundred forms of a unique energy organization called morontia material. [7] The matter of architectural spheres is organized by direct action of power centers and physical controllers. [8] The architectural worlds enjoy ten forms of life of the material order, three vegetables, three animal, and four not in the previous. [9] There is no organic evolution on them, there are no conflicting forms of life, no struggle for existence, no survival of the fittest. [10] Such headquarters worlds are not luminous in space; if Jerusem were very near Urantia, it would not be visible. [11]

Seas of glass are usually found on this spheres, and serve as receiving field for transport beings, as decorative value, and for portraying superuniverse reflectivity. [12]

The architectural worlds are so constructed that the living power centers can act as selective switches to directionize, modify, and concentrate the energies of space as they pour over these spheres. [13]

Superuniverse headquarters

Each superuniverse government presides near the center of the evolutionary universes of its space segment, it occupies a world made to order and is peopled by accredited personalities. [14] The headquarters worlds of the seven superuniverses partake of the nature and grandeur of Paradise, their central pattern of perfection. [15]

The headquarters spheres of the superuniverses are so constructed that they are able to function as efficient power-energy regulators for their various sectors, serving as focal points for the directionization of energy to their component local universes. They exert a powerful influence over the balance and control of the physical energies circulating through organized space. [16]

Spheres like Uversa are unusually favorable for astronomic observations, not only because of its central location, but also because there are no gigantic living or dead suns near. [17]

Local universes headquarters

Spornagia care for the material phases of architectural spheres in local universe headquarters. [18]

Salvigton

The first completed act of physical creation in Nebadon consisted in the organization of the headquarters world, the architectural sphere of Salvington, the one hundred headquarters worlds of the projected constellations and the ten thousand headquarters spheres of the projected local systems, together with their architectural satellites. [19] Creator Sons work in building his headquarters sphere upon arrival to his local universe. [20]
