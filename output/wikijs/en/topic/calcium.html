Calcium is, in fact, the chief element of the matter-permeation of space throughout Orvonton. [1]

By tossing its 19th electron back and forth between two orbits more than 25,000 times a second, a calcium atom is able partially to defy gravity and thus successfully to ride the light. [2] They are wanderers derelicts of space, together with sodium. [3]
