Orvonton's star clouds contain gaseous matter with an unbelievably enormous energy potential, comparable to separate nebulae in external space regions. [1]

The stellar gas clouds of Orvonton, comparable to separate nebulae, hold an enormous energy potential redistributed by near-by suns as solar emanations. [2]
