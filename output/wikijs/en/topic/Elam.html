Elam was the eastern Nodite headquarters. [1] Enoch, the first son of Cain and Remona, was head of Nodites at Elam. [2] The culture and commerce Nodite advocates migrated in large numbers eastward into Elam and there united with the mixed Sangik tribes. [3] Sethite priests moved eastward through the Elamite highlands to the Iranian plateau and Turkestan. [4]
