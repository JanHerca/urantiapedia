A vacillating Roman ruler sacrificed Jesus and was deposed after a false Messiah led troops to Mount Gerizim. [1] Jesus and the apostles camped on Mount Gerizim, preaching the good news of the kingdom and preparing souls for the fatherhood of God. [2] The Samaritans worshiped Yahweh on Mount Gerizim, but their temple was destroyed by John Hyrcanus. [3] Worship not on Mount Gerizim, but where you are, in spirit and in truth, for it is your faith that saves your souls. [4]
