The Urantia type of human personality is endowed with the ability to recognize the uniformity of physical causation, the obligation of moral conduct, and the fellowship worship of Deity, reflecting a cosmic citizenship and Deity kinship. [1]

Science, philosophy, and religion are compared and contrasted in their approaches to understanding reality and existence, with each discipline offering valuable insights that contribute to a more complete perspective on the universe and our place within it. [2]

The constitutive responses of science, philosophy, and religion survive the crash of civilizations, sustaining the essence of human self-consciousness. [3]

The reality response saves us from false a priori assumptions in science, philosophy, and religion, guiding us through the realms of physical senses, morals, and spiritual experience. [4]

Relationship among, also referred to as

The cosmic mind recognizes response on three levels of universe reality: causation, duty, and worship. [5] Mind establishes relationships between energy and spirit, reflecting a cosmic kinship in eternity. [6] In the universal reality, science represents fact, philosophy embodies idea, and religion embodies relation. [7]

The personal creature discerns the fact, law, and love of God through innate recognition-realization of energy reality, mind reality, and spirit reality. [8]

The pursuit of knowledge leads to fact consciousness, the search for wisdom leads to co-ordinate consciousness, the love for God leads to value consciousness, and the hunger for truth leads to the consciousness of true reality. [9]

In cosmic reality, energy is the thing, mind is the meaning, and spirit is the value that give objective validity to man's experience in and with things, meanings, and values. [10] Truth, beauty, and goodness are the universal values of science, philosophy, and religion, respectively. [11]
