Jesus settled on a consistent policy of living according to natural laws, even though his Personalized Adjuster warned that these laws could be greatly accelerated in certain circumstances. [1] Man reaps the harvest of efforts to comply with natural laws. [2]

The predictability of physical phenomena decreases as one moves farther from Paradise, encountering forces beyond our understanding despite our grasp of universal laws. [3]

Sin affects more than just the individual; its consequences ripple across all levels of existence, but only fully manifest in actions, not just thoughts or intents. [4]
