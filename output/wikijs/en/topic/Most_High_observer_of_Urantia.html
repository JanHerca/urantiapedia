The Most High observer of Urantia, who recently assumed emergency authority on the planet, immediately began his administration with a chosen staff, but did not assign duties to the three Personalized Adjusters who appeared in his presence. [1] The Most High observer of Urantia, trustee of Urantia sovereignty, rules in the kingdoms of men with authority and wisdom. [2]
