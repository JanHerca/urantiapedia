Despite repeated urgings, primitive men would not experiment with steam power due to their fear of its explosive nature, but were eventually persuaded to work with metals and fire. [1] Young Jesus pondered the physical world and its constitution as he observed escaping steam from boiling pots during the coldest winters in Nazareth. [2]
