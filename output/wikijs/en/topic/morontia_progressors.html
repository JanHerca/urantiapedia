Throughout morontia progression, assurance of truth replaces assurance of faith, leading to pure spirit insight in the spirit world. [1] Morontia progressors rely on the Adjuster for self-consciousness as they transition from material to spiritual existence. [2]

The pursuit of the ideal and striving to be Godlike is a continuous effort both before and after death, as life after death is no different in essentials from mortal existence. [3]

As morontia progressors, you may enhance and customize your personal abodes on the mansion worlds while experiencing the increasing perfection and grandeur of the material and morontia structures. [4] Morontia progressors may visit a superuniverse upon attaining spirit status. [5]

The morontia progressors of Endantum were witness to Michael's extraordinary and amazing career as a morontia mortal of ascending status on the fifth constellation's headquarters planet. [6]

Morontia progressors traverse the transition spheres of mortal ascension, advancing in intellect, spirit, and personality form until they attain spirit status at the end of their local universe sojourn. [7]

Not one Jerusem citizen fell in Lucifer's rebellion, showcasing the unwavering loyalty of the ascending mortals who stood strong against the forces of evil. [8]

No marriage between morontia progressors is needed in the worlds to come, for they are eternally the children of light resurrected into the progress of eternal life. [9] Morontia progressors are hosts to celestial visitors, providing ample opportunity to visit any realm experientially attained. [10]

Morontia progressors, remaining in full contact with the material world, will discern and fraternize with spirit beings on their journey through transition realms. [11] Morontia progressors never lose the ability to recognize beings of previous levels as they ascend inward in the scale of life. [12]

Translated souls from advanced planets in the fourth stage of light and life bypass the traditional morontia progression, heading straight to universe headquarters for their ascension training. [13] Morontia progressors utilize material and semimaterial means for atmospheric passage on architectural spheres like Edentia. [14]

Morontia progressors, as three-natured beings, advance intellect, spirit, and personality form within the local universe, under its care, before moving on to the superuniverse. [15]

Morontia bodies

Morontia progressors on Edentia are like angels, evolving from material to spiritual beings in a period of heavenly bliss. [16]

Morontia progressors undergo 570 ascending transformations, guided by Morontia Power Supervisors, as they evolve from material to spiritual beings in the local universe. [17] The morontia progressors do not have circulating blood or eat ordinary food, yet their forms are real like Jesus' body after his resurrection. [18] Morontia progressors on Jerusem possess an enormous range of vision to observe the diverse activities of the various groups of Sonship. [19]

The morontia forms of evolving human beings are highly individual and vary with the nature of their inner person, just as physical bodies reflect the character of their personality identity. [20]

The morontia mind individualizes unique and characteristic forms for all mortal survivors, just as the cosmic mind and adjutant mind-spirits design physical tabernacles for evolving human beings. [21]

Morontia progressors undergo necessary form changes and re-keying by selective assorters as they move through each sphere of existence in their ascension journey. [22]

Morontia progressors on Mansonia number one require food and drink, but there is no residual waste produced from their purely energy-based morontia vegetation diet. [23] Morontia progressors maintain their form until they reach first-stage spirits for transit to the superuniverse worlds. [24] Morontia progressors are provided with new physical bodies by the Life Carriers, exempt from diseases but subject to certain accidents. [25]

Training

The time spent on the seventy training worlds of transition morontia culture associated with the Edentia age of mortal ascension, is the most settled period in an ascending mortal’s career up to the status of a finaliter, encompassing Adamic training on Edentia. [26] The ascendant life is about equally divided between work and play, reflecting the morontial and spiritual equivalents of joyful mirth and laughter. [27]

Morontia progressors advance by relinquishing misconceptions of God and spirit tainted by materialism, as they strive for a purer understanding through spiritual growth. [28] On the mansion worlds, Morontia Companions will help you learn new languages just like you would down here. [29] No time limit is set for the progression of ascending pilgrims through the seven Havona circuits. [30]

On the morontia training worlds of Nebadon, earnest tasks will be given that gradually increase in difficulty, allowing for periods of rest and rejuvenation along the journey to Paradise. [31]
