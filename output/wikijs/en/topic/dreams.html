The absurdities of dreams not only testify to pressure of unexpressed emotions but also bear witness to the horrible distortion of the spiritual concepts presented by the Adjusters. [1] Celestials seldom use dreams to communicate. [2] Human dreams are the disordered, disconnected parade of uncoordinated sleeping mind. [3]

While we are asleep, the Adjusters try to register their creations in the higher levels of our material mind, and some of our grotesque dreams indicate their failure to make efficient contact. [4]

Interpretation of dreams

The great danger in all these psychic speculations is that visions and other so-called mystic experiences, along with extraordinary dreams, may be regarded as divine communications to the human mind. [5] The interpretation of dreams is largely a superstitious and groundless system of ignorant and fantastic speculation. [6] Ordinary dreams are purely physiologic and psychologic. [7]

Particular dreams

Some important dreams in history:

  • Zoroaster’s one that caused him to remodel his religion. [8]
  • The dream that informed a strange religious teacher of Jesus’ birth. [9]
  • The one that convinced Zacharias regarding John, his son, the Baptist. [10]
  • The one reconciled Joseph regarding Jesus’ birth. [11]
  • Young Jesus’ one disturbed by revolting of slaughters in Jerusalem temple. [12]
  • Peter’s one seeing Jesus walking on Sea of Galilee. [13]
  • Claudia Procula’s, wife of Pilate, about Jesus. [14]
  • The one that delivered Peter from fear of Jewish traditions about uncleanness. [15]

Primitives’ dreams

Primitive man believed dreams as real as waking experience. [16] Primitive man believed soul left body during dreams. [17] Probably the greatest single factor in the evolution of human society was the ghost dream. [18] Primitive man self-convinced of afterlife existence by dreams. [19]
