Lepers were forbidden to attend synagogue, but Jesus healed a man of leprosy out of compassion and faith. [1] Jesus performed healing at Iron on a leper, demonstrating compassion and power. [2] Jesus healed the ten lepers, but only the Samaritan returned to give thanks. [3]
