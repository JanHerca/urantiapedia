Adjusters are able to receive the continuous stream of cosmic intelligence coming in over the master circuits of time and space; they are in full touch with the spirit intelligence and energy of the universes. [1]

The transmittal of space reports is carefully supervised, and there is always a back registry to insure the proper reception of every report on every world in a given circuit. [2] Havona pilgrims may consult the broadcasts to ascertain who of their fellow pilgrims have departed for Havona since the time of their leaving Uversa. [3] Spirit melodies can be broadcast to an entire universe. [4] The broadcasts are received by a technique involving the polar crystal, the sea of glass. [5] The broadcasts of Paradise, the superuniverses, and the local universes are under the general supervision of celestial artisans. [6] All higher orders of celestial beings are able to utilize broadcasts for communication with their fellows scattered throughout the universe. [7]

On Salvington

John saw a vision of broadcasts on Salvington: “And out of the throne proceeded lightnings and thunderings and voices”. [8]

On constellations

The promulgation of the laws of constellations is done by broadcasters. [9]

On Jerusem

Of all preoccupations for an ascendant mortal on Jerusem, none is more engaging and engrossing than that of listening in on the never-ending stream of universe space reports. [10]

On a planet

The universe broadcast is extended to all inhabited worlds regardless of their spiritual status. Planetary intercommunication is denied only those worlds under spiritual quarantine. [11]

In a planet in light and life mortals spent a good part of the time scanning the latest broadcasts with the same keen interest we now read the latest edition of a newspaper. [12]

While planetary space reports are received at noon at the meridian of the designated spiritual headquarters, the transporters are dispatched from this same place at midnight. [13]

Space reports

From Havona space reports of glory are broadcasted, the good news about pilgrims’ attainment of Father. [14] The arrival of Grandfanda inaugurated the “space reports of glory,” the broadcast service of universe of universes. [15] The universal space reports are contained in circuits of cosmic mind. [16] The broadcasts of Paradise are the space reports of Havona. [17]
