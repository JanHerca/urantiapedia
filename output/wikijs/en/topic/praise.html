Andrew's lack of enthusiasm led to his failure to encourage others judiciously, stemming from his distaste for flattery and insincerity. [1] Jesus uninfluenced by praise, always living independent of human beings. [2] Seek only what honestly belongs to you and avoid seeking unearned recognition or undeserved sympathy while preaching truth and proclaiming the gospel. [3]
