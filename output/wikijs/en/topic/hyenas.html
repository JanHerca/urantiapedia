Many ancient tribes believed and taught their descent from various animals, such as African tribes teaching they descended from hyenas. [1]

Hyenas entered Europe from Africa, alongside other African animals, and virtually eliminated saber-toothed tigers and hippopotamuses as mammalian life rebounded post-glacier. [2]
