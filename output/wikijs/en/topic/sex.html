Historically

[13] Primitive peoples lacked the all-absorbing sex passion seen in more highly civilized peoples, as promiscuity was virtually absent. [14]

Temple prostitution was highly regarded as a sacred and elevating practice in ancient times, often associated with human sacrifice and redemption through sexual surrender.
