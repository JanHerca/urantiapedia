Nathan of Cana was the father of the groom of the wedding at Cana, where Jesus made the first real, although unintentional, miracle. [1]

Despite the father of the bridegroom providing plenty of wine, a shortage occurred during the marriage feast, prompting Mary to confidently seek help from Jesus. [2]
