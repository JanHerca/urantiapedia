Advocating the omnificence of primal Deity disenfranchises all other personalities in the universe. [1]

The freewill actions of spirits do not always reflect the perfection and immutability of the Eternal Son, who maintains absolute control over all spirit realities. [2]

Recognizing Deity omnipotence brings security and assurance in the journey to Paradise, while accepting the fallacy of omnificence leads to the colossal error of pantheism. [3]

God's actions may seem cruel or indifferent due to our limited perspective, but it is important to understand that God does not personally do everything, as omnificence is not a characteristic of the Universal Father. [4] God's love does not entail predestined decisions; man has relative freedom of choice. [5]

Jews saw God in almost everything that happened, but it is our mission to help men solve their spiritual problems and not ascribe all misunderstandings to the Father. [6]
