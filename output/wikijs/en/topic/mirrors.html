Confucius taught that earthly civilization mirrors the eternal heavenly order. [1]

Your spiritual capacity is measured by your faith and love, while your human strength is revealed in your ability to resist grudges and brooding; defeat is the honest mirror of your true self. [2] Each superuniverse reflects the nature and character of the presiding Master Spirit, guiding evolutionary growth in a profound manner. [3] Ethics mirrors internal spiritual progress, reflecting the highest recognized values and conceptions of God. [4]

Primary seconaphim, assigned to the Ancients of Days, serve as living mirrors reflecting the instant and unerring responses of beings across vast distances in the superuniverse. [5] Primitives revered mirrors with superstitious awe, as they believed it could separate the soul from the body. [6] To obtain heavenly help, forsake pride and come with a clean heart that reflects truth like a mirror. [7] Philosophy mirrors divinity's wisdom for all to absorb. [8]
