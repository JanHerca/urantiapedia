High Commissioners accompany Paradise Sons on bestowal missions and are encountered on universe assemblies and missions of the Sons of God. [1]

As race commissioners, the High Commissioners act as friends of the court, advocating for mercy, justice, and fair treatment for all mortal races on the planets they serve. [2] High Commissioners in group Universe Aids. [3] High Commissioners are recorded in the Uversa personality register. [4] Over 1.5 billion High Commissioners in Nebadon are ascendant beings of long experience and great service to their native realm. [5] Spirit-fused mortals may become High Commissioners serving the local universe in the ascension career beyond Adjuster fusion. [6] High Commissioners work with court advisor seraphim to ensure charges against mortals are stated justly and adjudicated mercifully. [7]
