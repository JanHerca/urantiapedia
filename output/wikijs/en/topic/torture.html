In ancient initiation ceremonies, self-torture and painful cutting were used to harden and impress youths. [1]

The olden ideas of self-discipline embraced physical torture, with priests teaching the virtue of suffering through castration, and Hebrews, Hindus, and Buddhists devoutly following this doctrine of humiliation. [2]
