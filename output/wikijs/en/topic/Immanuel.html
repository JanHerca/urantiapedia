Immanuel is the Union of Days numbered 611,121, assigned to Nebadon. [1] Immanuel is Michael's elder brother. [2] Immanuel means God with us, and is so called because he is the Father's representative at Michael's court. [3] He supervises the Faithful of Days, his co-ordinate brothers operating in the constellations. [4] Lucifer's rebellion. Knew of Lucifer's disaffection for 500 years. [5] Counseled Michael to allow the rebellion to annihilate itself. [6]

Regarding Michael's bestowal

Immanuel gave Michael instructions prior to his final bestowal on Urantia. [7] Immanuel would guarantee the integrity of Michael's personality during the time of his incarnation until the arrival of the Adjuster. [8] Assumed authority during Michael's incarnations. [9] Immanuel conveyed several messages to Jesus, the first during his childhood at his first Passover. [10] After baptism Jesus remembered Immanuel's instructions. [11] Immanuel sent Michael to Jesus the message of the release of the bestowal, indicating that he could terminate it if he wished. [12]

Suggested to Jesus that he continue his bestowal life to the end, accepting the will of the Father, who preferred that Jesus continue his human life to the end. [13]

The sovereignty of Michael

Immanuel has never acknowledged subordination to Michael, he is the only being in Nebadon not subordinate to Michael. [14] He participates in executive affairs but only when called upon. [15]

He would have left Nebadon if Michael had asserted premature sovereignty, but that did not happen, for Michael desired to attain his sovereignty by experience. [16] Michael asked him to remain forever in Nebadon once he had gained his sovereignty. [17]
