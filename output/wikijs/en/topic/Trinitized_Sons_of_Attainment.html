The Trinitized Sons of Attainment, including the Mighty Messengers, Those High in Authority, and Those without Name and Number, were ascendant mortals who attained Paradise, were embraced by the Paradise Trinity, and subsequently assigned to superuniverse service. [1]

Composition

The Trinitized Sons of Attainment, comprised of Mighty Messengers, Those High in Authority, and Those without Name and Number, are glorified ascendant mortals embraced by the Paradise Trinity and assigned to supernal service in the superuniverse government. [2]

  • Mighty Messengers.
  • Those High in Authority.
  • Those without Name and Number.

Functions

Trinitized Sons of Attainment, having been trinitized in the divine embrace of the Paradise Deities, function as co-ordinates of Stationary Sons of Trinity, realizing unrealized potentials in the present universe age. [3] In Paradise classification they are in the group 7. Trinitized Sons of Attainment. [4]

The Trinitized Sons of Attainment are responsible for addressing all issues stemming from the origin of individuals, races, or worlds, as the significance of origin is crucial in cosmic advancement and ethical considerations. [5]

Selection for Trinitized Sons of Attainment is inherently and automatically based on personal experience and spiritual values, ensuring diverse, non-standardized beings with equal authority. [6]

Tertiary seconaphim assigned to Trinitized Sons of Attainment are created serially and utilized interchangeably by superuniverse administrators and ascendant sons. [7]

After achieving Trinity embrace, the Trinitized Sons of Attainment briefly train under Eternals of Days before serving the Ancients of Days in the superuniverses. [8] Trinity embrace transforms mortal finaliters into Trinitized Sons, endowing them with ultimate achievements as 7th stage spirits. [9]

Trinitized Sons of Attainment are glorified ascendant mortals who, after passing through Havona, are embraced by the Trinity and serve the Ancients of Days in the superuniverses. [10]

The trio of Trinitized Sons of Attainment presents the viewpoint of mortal creatures through an ascendant commission including a Mighty Messenger, One High in Authority, and One without Name and Number. [11]
