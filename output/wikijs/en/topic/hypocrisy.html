The Father loathes hypocrisy in all its forms. [1] Beware of the leaven of the Pharisees, hypocrisy born of prejudice and nurtured in traditional bondage, for all shall be revealed in due time. [2]

Sanhedrin is disloyal to the truths they profess, rejecting the new light offered and seeking destruction for those preaching the gospel of the kingdom. [3]

The sin of rulers is saying what is good but not doing it, as exemplified by their binding heavy burdens on the people without helping them bear the weight. [4]
