Early Christians greeted with a holy kiss, embodying a fellowship of worship, sharing, and hopeful anticipation of Jesus' return. [1] Judas betrayed Jesus with a kiss, sealing his deceitful act with false words of respect and loyalty. [2] The ancient origin of social kissing lies in the ceremony of saliva exchange during adoption rituals. [3]
