Lot went to Salem with his uncle Abraham after invitations from Melchizedek. [1] Moses gathered descendants of Abraham, Nahor, and Lot into a self-sustaining nation of pastoral warriors. [2]

Lot chose to pursue his passion for trade and animal husbandry in Sodom over partaking in Abraham's grand plan to conquer Canaan and establish Salem's rule. [3]
