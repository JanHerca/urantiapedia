Affectionate parents never called upon to forgive children. [1] Children commanded to honor parents. [2] Disclose to children the love of Paradise Parent. [3] Divine love is source of parental love. [4] Highly educational and disciplinary responsibilities of parenthood. [5] Importance to children of love between parents. [6] Instinct to care for feeble infants. [7] Leaving parents for sake of kingdom. [8] No ascending mortal can escape parenthood experience. [9] No mortal reproduction after life in flesh. [10] Parent-child relationship is most tender and beautiful in mortal experience. [11] Privilege of giving sons to the Father. [12] Relationship with God is Father-child. [13] Rewarded by excellency of their children. [14] Selflessness inherent in parental love. [15]
