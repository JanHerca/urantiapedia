Paradise Citizens acquire capacity for Supremacy comprehension through observation and discovery. [1] Ascenders on Paradise fraternize with over 3000 orders of supernal intelligences, including various types of Paradise Citizens and Transcendentalers. [2] The appointed times and places for worship on Paradise cannot adequately accommodate the overflow of spiritual emotions of ascending beings. [3] Descending Paradise Citizens on the fourth Havona circuit achieve new comprehension of Creator Sons. [4]

The destiny of some Paradise Citizens is to serve in the Corps of Trinitized Finaliters, alongside the creature-trinitized sons on the inner circuit of Havona. [5]

Finaliters of the Mortal Finality Corps accompany secondary supernaphim ministers to the seven planetary circuits of the central universe when in Havona, serving both time pilgrims and training Paradise pilgrims. [6]

The first group of secondary supernaphim, the pilgrim helpers, welcome ascenders to the central universe as the Paradise pilgrims, who first landed on Havona in times of Grandfanda, begin their eternal journey. [7]

The gracious and brilliant Paradise Citizens assist the dedicated angels in their service of companionship, providing support to all beings on Paradise, particularly the ascendant mortals and those who are alone on the central Isle. [8] Paradise Citizens' personality registers differ across the different groups of beings. [9]

Paradise Citizens may serve as Evangels of Light attached to mortal finaliter companies, participating in transient finaliter assignments before resuming their former status. [10]

All Paradise Citizens, outside of the Trinity Teacher Sons and possibly the Inspired Trinity Spirits, are of fixed numbers, with new beings no longer being brought into existence. [11]

Paradise Citizens are nontime beings aware of the sequence of events, residing in a nonspatial realm where motion is volitional and distance has relative significance. [12] Paradise Citizens, often assisting Paradise Companions, are dedicated to providing companionship to all beings on the central Isle. [13]

Paradise Citizens pass through Havona from the first circuit outward, becoming fraternal beings of mutual contact and sympathy with angelic specialists of the billion study worlds. [14]

The high Paradise Citizens who were once executive assistants to the Eternals of Days are preparing creature-trinitized sons for future work on the inner Havona circuit. [15]

Trinitization by parents of creature-trinitized sons results in inseparable unity in assignments and functions within the Corps of the Finality and Architects of the Master Universe. [16]
