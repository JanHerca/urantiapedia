7 types

All living things strive for. [1] Attainment. [2] All perfecting finites eventually attain perfection. [3] Believers destined to achieve perfection in Jesus. [4] By fusion of creature mind with some fragment of pre-Trinity spirit. [5] By grace and individual effort. [6] Creature and Creator collaborate in. [7] Dependent on faith, love, desire to be like God. [8] Divine plan for. [9] Every facility and all power provided to insure. [10] Father’s supreme invitation-command. [11] Imitation of God is key to perfection; in spirit, now in doing Father’s will. [12] Not by passivity. [13] On 3rd circuit of Havona. [14] Progressive, except in Havona. [15]

Cosmic complement to evolution. [16] Defect-interruptions in perfection permit mind to glimpse reality. [17] Father’s eternal perfection. [18] Finaliters have attained. [19]

Hunger for perfection

Aroused by Adjuster. [20] Is desire to know God and be like him. [21] Required for comprehending faith. [22]

Not strange that everything does not work in. [23] Of purpose upon reaching Havona. [24] Of spiritual self-restraint equals complete personal freedom. [25] Struggle for perfection also pertains to physical level. [26] Undiluted on Paradise. [27]
