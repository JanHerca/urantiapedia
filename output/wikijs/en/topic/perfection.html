Types

There are seven conceivable types of perfection when considering all phases and forms of relativity. [1] In the divine universe, all living things strive for perfection of energy control and regulation. [2]

Attainment in the Father's eternal creation is a ceaseless progression towards spiritual perfection, leading the children of light from glory to glory. [3]

All finite beings, whether primary or secondary, will ultimately achieve perfection through growth and attainment, though with a time delay inherent in the evolutionary process. [4]

Believers are destined to achieve perfection in Jesus, who fully comprehends and embodies the perfection of man and reveals a God of perfection to humanity. [5] Perfected existence is achieved through the fusion of mortal minds with pre-Trinity spirit fragments. [6] Perfection is an acquirement through grace and individual effort, a genuine possession born from personal engagement with one's surroundings. [7] Creature and Creator collaborate in achieving universe perfection through the co-creation of eternal finaliters in the incarnations of God's Sons. [8] The essentials of spiritual destiny are dependent on faith, love, and the desire to be like God. [9]

The Eternal Son and the Father are in everlasting liaison in the successful prosecution of the divine plan for the creation, evolution, ascension, and perfection of will creatures. [10] Every facility and all power provided to insure your ultimate attainment of divine perfection in the family of divine sonship. [11] The Father’s supreme invitation-command is to be perfect, even as he is perfect in Paradise. [12] Imitating God is the key to perfection, survival, and service in eternity through choosing to do the Father's will. [13] Perfection is not achieved through idleness, but through purposeful and intelligent action in service to creation. [14]

On the 3rd circuit of Havona, the creatures of time and material personality have ascended to perfection, proving the feasibility of the ascension plan and demonstrating the righteousness of the command to be perfect. [15]

Perfection is a progressive attainment in all realms except Havona, where a pattern of perfection exists, with Creator Sons employing a variety of methods to organize and evolve their local universes. [16]

Hunger for perfection

The divine Adjuster arouses within you a hunger and thirst for divine perfection. [17]

Spiritual progress depends on maintaining a living connection with true spiritual forces and bearing spiritual fruit, driven by the desire to know God and be like him. [18] Perfection hunger in man’s heart is required for comprehending faith. [19]

Goals

The cosmic complement to evolution is the interaction between the perfect creatures of Paradise-Havona and the evolved finaliters from the universes, ultimately leading to the sublime heights of creature status. [20] The defects in the natural world allow the human mind to glimpse the reality of divinity, despite appearing imperfect due to limited perspective. [21] The Universal Father's eternal perfection is forever beyond mortal comprehension, steadfast in his unchanging purpose and divine nature. [22]

The mortal finaliters have attained the present limit of spirit progression and achieved the fullness of Deity worship, yet they have not reached finality of ultimate spirit status or experiential Deity attainment. [23] It is not surprising that missteps occur in the affairs of the gigantic creation that is our imperfect universe. [24] The pilgrim arrives on Havona with only perfection of purpose, fulfilling the Universal Father's command to be perfect. [25] Attainment of perfection through spiritual self-restraint leads to ultimate personal freedom in the universe. [26] The struggle for perfection on the physical level is essential for the completion of the evolution of the Almighty's physical control. [27] Perfection is undiluted on Paradise, where it is absolute and not attenuated by Trinity manifestations in the central universe. [28]
