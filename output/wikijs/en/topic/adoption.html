In antiquity it was the custom to kill all strangers; to gain friendship you need to obtain adoption into the clan. [1] Ancient ceremonies of adoption mean blood drinking or saliva exchange. [2] This custom made sure that someone be available after death for doing offerings. [3] Prince’s staff kept five hundred adopted children in homes. [4] To sojourn in the Garden a Urantian had to be “adopted.”. [5]
