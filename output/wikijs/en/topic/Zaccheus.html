Zaccheus, the chief publican in Jericho, eagerly climbed a sycamore tree to catch a glimpse of Jesus, who then surprised him by choosing to stay at his house for the night. [1] Jesus, upon visiting Zaccheus, taught and brought salvation to his home, declaring him a son of Abraham. [2]
