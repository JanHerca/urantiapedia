They communicated with Michael during Michael's time in the tomb. [1] They certified the mortal transit of Jesus. [2] The Urantia Papers were produced according to a mandate from the Uversa Ancients of Days. [3]

Ancients and Lucifer's rebellion

Lucifer attacked the Ancients of Days of Uversa calling them foreign potentates. [4] Lucifer was finally stopped by agents of the Ancients of Days from Uversa. [5] They authorized the imprisonment of all those involved in the rebellion except Satan, who was authorized to make periodic visits to the fallen worlds. [6] Confirmed the exceptional control that the Most Highs have assumed over the rebellious worlds. [7] They would have decreed the instant extinction of the rebels if their rebellion had occurred during Michael’s bestowal. [8] There are still some matters of the Lucifer rebellion to be evaluated before the Ancients of Days of Uversa decide to issue a verdict. [9]
