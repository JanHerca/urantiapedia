In the main continental nation of a nearby inhabited planet, all able-bodied citizens over eighteen work either at home, on farms, in recognized industries, on public works, or in compulsory labor in mines, making labor more honorable. [1] Jesus worked in the mines at Iron, healing a leper along the way despite being urged not to. [2]
