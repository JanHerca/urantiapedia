Gravita is also known as universe power. [1] They are the material organizations in superuniverses and are dual in constitution. [2]

Universe power includes all forms of energy which, while still responding to Paradise gravity, are directly responsive to linear gravity. This is the electronic level of energy-matter and all subsequent evolutions thereof. [3] Gravita comprise 30 energy systems. [4] Constitutes the Postgravity stages of Paradise gravity response. [5] Power centers and physical controllers partially control it. [6]

It is working energy useful to superuniverses. First the space-force is changed into space-energy and then into gravity controlled energy, energy that can be channeled and controlled by the Universal Power Directors. [7]
