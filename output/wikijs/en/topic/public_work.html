Apostles begin a new work as they depart from Bethsaida with Jesus on the first day of the week, January 19, in A.D. 27. [1]

Jesus’ plans for the remainder of his Urantia bestowal were carefully formulated day by day in the hills, leading to his ultimate decision to always choose the Father's will in his earth career. [2]
