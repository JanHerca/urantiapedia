Jesus graduated from the synagogue school at age thirteen, becoming a “son of the commandment” and a responsible citizen of Israel, attending Passover in Jerusalem. [1] Upon graduation, Jesus became a “son of the commandment,” participating in temple discussions with candor and hunger for knowledge. [2]
