The Wisdom of Solomon reflects the influence of both Platonic philosophy and Stoic doctrines among Hellenized Jewish beliefs. [1] Fate and chance dictate outcomes, as seen in the Wisdom of Solomon's statement that “the race is not to the swift”. [2]
