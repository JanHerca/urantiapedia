The apostles, interpreting the Master's words to justify their intense longings, believed Jesus would return to establish the kingdom after a brief visit with his Father. [1] Moral values do not grow out of wish fulfillment. [2] Wisdom comes from facing facts and adjusting them to ideals, not from preferring optimistic illusions to reality. [3]
