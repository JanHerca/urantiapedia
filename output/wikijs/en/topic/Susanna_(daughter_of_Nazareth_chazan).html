Susanna, daughter of Nazareth chazan, was appointed as chief of the women's corps by Jesus on January 16, an astonishing decision that empowered ten women to minister in his kingdom. [1] At Jesus’ 16th appearance, he instructed his disciples to proclaim the gospel of the kingdom and live as God-revealing sons of God on earth. [2]
