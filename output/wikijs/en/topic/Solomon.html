The ancestry of Joseph, the father of Jesus, did not include David or Solomon and did not trace directly back to Adam. [1]

Solomon, son of King David and Bathsheba, continued his father’s tyranny and taxation while building a temple, navy, and harem of 1000, following a legacy of corruption and power struggles in ancient Israel. [2]
