Internationalism, while a step in the right direction, can never bring lasting peace as long as nations hold onto their sovereign powers. [1]

The post-Adamic epoch is characterized by the dispensation of internationalism, as race blending leads to the decline of nationalism and the emergence of global brotherhood through representative government and a universal educational system. [2] Internationalism should be fostered through language, trade, sports, and religion. [3]
