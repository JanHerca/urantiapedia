Most High observers are empowered to exercise absolute and arbitrary authority over every celestial being on a planet and seize planetary government in times of crisis. [1] The instrumentality of Most Highs’ rule in kingdoms of men is exercised by Vorondadek observers on isolated worlds, such as Urantia. [2]

Vorondadek Sons serve as ambassadors, consuls, and Most High observers in local universes, exercising sovereign powers in critical situations, including the rare ability to exercise absolute authority over celestial beings on dark, isolated planets. [3]
