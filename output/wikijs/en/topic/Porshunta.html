Porshunta, the master mind of the extinct orange race, led a great revival of higher living at Armageddon as the oracle of “The Great Teacher.”. [1] Porshunta is one of 24 counselors for “The Great Teacher” worshiped by the extinct orange race she leads as their oracle. [2]
