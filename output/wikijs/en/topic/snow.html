Increased snowfall in highlands caused floods in Euphrates valley, forcing inhabitants to flee to eastern highlands. [1] No two snowflakes are ever alike, just like children and personalities - always unique within their forms. [2] Elevated northern highlands and shifting ocean currents caused glaciers to form on vast areas of North America and northern Europe.
