The interpreters of ethics play a crucial role in aiding Paradise arrivals as they navigate an array of majestic beings from the attainment of residential status to induction into the Corps of Mortal Finaliters, many of whom have already been encountered on the circuits of Havona. [1] Primary supernaphim are the interpreters of ethics. [2] Unions of Souls in reflective synchrony with interpreters of ethics help guide intelligent beings towards ethical relationships. [3]
