The threefold Deity personalization has seven associative possibilities. [1] Number seven is basic in the universe to transmission of character. [2] There are seven colors in natural spectrum. [3] The domain of life is characterized by number seven. There are seven architectural types of life design. [4] Number seven is fundamental to universe organization. Universe is operated in seven grand divisions. [5] In natural philosophy number seven was considered fundamental to nature. [6] In periodic table, physical and chemical properties recur by periods of seven. [7] Secoraphic types recur serially by seven. [8] Prematter has a sevenfold electronic organization. [9]
