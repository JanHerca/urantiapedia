The advanced Chinese civilization in Honan was greatly influenced by the infiltration of half Andonite and half Andite Andites from the west, stimulating the rapid development of the latent tendencies of the superior Chinese stocks. [1] Fifteen thousand years ago, Andites infiltrated Honan from the west, blending with the Andonite settlers in the progressive Chinese settlements. [2] The hydraulic problems of agriculturists in Honan required group cooperation to solve, promoting peace among farming groups. [3]
