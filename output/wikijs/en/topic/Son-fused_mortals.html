Adjusters serve mortal beings during life in flesh, building up souls and guiding them through ascension to the mansion worlds, even if they are not candidates for fusion with their Adjusters. [1] The Ancients of Days convene a court of inquiry to certify the lack of guilt in inhibiting Father fusion for Son-fused mortals. [2] As ascending sons, Son-fused mortals journey through the educational worlds of the universe, striving for identity fusion with their Adjusters. [3] Ascendant Son-fused mortals assist Celestial Guardians across the major sectors of Orvonton. [4] Spirit-fused mortals rise to the source of spirit endowment through fusion implemented in Reflective Spirits of higher universes. [5] Union with the individualized spirit of a Creator Son transforms a creature into a Son-fused mortal, a concept difficult to describe with words. [6] Son-fused mortals in personality registers unite with the individualized spirit of a Creator Son. [7]

The Trinitized Sons of Selection and Ambassadors are recruited from Spirit-fused and Son-fused mortals who have ascended to Paradise and are commissioned as representatives of the Ancients of Days in the superuniverses. [8] The purpose of Son-fused mortals is to provide each superuniverse with a permanent ascendant citizenship. [9]

Son-fused mortals, numbering less than one million in the superuniverse of Orvonton, sometimes journey to Paradise but are primarily confined to their nativity superuniverse, equal to Adjuster-fused associates in every way. [10] Some Son-fused mortals ascend to Paradise after undergoing necessary transformations. [11] The Son-fused mortals on Uversa supervise the abandonters in administering routine affairs. [12]

When synchronization issues impede fusion with the Father, the Creator Son's referees convene to investigate and certify, leading to the immediate fusion of the mortal with the spirit of the Creator Son. [13] Urantia mortals will not become Son- and Spirit-fused. [14]

Son-fused mortals, residing on Uversa, contribute to the eventual settlement of the seventh superuniverse, alongside their Spirit-fused counterparts, while Father-fused beings attain the Universal Father as their ultimate destiny. [15]
