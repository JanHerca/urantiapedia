Concept of I AM

Hypothesis of all that is unknowable of God. [1] Hypothetical undifferentiated stasis of original infinity. [2] Theoretical, philosophic concession. [3] As unity, duality, triunity, and sevenfold. [4] Permeates total infinity. [5] Full self-realization embraces all personalities. [6] Signifies also I WAS and I WILL BE. [7] Father revealed himself to Moses as I AM. [8] Cf: concept of Buddha Eternal. [9]

In the past

Became Father of Original Son, Source of Paradise. [10] Exists before existentials and after all experientials. [11] Spirit expression perfectly unified in. [12] In Supreme, I AM has achieved liberation from limitations inherent in infinity. [13]

In the future

Final unification of energy-spirit divergence in. [14] Personality of I AM partially experiencible on spiritual levels even by mortals. [15] Actualization of Trinity of Trinities would make I AM experientially attainable. [16] Union of Unqualified, Universal, and Deity Absolutes. [17]

Primal cause

Absolute primal causation in infinity. [18] All reality has origin in. [19] One and only self-caused fact in universe. [20] Original monothetic Creator personality; First Source and Center; Universal Father. [21] Primal cause and source of Trinity and Absolute. [22] The Infinity. [23] The Original is source manifestation of I AM. [24] Unqualified Absolute is unrevealed cosmic infinity of. [25]
