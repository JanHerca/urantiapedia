The angels promote teamwork and teach that few duties exist for the lone servant in the universe. [1] A servant is not greater than his master, learn the lesson of humble service from the example set by the Master in washing his disciples' feet. [2] The parable of the slothful servant teaches a lesson about discipleship and accountability in the gospel of the kingdom. [3]
