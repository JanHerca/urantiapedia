The ancients believed in powerful love charms made from various mystical ingredients. [1] The Andite contribution to the evolving relations between the sexes includes increasing love, romance, and personal selection in premarital courtship. [2]

Imaginative courtship fueling divorce rates, exacerbated by women's autonomy, economic independence, and easy divorce as a regression towards primitive societal stages. [3]

Prince’s staff fostered courtship to bring about intertribal marriages and refine military war dances for valuable social ends, introducing competitive games to the serious early tribes. [4]
