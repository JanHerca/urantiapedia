Martha, Jesus' second sister, was born on Thursday night, September 13, A.D. 3. [1] Martha, an expert weaver, helped alleviate financial pressure for the family. [2] Jesus’ relation with Martha was fairly good, but he had trouble at home with Joseph and Jude. [3] Martha, slow in thought and action but dependable and efficient, was the steady force among her siblings. [4] Jesus returned to Nazareth in October to attend Martha's wedding, before leaving for over two years until Simon and Jude's double wedding. [5]
