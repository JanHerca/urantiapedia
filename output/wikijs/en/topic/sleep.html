Adjusters may detach during sleep, allowing for experiences beyond the first heaven of the mansion worlds. [1] Supreme and self-acting Adjusters sometimes effect spiritual transformations during sleep. [2] It was custom to speak of death as sleep, but Jesus clarified: “Lazarus is dead.”. [3] The ancients believed that souls left the body during sleep, with dreams being the experiences of the absent soul. [4] Young Jesus' disturbed, revolting dreams greatly upset his sleep and left his heart torn. [5]

Early ownership of sleeping space evolved into the tribal chiefs assigning homesites, eventually leading to ownership being conferred by a fire site and established title to the adjacent land. [6]
