The fourth order Supreme Power Centers downstep superuniverse power for constellations and systems. [1]

One hundred power centers of the fourth order are stationed on each local universe capital to modify power circuits and assist in energy distribution within the constellations and systems. [2]

Local universe centers assist Creator Sons in universe organization by providing intensified lanes of energy for interplanetary communication and playing a crucial role in energy mobilization and universe material organization. [3] Presented by an Archangel in collaboration with the Chief of Nebadon Power Centers. [4] Uversa personality register records local universe centers. [5] Local universe centers provide energy lanes for interplanetary communication, facilitating efficient transmission between key inhabited locations. [6]
