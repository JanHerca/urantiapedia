Ancient origin of sacred meal. [1] At first, Remembrance Supper was celebrated as social meal with sacrament at end. [2] Commemorates bestowal of Spirit of Truth. [3] Establishing. [4] Jesus ordained we gather together to partake. [5] Master really present at. [6] New Passover; only ceremony associated with Jesus’ life. [7] Refreshing truce between ego and altruistic urge. [8]
