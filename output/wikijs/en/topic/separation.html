Religion functions best when the religious group is isolated from all other groups to focus on the spiritual membership of the kingdom of heaven. [1]

It is time to openly break with the religious rulers in Jerusalem, as demonstrated through Jesus' deliberate challenge to the Sanhedrin and Jewish teachers, provoking discussions and compelling them to take notice of his miraculous works. [2]
