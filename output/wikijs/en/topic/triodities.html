Triodities are directly concerned with the cosmic appearance of experiential Deities such as the Supreme, Ultimate, and Absolute, while triunities focus on the functional unification of infinity. [1] The existential triodities encompass all absolute actuality and all infinite potentiality, originating from the Paradise Trinity. [2]

The growth of Supremacy derives from the triodities, Trinity, Almighty, and the Conjoint Actor, leading to a dual motion intensively towards Paradise and extensively towards the Absolutes. [3] Infinity is transmitively associated in triodities, allowing for new cosmic experientials despite its eternal presence in the seven Absolutes. [4]

Non-Father triune relationships, known as triodities, are distinct from Father triunities and involve interrelationships among absolute actuals and potentials that contribute to the functional unification of infinity and the emergence of experiential Deities. [5] The Supreme, divine channel of triodities, guides the cosmic evolution towards unity with Paradise Deity. [6]

Triodities of actuality

The Triodity of Actuality, comprised of Eternal Son, Infinite Spirit, and Paradise, is the master pattern for all existents in the cosmos, centering spirit, mind, and energy in their interrelationship. [7]

The center of existents in the cosmos lies within the triodity of actuality, where spirit, mind, and energy converge through the Son, the Spirit, and Paradise. [8]

Triodities of potentiality

The Triodities represent the all-powerful activator, universal mechanizer, and associate co-ordinator of the supremely unified universe of universes. [9]

The interassociation of infinity reservoirs yields the integration of all latent energy reality, infinitely potent in spirit, mindal, and cosmic aspects. [10]
