The Ancients of Days confirmed and continue to uphold the assumption of control by the Edentia Most Highs over the rebellious worlds of Satania, including jurisdiction over the wicked rebel Lucifer. [1]

The Most Highs of Norlatiadek broadcast to local systems daily, offering continual foretastes of advancing beauty and grandeur to mortal survivors on their eternal journey inward. [2] Children of Adam under 20 became wards of the Most Highs of Norlatiadek, as they chose to go to Edentia with their parents or remain on Urantia. [3]

Vorondadek Son #617,318 is the Constellation Father in Norlatiadek, having served in various constellations in Nebadon before assuming his current role. [4] The Faithful of Days advised the Most Highs of Norlatiadek to allow rebels free course so that evil may be swiftly uprooted. [5]

The headquarters of the Most Highs of Norlatiadek is the massive architectural sphere of Edentia, centerpiece of a cluster of 771 spheres that house the government of the constellation. [6]

Lucifer's disaffection, known by the Most Highs of Norlatiadek 500 years before rebellion, originated in his own mind without suggestion from any other being, despite his intimate and cordial relations with the Creator Son. [7]

Lanaforge's message to the Constellation Father of Norlatiadek assured that every ascendant mortal from Jerusem emerged triumphant and victorious from the fiery trial, a security against rebellion and safeguard against sin. [8]

Melchizedek introduced the concept of El Elyon, the Most High, as the universal Deity to Abraham and his followers, who believed in this expanded idea of God. [9] During his time in the tomb on Urantia, the Constellation Father of Norlatiadek received instructions from Michael. [10] The Most Highs of Norlatiadek find residence in Jerusem circles during visits of inspection to the local system. [11]

The rotation of the Most Highs on Edentia remains suspended from the time of the Lucifer rebellion, indicating their continuous reign until Lucifer and his associates are dealt with. [12]

The Most Highs of Norlatiadek maintain supervision over Satania, where no changes have been made in system administration since the upheaval, with Lanaforge showing great interest in world welfare. [13]

Actions

The Constellation Father of Edentia appeared to Jesus and Gabriel in person, declaring the completion of records and bestowal release for his Urantia incarnation. [14]

Approved by the Constellation Fathers of Edentia, members of Van's supreme court were directed to oversee all matters of earthly concern in the supreme court of tribal coordination and racial cooperation. [15] The Most Highs of Norlatiadek authorized dispatch of Material Sons to Urantia for biologic uplift. [16] The confirmed rule of Melchizedek receivers was overseen by the Most Highs of Norlatiadek in administering the affairs of Urantia. [17] The Most Highs of Norlatiadek consent to executions by conciliating commissions as the ultimate authority. [18]

The plea by Melchizedek Urantia receivers for provision from the Most Highs of Edentia was dismissed, leaving them in sole charge of maintaining truth on Urantia until the arrival of a bestowal Son. [19] The Most Highs of Norlatiadek exercise special care over fallen worlds like Urantia in Satania, aiding in their oversight and guidance. [20]

During the chaotic period of Lucifer's rebellion, the Most Highs of Norlatiadek imposed quarantine on Satania, testing the loyalty of beings in the system. [21]

The message to Urantia Life Carriers from the Most Highs of Norlatiadek forbade interference with established life patterns, with instructions to abstain from intervening in human progress. [22] The Most Highs of Norlatiadek ordered internment of rebel midwayers, ensuring they are no longer able to roam this world in mischief. [23]

The Most Highs of Norlatiadek seized planetary authority upon the Lucifer rebellion, temporarily exercising control over the wayward world of Urantia due to system and planetary conditions stemming from the rebellion. [24]

The Most Highs of Norlatiadek selected Adam and Eve for their mission on Urantia, after receiving approval from Lanaforge and the Melchizedek examiners. [25] After sustaining Van's appeal, the decision supporting him was marooned in transit, unnoticed until recently discovered by Urantia midwayers. [26]
