Chemical facts

Chemistry has its ancient origin in alchemy. [1] Chemical behavior is wholly dependent on the activity of the freely revolving electrons. [2]

Chemism, in origin, nature, and destiny, is same thing as light, heat, electricity, magnetism, energy, and matter. An there are other material realities as yet undiscovered. [3]

At any moment of time, in the blood stream of any human being there exists the possibility of upward of 15 millions chemical reactions between the hormone output of a dozen ductless glands. [4]

Beyond chemistry

The highest chemistry could not predict that the union of two gaseous hydrogen atoms and one of oxygen would result in a new and qualitatively superadditive substance—liquid water. [5] Chemistry alone cannot explain how a human being evolved. The laws of physics are not responsive to training; they are immutable and unchanging. [6] The concepts of truth, beauty, and goodness are not inherent in either physics or chemistry. [7]

We cannot pray to a chemical formula, supplicate a mathematical equation, worship a hypothesis, confide in a postulate, commune with a process, serve an abstraction, or hold loving fellowship with a law. Due to this God is a personal Deity. [8]
