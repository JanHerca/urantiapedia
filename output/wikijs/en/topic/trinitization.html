Accomplishes what otherwise is nonattainable. [1]

By creatures

Adamic-finaliter pairs very successful at trinitization. [2] Always successful between finaliters and Paradise-Havoners (22:7.10, 14). Apparently an unlimited technique. [3] By 2 finaliters is paradisiacal effort to actualize original identical concept of Almighty Supreme; sometimes fails. [4] By finaliters and Paradise-Havoners repercusses in Supreme-Ultimate Mind. [5] By Paradise-Havoners. [6] Creature-trinitized sons in personality registers. [7] Engaged in only by Deities, finaliters, Paradise Citizens and Havoners. [8] Parents thereafter spiritually inseparable; participate only once. [9] Special corps exists for mixed finaliter and ParadiseHavoner parents. [10] Supreme creative performance of spiritual beings. [11] Unbelievably long time sometimes consumed. [12]

By Deities, trinitization constitutes authority to represent Trinity. [13] Precipitates unrealized potentials out of future time. [14] Secret of Vicegerington, Solitarington. [15] Technique by which Father divests himself of unqualified spirit personality. [16]
