Trinitization may accomplish what is otherwise nonattainable, leading to the potential union of the Supreme-Ultimate and the factualization of God the Absolute. [1]

By creatures

Adamic-finaliter pairs are integral leaders in finaliter companies, greatly enhancing their trinitization potential and service capabilities. [2] Always successful between finaliters and Paradise-Havoners. [3]

Trinitization, apparently an unlimited technique, raises questions about the future administration of the grand universe and the role of Solitary Messengers in the service of sons of destiny. [4]

Trinitization, constituting the secret of authority to represent the Trinity, is an extraordinary undertaking that sometimes fails despite paradisiacal efforts to actualize an original identical concept of Almighty Supreme. [5]

The trinitization by finaliters and Paradise-Havoners repercusses in the Supreme-Ultimate Mind, creating supercreational sons of destiny who embody aspects of the unrevealed master universe function. [6] Paradise-Havoners trinitize high spiritual aspects of the Supreme Being. [7] Creature-trinitized sons in personality registers ascend alongside other trinitized Sons. [8] Trinitization is a unique adventure of concept-identity, engaged in only by Deities, finaliters, Paradise Citizens, and Havoners. [9] The parents of creature-trinitized sons are spiritually inseparable, participating in the process only once. [10]

A special corps exists for mixed finaliter and Paradise-Havoner parents who, though unified in their assignments, are still recognized as two personalities in the Corps of the Finality and of the Architects of the Master Universe. [11]

The act of trinitization, a supreme creative performance of spiritual beings, remains a secret only understood by those who have passed through unique experiences on Vicegerington and Solitarington. [12]

Faithful beings sometimes spend an unbelievably long time bringing their chosen universal truth into being, with some facing ultimate disappointment despite their efforts. [13]

By Deities

Trinitization grants authority to represent the Trinity, a secret known in Vicegerington. [14]

Trinitized Sons of Attainment, trinitized in divine embrace, function as co-ordinate associates of Stationary Sons, precipitating unrealized potentials out of future time in the present universe age. [15]

The secret of authority to represent the Trinity is found in the secrets of Vicegerington and Solitarington, where trinitization reveals beings created or eternalized by the Paradise Trinity. [16]

Through the technique of trinitization, the Universal Father achieves freewill liberation by divesting himself of unqualified spirit personality, allowing for the endless bestowal of himself upon his ever-expanding universe. [17]
