Transition is always accompanied by confusion, and there will be little tranquillity in the religious world until the great struggle between the three contending philosophies of religion is ended. [1] The difficulties of religious transition create challenges but also opportunities for growth and harmony in human fellowship. [2]

Forewarn all believers to traverse the fringe of conflict during the transition from earthly to spiritual life, embracing responsibilities and obligations with the light burden of truth. [3] Great danger of breakdown during societal transition requires careful guidance from those well-versed in history and experience of social evolution. [4] Religion should stabilize ideals during dangerous times of transition to ensure a smooth evolution of civilization. [5]

A nation's intellectual level determines whether a mechanical age will liberate or devastate, as science guided by wisdom can successfully navigate the transition difficulties caused by unemployment from laborsaving machinery. [6]
