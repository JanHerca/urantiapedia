Neither do men light a candle and put it under a bushel, but on a candlestick; and it gives light to all who are in the house. [1] In the twentieth century, candles are still burned in death chambers, and men still sit up with the dead. [2] In the twentieth century candles still burn as a part of the ritual of many religious services, remnant of ancient fire worship. [3]
