All cosmic energy units execute their missions by revolving around the universal orbit as spheres in the endless circuits of the master universe, with only Havona at the center remaining stationary. [1]

Space bodies appear according to plans of Master Architects, reflecting the organization and principles governing the segmentation of the total energy charge of a superuniverse. [2] Rare collisions among giant space bodies are far less common than the disruptions of smaller bodies caused by gravitational forces. [3] Collisions among space bodies release energy but do not occur in established circuits. [4] God knows all things and calls them all by name, revealing his presence in the vast expanse of the universe. [5]

Space bodies of various origins form through concentric contraction rings, whirled stars, gravity-explosion planets, centrifugal planetary daughters, gravity-deficiency spheres, contractural stars, cumulative spheres, burned-out suns, collisional spheres, and architectural worlds.
