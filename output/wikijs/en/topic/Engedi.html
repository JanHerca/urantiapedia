Old city near to Dead Sea. Jesus and Abner visited Nazarite colony at Engedi. [1]

For two and a half years John lived at Engedi, and he persuaded most of the brotherhood that “the end of the age was at hand”; that “the kingdom of heaven was about to appear. [2] Engedi waas the southern headquarters of Nazarites and other ascetic herdsmen. [3]
