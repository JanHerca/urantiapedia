Finaliters are the high spirit personalities who have actually completed the Paradise ascension, and who have returned to the very worlds where we are beginning this long journey, as the pledge of assurance that we may and can complete the stupendous undertaking. Acting planetary chief of finaliters has the third seat in morontia temple, on the left of the Paradise Son seat. [1]

One or more companies of the mortal finaliters are constantly in service on Urantia. There is no domain of universe service to which they are not assigned; they function universally and with alternating and equal periods of assigned duty and free service. [2]

Although the finaliters and their structures are not ordinarily perceptible to morontia vision, we will be more than thrilled when the energy transformers and the Morontia Power Supervisors enable us to glimpse them. [3]

When a local universe is establish in light and life the finaliter corps, for the first time, acknowledges the jurisdiction of an extra-Paradise authority, the supreme council. Heretofore the finaliters have recognized no supervision this side of Paradise. [4]

Subsequent to our passage through the Paradise regime and after our admission to the Corps of the Finality, we are granted clearance for Sonarington since we are sons of God as well as ascenders—and we are even more. [5] The finaliters are invisible to mortals, as also is the Prince-Sovereign except when in the morontia temple. [6] Midsoniters are wards of finaliters. [7]

Midwayers are contact personalities for finaliters in light and life stages up to the fourth; very few of them ever remain beyond the fourth stage of light. [8]

Admission

None become finaliters without having passed through parental relationship. [9]

The Trinity oath of eternity is administered by the chief of Transcendentalers, the presiding head of the Architects of the Master Universe when finaliter arrives on Paradise. [10] We are mustered into the Mortal Corps of the Finality when we do the finality oath to Trinity. [11]

Destiny

Father-fused mortals are potential finaliters; their destination is the Universal Father, and him they do attain, but within the purview of the present universe age, finaliters, as such, are not destiny attainers. They remain unfinished creatures—sixth-stage spirits—and hence nonactive in the evolutionary domains of prelight-and-life status. When a mortal finaliter is Trinity embraced—becomes a Trinitized Son, such as a Mighty Messenger—then has that finaliter attained destiny, at least for the present universe age. [12] Finaliters will become 7th stage spirits, with attainment of the Supreme. [13] Finaliters have hitherto unattained wisdom after they are sometime inducted into the seventh stage of spirit existence. [14]

Through the ministry of the indwelling Adjusters the finaliters are enabled to find the Universal Father, but it is by these techniques of experience that such finaliters come really to know the Supreme Being, and they are destined to the service and the revelation of this Supreme Deity in and to the future universes of outer space. [15] Finaliters’s minds will unify with the then-actualized Supreme Being. [16] The seventh-stage-spirit destiny of the mortal finaliters is the group attainment of ultimate status. [17]

As finaliters we will be domiciled on Paradise, but Ascendington will be our home, even when we enter service in outer space. Through all eternity we will regard Ascendington as our home. When we become seventh-stage spirit beings, possibly we will give up our residential status on Paradise. [18]

The central universe is not only man’s established destiny, but it is also the starting place of the eternal career of the finaliters as they shall sometime be started out on the undisclosed and universal adventure in the experience of exploring the infinity of the Universal Father. [19] Finaliters may fully comprehend time-space divinity of God the Sevenfold. [20] May join at some point to Mighty Messengers group. [21] May join Those High in Authority. [22] May join Those without Name and Number. [23] They may realize the supremacy and the ultimacy of God. [24]

It is speculated that finaliters, in eternity, were eventually to become the children of the Deity Absolute. And while they may be able to grasp the ultimate, absonite nature of the Father, it is believed they will never be able to attain the superultimate levels of absolute Deity. It may be possible that the finaliters will partially attain the Deity Absolute, but even if they should, still in the eternity of eternities the problem of the Universal Absolute will continue to intrigue. [25]

Throughout Orvonton it is believed that a new type of creation is in process, an order of universes destined to become the scene of the future activities of the assembling Corps of the Finality. [26] Beyond doubt, the Creator Sons and their mortal children are preparing for some future and unknown universe service. [27] Finaliters will become the most effective universe administrators ever to be known in all creation. [28]

Nature of finaliters

Finaliters are sixth stage spirits, and their status is not final. [29] Finaliters of the Paradise corps are the experiential children of the Supreme. [30] Throughout all eternity an ascendant mortal will exhibit traits indicative of the presiding Spirit of his superuniverse of nativity. [31] Not until we are a finaliter, not until we have attained perfection of loyalty, can we self-realize finality of liberty. [32] Nature of the Paradise finaliters is the cosmic consequence of the blending of creature and Creator. [33]

For a finaliter there remains no possibility that such a righteous spirit would cast any negative shadow of potential evil when exposed to the searching luminosity of the divine light of the infinite Rulers of Paradise. [34] The Corps of the Finality embrace, among others, those mortals of time and space who have attained perfection in all that pertains to the will of God. [35] Only Adjuster-fused or other Father-fragment-fused beings become finaliter. [36] Threefold spirit endowment is perfectly unified in finaliters. [37]

The evolving immortal soul of man, the joint creation of the material mind and the Adjuster, ascends as such to Paradise and subsequently, when mustered into the Corps of the Finality, becomes allied in some new way with the spirit-gravity circuit of the Eternal Son by a technique of experience known as finaliter transcendation. [38] Finaliters are unique beings consisting in eternal union of perfect Adjusters and perfected creatures. [39]

Residence

The fifth group of seven primary worlds in the Salvington circuit are the Nebadon worlds of the finaliters. [40] Ascendington is their home address. [41] They can have personal residences on Paradise. [42] Transition world number 1 of local system is the Finaliter World. [43]

Though the finaliter world is a sphere of exquisite physical beauty and extraordinary morontia embellishment, the great spirit abode located at the center of activities, the temple of the finaliters, is not visible to the unaided material or early morontia vision. [44]

When a system is settled in light and life, and as the mansion worlds one by one cease to serve as mortal-training stations, they are taken over by the increasing finaliter population which accumulates in these older and more highly perfected systems. [45]

The morontia area assigned to ascending mortals resident on Edentia is located in the mid-zone of the thirty-fifth triangle adjoining the headquarters of the finaliters, situated in the thirty-sixth triangle. [46]

The circles of the finaliters in Jerusem have a unique structure at the center. And this same vacant temple is found on every system headquarters world throughout Nebadon. This edifice on Jerusem is sealed with the insignia of Michael, and it bears this inscription: “Undedicated to the seventh stage of spirit—to the eternal assignment”. [47] Finaliters currently only use one tenth of 1% of Havona worlds. [48]

Service

Finaliters are not dependent on seraphic transport to travel. [49] Mortal-finaliter companies, when stationed on Paradise, are granted a recess every millennium of Havona time. [50]

In the Corps of the Finality, when it is desired to arrive at or to portray a complete Trinity relationship to the evolutionary creation, always a group of seven finaliters is assembled, one from each superuniverse. [51] They assume duties of most celestials after morontia temple appears. [52]

Paradise Citizens, in their pilgrimage through the Havona circuits, are attended by volunteers from the Mortal Finality Corps, an arrangement that has prevailed since the completion of the first finaliter group. [53] The chief of the Satania finaliters serves on Jerusem executive council. [54] Finaliters may attempt trinitization. [55] Mortals serve under jurisdiction of finaliters in light and life. [56] They are nonactive in prelight-and-life evolutionary domains. [57] They serve on commissions approving Havona pilgrims’ circuit advancement. [58]

Finaliters serve successively in superuniverses other than those of nativity, thereby superimposing experience upon experience until the fullness of the sevenfold diversity of possible creature experience has been encompassed. [59] Spirit-fused mortals do not become finaliters, but they do eventually become enrolled in the local universe Corps of Perfection. [60] Tabamantia is a veteran finaliter, chief of corps of universe directors in charge of experimental worlds. [61]
