The organic unity of cosmic events is underlined by the living presence of the Supreme Being, manifesting as the Immanence of the Projected Incomplete through seemingly fortuitous coordination of unrelated universe happenings. [1]

The Supreme anticipates future evolution as a reflection of the Immanence of the Projected Incomplete, where finite and superfinite blend and blur, creating a forecast of universe presence on supertime levels. [2]
