Almost one half of the transition ministers on Urantia joined the Lucifer rebellion, but Van and other noble beings remained loyal defenders of Michael and his universe government. [1] The 6th order seraphim transition ministers assist in guiding beings from earthly life to morontia existence on the mansion worlds. [2] Almost ten percent of the transition ministers were ensnared in the Lucifer rebellion, as symbolized by John's vision of the great red dragon. [3]

Morontia world seraphim minister tirelessly to aid creature transition from material to spiritual, guiding souls on the seven mansion worlds towards perfection and unity with the divine. [4] Transition ministers are the seraphim closest to humans, often igniting the will-to-service through personal friendship. [5]

Seraphim of transitional ministry, through seven orders of assignment, guide ascending souls on the mansion worlds towards perfection attainment and spiritual progression. [6]

  1. Seraphic evangels.
  2. Racial interpreters.
  3. Mind planners.
  4. Morontia counselors.
  5. Technicians.
  6. Recorder-teachers.
  7. Ministering reserves.

Transition ministers in Jerusem circles provide guidance for souls moving through various stages of spiritual growth. [7]
