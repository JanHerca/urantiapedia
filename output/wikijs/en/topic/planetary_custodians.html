Seventy guardians hold supreme morontia authority over the morontia spheres, guiding ascending creatures through their transformative journeys without the need for unconsciousness. [1] Planetary Custodians are registered in Uversa personality register. [2]

The Planetary Custodians, as the local council of supreme morontia authority, grant morontia forms to all ascending creatures on the spheres, enabling them to progress through the phases of morontia life without surrendering consciousness. [3]
