Trinitized #3#, numbering 3.5 billion, are Trinity-embraced ascendant mortals of Son- or Spirit-fused nature, serving mostly in the minor sectors of the superuniverse. [1] Trinitized Ambassadors serve as emissaries of Ancients of Days on minor sectors within the superuniverse. [2] In Uversa personality register, Trinitized Ambassadors are recorded. [3]

Trinitized Ambassadors serve on Spirit- and Son-fusion worlds, representing superior minds selected for the Trinity embrace to assist superuniverse rulers in understanding and administering the interests of their respective groups. [4]

Trinitized Ambassadors, the second order of Trinitized Sons of Selection, are recruited from Spirit- and Son-fused ascendant creatures who have reached Havona and attained Paradise, then trinitized in classes of seven thousand and commissioned as ambassadors in the superuniverses. [5]
