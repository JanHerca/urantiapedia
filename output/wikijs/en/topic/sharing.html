The divine pattern of co-ordinate participation in creation is the master design for all Sons and Daughters of God, fostering a magnificent adventure of experiential perfection through sharing with the time-space Creators. [1]

Sharing is Godlike - divine, as it embodies the self-distribution and selflessness of divine personality and characterizes the willingness to share life and reality with others, mirroring the example set by the Universal Father, Eternal Son, and Conjoint Actor. [2]

The understanding of the eternal purpose of God was clear: the will of the Absolute Deity always benefits, never destroys, and it is more blessed to give than to receive. [3] Judas did not confide, isolating himself and fostering destructive tendencies that led to his downfall. [4] True knowledge is possessed only by sharing, safeguarded by wisdom, and socialized by love. [5] Sharing one’s spiritual life with others favors spiritual growth and contributes to the economy of religious growth. [6] Sharing life and all that relates to it with a partner is Godlike. [7]
