Rebecca, daughter of Joseph of Arimathea, was a member of the women’s corps commissioned by Jesus for ministering work in the kingdom. [1] The daughter of Joseph of Arimathea related the resurrection to Nicodemus, David, and others who doubted the story. [2] During Jesus' 16th appearance, he instructed his disciples to proclaim the gospel of the kingdom and to love and serve all mankind. [3]
