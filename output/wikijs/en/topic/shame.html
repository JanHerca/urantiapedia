Do not be ashamed of Jesus and his words in this sinful generation; take up your responsibilities daily and follow him to gain eternal life. [1] Judas, influenced by the ridicule of his friends, was ashamed of Jesus and ultimately betrayed him. [2]

Religion should be motivated by the revelation of God's fatherhood and the enhancement of human brotherhood, not by fear and shame as unworthy motivations for religious living. [3]
