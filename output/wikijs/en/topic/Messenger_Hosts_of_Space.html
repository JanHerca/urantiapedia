The Messenger Hosts of Space appear in the Uversa personality register as a group in the Personalities of the Infinite Spirit, and are classified as follows:

  1. Havona Servitals.
  2. Universal Conciliators.
  3. Technical Advisers.
  4. Custodians of Records.
  5. Celestial Recorders.
  6. Morontia Companions.
  7. Paradise Companions.

See also: UB 25; UB 30:2.65; UB 30:2.74.
