The Most Holy Sphere on upper Paradise, reserved for worship and spiritual attainment, is a realm of divine beauty and purely spiritual reality. [1]

There are no physical materializations on the Most Holy Sphere, but souvenirs of earthly days can be found in the Holy Land sectors and historic areas of peripheral Paradise. [2] The Most Holy Sphere on upper Paradise is reserved for worship, trinitization, and high spiritual attainment. [3] Trinitized Sons of God have their sole homes on the Isle of Paradise near the Most Holy Sphere. [4]
