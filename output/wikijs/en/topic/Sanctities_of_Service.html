Tertiary seconaphim are collectively assigned to Mighty Messengers, Those High in Authority, and Those without Name and Number, created serially in seven reflective types. [1] The seconaphim, as tertiary angels, reveal the true and hidden motives of any service, embodying the Sanctities of Service. [2]
