Do not be discouraged by your mistakes, for what is remembered in time will be forgotten in eternity. [1] God, the faithful Creator, makes no mistakes and is the source and destiny of all souls. [2]

The Thought Adjuster will recall and rehearse only memories with spiritual meaning; memories of past life without spiritual significance will perish with the material brain. [3]
