We must accept challenges without complaint and to face difficulties and uncertainties without fear. [1] The apostles' five-month ordeal of fishing and preaching turned out to be grueling but it sparked a love and fidelity to Jesus above all else. [2] Mortal life is short and intense test, but there are many other stages of proof in the future existence. [3] Difficulties may challenge mediocrity and defeat the fearful, but they only stimulate the true children of the Most Highs. [4]

The great challenge

The great challenge to modern man is to achieve better communication with the divine Monitor that dwells within the human mind. [5]

The religious challenge of this age is to that forward-looking men and women with spiritual insight construct a new and appealing philosophy of living. [6]

The great challenge that has been given to mortal man is this: Will you decide to personalize the experiencible value meanings of the cosmos into your own evolving selfhood or will let others to make personal contributions to Supreme? [7]
