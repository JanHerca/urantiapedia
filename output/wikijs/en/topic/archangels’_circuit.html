Michael messages sometimes go direct via archangels’ circuit. [1]

On Urantia

The mind circuit started first operational in days of Andon. [2] The circuit of the archangels operated for the first time from Urantia after Jesus’ resurrection. [3]

In recent years the archangels’ circuit has eluded the quarantine of Urantia but that means of communication is largely limited to the transactions of the archangel corps itself. [4]

On Urantia much of the work of the beings involved in interplanetary and interuniverse communications has to be done through the circuit of the archangels. [5]
