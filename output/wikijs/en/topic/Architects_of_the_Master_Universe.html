The Architects of the Master Universe are the governing corps of the Paradise Transcendentalers. This governing corps numbers 28,011 personalities possessing master minds, superb spirits, and supernal absonites and they are organized in seven levels or groups. [1] There is a tradition on Paradise that tells that the 28,012th Master Architect failed to absonitize, perhaps because the limit of absonity was reached. [2] There is a very close association between the Master Architects and the Paradise Creator Sons. [3]

The function of Creator will and creature will, in the grand universe, operates within the limits, and in accordance with the possibilities, established by the Master Architects. [4]

It is in connection with the segregation of potentials that the Architects of the Master Universe exist as the veritable embodiments of the Deity concept of the universes. [5] They appear in Paradise classification inside the Eventuated Transcendental beings. [6]

They are members of the first experiential Trinity, the Ultimate Trinity, that consist of the Supreme Being, the Supreme Creator Personalities, and the absonite Architects of the Master Universe. [7] Personalized Adjusters are the all-wise and powerful executives of the Architects of the Master Universe. [8]

Only Solitary Messengers and Inspired Trinity Spirits maintain any organic association with the Transcendentalers and the Architects of the Master Universe. [9] They are counted as transcendental beings, beings that are not finite neither absolute. [10]

Function

There are seven Master Architects associated with the Seven Master Spirits on Paradise and Seven Supreme Executives on the seven special worlds of Infinite Spirit. [11] They administer creation prior to appearance of specific rulers. [12] The administrative co-ordination of the master universe is the function of the Architects of the Master Universe. [13]

The Master Architects contribute technical approval of the assignment of the Creator Sons to their space sites for the organization of the local universes. [14] They are custodians of those things which transcend the creational limits of the present universe age. [15] They designed stable yet flexible units of matter. [16]

The Architects of the Master Universe have at their disposal numerous groups of assistants and helpers, including two vast orders of force organizers, the primary eventuated and the associate transcendental. [17] The diverse levels of creation are all unified in the plans and administration of the Architects of the Master Universe. [18]

The act of maturing the qualified possibilities of the three great potential Absolutes is the absonite function of the Architects of the Master Universe and their transcendental associates. [19] It is possible but not sure that some Master Architects helped to plan Havona. [20] They permit to pairs of finaliters to essay trinitization of a shared concept. [21] They are who have planned the development of the outer space levels. [22]

They provide Creator Sons and Ancients of Days with a permanent ascendant population that will be increasingly competent to rule the realms in the universe ages to come. [23] Spheres of space —suns, dark islands, planets, etc. — appear in accordance with precreative transcendental plans of Architects of the Master Universe. [24] The resultant creature-trinitized become the wards of the Architects of the Master Universe. [25]

Senior Architect

He is the co-ordinating head of all Paradise intelligences below the level of Deity. [26] Co-ordinates Paradise and sacred spheres. [27] He is chief of Transcendentalers. [28] He oversees the seven finaliter corps. [29]
