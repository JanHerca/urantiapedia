Come to Jesus, all you who labor, and find rest for your souls. [1] The parable of laborers in the vineyard illustrates the generous and fair nature of the kingdom of heaven. [2] Pray always for more laborers for the kingdom, ready to say, “Here am I; send me,” through consecrated devotion and daily worship. [3] The laborer is worthy of his hire as they go forth proclaiming peace on earth and good will among men. [4]
