The ascendant plan of mortal progression involves ascending by teaching those behind us in the scale of progression, continually giving out new truth and experience as acquired. [1] Beware of false teachers who will arise and lead many astray, for they will deceive even in times of great tribulation. [2] Teachers of the kingdom are to respect the personality of man, avoid coercion, and appeal directly to the divine spirit within each individual. [3] Jesus maintained his integrity by remaining a learner, unlike the proud professors at the university in Alexandria. [4] Teachers must be free beings, real leaders, in order to ensure that education prioritizes philosophy and the search for wisdom. [5]

Perfectors of Wisdom are the ultimate source of wisdom for all teachers, guiding the application of universe knowledge and providing fountains of discretion and wellsprings of discrimination in all institutions of learning. [6]
