Always show respect for the personality of man; spiritual victories are won only by spiritual power, not by force or coercion. [1]

Education transforms conciliators into interpreters of mysteries, evolving from arbiters to teachers who respect the experience and opinions of others. [2] Show adequate respect to superiors, a divine injunction. [3]

Hap taught respect for elders and the property of others, as we seek to honor divine teachers and uphold truth with green pastures and fruitful flocks. [4] All service is sacred and exhilarating; higher spiritual beings do not look down on lower servitals. [5] Jesus disapproved of outward shows of respect that the twelve initially displayed in the garden at noon. [6] Jesus consistently demonstrated profound respect for every person he encountered, leaving a lasting impression on his disciples. [7]
