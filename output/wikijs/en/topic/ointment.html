The former brothel keeper anointed Jesus' feet with perfumed lotion in a heartfelt display of gratitude and devotion at Simon's house. [1] Jesus’ body was embalmed with myrrh and aloes by Joseph and Nicodemus in the tomb, while the women waited nearby to properly prepare him for burial. [2] Judas resented Mary’s waste of incense at Jesus' feet, leading to a lifetime of accumulated hate and revenge. [3] Mary of Bethany poured spikenard on Jesus' head and feet. [4]
