Deformed or premature babies were regarded as the young of animals which had found their way into a woman’s body as a result of careless bathing or through malevolent spirit activity. [1] Before the Prince’s arrival, bathing had been an exclusively religious ceremonial. [2] Greeks practiced the annual ritual bath. [3] Jesus refused to go to public baths because of the sex promiscuity which there prevailed. [4]
