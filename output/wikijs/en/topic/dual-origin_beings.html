Dual-origin beings are those of origin in any two of the Paradise Deities or otherwise created by any two beings of direct or indirect descent from the Paradise Deities. [1]

Creation of Havona Servitals, the joint creative work of the Seven Master Spirits and their associates, the Seven Supreme Power Directors, is a pattern for the long list of reproductions of the dual order in the evolutionary universes. [2] They appear in the second group in Paradise classification of beings. [3]
