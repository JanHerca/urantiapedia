The agencies of Infinite Spirit and cosmic mind inject spontaneity into physical creation, preventing uniformity and determinism in the finite universe of matter. [1]

All Paradise conduct is wholly spontaneous, in every sense natural and free, yet guided by the directors of conduct to prevent confusion and uncertainty on the eternal Isle. [2] Believers have originality of living a spontaneous and devoted life of genuine personal religious experience. [3] Choose wisely whether your inner life will be spontaneous and haphazard or controlled and constructive. [4] All personalities are endowed with divine spontaneity, drawing them towards the Universal Father. [5]

The inexhaustible evolutionary capacity of the universe is driven by the infinity of spontaneity, harmoniously co-ordinated by a purposive and dominant mind towards a universal and progressive evolution. [6] Jesus' spontaneous kindness expanded the neighborhood to include the whole world, making all men one's neighbors. [7] Prior to the instruction of the Dalamatia teachers, primitive lives were devoid of spontaneity and freedom. [8] Prayer is a personal and spontaneous expression of the soul's attitude towards the spirit, leading to spiritual growth and communion with divinity. [9] The spontaneous friendliness of spirit-born sons surpasses mere tradition in the new religion of enhanced self-realization and universal truth. [10] True worship is the spontaneous expression of beings who have learned enough about God to attain his presence and experience cosmic consciousness. [11]
