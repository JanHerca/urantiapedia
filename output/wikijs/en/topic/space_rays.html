Ultimatonic rays consist of ultimatons or electrons and are the initial activity of the ultimatons as they aggregate into electrons, leading to the storage of energy in matter. [1] Outer-space energy rays frequently commingled with highly energized particles. [2] Implanted life, fully resistant to short space rays, thrives in the cosmic chemical laboratories of vast hydrogen clouds and binary stars. [3] Many mesotrons in space rays impinge upon your planet abundantly. [4]

Short space rays, the dominant form of radiant energy in the universe, are produced through various cosmic occurrences and atom disruptions in hydrogen clouds interspersed throughout space. [5] Short space rays accompany atomic birth or dissolution, originating from the superdense Milky Way plane. [6]
