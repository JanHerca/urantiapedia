Many of Jesus’ followers believed Messiah would appear to establish the New Jerusalem, connecting it with the end of the world and the new heavens and earth. [1] John the Revelator witnessed the new Jerusalem descending from God, as the culmination of the Teacher Sons' mission. [2]
