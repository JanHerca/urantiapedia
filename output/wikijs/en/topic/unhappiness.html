Deficient unification engenders unhappiness, but a confluence of cosmic insight and emotional reactions can create an effective philosophy of living, leading to improved character and a unified personality. [1]

The presence of the divine Spirit prevents the consuming thirst and hunger of mortal discontent, providing a well of satisfaction and spiritual illumination that transcends material joys and earthly satisfactions. [2]
