Roman soldiers in Jerusalem were bribed to say disciples took Jesus’ body as they fled in fear from the tomb. [1] The Roman soldiers in Jerusalem fled when the stone rolled away from Jesus' tomb. [2]

Sensitive to Jewish disrespect, the Roman soldiers in Jerusalem promptly placed Jude under arrest for expressing fiery indignation towards a guard who made improper remarks. [3]
