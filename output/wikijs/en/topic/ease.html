To accept the guidance of a seraphim rarely means attaining a life of ease. [1] The animal nature and the lower forms of will creatures do not progress favorably in environmental ease. [2]

One of the purposes of the morontia career is to effect the permanent eradication from the mortal survivors of such animal vestigial traits as procrastination, equivocation, insincerity, problem avoidance, unfairness, and ease seeking. [3]
