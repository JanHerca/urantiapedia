Faithfuls of Days are the Paradise advisers to the rulers of the one hundred constellations in each local universe. There are 70 million in existence. [1] They are in constant communication with their order. [2] They appear in personality registers under triune-origen beigns in the Stationary Sons of the Trinity group. [3] They are instructed to retire after Michael’s completion of sovereignty but Michael requested them to remain forever on duty in Nebadon. [4] They are Supreme Trinity Personalities. [5]

Service

They are part of the supreme council of unlimited authority when a universe become settled in light and life. [6]

The Faithfuls of Days are the last link in the long administrative-advisory chain which reaches from the sacred spheres of the Universal Father near the center of all things to the primary divisions of the local universes. [7] The Faithfuls of Days, like the Union of Days, never proffer advice or offer assistance to the constellation rulers unless it is asked for. [8] They are the representatives of Unions of Days. [9]

Their Paradise reserve corps is the Advisory Commission of Interuniverse Ethics and Self-government. Faithfuls of Days rotate in service in accordance with the rulings of the supreme council of their reserve corps. [10] They have their own staffs of Havona and Paradise personalities. [11]

They are advisers to the Trinity and representatives of the constellations. Every constellation has one. Just as a Creator Son has a Union of Days assisting him in the local universe, the Vorondadek Sons who rule the constellations have a Faithful of Days assisting them. [12]
