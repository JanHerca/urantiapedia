Early commerce in wood accelerated urban life and trade in central Asia. [1] The ancient practice of rapping on wood perpetuates customs of tree worship. [2] Jesus, a skilled carpenter and boatbuilder from Nazareth, revolutionized boatmaking techniques in Galilee with his innovative designs. [3] Jesus wrote on smooth white boards, reflecting the improved economic condition of his family in Nazareth. [4]
