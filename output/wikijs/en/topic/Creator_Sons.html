They are listed among Supreme Creators. [1]

An almost infinite variety characterizes the plans of the Creator Sons for organizing, evolving, disciplining, and settling their respective local universes. [2] Creator Sons come near the creatures of their inhabited worlds and “draw all men to themselves”. [3] Their current number is over 700,000, and is constantly growing. There is no limit to the number of them that can be. [4] Creator Sons of God aspire to duplicate in time and to reproduce in space the pattern universe, Havona, in his own local universe. [5] He who has seen a Creator Son has seen Father. [6] The experiential discovery-career of the realization of God begins with the recognition of the divinity of the Creator Son of the local universe. [7] They appear in personality registers. [8]

Who are they

Creator Sons are personality centers for the spiritual forces of the Paradise Father-Son. Creator Sons are the final power-personality focalizations of the mighty time-space attributes of God the Sevenfold. [9] In a sevenfold Creator Son, Creator and creature are forever blended in understanding, sympathetic, and merciful association. [10] These Sons devote themselves to the effective execution of the Paradise plan of progressive mortal ascension. [11] In spirit and nature, if not in all attributes, each Paradise Son is a divinely perfect portraiture of the Original Son. [12]

Each Creator Son is different from every other; each is unique in nature as well as in personality; each is the “only-begotten Son” of the perfect deity ideal of his origin. [13] Each Creator Son is endowed with spirit gravity in his own realms. [14] To the children of a local universe a Michael Son is, to all practical intents and purposes, God. [15] All Sons of God who take origin in the persons of the Paradise Deities are in direct and constant communication with the Eternal Mother Son. [16] A Michael Son is, to all practical intents and purposes, God. He is the local universe personification of the Universal Father and the Eternal Son. [17] They are the most powerful divine beings capable of direct administration of evolutionary universes. [18] The Universal Father is in every way divinely present in the Creator Sons. The Father and his Sons are one. [19] These primary Paradise Sons are the real revealers of the Father’s loving nature and beneficent authority. [20] The Paradise Creator Sons of the Universal Father are a revelation of an otherwise invisible being, invisible. [21] The Creator Sons going out from Paradise are, in actuality, transformative creators in the cosmic sense. [22] In their very bestowals the Sons of God have become to all evolutionary creatures “the way, the truth, and the life. [23]

Names

  • Co-ordinate Creator Sons. [24]
  • Creator Father-Son. [25]
  • Michael Sons. [26]
  • Paradise Michaels. [27]
  • Primary Paradise Sons. [28]
  • Universe Sons. [29]

Origin

When the fullness of absolute spiritual ideation in the Eternal Son encounters the fullness of absolute personality concept in the Universal Father there flashes into full-fledged being a new and original Creator Son. [30]

The Paradise Sons of God are of threefold origin: The primary or Creator Sons are brought into being by the Universal Father and the Eternal Son. They constitute the order of Michael. [31] Creator Sons are divinely like their Paradise parents, but none exactly resembles another. That is why their domains are very diverse. [32] Observation of Creator Sons discloses that some resemble more the Father, some the Son, while others are a blend of both their infinite parents. [33]

Every time the Universal Father and the Eternal Son jointly project a new, original, identical, unique, and absolute personal thought, that very instant this creative idea is perfectly and finally personalized in the being and personality of a new and original Creator Son. [34]

Each Paradise Michael is the absolute of the dual deity concepts of his origin; thus he embodies actual phases of the infinity of the Universal Father and the Eternal Son. [35] A Creator Son spends a long period of preliminary training. [36]

A Creator Son is permitted to choose the space site of his future cosmic activity, but before he may begin even the physical organization of his universe, he must spend a long period of observation devoted to the study. [37] Paradise reserve corps teaches on fourth Havona circuit. [38]

Capabilities

These Paradise Sons of the order of Michael are perfect personalities, even the pattern for all local universe personality. [39] The personal presence of a Creator Son in his local universe is not necessary to the smooth running of an established material creation. [40] They are personally conscious of acts and emotions of descending Sons in own domains. [41] They posses an almost unlimited and timeless healing powers. [42] They have power to lay down life and take it up again. [43] The Creator Sons most certainly can and sometimes do, even as do mortals. [44]

Assitants

The Master Architects contribute technical approval of the assignment of the Creator Sons to their space sites for the organization of the local universes. [45]

The one hundred Constellation Fathers, the actual presiding heads of the constellation governments, constitute the supreme advisory cabinet of the Creator Son. [46] There is a very close association between the Master Architects and the Paradise Creator Sons. [47] Universe Centers assist in universe organization to Creator Sons. [48] Tertiaphim serve as Creator Sons liaisons with Ancients of Days. [49] Secoraphic Voices are able to reflect the minds of Creator Sons to Ancients of Days. [50]

Bright and Morning Stars

They are first-born of Creator Sons. [51] Creator Sons delegate executive power to Bright and Morning Stars. [52] They direct the universes in absence of Creator Sons. [53]

Creature bestowals

A Creator Son has within himself the power to bestow himself in the likeness of any of his created sons; he has within himself the power to lay down his observable life and to take it up again; and he has this power because of the direct command of the Paradise Father. The attribute of bestowal is inherent in the Paradise Sons of the Universal Father. [54] By far the most intriguing miracles of the universe are the incarnational bestowals of the Paradise Sons. [55]

The seven bestowals of a Creator Son in the form of seven creature types pertain to the revelation of the seven primary expressions of the will and nature of Deity. [56] Creator Sons gain practical mercy, fair judgment, and the patience born of existence by living experience in bestowals. [57]

The career of sevenfold bestowal is the supreme goal of all Paradise Creator Sons. These bestowals are the last steps in the education and training of Creator Sons for the sublime tasks of ruling the local universes in divine righteousness and by just judgment. [58] The creature-bestowal experiences of the Michaels qualify them to portray the experiential divinity of the Supreme Being. [59]

Creator Sons bestowals must meet the requirements of their order of divine sonship, embraced in the mandate of the Eternal Son on Paradise, and meet the last and full measure of the present will of the Paradise Father. [60] Incarnation, the secret of Sonarington, is the technique of the Son’s escape from the otherwise all-encompassing fetters of personality absolutism. [61] The bestowal of the Sons of God upon the evolutionary creations is to personalize and factualize, to incarnate and make real, the love of the Father. [62] About the value of bestowals, see the links.

Last bestowal as a mortal

After the bestowal in mortal form of a Creator Son or its Avonal equivalent, and after the pouring of the Spirit of Truth into the world of bestowal, all normal-minded will creatures of that world will receive an Adjuster as soon as they attain the age of first moral choice. [63] A Michael-bestowal world becomes the individual and personal ward of a Master Son and, as such, is wholly subject to his own plans and rulings. [64]

The Creator Son, whose personality is comprehensible by man, compensates for mortal inability to grasp the full significance of Eternal Son of Paradise. [65]

Creator Sons become Master Sons after passing the seventh and last bestowal, and receives from the Universal Father supreme authority and jurisdiction over his universe. [66] Creator Sons may not beget offspring on bestowals. This is the only exception to the rule of live ordinary live. [67]

Without the bestowal service of the Paradise Sons and the loving ministry of the creatures of the Infinite Spirit, beings of material origin could hardly hope to attain the Eternal Son. [68]

In the final bestowal a Creator Son appears as a member of one of the higher mortal races on some inhabited world, usually as a member of that racial group which contains the largest hereditary legacy of the Adamic stock. [69] The Eternal Son is the eternal Word of God. On mortal bestowals, Creator Sons are Word made flesh. [70] Once in each local universe, Creator Sons bestow themselves on some evolutionary world, as Michael of Nebadon do in Urantia. [71]

When a Paradise Son visits an evolutionary world and becomes like one of its people, his presence terminates a dispensation and constitutes a judgment of the realm. [72]

Spirit of Truth

Eternal truth is the special ministry of the Paradise Sons who not only bestow themselves upon the mortal races but even pour out their Spirit of Truth upon all peoples. [73]

The Creator Sons are endowed with a spirit of universe presence in many ways analogous to that of the Seven Master Spirits of Paradise. This spirit is an inherent endowment of the Creator Son. [74] Spirit of Truth is sent inmediatly after mortal bestowal of a Creator or Avonal Son. [75]

Function of Creator Sons

Supplications of all kinds belong to the realm of the Eternal Son and the Son’s spiritual organization. Prayers, all formal communications, everything except adoration and worship of the Universal Father, are matters that concern a local universe; they do not ordinarily proceed out of the realm of the jurisdiction of a Creator Son. [76] The Son functions as a father in his local universe. [77] The universes of time and space are all created and organized by the Paradise corps of the Creator Sons. This implies: [78]

  • Start a original personal staff of the Creator Son with a thousand tertiaphim. [79]
  • Choose the space site for a new universe. [80]
  • Achieve universe materialization and equilibrium. [81]
  • Create their children through the liason with the Mother Spirit. [82]
  • Create Material Sons. The Universe Mother Spirit does not participate in the production of these beings. [83]
  • Start the headquarters sphere work when the Creator Son arrives to the local universe. [84]
  • Initiate new creature designs, with the approval of the Eternal Son. [85]
  • Materialize visible matter from pre-existent space energy. [86]
  • Power Centers coordinate with Creator Sons’s plans. [87]
  • Defend and if needed retrieve personal creation if ever his authority or administration is challenged, attacked, or jeopardized. [88]

The fourth Havona circuit is sometimes called the “circuit of the Sons. ” In this circuit the reserve corps of the Paradise Michaels maintain special service schools of mutual ministry to both the ascending and descending pilgrims;. [89]

The Creator Sons in the Deity association of God the Sevenfold provide the mechanism whereby the mortal becomes immortal and the finite attains the embrace of the infinite. [90] The Paradise Sons act as rehabilitators of that which misguided creature will has placed in spiritual jeopardy. [91] Creator Sons represent Father and Son in their creations. [92]

Fusion with a Son

Mortals can do fusion with a Son. See more in the link about Son-fused mortals. In this cases fragmented entities of Creator Sons fuse with mortals. [93] “fusion spirit” of a Creator Son is implemented in the Reflective Spirits of the higher universes. [94]

Relationship with his creatures

Creators never sit in judgment on their creatures; that is the exclusive function of creatures of high training and actual creature experience. [95] The mortal creature must find God. The Creator Son never stops until he finds man—the lowest will creature. [96] There exist an intimate personal communion between the Creator Sons and their mortal creatures. [97]

Destiny

The nature, mission, ministry, associations, origin, and destiny of the Creator Sons is studied in the second stage of light and life. [98]

Speculation concerning the function of these Creator Sons in connection with the outer universes now in process of preliminary assembly is wholly futile. But it is a widely held assumption that the Michaels, the Creator Sons, are destined to function in the outer universes. [99]

Limitations

A Creator Son, after his elevation to the full sovereignty, cannot formulate entirely new types of creatures. [100] His personal power is limited by the pre-existent gravity circuits centering at Paradise. [101] They cannot bestow personality, only Universal Father can do it. [102] New creature designs and types must have the consent of the Eternal and Original Mother Son. [103] Infinite Spirit must agree to any new transformations of energy-matter. [104]

The Sons of the local universes can decree the survival of mortal man, but only the Ancients of Days may sit in executive judgment on the issues of eternal life and death. [105] A Creator Son is not handicapped by time, but he is conditioned by space; he cannot personally be in two places at the same time. [106]

While the Creator Sons fully share his divinity, even phases of his absoluteness, they are not altogether limited by that finality of wisdom which directs the Father’s infinity of will. [107]

A Creator Michael takes an oath to the Trinity not to assume supreme sovereignty until the seven creature bestowals have been completed and certified by the superuniverse rulers. [108]

The Eternal Son is limited in transmittal of creator prerogatives. But when these co-ordinate Sons have been produced, the prerogatives of creatorship are apparently not further transmissible. Creator Sons are never able to transmit or delegate the prerogatives of creatorship to his Sons. [109] A Creator Son is unable to leave his headquarters world until such a time as gravity stabilization of the realm has been effected. [110] No way to limit time element of creator prerogatives. [111]

Local universe Mother Spirits

Mother Spirits are effective helpers and ministers of Creator Sons. [112] Creator Sons’s spiritual presence can be invested in Mother Spirits during Son's absences. [113]

After the completion of his seventh bestowal the triumphant Creator Son elevates the Universe Mother Spirit to cosovereignty and acknowledges the Spirit consort as his equal. [114] Mother Spirits may attain union with Creator Sons in eternity. [115] Both the Son and the Mother Spirit participate in creation of local universes. [116] If the Creator Sons are destined to the outer universes in the future, the Divine Ministers will undoubtedly accompany them. [117]

Local universe sovereignty

The sovereignty of a Creator Son in a local universe passes through six, perhaps seven, stages of experiential manifestation. [118] Until they earn the sovereignty, Creator Sons rule as Vicegerents of Father. [119] These Sons are able to administer only that which they bring into existence. [120]

When the Eternal Son bestows a Creator Son upon a projected local universe, that Creator Son assumes full responsibility for the completion, control, and composure of that new universe. [121] A Creator Son could assert full sovereignty over his personal creation at any time, but he wisely chooses not to. [122]

A Creator Son is given the range of a universe by the consent of the Paradise Trinity. Such action constitutes title of physical possession, a cosmic leasehold. [123]

In accepting the initial vicegerent sovereignty of a projected local universe, a Creator Michael takes an oath to the Trinity not to assume supreme sovereignty until the seven creature bestowals have been completed and certified by the superuniverse rulers. [124] Local universe sovereignty of Master Michaels is like future sovereignty of Supreme. [125] All power is concentrated in hands of Creator Sons upon achievement of sovereignty. [126]

In settling the question of sovereignty in a local universe, the Creator Son is not only demonstrating his own fitness to rule but is also revealing the nature and portraying the sevenfold attitude of the Paradise Deities. [127]

The Creator Son rules supreme in all matters of ethical associations, the relations of any division of creatures to any other class of creatures or of two or more individuals within any given group. [128]

Universal Father

While the Universal Father most certainly rules over his vast creation, he functions in a local universe administration through the Creator Sons. [129]

The Universal Father speaks in acknowledgment of the eternal union of the Creator Son and the Creative Spirit and in confirmation of the bestowal of certain joint powers of administration. [130] The Father gives life to Creator Sons without measure. [131] What a Creator Son desires and his Father wills IS. [132]
