The highest religious doctrines are dead and powerless unless transformed through divine spirit and power, as declared by your teacher of old. [1] Experience always has advantage over theory. [2] Facts always support spiritual faith, while theories may challenge it. [3] You cannot confide in a postulate, just as you cannot pray to a chemical formula or worship a hypothesis. [4]

Imprisoning divine truth in human formulations leads to post-mortem salvage of dead theories, while living truth thrives in spirit-discerned realities. [5]
