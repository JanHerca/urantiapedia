The Andites were the best all-round human stock to appear on Urantia since the days of Adamites; most skillful and sagacious militarists ever to live on Urantia. [1]

The Andites were a great people, but the crucial factor in determining the course of their civilization was the increasing aridity of Iran, Turkestan, and Sinkiang, which forced them to invent and adopt new and advanced methods of wresting a livelihood from their decreasingly fertile lands. [2] They drove Andonites out of Europe. [3] It was the violet race and their Andite successors who first attempted to divorce the mathematical from the volitional. [4]

These early Andites were not Aryan; they were pre-Aryan. They were not white; they were pre-white. They were neither an Occidental nor an Oriental people. But it is Andite inheritance that gives to the polyglot mixture of the so-called white races that generalized homogeneity which has been called Caucasoid. [5]

Migrations of Andites

Mesopotamia and Egipt

Andites migrated from Mesopotamia to Egipt and settled along the Nile. [6]

Five per cent of the Andites, the very superior culture of the coastal district about the mouths of the Tigris and Euphrates who had kept themselves free from intermarriage with the inferior neighboring tribesmen, refused to leave their homes. [7] The Andites had almost entirely evacuated this region by 6000 B.C. [8] These early migrations extended over a period of ten thousand years, from 25,000 to 15,000 B.C. [9] Sumerians were the last Andites of Mesopotamia. [10]

Europe

They migrated to the Occident. [11] Also they do it in the Orient. [12] Turkestan was the first destiny before turning around to Europe and the east. [13]

Andites poured into Europe and Mediterranean region in a steady stream; there were seven major invasions, the last arrivals coming on horseback in three great waves. [14] For three thousand years the military headquarters of the northern Andites was in Denmark. [15] Andites were the ancestors of the so-called Nordic races, the forefathers of the Scandinavian, German, and Anglo-Saxon peoples. [16]

The Andites not only migrated to Europe but to northern China and India, while many groups penetrated to the ends of the earth as missionaries, teachers, and traders. [17] They mingled with the remnants of the commingled Saharans and the southern blue men of Europe. [18]

India

Some Andites were the so-called Dravidian and later Aryan conquerors of India; and their presence in central Asia greatly upstepped the ancestors of the Turanians. [19] Andites were racially obliterated in India. [20]

Japan and South America

One hundred and thirty-two of Andites, embarking in a fleet of small boats from Japan, eventually reached South America and by intermarriage with the natives of the Andes established the ancestry of the later rulers of the Incas. [21]

Other

The North American Indians never came in contact with even the Andite offspring of Adam and Eve. [22] Siberian Andonites were assimilated by northen Andites. [23]

Influence in the races

And to every nation to which they journeyed, they contributed humor, art, adventure, music, and manufacture; proclivities toward adventure and exploration; love, romance, and personal selection in premarital courtship. [24] There is real danger in the combination of restlessness, curiosity, adventure, and pleasure-abandon characteristic of the post-Andite races. [25]

In races containing Andite inheritance, children are not so placid; these more imaginative and adventurous youths require more training and discipline. [26] The unifying factor in white race is Andite inheritance. [27]

Origin

This race began with the 1570 sons and daughters that Adam impregnated in a group of Nodite women coordinated by Eve. [28] The term Andite is used to designate those peoples whose racial inheritance was from one-eighth to one-sixth violet. [29] As they moved westward across the Russian plains, absorbing the best of the blue man and exterminating the worst, they became blended into one people. [30]

They embraced most of the highest types of the surviving remnants of the Adamite and Nodite races and, later, some of the best strains of the yellow, blue, and green men. [31] The union of Nodites and Adamites caused the renaissance of the Garden and formed the Andites. [32]

Traditions

They had red or blond hair. [33] Originally they were not cannibalistic, but they were after become grossly admixed with the evolutionary races. [34] The early Andites thought their ghosts returned to the ancestral homelands. [35] They practiced in-marriage a lot. [36] They domesticated many animals that were domesticated twice in the past and then were not anymore. [37] But the horse was the evolutionary factor which determined the dominance of the Andites in the Occident. [38] They worked metals: bronze, iron, gold, copper. [39] Human society would be greatly improved if the civilized races would more generally return to the family-council practices of the Andites. [40]
