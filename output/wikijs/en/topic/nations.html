As sovereignty consolidates into larger nations, minor wars diminish, but conflicts escalate as powerful nations brush against each other, signaling the potential for global conflict. [1]

The failure to increase in population prevents the full realization of national destiny, but excessive growth leads to lower standards of living or territorial expansion through conquest. [2]

The Spirit of Truth, a universal world influence, is designed to unify mankind by lessening self-assertiveness in individuals, groups, nations, and races, thus preventing destructive wars. [3]
