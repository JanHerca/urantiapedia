The Infinite is the source of universal reality. [1] The Infinite denotes the plenitude implied in the primacy of the First Source-Center. [2] The personality of the Infinite is revealed in the form of the Universal Father. [3] A theoretical moment is postulated when The Infinite One was differentiated from The Infinite. [4] Finite mortal man can never hope to comprehend the Infinite, except when focused on the personality arising in the human life of Jesus. [5]

Sooner or later all personalities perceive that all growth and achievement in the quest for the Infinite is proportionate to achievement in likeness and identity with the Father. [6] Only infinity can reveal the Infinite. [7]
