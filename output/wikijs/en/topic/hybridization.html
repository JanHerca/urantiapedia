Hybridization of superior and dissimilar stocks creates new and vigorous strains, enhancing creativity and fertility across plants, animals, and the human species, despite social prejudices against race blending. [1]

Interbreeding diverse peoples with superior inheritance factors can lead to the creation of stronger and better races, but attempting this on Urantia under current racial conditions would be disastrous. [2]
