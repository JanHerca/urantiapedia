Serapatatia, a brown-tinted leader of the Nodite Syrian confederation, inherited his influential authority from a long line of esteemed ancestors. [1] Cain fulfilled predictions of promoting peace between Nodites and Adamites. [2] Serapatatia's act of drowning himself from fear and remorse left Eve in unending sorrow and suffering. [3]

Serapatatia, entirely honest and never conscious of being used by Caligastia, became deeply impressed with the righteousness of Adam’s cause and joined in establishing an affiliation with the work of Adam and Eve in the Garden. [4] Serapatatia's impatience hindered his loyalty to the long-term plan for uplifting the confused peoples of Urantia. [5] Eve, the Nodite leader Serapatatia with whom she privately met, unwittingly strayed from Adam's plans, jeopardizing their planetary trust. [6] Bitter warfare erupted between the tribes of Serapatatia and the Adamites after news of the Nodite settlement annihilation reached them. [7]
