Preventing rebellion holds greater value than loyalty in the face of actual rebellion, leading ascendant beings to become Mighty Messengers in the superuniverse service. [1]

Repentant rebels, after rehabilitation, are reassigned to custodial duties and physical administration roles, never returning to their original positions. [2] It will take ages to retrieve the handicaps of rebellion that have plagued Satania for two hundred thousand years. [3]

The ascension plan is the surest defense against rebellion, as proven by the triumphant survival of 187,432,811 ascending mortals during the Lucifer rebellion. [4] Rebellion by a Planetary Prince isolates his planet until a successor is properly installed. [5] Creator Sons, as part of the Bestowal Plan, act as retrievers of spiritual jeopardy caused by rebellion. [6] Do not suppress rebellion; allow it to pursue its natural course of self-obliteration for the ultimate eradication of evil and sin. [7]

The spiritual rebellion among the Sons does not reflect any flaw in their creation, but rather serves to deepen their understanding of beings in the realm of time and space. [8]

In Nebadon

The first rebellion by Lutentia of Palonia in system 11 of constellation 37 caused widespread and disastrous repercussions in the universe of Nebadon. [9] The second rebellion in system 87 of constellation 61 led to the dispatch of a Material Son to planet 217 by the supreme council on Salvington. [10] About the third rebellion by Lucifer in Satania see the links.

The Ancients of Days mandated instantaneous extinction of new rebels during Michael's bestowal, ensuring the secure administration of Nebadon throughout his voluntary mission. [11]

Michael's successful bestowal ends agelong conflicts, ensuring no future challenges can arise, cementing his triumph and settling the status of agelong enemies in Satania and beyond. [12]

Over seven hundred Sons of the Lanonandek order rebelled in our local universe, with only three of them being System Sovereigns, demonstrating the peril of personal disloyalty to the will of the Michael Son and the potential for confusion in system affairs. [13]

After rebellion

The kingdom of order triumphed over rebellion through loyalty, intellect, and truth, despite the secession of Lucifer and the ensuing chaos. [14] Local universe Mother Spirits do not contest rebellion, but sustain the Son in stabilizing government and upholding authority against evil and sin. [15]

Observing normal planets would disclose what Urantia has lost through rebellion and embracing evil, as demonstrated by the advanced civilization and spiritual dawn achieved under the magnificent administration of the fifth dispensation of world affairs. [16]

Primary supernaphim command seraphic hosts on isolated worlds to oversee their ministering duties, especially when a Paradise Son completes their mission and ascends to the Universal Father, returning as the accredited deliverer of that world. [17] Tertiary Circuit Supervisors isolate worlds for rebellion against the Universal Father. [18] The universe governments prioritize the personal interests of individuals, advancing souls to transitional beings despite the risk of rebellion. [19] About worlds isolated by rebellion see link on quarantine.
