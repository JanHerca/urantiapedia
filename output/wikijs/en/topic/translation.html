Definition

Son-seized mortals of the primary modified order of ascension are exempt from death, translated from among the living to appear immediately in the presence of the Sovereign Son. [1]

Translation to the morontia state, where the soul fuses with the Adjuster in mortal life, increases with planetary evolution, leading to exempt superb mortals and direct transit to morontia-training worlds. [2]

How it works

The archangel supervises the direct transit of translated souls to the morontia world from the resurrection hall. [3]

In a morontia temple, witnessing the translation of living mortals to the morontia existence is a profound and joyous occasion in the era of light and life. [4]

The dispensation where mortals are translated on advanced worlds signifies a universal spiritual allegiance as natural death becomes less frequent in the progression of evolutionary beings. [5] The mortal resigns planetary duties and bids farewell before translation to the inner temple. [6]

Translation occurs many times before mortal man can attain the spiritual vision to see even one of the Seven Master Spirits, reflecting the inherent difficulty of approaching the infinite Father. [7] Translation often occurs in the planetary atmosphere. [8] Certain mortals of Adjuster-fusion status are exempt from translation in order to fulfill crucial administrative roles on the planet. [9] Translated souls proceed by Adjuster transit to resurrection hall, supervised by an archangel assigned to the morontia world. [10]

The spiritual flames of translation instantly consume the material body, allowing for the ascension of mortals to the morontia existence in a beautiful and joyous ceremony. [11]

Translation from the mortal realm occurs on mansion worlds, system, or constellation, where evolving beings progress towards a state of escape from natural death, ultimately reaching the high state of planetary evolution necessary for the elimination of death. [12]

Examples

55 Amadonites of immortal status translated upon the arrival of receivers to reunite the translated Amadonites, who provided leadership through the postrebellion era. [13] Elijah, a fearless warrior for righteousness, restored the concept of God in the northern kingdom during a time of spiritual decadence. [14] Enoch was the first mortal of Urantia to fuse with the Thought Adjuster during life in the flesh. [15]

After ending his emergency bestowal on Urantia, Machiventa Melchizedek disappeared, leaving his followers to establish the truth of the one and only God. [16] Van and Amadon were translated to Jerusem after the arrival of Adam and Eve on earth. [17]
