Majeston, the 50th Reflective Spirit, is the reflectivity chief and Paradise center of the unique and mysterious beings created by the Supreme Creator Personalities in union with the Paradise Trinity. [1]

The Supreme Being, as the father of Majeston, is the co-ordinator of all creature-Creator universe activities, with the Deity Absolute actualizing Majeston in response to his creative mandate. [2] Majeston, created by Trinity and Seven Master Spirits, is a divine being with a mysterious origin. [3]

Majeston, the only personality of divinity created directly by the Supreme Being, is not part of our catalog of Paradise personalities and is exclusively dedicated to universe reflectivity. [4] Majeston embodies the focalized presence of the Supreme Mind in the cosmic realm. [5]

Majeston, the infallible center and chief of reflectivity, is the personalized source of satisfaction for the Daughter Spirits of the Infinite Mother Spirit, syncing the superuniverse governments with the Paradise center of universal reflectivity. [6] Majeston is a true person at the center of reflectivity phenomena in all seven superuniverses. [7] Majeston nominates Paradise Companions, the highest honor for angelic hosts commissioned by the Seven Master Spirits on central Isle. [8]

Majeston presides over millennial conclaves of the Seven Supreme Executives on Paradise, communicating with all intelligent hosts of creation through universal reflectivity. [9] The Unnamed Reflectivator Liaisons of Majeston. [10] The Reflective Image Aids represent influential beings in the superuniverse governments, including the unnamed liaison reflectivators of Majeston. [11] Majeston, fathered by the Supreme Being, is the synthetic co-ordinator of all creature-Creator activities in the universe. [12]
