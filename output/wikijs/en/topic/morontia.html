Definition

Morontia bridges the gulf between material and spiritual, creating a phase of universe reality unknown on Urantia. [1]

Morontia material, consisting of unique energy organization created by modifying revolutions of primary units of matter, exists on architectural spheres with double the elements of evolved planets. [2] Mankind must see that matter is the skeleton of morontia, reflecting the enduring spirit reality. [3] The morontia soul is an experiential acquisition, born from the mortal mind and the indwelling spirit, neither material nor spiritual. [4] Morontia is the vast level between material and spiritual, where the warp is spiritual and the woof is physical. [5]

Art, mortal morontia, bridges the gap between material and spiritual existence, humanizing science and being spiritualized by true religion, while poetry attempts to transcend material realities for spiritual values. [6]

Jesus in morontia

Celestial transformers and midwayers enabled human eyes to see Jesus in his morontia form. [7]

Nature of morontia

Our pursuit of the ideal and striving to be Godlike continues both before and after death, as everything good we do in life directly contributes to our enhancement in the future morontia existence. [8]

Morontia and spirit domiciles, invisible to human vision, are constructed and remodeled by home designers and builders, creating real and beautiful abodes for spirit beings. [9]

Creatures of morontia on Edentia and its associated worlds respirate a unique mixture of gases, including morontia gas, within a tranquil, eternal atmosphere devoid of storms or seasonal changes. [10] The morontia form of Jesus could be made indifferent to ordinary matter, enabling it to escape the sealed sepulchre. [11]

The morontia realms have been known since the early days of the Planetary Prince and serve as liaison spheres between the material and spiritual levels of creature existence. [12]

Life in morontia entails real contact with energies and materials of both physical and morontia levels, facilitated by seraphic technicians who help ascending pilgrims acclimatize to each new level. [13] Morontia life begins at the conception of the soul on inhabited worlds, granting ascending mortals the potential for supermortal function. [14] Morontia mathematics enhances and articulates the enlarged dimensional experiences on the morontia level. [15]

On the mansion worlds, morontia students consume energy-rich food without creating any waste, transitioning from near human to increasingly spiritual beings. [16]

Melchizedeks certify attainment of morontia wisdom among Jerusem citizens before further evaluation by other celestial beings and final approval by the citizenship registrars. [17] Skillful management of morontia and spirit energies creates energy impingements that result in melody. [18]

Morontia beings must regularly enter divine rest to replenish their energy, a necessary practice for the effective utilization of spiritual-energy intake. [19] Life on each ascending sphere is a modified form of plant life evolving from material to spiritual through selective re-keying. [20]

Morontia material is formed through the regulation and conversion of morontia energy by liaison stabilizers, essential for the physicalization of existence. [21] Morontia substance and mind, created by Morontia Power Supervisors, reveal unrevealed activities of the Master Spirits in the nether side of Paradise. [22]

Morontia energy

Morontia circuits are distinct from physical and spiritual circuits on the transition worlds, coordinated by Circuit Regulators exclusively planetary, to energize the morontia spheres. [23] The morontia realm is controlled by Universe Power Directors. [24]
