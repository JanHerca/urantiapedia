The mores and folkways developed unconsciously out of a shared desire to avoid pain and humiliation while pursuing pleasure and power. [1] In a world of inevitability, pleasure and happiness can only be truly appreciated in the face of pain and suffering. [2] Andonites not so sensitive to pain, they were imitative and lacked a developed sense of humor like early human beings. [3]

Pain and suffering, essential to progressive evolution, inhibit activity and prompt biologic adaptations as anxiety reverts to the natural state of our savage ancestors. [4]

Heat, through methods like sunlight and hot stones, was used to relieve pain and treat ailments, as early man believed the spirit causing sickness could be eliminated by sweating. [5] Life Carriers’ healing technique affords relief from pain and better control over cell proliferation. [6] No pain on Paradise, only eternal joy and light in the presence of God and his Son. [7]
