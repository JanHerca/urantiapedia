The Universal Father, with divine selflessness, relinquishes authority, delegates power but always remains primal, holding the all-powerful veto scepter over the destiny of creation. [1]

In a settled system, the system conclave, subject to the veto of the Constellation Fathers, becomes supreme in governing the self-governing inhabited worlds. [2]

The Most High observer wields veto power over the planetary government's administration, making the description of Urantia's government complex and unique. [3]
