1 of 3 original selections for Garden of Eden. [1] After default, Adam abandoned Garden of Eden; caravanned to. [2] Caravan to second garden took 1 year. [3] Civilization artificial; had not evolved. [4] Cradle of civilization for 30,000 years. [5] Culture terminated by infiltration of inferiors. [6] Defense wall extended 56 miles between rivers. [7] Farming and gardening chief pursuits. [8] Herds, seeds, plant bulbs, and cereals caravanned to. [9] Life in Mesopotamia. [10] Located between Tigris and Euphrates rivers. [11] Religious life difficult to organize; Nodites in charge. [12]
