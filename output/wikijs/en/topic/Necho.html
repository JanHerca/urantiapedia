Necho wiped out King Josiah, leading Judah into Egyptian bondage under the Baal political party's control of courts and priesthood, promoting Baal worship as an economic and social system. [1] Judah fell under Babylon's rule after Necho was overthrown by Nebuchadnezzar, leading to a decade of grace followed by rebellion. [2]
