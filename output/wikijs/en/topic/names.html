After fusion, mortals receive their real universe names. [1] Hereditary kingship and aristocracy were established to prevent anarchy through name ownership. [2]

Human subjects are often known by numbers of their Adjusters until receiving real universe names after Adjuster fusion, signaled by the bestowal of the new name by the destiny guardian. [3] Parental love and pride in children's success transcends biological instincts, evolving into a new realm of name pride and family pride. [4] Primitive man believed that names were sacred entities that could be pawned, like today's signed notes. [5] Translating the names of celestial beings into human language proves to be a challenging task for revelators. [6]
