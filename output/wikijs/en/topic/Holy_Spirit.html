The Holy Spirit is the spirit of the personal presence of the Divine Minister, the Mother Spirit of a local universe. The work of the Adjutant Mind-Spirits is prior to the work of the Holy Spirit and is preparatory to its arrival. [1] John the Baptist revealed to his disciples that though he baptized with water, Jesus would baptize them with the Holy Spirit. [2] Jesus instructed the apostles concerning the nature of the Holy Spirit, but they failed to fully understand. [3] The Master Spirit of Orvonton is likely to have participated in the bestowal of the Holy Spirit. [4] Mortals can know the presence and be conscious of the presence of the Holy Spirit. [5] The Holy Spirit “searches all things” and is able to communicate to other beings all that it knows about us. [6] Spiritual reason, the intelligence of the soul, is the endowment of the Holy Spirit, the gift of the Creative Spirit to man. [7]

Nature of the Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit is an indigenous circuit limited to the local universe. [8] Represents the spiritual circuit of the Mother Spirit of the local universe. [9] Does not indwell individual minds as an isolated entity. [10] The Holy Spirit is increasingly effective as mortals obey divine guidance. [11] Is the initial supermind bestowal; It is universally bestowed. [12] This Spirit enables its possessor to be able to consciously or unconsciously choose the presence of the Adjuster. [13] Its ministry is most effective in the light and life stages; in the fourth stage advanced mortals achieve conscious contact with the Holy Spirit. [14]

Associated ceremonies

John the Baptist preached a baptism with water, but Jesus announced a baptism with the Holy Spirit. [15] The ancient Christian ceremony of the bestowal of the Holy Spirit was accompanied by a breath upon the candidates. [16]
