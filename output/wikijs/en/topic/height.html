Adam and Eve, members of the senior corps of Material Sons on Jerusem, were eight feet tall. [1] Anxiety cannot add handbreadth to your stature or a day to your life. [2]

Unexpected strains of giantism among the green and orange peoples show variation at the expense of the original endowment, with mortal stature tending to decrease from the red man down to the indigo race. [3]
