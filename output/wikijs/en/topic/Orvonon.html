Orvonon brought spiritual awakening to the isolated indigo race in Africa, who, though later forgetting his message, maintained a desire to worship the Unknown. [1]

Orvonon, who led the indigo races and served “The God of Gods,” now is one of the special 24 counselors of Jerusem designated by Jesus after his depart from our world. [2]
