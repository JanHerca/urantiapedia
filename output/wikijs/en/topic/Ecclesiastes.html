Ecclesiastes is a book of the Bible within the group called Wisdom Books. As other Wisdom Books like Wisdom of Salomon, exemplifies Platonism and Stoicism. [1] The frank pessimism of Ecclesiastes was a worldly wise reaction to these overoptimistic beliefs in Providence. [2]
