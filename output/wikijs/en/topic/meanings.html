Meaning is the appreciative consciousness of values that experience adds to our perception of reality. [1]

In the universe reality, values can be modified and augmented, even in relations with infinite Deity, leading to the increased actualization of divine values through expanded comprehension of reality meanings in experiential universes. [2] Meanings are recognized and understood in inner human experience, beyond the sensory world. [3] New religious insights arise out of conflicts, where new meanings only emerge amid conflict. [4] Religion thrives on faith and insight, discovering spiritual meanings in well-known facts rather than seeking new experiences. [5] Meaning requires unraveling and correlation in the mind for true unity and understanding in human experience. [6]
