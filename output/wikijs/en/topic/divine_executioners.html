Divine executioners, being fourth creatures—quasi-material beings—are almost, but not quite, visible to the short-range vision of the mortal races. [1] They assume role of mercy-justice interpreters from minor sectors on and become commissions’ physical voices. [2] Solitary Messengers may function as divine executioners for high tribunals. [3] They divested of retributive power at superuniverse level and becomes the physical voice of the spirit trio. [4] They are the executioners of Ancients of Days; they can extinguish personality existence. [5] They posses a great power to put into effect decisions of conciliating commissions. [6] Master Physical Controllers can be provisionally subject to the orders of the divine executioners. [7] They are the quasi-physical members of conciliating commissions. [8]

Though executing decrees in defiance of neither natural law nor the ordained usages of the realm, they do ofttimes effect their strange doings and enforce the mandates of the conciliators in accordance with the higher laws of the system administration. [9]
