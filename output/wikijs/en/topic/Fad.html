Fad was the director of the faculty in charge of disseminating and preserving knowledge, one of the ten councils of the Caligastia staff on Urantia. [1] Fad and 5 members of his council were opposed to the rebellion. [2] Fad formulated the first 25-character alphabet and introduced a writing system. [3] He sponsored a method of teaching by doing.
