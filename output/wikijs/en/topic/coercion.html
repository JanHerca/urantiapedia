The domination of the Spirit is never tainted with coercion nor compromised by compulsion. [1]

The evolution of religious observances progressed from placation, avoidance, exorcism, coercion, conciliation, and propitiation to sacrifice, atonement, and redemption. [2]

Primitive man believed that something special must be done to win the favor of the gods. The rituals of avoidance, exorcism, coercion, and propitiation all merge into one another. [3]
