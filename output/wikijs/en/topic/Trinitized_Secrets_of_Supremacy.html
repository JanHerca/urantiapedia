The highest order of the Stationary Sons of the Trinity, the Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy, direct the sacred spheres nearest Paradise, each presided over by ten supreme administrators. [1] The Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy reveal the intertwined secrets of the Supreme, Ultimate, and Supreme-Ultimate. [2]

The Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy collectively know all secrets, directing Ascendington and other worlds of the Father, little comprehended by other orders. [3]

The Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy are fully known only by their special world groups, little comprehended by other orders, and ardently loved by those who attain Paradise. [4] The Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy oversee the Paradise worlds of the Father, the highest order of Stationary Sons of Trinity. [5] The Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy are inscribed in personality registers. [6] 70 Trinitized Secrets of Supremacy oversee the seven innermost worlds in the personal circuit of the Universal Father. [7]
