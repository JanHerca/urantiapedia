Even student visitors are allowed on all inhabited planets, even in isolation, to visit within any realm they have experientially attained. [1] Student visitors are registered in the Uversa personality register. [2]

Various types of celestial visitors, including observers, exchange pupils, and student helpers, populate the courtesy colony on Uversa, with over one billion individuals present at any given time. [3]
