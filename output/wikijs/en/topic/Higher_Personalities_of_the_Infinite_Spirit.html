“In personality register, Higher Personalities of the Infinite Spirit include Solitary Messengers, Universe Circuit Supervisors, Census Directors, Personal Aids of the Infinite Spirit, Associate Inspectors, Assigned Sentinels, and Graduate Guides.”. [1]

Master Spirit Number Three directs many high personalities of the Infinite Spirit, presiding over their assemblies and speaking for the Infinite Spirit in council. [2]

Orders

The orders of Higher Personalities of the Infinite Spirit function in seven divisions throughout the grand universe. [3]

  1. Solitary Messengers.
  2. Universe Circuit Supervisors.
  3. Census Directors.
  4. Personal Aides of Infinite Spirit.
  5. Associate Inspectors.
  6. Assigned Sentinels.
  7. Graduate guides.

Home

Higher Personalities of the Infinite Spirit find residence in Jerusem circles alongside messenger hosts, ministering spirits, and other celestial beings. [4]
