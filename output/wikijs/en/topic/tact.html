Tact is essential for success in securing the support and cooperation of others. [1]

Tact is the fulcrum of social leverage, enabling wise souls to avoid unnecessary misunderstandings and navigate troublesome interactions with tolerance and grace. [2] The master gains victory gracefully through tact and tolerance, avoiding unnecessary battles. [3] Jesus, had it not been for a tactical blunder, would have managed the crowd and disarmed even his violent enemies with good-naturedness. [4]
