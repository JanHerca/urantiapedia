There was a beautiful park called Magadan south of Bethsaida-Julias. [1] South of the city of Bethsaida-Julias, in Magadan Park, is where the miraculous feeding of some five thousand people took place. [2]

Jesus had very harsh words towards the inhabitants of this city: woe upon the light-rejecting inhabitants of Chorazin, Bethsaida-Julias, and Capernaum, the cities which did not well receive these messengers. [3]
