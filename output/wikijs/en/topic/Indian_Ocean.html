100,000,000 years ago, the Indian Ocean was more or less as it is today. [1]

Once the Strait of Gibraltar closed, Spain was connected with Africa by an old land bridge, later enabling the Mediterranean to flow into the Atlantic through a narrow channel extending across France, with mountain peaks and highlands appearing as islands above this ancient sea, before ultimately being connected with the Indian Ocean.
