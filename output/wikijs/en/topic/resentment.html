Judas's subconscious resentment towards Jesus, rooted in the unhealed wounds of disappointment and bitterness, ultimately turned his love into hate. [1] Creative imagination inhibited by resentment stifles the potential for constructive and spontaneous creativity in individuals. [2]

Judas became increasingly a victim of resentment, brooding over personal disappointment and growing suspicious of his friends, even betraying his Master. [3]

Discover true values in life by loving God and understanding and sympathetically discerning other's viewpoints to overcome resentment and foster tolerance, friendship, and ultimately love. [4]
