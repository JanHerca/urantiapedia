When a Paradise Son is bestowed upon such a world, completes his mission, ascends to the Universal Father, is accepted, and returns as the accredited deliverer of this isolated world. [1] Warnings from Jesus: After I leave you, take heed that no man deceive you, for many will come as deliverers and will lead many astray. [2]

Personas who acknowledged

After baptism, John knew Jesus was the Deliverer, and so he said him. [3] Jesus acknowledged to Nalda the samaritan that he was the Deliverer. [4] Peter also acknowledged Jesus to be the Deliverer. [5]
