Variety is essential to the concept of beauty, encompassing the drama of unifying contrasts between Creator and creature, achieving the apex of cosmic art. [1] Variety is indispensable for the wide functioning of natural selection and the differential survival of superior strains. [2] Taking yourself too seriously decreases the likelihood of success; variety is restful, monotony wears and exhausts. [3]
