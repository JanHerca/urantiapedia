Master Physical Controllers are the mobile subordinates of Supreme Power Centers, overseeing individual worlds and receiving encircuited lines of power from their system's power center. [1] 500,000 Master Physical Controllers in Satania regulate and manipulate the basic energies of space with complete control. [2] The Master Physical Controllers, serving throughout the grand universe, are directed by the Council of Equilibrium from Paradise. [3] Master Physical Controllers exhibit distinct traits in personality registers. [4]

Infinite Spirit ministers to primitive mind through physical controllers, bestowing the unique feature of mind upon all life forms with the direction of mind being a ministry of mind-spirit or mind-energy personalities. [5]

Master Spirit Number Five, adviser to power directors, power centers, physical controllers, and all personalities originating from the Father and the Conjoint Actor, speaks in the councils of the Seven Master Spirits about the Father-Spirit attitude. [6] The grand rendezvous of the Master Physical Controllers is at the minor sector headquarters worlds. [7]

Events involved in them

The Master Physical Controllers assisted Life Carriers on Urantia until their work was completed and they were permitted to depart, leaving behind a select group to continue their mission on the planet. [8] Salvington was built in 1 billion years as the first act of physical creation in Nebadon. [9] The physical controllers enabled apostles to witness Jesus' transfiguration with brilliant celestial beings. [10]

700,000,000,000 years ago, physical controllers were dispatched from the formation of Andronover to provide support and cooperation to nine surrounding material creations rapidly evolving within this gigantic space wheel. [11] Master Physical Controllers brought healing at sundown, restoring health, healing diseases, and saving souls with creative power. [12]

Machiventa Melchizedek volunteered to personalize on earth as a temporary man of the realm, with the materialization of this Melchizedek Son completed by the Master Physical Controllers. [13]

Functions

Master Physical Controllers, functioning collectively in batteries of hundreds, thousands, and even millions, accelerate or retard energy currents like catalytic agents in cooperation with Supreme Power Centers. [14]

Subpersonal living things activate energy-matter, evolving from physical controllers to adjutant mind-spirits, with the possibility of self-destruction inherent in free will. [15]

The Master Physical Controllers manipulate vital energies crucial for interplanetary transport and communication, utilizing undiscovered forms of energy for laying lines and enhancing vision and sound equivalents. [16]

The Master Physical Controllers, in collaboration with Morontia Power Supervisors, construct morontia temples on planets based on approved plans from the system capital. [17]

Primary midwayers can achieve liaison co-operation with diverse energy controllers, while secondary midwayers work with physical controllers to utilize the full energy gamut. [18] The chief of the Satania physical controllers serves on the Jerusem executive council. [19] The Master Physical Controllers distribute power to inhabited worlds with perfect coordination and efficiency to ensure satisfactory distribution. [20] Physical controllers elevate mortal bodies into the atmosphere for fusion on presettled worlds without morontia temples. [21]

The Master Physical Controllers formulate and control mechanical-nonteachable mind, preadjutant mind levels across the vast network of the universe's functions. [22]

Master Physical Controllers can collectively influence energy control in batteries of millions, by varying positions and formations to upstep, accelerate, detain, condense, retard, and transform energy, functioning in cooperation with Supreme Power Centers. [23] The Master Physical Controllers help midwayers humanize in life and light on their journey to Paradise ascension. [24] The Master Physical Controllers oversee energy management on individual worlds within their systems. [25]

Master Physical Controllers insulate planets against powerful energy currents, preserving nature from the disfigurement caused by the rebellion and misconduct of creatures who do not worship her as God. [26] The architectural spheres are beautifully illuminated, heated, and energized by the Master Physical Controllers on Jerusem. [27]

The Master Physical Controllers make midhwayers visible in light and life, promoting their advanced planetary status and enabling mortals to recognize their presence. [28] The physical controllers mobilize, transform, manipulate, and transmit nearly all physical energies. [29] The Master Physical Controllers modify revolutions to create morontia substance on architectural spheres with double the elements of evolved planets. [30] The Master Physical Controllers readjust worlds in light and life during the fifth stage of settled existence. [31] Master Physical Controllers render Planetary Princes and celestial beings visible to mortal creatures for communication and collaboration. [32]

The Master Physical Controllers serve on the archangel commission of Life Carrier transmutation, presided over by the chief of archangels, to enable Life Carriers to function on physical levels of electrochemistry during life implantation. [33]

The Master Physical Controllers, directly governed from Paradise by the Seven Supreme Power Directors, serve throughout the grand universe and are directed by the Council of Equilibrium. [34] Some Master Physical Controllers may react to impulses of the Universal Absolute, influencing certain energies beyond their perfect control. [35]

Superuniverse power centers and physical controllers regulate the gravita energy systems, operating with highly specialized organization and intelligence. [36]

The Master Physical Controllers transmute power to sustain life on worlds where respiration is impossible, creating beings able to exchange energy through light-energy and direct power transmutations. [37]

Nature

Physical controllers and power centers, devoid of nostalgic reverie, remain ever diligent in their duty, absent from occasions of rejoicing. [38] The Master Physical Controllers utilize their antigravity endowments to establish physical equilibrium in the grand universe. [39] The Master Physical Controllers are the direct offspring of Supreme Power Centers, and their numbers include the following: [40] The Master Physical Controllers are part of God the Sevenfold. [41] The Master Physical Controllers are always thoroughly businesslike in all their reactions. [42]

The Master Physical Controllers traverse local space like Solitary Messengers, aided by their unique metamorphosis abilities and the assistance of fellow beings to overcome gravity and inertia. [43] Physical controllers and power centers are inherently perfect in action, undergoing no training and experiencing no evolution in ranks. [44]

The Master Physical Controllers adapt their techniques on each planet based on the calculations of physicists, accounting for a variety of celestial factors such as highly heated suns and cold dark giants. [45]

Government

The Master Physical Controllers operate independently and are not subject to administrative direction by superuniverse government. [46]

The Master Physical Controllers on Edentia and Jerusem expertly supervise the enormous power systems energizing the spheres, surpassing the glories of the training worlds of Jerusem. [47] The Master Physical Controllers are subject to divine orders from conciliating commissions. [48] Seraphim technicians are liaisons with Master Physical Controllers, assisting new ascenders in adjusting to morontia spheres. [49]

Types of Master Physical Controllers

The Master Physical Controllers are the versatile, intelligent, and essential beings responsible for managing and controlling the vast and varied energies of the grand universe, serving under the direction of the Supreme Power Centers and assisting in the maintenance of energy balance and power equilibrium throughout the superuniverses. [50]

  2. Mechanical controllers.
  3. Energy transformers.
  4. Energy transmitters.
  5. Primary associators.
  6. Secondary dissociators.
  7. Frandalanks and chronoldeks.
