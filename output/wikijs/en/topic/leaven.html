Beware of the leaven of the Pharisees, which is hypocrisy, and strive for true religion based on living truth and spiritual realities. [1] The parable of the leaven illustrates how the kingdom of heaven grows and spreads, transforming everything it touches. [2] Place the leaven of new truth in the midst of old beliefs, and do not forget to preach only the good news. [3]
