They were a group of 100 ex-mortals chosen by Caligastia from among 785,000 citizens of Jerusem to accompany and assist him in his mission as prince. All volunteered. [1] 50 were men and 50 women; they served in pairs and the pairs lived together. [2] Were brought to Urantia by seraphic transportation. [3] They lived in dwellings provided with abundance of land. [4] They were the parents of 50,000 primary intermediates; each pair produced 1,000 of them. [5] 40 of 100 refused to join the rebellion. [6] They constituted 10 autonomous councils presided over by Daligastia, with 10 members each. [7]

  1. The council on food and material welfare. It was led by Ang and 3 members remained loyal. [8]
  2. The board of animal domestication. Led by Bon; none remained loyal. [9]
  3. The advisers regarding the conquest of predatory animals. Led by Dan; none remained loyal. [10]
  4. The faculty on dissemination and conservation of knowledge. Directed by Fad; 5 members remained loyal. [11]
  5. The commission on industry and trade. Hosted by Nod; none remained loyal. [12]
  6. The college of revealed religion. Hosted by Hap; all remained loyal. [13]
  7. The council of health and life. Directed by Lut; none remained loyal. [14]
  8. The council of art and science. Led by Mek; all remained loyal. [15]
  9. The governors of advanced tribal relations. Directed by Tut; none remained loyal. [16]
  10. The supreme court of tribal co-ordination and racial co-operation. Driven by Van. Formed by members previously approved by the Most Highs; all remained loyal. [17]

Andonite counterparts

Of these Andonites 56 remained loyal against the rebellion; 44 rebelled. [18] The 55 loyalists, all except Amadon, were transferred to Jerusem upon the arrival of the Melchizedek receivers. [19] Inserted into their bodies a complement of the system's circuits and used the tree of life. [20] The hundred Andonites had received a complement of life that allowed them to feed on the tree of life and indefinitely extend their mortal existence. [21]

Physical aspects

Andonic life plasm was allowed to be transplanted by Avalon surgeons into the body of the hundred Andonite corporeal members. [22] Before the rebellion, they did not reproduce sexually. [23] They were constituted as sexual creatures in order to mate with Adam's offspring. [24] Were equally divided as to sex. [25] They had the skin color and language of the Andonites. [26] Their material bodies were constructed ones. [27] They had a nonflesh diet. [28] On Jerusem they had no reproductive prerogatives. [29] The tree of life allowed them to extend their lives indefinitely. [30]

Loyalists

All hundred loyal except Van were transferred to Jerusem. [31] The loyal ones had in their possession the tree of life. [32] Helped to prevent the destruction of spiritual truth. [33]

Rebels

They were degraded to mortal status and died natural deaths. [34] They were deprived of the vital circuits of the system. [35] They were expelled from Dalamatia by semi-savage hordes. [36] They have an unknown fate. His Adjusters still tarry on Jerusem. [37] Resorted to sexual reproduction to increase their numbers. [38]

Work of the Caligastia one hundred

The plan was to procreate successors before retiring at the arrival of Adam and Eve. [39] Adopted many Andonic and Sangik children. [40] They were all agriculturists and horticulturists. [41] All worked using physical labor. [42] First introduced pottery to the world. [43] Assembled superior individuals. [44] They did not employ any method that was not natural on Urantia. [45] Remarkably accelerated the normal and natural evolution of the races. [46] Taught agriculture. [47] Taught the Andonites, and the red, blue, and yellow races. [48] Used natural means to improve the race. [49] The blue man is the one who benefited most from the teachings of the hundred. [50]
