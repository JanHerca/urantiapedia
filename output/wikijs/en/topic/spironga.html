The Spironga, created by Gabriel and Father Melchizedek, execute routine spirit tasks in Nebadon as spirit helpers of the local universe. [1] The spironga, efficient natives of the constellations, function from mansion world #2 onward. [2] In Paradise classification, Spironga is categorized as number 4. [3] The spironga on Jerusem, like midwayers on Urantia, labor for the upkeep and embellishment of morontia creations. [4]
