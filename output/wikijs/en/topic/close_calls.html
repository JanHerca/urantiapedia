Andon and Fonta nearly lost their lives. [1]

Our prehuman ancestors were on the brink of extinction several times. The ancestral frog, the mother of the dawn-mammals, and the mother of the Primates twins, who suffered the closest call caused by lightning, almost died several times. [2]
