The attainment of the Universal Father is the passport to eternity, notwithstanding the remaining circuits to be traversed. [1] In hypothetical beginning there was no differentiation throughout all infinity. [2] In eternity all is, even if unrevealed in time and space. [3] There are records kept since personification of Infinite Spirit. [4] Time is the moving image of eternity and space is the fleeting shadow of Paradise realities. [5]

Conception of eternity

Circular simultaneity, not linear sequence. That which formerly appeared as a succession of events then will be viewed as a whole and perfectly related cycle; in this way will circular simultaneity increasingly displace the onetime consciousness of the linear sequence of events. [6]

It is more fitting to conceive of eternity as a cycle and the eternal purpose as an endless circle, a cycle of eternity in some way synchronized with the transient material cycles of time. [7]

It is needed to concede human philosophy a point of origin; even personalities far above the human level require a concept of “beginnings.” Nevertheless, the Paradise-Havona system is eternal. [8] In the evolutionary universes eternity is temporal everlastingness—the everlasting now. [9] Time-space creatures cannot comprehend eternity. [10]
