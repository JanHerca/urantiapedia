The entire council lost in rebellion, with only forty out of the one hundred saved in the end. [1]

Nod, former chairman of the council on industry and trade, led the commission in fostering industry and promoting trade among the peace groups, contributing to the elevation of living standards and expanding trade in improved salt. [2]

The Nodites rebelled against Prince and followed their chosen leader, Nod, discovering their mortal status and eventually becoming known as the Nodites in the land of Nod. [3]
