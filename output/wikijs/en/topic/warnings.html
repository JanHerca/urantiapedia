Adam and Eve were warned of dire consequences for departure from the divine plan long before they left Jerusem. [1] Misunderstood warnings as unjust interference with personal freedoms led Lucifer and Caligastia astray. [2] A sojourner on the seventh mansion world has access to the Satania prison worlds, serving as a solemn warning against rebellion. [3] Warning only intensifies evil determination once love is dead. [4] Despite numerous warnings from the Master, divine guidance often proves ineffective in dealing with embittered human nature. [5]
