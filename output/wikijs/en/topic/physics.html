Despite the power of mathematics and material science, they could not have predicted the miraculous creation of a water molecule. [1]

The laws of physics may be immutable and unresponsive to training, but the endowment of mind allows for adaptation and learning beyond the limitations of chemistry. [2] Truth, beauty, and goodness are not inherent in physics or chemistry, so a machine cannot know or value them. [3]
