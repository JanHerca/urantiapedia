One of the gates in Jerusalem temple, mentioned by Acts of Apostles. Modern archaeology does not know exactly which gate this corresponded to, and it is sometimes identified with the gate called “Nicanor,” which connected the so-called “courtyard of the women” with the “courtyard of the priests.” However, the description in The Urantia Book clearly identifies this gate with the “eastern gate” of the sanctuary enclosure.
