And justice. [1] Bear with erring brother. [2] Creature’s need wholly sufficient to insure God’s full mercy. [3]

Defined as

Applied love; inevitable offspring of goodness and love. [4] Culmination of justice, fairness, and patience. [5] Divinity correlated in personality. [6] Expression of righteousness in attitude of love. [7] God’s attitude of love for each individual. [8] Justice tempered by wisdom and perfection of knowledge. [9] Leading to perfection. [10] Quality of growth. [11] Saving interval between seedtime and harvest. [12] Season of grace before full ripening of evil. [13] Spiritual deliverance from anger, desire for selfish power or revenge. [14]

Eternal Son is mercy. [15] Evidence man is indwelt by God. [16] Extension of mercy to wrongdoer may work hardship on other family members. [17] Forgive 70 times 7 times. [18] In doubt, Judges invariably rule in personal interests of individual. [19] Let decisions favor those in need. [20] Merciful shall obtain mercy. [21] Mercy credits always far in excess of ability to exhaust; of lavish proportions established to insure survival. [22] Mercy ministry is always work of the individual. [23] Not thrust upon those who despise it; cast not pearls before swine. [24] Requires adjudication consonant with creature nature. [25] Show mercy to those who abuse you. [26] Spiritual growth favored by refusal to presume on divine mercy. [27] Time lag of mercy; wisdom of delay. [28] When mercy exhausted, justice prevails. [29]
