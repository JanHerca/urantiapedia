Hertzian waves utilized for broadcasting on Urantia. [1]

The role of upper air belts in radio broadcasting involves reflecting terrestrial broadcast waves back to the ground, enabling long-distance transmission despite occasional disturbances from outer ionospheric storms. [2] Hertzian waves, found thirty octaves below visible sunlight in the superuniverse, are utilized for radio broadcasting on Urantia. [3]
