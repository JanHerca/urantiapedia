A very superior civilization is evolving on an isolated continental nation, self-sustaining and able to live indefinitely without importing from neighboring planets. [1] No Paradise Sons missions on neighboring planet, yet Urantians should not be divided by cultural differences. [2]

With permission from Lanaforge and approval from the Most Highs of Edentia, I can describe the social, moral, and political life of an advanced human race on a neighboring planet in the Satania system. [3]

Other nations should take note of the advanced culture and government of this neighboring planet, and consider the potential benefits of promoting mutual exchange and education for the advancement of civilization. [4]

Neighboring planet's Planetary Prince and Material Son defaulted, leading to isolation and lack of a Magisterial Son, following in the footsteps of Urantia's fate. [5] The pouring out of the Spirit of Truth on Urantia has significantly advanced its readiness for planetary government and world-wide peace under law. [6]
