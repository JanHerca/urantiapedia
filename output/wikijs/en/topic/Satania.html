The Master Physical Controllers serve with power centers throughout the grand universe, while in local systems like Satania, functions are more easily understood as it is one of 100 local systems forming the administrative organization of Norlatiadek, surrounded by neighboring systems like Sandmatia, Assuntia, Porogia, Sortoria, Rantulia, and Glantonia, all of which, like Satania, are evolutionary and progressive. [1] Urantia is planet 606 of Satania with over 600 inhabited worlds in the system. [2]

In Satania, the language is learned with the help of brilliant beings serving as teachers, while Morontia Companions act as interpreters for visitors mastering new tongues. [3] On Satania, a standard day in Jerusem is equivalent to slightly less than three Urantia days. [4] 500,000 physical controllers in Satania supervise the circuitizing and channelizing of energy in organized space. [5]

Administration

The administration of Satania functions on a representative plan, with each system selecting representatives to sit in the constellation legislature every one hundred years, chosen by the Jerusem council of one thousand electors, all graduates of the highest school of the Melchizedek College of Administration. [6] Lucifer and Satan were able to freely roam Satania until Michael's bestowal mission on Urantia was completed. [7]

The Ancients of Days sustained the Most Highs in their seizure of Satania government during the Lucifer secession, ensuring continued control until resolution of pending appeals. [8] The System Sovereign of Satania is currently supported by seven Lanonandek Sons. [9]

Jerusem, the headquarters of Satania, is an average capital of a local system, typical of similar spheres, where order, good will, and heavenly conditions are being restored efficiently. [10] Melchizedek taught the concept of one God, a universal Deity, associated with the System Sovereign, Lanaforge, post-Michael's bestowal on Urantia. [11] Despite the rebellion in Satania, there have been no changes in the technique of system administration. [12]

No Satania worlds have been lost to rebellion since the original 37, preventing further penetration of archrebels' doctrines of discontent and self-assertion. [13] I am authorized to narrate something of the social, moral, and political life of the neighboring planet in Satania. [14]

Astronomic situation

Satania is a local system within Norlatiadek, with immediate neighboring systems like Sandmatia, Assuntia, Porogia, Sortoria, Rantulia, and Glantonia, all of which are evolutionary and progressive. [15] Satania is local system #24 in the constellation of Norlatiadek, with its headquarters world being Jerusem. [16]

Urantia was among the least in all creation until Michael's bestowal elevated it to a position of honor, situated on the outskirts of Satania, the outermost system of Norlatiadek. [17]

Anova, the first inhabited world in Satania, thrives as one of forty-four satellites orbiting a dark planet illuminated by three neighboring suns, its civilization in a state of advancement. [18] The Satania system, on the periphery of Nebadon, is distant from the center of the seventh superuniverse of Orvonton. [19] The power center of Satania, a dark island at the astronomic center, directs energy streams to evolutionary planets. [20]

System comprises

Only nine worlds in Satania are inhabited by nonbreather intelligent beings. [21]

Satania is an incomplete system with 619 inhabited worlds, including Urantia as the 606th planet to reach the evolutionary stage of human beings, with 36 planets approaching the life-endowment phase. [22]

The administrative center of Satania is comprised of 57 architectural spheres, including Jerusem, the seven major satellites, and the forty-nine subsatellites, with Jerusem itself being almost one hundred times the size of Urantia. [23] There are 61 decimal planets in Satania where life experiments are conducted to modify or improve standard universe types of living beings. [24] There are currently 619 inhabited worlds in the planetary group of Satania, with 200 more evolving toward life. [25] Satania comprises 619 inhabited worlds in over 500 physical systems, with your world, Urantia, being number 606 in the planetary group. [26] Satania is composed of over 7000 physical systems, each with unique origins, surrounding a dark island near the system government. [27] There are almost 2000 brilliant suns in Satania, with only three brighter than your own average blazing orb. [28]

Rebellion situation

Satania was isolated by quarantine upon rebellion, disrupting its representation on the supreme council at universe headquarters. [29] The seven prison worlds circling the Father's world in Satania serve as detention spheres for the interned groups of the Lucifer rebellion. [30]

Satania's readmission into the constellation family awaits the completion of the adjudication of the affairs of the Lucifer rebellion by the tribunal of the Ancients of Days. [31] Ages required to retrieve handicaps of rebellion on Satania will persist for an eternity due to the consequences of the Lucifer rebellion. [32]
