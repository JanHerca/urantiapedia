The blue men became expert hunters and trappers; by fencing the rivers they caught fish in great numbers, drying the surplus for winter use. [1] Jesus’ uncle in Magdala was a fisherman. [2] Jesus was expert fisherman. [3] Jude the Jesus' brother ran away to become fisherman. [4] Apostles were fishermen most of them. [5] Jesus enjoined apostles to devote themselves to fishing for two weeks, adding, “And then will you go forth to become fishers of men. [6]
