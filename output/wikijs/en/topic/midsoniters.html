In each local system of inhabited worlds throughout Nebadon, there is a single sphere designated as a midsonite world where Melchizedeks serve as life carriers, choosing Mother Eves from volunteers selected by the System Sovereign. [1] Midsoniters come to local systems in light and life, serving as counselors and advisers, inculcating supreme mota meanings with finaliters. [2] Midsonite parental groups volunteer to help at the system probationary nursery supervised by Material Sons and Daughters from Jerusem. [3] Midsoniters from universe headquarters worlds promote spiritual growth by inculcating supreme mota meanings into mortal understanding. [4] Midsoniters exist in a mysterious realm between mortal and immortal, human and divine, with a destiny shrouded in uncertainty yet untouched by death. [5] Midsoniters exist in a unique state between mortality and immortality, not being Adjuster indwelt, but never having experienced death. [6]

The midsoniters are the progeny of Melchizedek Life Carriers and Material Daughters, created in a unique process involving a Melchizedek Son and a Daughter of the material order of sonship. [7] Midsoniters live as reproducing beings for 1,000 years before being removed by seraphic transport and becoming nonreproducing forever. [8]

The purpose of the midsonite creatures remains unknown, as they gather on the seventh finaliter world in preparation for a future event in universe evolution, despite the finaliters' refusal to discuss their wards' destiny. [9]
