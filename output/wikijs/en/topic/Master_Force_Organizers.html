The Master Force Organizers can affect the genetic and composite motions of astronomic systems within the master universe. [1] The Master Force Organizers function in outer space alongside Solitary Messengers and Inspired Trinity Spirits. [2] Force organizers control universe making, ensuring endless metamorphoses of creation and destruction. [3]

The Frandalanks with Universe Power Directors were created by all three orders of energy-control beings, functioning interchangeably in the central and superuniverses. [4] The Master Force Organizers, directed by Architects of the Master Universe, function exclusively in the grand universe under their supervision. [5] In Paradise classification, Primary Eventuated and Associate Transcendental Master Force Organizers exist. [6]

The Master Force Organizers initiate changes which transform absolute into energy, in alignment with the prerogatives of the Universal Father and Creator personalities. [7]

Primary Master Force Organizers transform primordial space-forces of the Unqualified Absolute into puissant energy, creating nebulae and initiating energy cyclones of space. [8] The Master Force Organizers manipulate primordial forces in unorganized space throughout the master universe. [9] 987,000,000,000 years ago, acting inspector #811,307 reported to the Ancients of Days about the formation of the Andronover nebula. [10]

Before the days of Universe Power Directors, Master Force Organizers supervising superuniverse energy transmute space potency into primordial force for the evolution of physical reality. [11]

The Master Force Organizers endure extreme temperatures and conditions, surpassing even the abilities of Orvonton's power centers and physical controllers. [12]

Transcendental beings

The two orders of Paradise force organizers are Primary Eventuated and Associate Transcendental Master Force Organizers. [13] Types of Master Force Organizers.

  1. Primary Eventuated Master Force Organizers. [14]
    • Master Force Organizers bring forth puissant energy to the level of initial response to Paradise gravity, setting in motion gigantic energy systems responsive to the collective influences from the nether side of Paradise. [15]
    • The Primary Master Force Organizers inaugurated the energy whirl of Andronover then withdrew at right angles, leading to the emergence of a new physical creation in Orvonton from the Andronover nebula. [16]
    • The Primary Master Force Organizers passively modify space potency into segregata, transforming it into primordial force responsive to the Universal Father's grasp and emerging as pure energy on Uversa. [17]
    • Primary Master Force Organizers are the nebulae creators of the universe wheels in outer space. [18]
    • Primary Master Force Organizers transmute segregata into puissant energy, manipulating primordial space-forces into nebulae creators. [19]
  2. Associate Transcendental Master Force Organizers. [20]
    • The Council of Equilibrium, comprised of high commissioners dispatched by the Seven Master Spirits from the personnel of the Associate Master Force Organizers, governs and directs the Master Physical Controllers throughout the grand universe. [21]
    • When the Creator Son arrives, the Master Force Organizers leave the local universe. [22]
    • The Master Force Organizers transform space energy into gravity-responsive systems, laying the foundation for universe organization. [23]
    • Paradise Force Organizers metamorphose cosmic force in outer space, ancestral to the energy of the grand universe, while Orvonton power directors are uninvolved with these distant realms. [24]
    • Upon the manifestation of linear gravity response, Master Force Organizers retire in favor of Universe Power Directors, as gravity energy emerges as the precursor of all universe matter, influenced by the force manipulation of the former. [25]
    • When puissant energy responds to Paradise gravity, Secondary Eventuated Master Force Organizers take over from primary organizers. [26]
    • Ultimatons, transmuted by Master Force Organizers, accelerate revolutionary velocity but require power directors to attain critical escape velocity for deindividuation. [27]
    • Primary Master Force Organizers transmute puissant energy into gravity-energy, creating nebulae and initiating the energy cyclones of space. [28]
