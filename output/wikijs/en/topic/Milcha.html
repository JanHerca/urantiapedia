At Jesus' 16th appearance, he instructed the most representative group of believers on the reality of the fatherhood of God and the truth of the sonship of man, urging them to proclaim the gospel of the kingdom with faith and love. [1] Milcha, cousin of Apostle Thomas, was among the ten women Jesus chose to minister for the kingdom. [2]
