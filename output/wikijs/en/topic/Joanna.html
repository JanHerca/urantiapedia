Joanna arranged a meaningful meeting in Herod's new palace, focusing on women's roles in religious life. [1]

At Jesus' appearances, he instructed the women believers to proclaim the good news of the liberty of mankind through the gospel of sonship with God in the kingdom of heaven. [2] Joanna, along with five other women, discovered the empty tomb of Jesus early on Sunday morning. [3]

Joanna, treasurer of the women's corps and wife of Chuza, steward of Herod, was among the ten women appointed by Jesus for the ministering work of the kingdom, establishing their organization with Susanna as chief and treasurer. [4]
