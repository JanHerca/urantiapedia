Space potency, as the primal endowment of Paradise, encompasses absoluta potencies existing within space, beyond the reach of gravitational interactions. [1] Absolute presence circuits are independent of nether Paradise force center and not related to force, energy, or power circuits. [2] Pilgrims in Havona can freely explore achieved space but must rely on supernatural help to navigate unachieved space. [3] Adjusted-fused personalities traverse space freely before receiving morontia investiture in the higher morontia spheres. [4]

Seven distinct space conditions and motions exist from the periphery of Paradise to the inner borders of the seven superuniverses, including quiescent midspace zones, clockwise processional circuits, and dark gravity bodies revolving counterclockwise and clockwise. [5] About force-charge of space see link on absoluta.

Time, space, and experience are both the greatest aids and most formidable obstacles to reality perception for finite creatures attempting to conceive infinite unification. [6] The mind transcends space, a reality often overlooked due to the confusion surrounding the concept of space as related to time. [7]

The motions of space are complex and mysterious, with the Unqualified Absolute's limited function within it raising questions about the origin and nature of motion itself. [8] Pilgrims may freely traverse achieved space without being ensupernaphimed during their sojourn in Havona. [9]

The extra-Paradise reflectivity service operates independently of space, time, and known subabsolute universe circuits within the seven superuniverses. [10] The storage of energy into ultimatons causes measurable vibrations in the content of space, known as ultimatonic rays. [11]

In certain sectors of space, small planets slowly accumulate from a vast amount of matter, growing through meteoric accretion and minor collisions to become inhabited worlds. [12]

Deities and space

The Infinite Mind is free from the constraints of space, while lower levels of mind are burdened by its limitations. [13] Divine omnipresence transcends space as no barrier exists to Deity. [14] Personalities of Infinite Spirit transcend space in their interactions with temporal beings, though time constraints remain a factor. [15] Spirit is independent of space, while motion and gravity are intertwined in the universe. [16]

Nature of space

Space is a bestowal of Paradise, pervaded by the ancestral space potency of the Unqualified Absolute, extending beyond the master universe. [17] Space came into being with the creation of Havona, as the God of Action functions and one billion perfect spheres flash into existence. [18] Space is an ultimate condition within the relative absoluteness of midspace. [19] Space converts energy into matter, forming electronic associations and basic nuclear matter in the vast expanse. [20]

Space does not exist on Paradise, but is relative to it, as the First Source and Center has concentrated all absolute potential for cosmic reality in Paradise, making subinfinite, even time-space, creation possible. [21] Space is not empty and contains 100 ultimatons per cubic inch, modified by energy and matter within organized space. [22] Space extends unknown distance beyond the master universe, pervaded by the ancestral space potency of the Unqualified Absolute. [23]

Space is filled with gravity-responding energy currents, power circuits, ultimatonic and electronic energies, preventing the theoretical absolute zero and making it practically not empty. [24] Time and space are inherently connected in the seven superuniverses, except in the transcendent realm of Paradise. [25]

The interelectronic space of an atom is activated by synchronized wavelike manifestations, behaving unpredictably due to a space-force reaction of the Unqualified Absolute. [26]

Gravity's presence prevents the attainment of absolute zero in interstellar space, filled with energy currents and organizing electronic energies, making space not truly empty despite its scarcity of matter. [27]

Space is measured by time, not merely an intellectual concept, but a reality that transcends material objects and approaches ultimacy with consciousness. [28] Space is the womb of several forms of matter and prematter, not force, energy, or power. [29] Space is not pattern, but gravity controls energy-matter. [30] Space is real and contains motion. [31]

Supreme Power Centers oversee the balance and distribution of energies within our local universe, with light and electricity being secondary manifestations. [32]

Space, the nearest of all nonabsolute things to being absolute, poses a real difficulty in understanding it due to its simultaneous existence within material bodies. [33] The concept of space is neither absolute nor infinite, as it transcends time in unity with the Ultimacy of Deity. [34] Space, nonresponsive to gravity, neutralizes gravity and acts as an equilibrant on explosive actions in the universe. [35] Space is a property of all material bodies. [36]

Through the integrating insight of personality, man perceives time by analysis and space by synthesis, co-ordinating and associating these dissimilar conceptions in a system of associated points perceived by synthesis. [37] Space is a transcendental reality. [38]

The vertical cross section of total space, resembling a Maltese cross centered on Paradise, symbolizes the alternating contraction and expansion of all space in a cosmic respiration cycle. [39] Space is the womb of various forms of matter and prematter, distinct from the origin of physical force and energy. [40]

Pervaded space

The six space levels constitute major divisions of the master universe, each representing different stages of pervaded space and creation. [41]

Space is pervaded by the ancestral space potency of the Unqualified Absolute, extending outward from Paradise through the grand universe and outer regions. [42] Space exerts an antigravity influence upon linear gravity, neutralizing its action and maintaining equilibrium in the universe. [43]

Space expands and contracts in cycles lasting more than a billion Urantia years, with pervaded space reaching the midpoint of expansion while unpervaded space nears the midpoint of contraction. [44]

The mystery of space respiration involves the alternate contraction and expansion of all space, affecting both horizontal pervaded space and vertical unpervaded space reservoirs, forming a V-shaped plane at right angles to peripheral Paradise. [45]

Unpervaded space

In unpervaded space, the Isle of Paradise bestows cosmic force and gravity, guiding the creation of universes through supergravity. [46]

Now in contraction phase, space cycles extend for over a billion Urantia years, with universes expanding and contracting in alternating phases approaching equidistant limits near Paradise. [47] Space unpervaded by forces, energies, and presences existing in. Unpervaded space remains a mystery, existing in contrast to the forces and energies of pervaded space. [48] Space, shaped like an hourglass above and below Paradise, breathes in a mysterious rhythm of contraction and expansion. [49]

The ebb and flow of space respiration impacts both the horizontal expanse of pervaded space and the vertical expanses of unpervaded space, serving as the equilibrium between universe and reservoir space. [50]

Space respiration

The mechanism of space respiration remains unknown as space alternates between contraction and expansion, affecting both horizontal and vertical extensions throughout the vast space reservoirs surrounding Paradise. [51]

The 2 billion year cycle of space respiration encompasses the expansion and contraction phases of the universes, with both pervaded and unpervaded space reaching equidistant points from Paradise over a span of approximately two billion Urantia years. [52] The expanding universe accounts for the impression of outward flight and uniform expansion of all pervaded space. [53]

Space respiration is a mysterious process in which pervaded and unpervaded space alternate between contraction and expansion, flowing through transmuting regulation channels just underneath nether Paradise. [54] Force and energy circulate throughout the universes, extending throughout all pervadable space in agelong cycles of expansion and contraction. [55]

Within the vast expanse of time, Urantia and its universe are embarking on a long and uncharted journey into new space, guided by orderly processes and predetermined paths rather than wandering aimlessly through the cosmos. [56]

Material masses in pervaded space move alternately against and with Paradise gravity during universe expansion and contraction, constituting space work but not power-energy work. [57] The mechanism for space respiration in the vast space reservoirs above and below Paradise remains unknown. [58] The primary motion of space is its respiration - the motion of space itself. [59] The synchronized pulsations of nether Paradise and space respiration encircle all force and energy throughout the universes. [60]
