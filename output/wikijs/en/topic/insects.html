About the types named in The Urantia Book, see links below. Insects with extraordinary appearance quickly spread worldwide, including massive dragonflies and giant cockroaches. [1] Insects are born fully educated, while humans have the power to shape civilization through educational training. [2] Fossils of insects preserved in lithographic stone tell the ancient tale of a once submerged Europe during the time of the Andes Mountains' emergence. [3] Insects are nonprogressive species of early and lower animals in the evolutionary tree of life. [4] The insects evolved resting stages of suspended animation during times of climate change. [5] Misinterpretation of the golden rule led to the worship of insects and other animals. [6]
