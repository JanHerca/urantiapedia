Spirit messages, interpreted through omens and signs, revealed the will of the spirits in a cult of bribery. [1] Civilized races cursed with belief in signs, dodging ill luck through divination, dreams, omens, and superstitious remnants. [2] Jesus' resurrection after 3 days was the only sign he gave to show he is the Son of God. [3] Medicine men put great trust in signs, omens, stars, and spirits of the dead. [4]

No sign shall be given to this faithless and sign-seeking generation, other than that which they already have, and that which they shall see when the Son of Man departs. [5]

Pharisees requested Jesus to give a predetermined sign but he refused, saying no sign shall be given except for what they already have and what they will see when the Son of Man departs. [6]
