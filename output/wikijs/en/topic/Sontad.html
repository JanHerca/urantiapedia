Sontad, the first child of Andon and Fonta, assumed leadership of the clan upon his parents' death, assisted by his wife and eldest sister, and together they buried their deceased family members. [1]

Sontad was fused with his Adjuster on Jerusem, like Andon, Fonta, and some of their children, while most of their descendants only achieved Spirit fusion. [2]
