“do you resemble weaklings?”. [1] God gives power to the faint. [2] Have always contended for equal rights. [3] Indulge in resolutions; the strong act. [4] Mercy toward the weak is divine trait. [5] Minister to. [6] Only brotherhood prevents strong from oppressing the weak. [7] Should not be allowed to take advantage of “golden rulers”. [8]
