The 2nd experiential Trinity includes God the Supreme, God the Ultimate, and the unrevealed Consummator of Universe Destiny, functioning on both personal and superpersonal levels towards the experientialization of Absolute Deity. [1]

The Trinity Absolute, comprising God the Supreme, God the Ultimate, and the unrevealed Consummator of Universe Destiny, represents the ultimate unification of personal, superpersonal, and nonpersonal levels towards the experientialization of Absolute Deity. [2] The Trinity Absolute could only be fully realized after the entire master universe had evolved to completion. [3]

The Trinity Absolute represents the final expression of infinity, the absolute actualization of all potentials, and the ultimate culmination of transcendental and evolutionary-experiential realities. [4] The full function of the Trinity Absolute transcends the space-stage of the master universe. [5]

The actualization of God the Absolute is in consequence of the unification of the Absolute Trinity, leading to the experiential realization of absolute divinity and the unification of absolute meanings on absolute levels. [6] The Trinity Absolute involves all triunity relationships, undeified and deified, making it challenging for personalities to grasp. [7]
