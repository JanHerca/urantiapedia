The early Nordics thought that eclipses were caused by a wolf that devoured a portion of the sun or moon. [1]

The vanishing red man had a prophet within the past hundred years, the Shawnee Tenskwatawa, who predicted the eclipse of the sun in 1806 and denounced the vices of the white man. [2]
