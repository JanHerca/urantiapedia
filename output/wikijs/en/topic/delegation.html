Father

God is neither self-centered nor self-contained; he never ceases to bestow himself upon all self-conscious creatures of the vast universe of universes. [1]

The Universal Father only reserve to himself the exercise of only those powers and that authority which he apparently finds it impossible to delegate or to bestow. [2]

The Father have apparently resigned the exercise of all direct personal power and authority throughout the grand universe, in behalf of the Supreme Creators. [3]

Jesus

Jesus did not do what others could acceptably perform. [4] Jesus installed his brothers as heads of Joseph’s house. [5]
