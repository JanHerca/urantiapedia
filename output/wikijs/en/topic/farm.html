Jesus wanted to have a farm but he did not achieve that dream. Even so, they had at home three cows, four sheep, many chickens, a donkey, pigeons, and a dog. [1] Jesus’ mother’s brother had a farm on the Megiddo road near Sarid. [2] Jude the Jesus brother ran away to his uncle’s farm. [3]
