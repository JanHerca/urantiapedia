The Word of God, bestowed in flesh, is divine sustenance for the soul. [1]

Divine truth can be discerned regardless of its human origin; seek the word of God beyond old records and in the spiritual simplicity of a sincere child. [2] The divine Sons are the “Word of God,” while the children of the Spirit are the “Act of God.”. [3] The Eternal Son is the perfect expression of the Word of God, residing at the center of all creation. [4] The word of God, revealed in the Son as the bread of life, is the united life of the Father and the Son in one gift. [5] Belief in the gospel is belief in Him who sent me, the light of the world, offering salvation and eternal life through truth and mercy. [6] Paul of Tarsus never imagined his letters would be seen as the word of God when he wrote them. [7] Take a stand against traditional scriptures regarded as the word of God and embrace a spirit of adventurous and progressive faith.
