Skulls became potent fetishes, believed to hold powerful spiritual significance. [1] Ancient medicine men performed trephining surgeries to release headache spirits, evolving from spiritual to physical healing practices. [2] The skull types found in the graves of ancient Mesopotamia evidence the thorough blending of races by 6000 B.C. [3]
