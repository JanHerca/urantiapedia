Transit personalities can utilize all three universally distributed lines of energy, each with a velocity of 186,280 miles per second, to attain varying speeds on journeys exceeding 559,000 miles per second, affected by mass, proximity of matter, and universe power circuits. [1]

Adjuster transit time from Divinington to Urantia is 117 hours, 42 minutes, and 7 seconds, with practically no time intervening between dispatch and appearance in chosen subjects' minds. [2]

Divine Counselor traveled 200,000 light years in 109 days, while our order of creation intercommunicates instantly across vast distances within the superuniverse of Orvonton. [3]

It is impossible to accurately determine, simultaneously, the exact location and velocity of a moving object; any attempt at measurement of either inevitably involves change in the other. [4]

The seraphic transports achieve speeds reaching almost 559,000 miles per second by superimposing energy and power velocities, utilizing universal lines of energy. [5]

Our astronomic estimates of velocities within our superuniverse and its associate realms are reliable, but become wholly unreliable when applied to the outer realms of outer space due to various factors of error and distortions. [6]

Top speed

  • The transportation system on Jerusem allows material beings to travel at speeds ranging from two to five hundred miles per hour, with transport birds flying at a steady 100 mph. [7]
  • The transportation system on Jerusem allows for speeds ranging from two to five hundred miles per hour, with the air mechanisms of the Material Sons traveling at around five hundred miles per hour. [8]
  • Midwayers can reach speeds of 372,560 miles per second, utilizing gravity for instantaneous transit, unlike angels and men restricted by time and space. [9]
  • Nonenseraphimed beings are limited to velocities of 186,280 miles per second, while others can travel at double or triple that speed. [10]
  • Enseraphimed ambassadors can reach far-distant universes at velocities up to 558,840 miles per second. [11]
  • Solitary Messengers, with a velocity of 840 billion miles/second, are uniquely suited for situations requiring personalized service to avoid time loss and synchronize with universal currents. [12]
  • Gravity traversers move instantaneously through the universe, surpassing the limitations of time and space experienced by angels, men, and other personal beings. [13]
