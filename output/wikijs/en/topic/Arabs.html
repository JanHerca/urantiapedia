Arabs made use for adoption ceremony of the oath taken while the hand of the candidate rested upon the generative organ of the tribal native. [1]

Swarthy nomadic tribes of modern Arabs are descendants of a blend of blue race settled in old Persian peninsula, a part of yellow race, and some part of Adamite races. [2] The Arabs and the natives of Africa are among the more recent pastoral peoples. [3]
