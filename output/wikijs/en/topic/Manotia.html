Manotia, a supreme seraphim who has achieved all seven seraphic services, now serves as the associate chief of seraphim on Urantia. [1]

Manotia, the seraphic hero of the Lucifer rebellion, displayed unparalleled courage and loyalty during the tumultuous events on Jerusem, ultimately saving the majority of the administrator angels from falling into the rebel ranks. [2]
