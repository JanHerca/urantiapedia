The Supreme Power Directors operate collectively and individually on cosmic phenomena below the level of gravity energy, akin to the Seven Supreme Executives and the Seven Master Spirits in their respective domains. [1]

The Seven Supreme Power Directors, personalized by the Seven Master Spirits, are eternally united in functional subservience, working in a unique partnership to coordinate physical and spiritual energies. [2]

The Seven Supreme Power Directors oversee the power regulation of the superuniverses individually from peripheral Paradise, while collectively administering Havona from their force-focal headquarters. [3]

The Seven Supreme Power Directors have existed from near eternity, overseeing the creation of over ten billion associates with the guidance of the Master Spirits. [4] The seven Supreme Power Directors are integrated in personality registers. [5] The seven Supreme Power Directors manipulate energy, resisting the pull of physical gravity, solely focused on power and energy modification. [6] The Supreme Power Directors operate independently from all governing bodies in the universe. [7] The Supreme Power Directors are part of God the Sevenfold. [8]

The Seven Supreme Power Directors, created by the Seven Master Spirits, regulate physical energy in the grand universe - the first semimaterial progeny derived from true spirit ancestry. [9]

The power potential of the Almighty Supreme is centered in the Seven Supreme Power Directors, who collaborate with the Seven Master Spirits to manifest certain evolutionary potentialities in the universe. [10] The Seven Supreme Power Directors recruit Solitary Messengers as explorers of undirected assignment. [11] The seven Supreme Power Directors resist gravity to manipulate energy, having no connection to its origin or physical nature. [12] The Seven Supreme Power Directors are the semimaterial, quasi-physical regulators of the grand universe, created by the Seven Master Spirits. [13]

The Seven Supreme Power Directors maintain force-focal headquarters on peripheral Paradise, overseeing the powerful energies that flow to the seven superuniverses. [14]

The Seven Supreme Power Directors, in collaboration with the Seven Master Spirits, created the Havona Servitals, embodying a pattern for creation seen throughout the evolutionary universes. [15]
