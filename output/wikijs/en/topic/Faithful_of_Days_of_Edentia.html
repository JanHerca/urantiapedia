The Faithful of Days is the representative of the Trinity of Paradise and Immanuel in Edentia. The Mount Assembly of Edentia is the residence of the Faithful of Days. [1] His residence on Edentia is comparatively modest relative to that of the ruling Vorondadeks. [2] He advised the Most Highs to allow Lucifer's rebels a free course of action. [3] The Faithful of Days presented Michael's plan of salvation to Lucifer, but was rejected. [4] Ascending mortals meet on Edentia with the Faithful of Days, the first Trinity-born personality they know. [5]
