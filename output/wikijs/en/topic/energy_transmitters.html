Functions

A battery of one thousand energy transmitters provides the initial momentum for the seraphic departure. [1]

The Constellation Fathers dispached a resolution supporting Van against Lucifer's rebellion but the verdict failed to reach Van because the order had been blocked in a relay energy transmitter due to Urantia's isolation. Energy transmitters can receive and transmit intelligence, but they cannot initiate communication. [2] When energy is to be diverted to a new circuit, the transmitters deploy themselves in a line along the desired energy path. [3] These beings, together with the energy transformers, are indispensable to mortals on nonbreathing planets. [4] They are living superconductors; they can actually induce an increased energy flow in the desired direction and augment feeble energies. [5] They provide the emergency lines of communication in the local systems and on the individual planets. [6] They can render a distant scene “visible” as well as a distant sound “audible.”. [7]

Nature

They appear in Uversa personality register. [8] They are one of types of Master Physical Controller. [9] They seem fully able to receive our communications but quite powerless to make response. [10] They are totally automatic and mechanical in response to the impulses of their superiors and in reaction; they are nonpersonal. [11]
