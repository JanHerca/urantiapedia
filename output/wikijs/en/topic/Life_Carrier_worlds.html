The Melchizedeks oversee the Life Carrier worlds in the 4th Salvington circuit, which consist of seven primary spheres, each with specific purposes and characteristics, surrounded by satellites focused on different aspects of life and evolution. [1]

The central emplacement of adjutant mind-spirits on World Number One is devoted to the study of universal life in all of its known phases of manifestation. [2]
