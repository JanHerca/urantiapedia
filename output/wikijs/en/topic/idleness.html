Be mindful not to diminish the self-respect of those who are timid and fearful, for idleness can be destructive. [1] Some continental nations are beginning to foster a new social disgust for both idleness and unearned wealth. [2] Escape the evils of inaction and embrace the power of living faith to overcome fear and achieve greatness. [3] Jesus did not teach nor countenance idleness or improvidence. [4] Jesus remained industrious while awaiting his hour, working alongside his brother James in the boatshop for several months. [5] A society cannot progress if idleness is tolerated and self-sustaining work is not enforced. [6] Supporting his family safeguarded Jesus against idle meditation and mystic tendencies as he grew up to manhood. [7] Strength is achieved through action, charm through moderation; the weak linger in resolutions, but the strong make their mark. [8]
