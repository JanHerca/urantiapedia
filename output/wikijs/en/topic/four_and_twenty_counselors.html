Advisory supervisors of rebellion-isolated worlds. [1] All were real leaders on Urantia; all Jerusem citizens, except Machiventa. [2] Also known as Urantia advisory council. [3] Approve morontia progressors’ plans for future course. [4] Assigned Sentinels are ex officio heads of. [5] Direct many matters resulting from Michael’s bestowal. [6] Established by Michael. [7] Function somewhat like a legislative branch. [8] Last of group assembled 1900 years ago. [9] Meet around throne of presiding archangels. [10] Nominated by cabinet of Lanaforge. [11] One always present on Urantia as resident governor general. [12] Pass upon ascenders’ attainment of socialization. [13] Personal agents of Michael and Gabriel on Jerusem. [14] Recruited from all Urantia races. [15] Similar commissions exist for all quarantined worlds. [16] They are exempt from the ascension regime for the time being. [17]
